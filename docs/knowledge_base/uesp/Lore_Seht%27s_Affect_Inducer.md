# Seht's Affect Inducer

Seht's Affect Inducer is a device that could manipulate a person's emotions. Created by [Sotha Sil](Lore_Sotha_Sil.md), the device was originally entrusted to [Barilzar](Lore_Barilzar.md).

Around [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), High Magistrate [Rinwaray](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Rinwaray) of The [Divine Prosecution](Lore_Divine_Prosecution.md) (posing under the identity of Penewen with an enchanted necklace) manipulated [Telenger the Artificer](Lore_Telenger_the_Artificer.md) to use his teleportation device, The [Hyperagonal Locational Determinator](Lore_Hyperagonal_Locational_Determinator.md) on her to teleport her into the [Clockwork City](Lore_Clockwork_City.md). She planned to obtain the Affect Inducer from the city so she could use it on Proxy Queen Alwinarwe for her own political gain and to revert the [Summerset Isles](Lore_Summerset_Isles.md) to isolationism.

Her efforts were stopped when [Razum-dar](Lore_Razum-dar.md) caught wind of her plot and followed her tracks through the Clockwork City, the [Evergloam](Lore_Evergloam.md), [Artaeum](Lore_Artaeum.md) (accidentally) and eventually caught her in [Alinor](Lore_Alinor_(city).md) where he managed to subdue her.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup>
