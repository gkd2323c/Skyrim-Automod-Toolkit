# All-Maker Stones


All of the stones are initially tainted by the evil power of the [dragon priest](Skyrim_Dragon_Priest.md) [Miraak](Skyrim_Miraak_(person).md), which causes the inhabitants of Solstheim to become entranced when they pray at them. After cleansing the stones via the [Bend Will](Skyrim_Bend_Will.md) [shout](Skyrim_Dragon_Shouts.md), you may activate them to receive special powers. Powers must be regained from the stones after use. All except the Tree Stone can be cleansed before you finish [At the Summit of Apocrypha](Skyrim_At_the_Summit_of_Apocrypha.md).

## Related Quests
- **[The Fate of the Skaal](Skyrim_The_Fate_of_the_Skaal.md)**: Find a way to free the citizens of [Skaal Village](Skyrim_Skaal_Village.md).
- **[Cleansing the Stones](Skyrim_Cleansing_the_Stones.md)**: Use the [Bend Will](Skyrim_Bend_Will.md) [shout](Skyrim_Dragon_Shouts.md) to cleanse each of the remaining All-Maker Stones.
- **[At the Summit of Apocrypha](Skyrim_At_the_Summit_of_Apocrypha.md)**: Confront [Miraak](Skyrim_Miraak_(person).md) at the summit of [Apocrypha](Skyrim_Apocrypha.md) and defeat him once and for all.

## Dialogue
The entranced people working on the stones will repeat the following words mechanically:

*"Here in his shrine."* (*"Here in his temple."*) *"That they have forgotten."* *"Here do we toil."* *"That we might remember."* *"By night we reclaim."* (*"Here we reclaim."*) *"What by day was stolen."* (*"What faithless minds have stolen."*) *"Far from ourselves."* *"He grows ever near to us."* *"Our eyes once were blinded."* *"Now through him do we see."* *"Our hands once were idle."* *"Now through them does he speak."* *"And when the world shall listen."* *"And when the world shall see."* *"And when the world remembers."* *"That world will cease to be."* If you activate any of the stones before cleansing them, you will be in an entranced state, and [Miraak](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Miraak) will repeat the following words mechanically:

*"Here in my shrine."* (*"Here in my temple."*) *"That you have forgotten."* *"Here do you toil."* *"That you might remember."* *"By night you reclaim."* *"What faithless minds have stolen."* *"Far from yourself."* *"I grow ever near to you."* *"Your eyes once were blinded."* *"Now through me do you see."* *"Your hands once were idle."* *"Now through them do I speak."* *"And when the world shall listen."* *"And when the world shall see."* *"And when the world remembers."* *"That world will cease to be."*
## Notes
- During the quest [The Fate of the Skaal](Skyrim_The_Fate_of_the_Skaal.md), the [Wind Stone](Skyrim_Wind_Stone.md) is cleansed of Miraak's influence by using the Bend Will shout. In [Cleansing the Stones](Skyrim_Cleansing_the_Stones.md), the Bend Will shout continues to be used to cleanse four of the other stones. The final stone, the [Tree Stone](Skyrim_Temple_of_Miraak.md), is freed once the [main quest](Skyrim_Dragonborn_Quests.md#Main_Quest) is completed. It is also possible to cleanse all stones except the Tree Stone before starting the quests The Fate of the Skaal or Cleansing the Stones.
- When [sleeping](Skyrim_Sleeping.md) on Solstheim, if you haven't completed Cleansing the Stones and you're either outdoors or in a dwelling, you may wake up at a randomly selected All-Maker Stone, as long as the selected stone is still tainted. - The first time you sleep in an appropriate location, this random selection is guaranteed to occur, and as long as the selected stone has not already been cleansed, you will awake there. Thereafter, there is a 33% chance that a stone will be randomly selected for this purpose.
- The Water Stone is not included in the list of stones you can wake up at.
- If you activate any of the stones before cleansing them, you will be put into the same state as the rest of the occupants. However, unlike everyone else, you can exert your will and break free from the trance.
- Unlike the [Standing Stones](Skyrim_Standing_Stones.md) in mainland Skyrim, you can have all the powers of every All-Maker Stone on Solstheim at the same time; powers from previously activated stones are not removed from your Powers menu when activating another stone. You can activate them to get the powers at most once per day.
- If you command a follower to use one of the stones, they will begin to worship it, like others at the stone. In this case, it is possible for the follower to remain worshipping the stone permanently.
- Before you cleanse the stones, if you talk to someone who only works on the stones at night, they will say "The Master calls." as a reference to [Miraak](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Miraak) when they head to the designated stone each night. They will use the same quotes as the entranced people until morning.
- The powers granted by the stones work underwater.
- All stones, except for the Tree Stone, will be marked as "clear" on the map once you cleanse them. The Tree Stone will be marked as "clear" when the Temple is cleared out.
- The book [*Aevar Stone-Singer*](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Aevar_Stone-Singer) is about the stones.
- The All-Maker Stones also appear in *[Bloodmoon](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Bloodmoon:Bloodmoon)* as the [Sacred Stones](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Bloodmoon:Landmarks#Sacred_Stones).
