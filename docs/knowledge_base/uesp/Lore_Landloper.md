Landlopers are scaled, cervine-like creatures believed to be the product of ancient [Argonian](Lore_Argonian.md) [flesh-shaping](Lore_Flesh_Sculpture.md) [magic](Lore_Magic.md). The Stone-Nest tribe of the isle of [Solstice](Lore_Solstice_(island).md), once known for manipulating flesh as skillfully as they shaped [stone](Lore_The_Stone.md), crafted these creatures through powerful arcane practices. Though this art, rooted in traditions far older than the [Duskfall](Lore_Duskfall.md), has long been lost, the Landlopers remained as living echoes of those forgotten rituals.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SNL-1)</sup>

Muzah-Tei of the [Antiquarian Circle](Lore_Antiquarian_Circle.md) recorded his thoughts on these beings in her work *Stone-Nest Scribbles*.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SNL-1)</sup> The Stone-Nest tribe was known for their use of ancient magicks to alter living beings. Some of their arts were believed by some to stem from [Daedric](Lore_Daedra.md) origins. They were used to create [Vahath Behemoths](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Vahath_Behemoth), another type of creatures born of magical alteration.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Morlia-2)</sup>
