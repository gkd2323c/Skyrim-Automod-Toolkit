# Night of Green Fire


This night appears to have been part of a broader effort by the Thalmor, whose agents were also harassing and possibly murdering refugees in [Balfiera](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Balfiera) and Sentinel around this time; the victims were all of mixed blood or had associations considered by the Dominion to be "unclean."<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TIC-2)</sup>
