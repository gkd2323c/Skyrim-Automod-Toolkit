# Order of the Braided Vine

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e3/ON-misc-Druid_Laurel.jpg/350px-ON-misc-Druid_Laurel.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-misc-Druid_Laurel.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-misc-Druid_Laurel.jpg) Druids of the Stonelore Circle The Order of the Braided Vine was a [Breton Knightly Order](Lore_Breton_Knightly_Orders.md) active in the [Systres Archipelago](Lore_Systres_Archipelago.md) in the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md). It was a minor Knightly Order founded and primarily composed of the [Druids](Lore_Druids.md) of the [Stonelore Circle](Lore_Stonelore_Circle.md). Members of the Order were dedicated to the protection of the landmarks and places sacred to the Druids, such as certain groves and various island locations. It is unknown if this order was created to pique interest in druidism, or if it was a genuine attempt to meld their lifestyle into modern [Breton](Lore_Breton.md) culture.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SKO-1)</sup>
