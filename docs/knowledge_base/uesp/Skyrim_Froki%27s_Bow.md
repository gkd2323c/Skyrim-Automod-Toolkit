[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/ba/SR-item-Froki%27s_Bow.jpg/200px-SR-item-Froki%27s_Bow.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Froki%27s_Bow.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Froki%27s_Bow.jpg) Froki's Bow [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/73/SR-interior-Graywinter_Watch.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Graywinter_Watch.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Graywinter_Watch.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Graywinter_Watch.jpg) Froki's Bow, with a nearby dead bandit Froki's Bow is a [long bow](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Long_Bow) that was made by and was the weapon of choice of [Froki Whetted-Blade](Skyrim_Froki_Whetted-Blade.md) that he lost within [Graywinter Watch](Skyrim_Graywinter_Watch.md). The bow has an enchantment that does ten points of [stamina damage](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Stamina_Damage). [Tempering](Skyrim_Grindstone.md) Froki's Bow requires an [iron ingot](Skyrim_Iron_Ingot.md) and the [Arcane Blacksmith](Skyrim_Arcane_Blacksmith.md) perk.

Froki will send you in the direction of his old bow as part of [Kyne's Sacred Trials](Skyrim_Kyne%27s_Sacred_Trials.md), when he sends you to face the final guardian spirit, the [Guardian Troll Spirit](Skyrim_Guardian_Troll_Spirit.md). Towards the back of Graywinter Watch, the bow can be found upon a barrel, near a few arrows and a copy of the [Archery](Skyrim_Archery.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[Vernaccus and Bourlor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Vernaccus_and_Bourlor)*. Retrieving the bow and taking it to Froki after completing the quest will allow you to show it to him, whereupon he will reminisce about his old weapon and allow you to keep it: *"I don't believe it. I made that bow myself. Been through some messes with it, let me tell you. You know what? I want you to keep it. My great hunts are behind me, but yours are yet to come."*
