# Hero of Sovngarde

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/87/SR-npc-Hero_of_Sovngarde.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Hero_of_Sovngarde.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Hero_of_Sovngarde.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Hero_of_Sovngarde.jpg) Sparring match in the Hall of Valor Several **Heroes of Sovngarde** are found in the [Hall of Valor](Skyrim_Hall_of_Valor.md), located in [Sovngarde](Skyrim_Sovngarde_(place).md). All of them are [essential](Skyrim_Essential_NPCs.md) [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [warriors](Skyrim_CombatWarrior1H.md). They can be found sitting for a drink, listening to the [bards](Skyrim_Bard.md) play and [conversing](#Conversations) with each other. From time to time, those of the Heroes who belong to the [Sovngarde Sparring Faction](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:MQSovngarde Sparring Faction) will [offer](#Sparring_Conversations) one of their fellows to satisfy their thirst for battle in a friendly [combat](Skyrim_Combat.md) match in the west wing of the Hall.

## Dialogue
All of the Heroes share the same dialogue:

- *"Why does [Shor](Lore_Shor.md) forbid us to face [Alduin](Skyrim_Alduin.md)? I fear no [worm](Lore_Dragon.md), World-Eater though he be."*
- *"Far you've travelled, your trials long, to taste of Sovngarde's sweet delights."*
- *"Seek ye food or song unending? Fighting, drinking, fellowship and boasting? Find welcome here, and warm your [soul](Lore_Soul.md)."*
- *"[Dragonborn](Skyrim_Dovahkiin.md)! Have you come to cleanse Sovngarde at long last of Alduin's foul mist?"*
- *"I long to fight that foul worm, but by Shor's command we curb our wrath."*
- *"No gloom can hold my heart for long in Shor's bless'd hall, where no shadows lie."*
- *"A cup, Dragonborn, to death and glory!"*
- *"Take your ease, let troubles fade."*
- *"By Shor's command we curb our wrath, so we feast and sing, til the final doom."*
- *"Shor's power protects this hall; but beyond is fear, brought by Alduin."*
- *"Shor's high seat stands empty; his mien is too bright for mortal eyes."*
- *"Hail, Dragonborn. That honor is also mine - to our shared [birthright](Lore_Dragonborn.md) you'll bring new glory!"* (this line is said by exclusively four different NPCs, one male and three females)

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/59/SR-npc-Hero_of_Sovngarde_Male2.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Hero_of_Sovngarde_Male2.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Hero_of_Sovngarde_Male2.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Hero_of_Sovngarde_Male2.jpg) Hero of Sovngarde This male Hero prefers to watch sparring matches between his fellow Heroes rather than taking part in them. Thus he stays in the west wing most of the time, wandering from one spectator's seat to another or sipping mead. He rarely leaves fighting area for a brief feast at the table by the grand fire in the central part of the Hall. On his way back to the sparring ring area he may linger for a moment at the fire warming his hands or raising a mug with another Hero, but then he rushes back to await yet another combat match. This Hero wears [studded armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Studded_Armor) with a pair of [hide boots](Skyrim_Hide_Boots.md). He carries both a leveled [one-handed weapon](Skyrim_One-Handed_Weapons.md) and a leveled [two-handed weapon](Skyrim_Two-Handed_Weapons.md), both of which may be up to [honed ancient Nord](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Honed_Ancient_Nord) in quality. He also has a fifty-percent chance to carry an [iron shield](Skyrim_Iron_Shield.md).
