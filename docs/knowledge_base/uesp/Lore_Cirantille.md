Cirantille was a respected [Altmer](Lore_Altmer.md) scholar <sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Ddt H-1)</sup> active during the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md) of the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOCira-2)</sup> Her works covered numerous subjects, often focusing on the cultures of the different races of [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md). Cirantille's writings were from a pro-Altmeri stance and often were prejudiced against, or disparaged the subjects of her work.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Cira Books-3)</sup>

## History
There is little known about her early life, but from her works, she is known to have travelled around Tamriel. These places included [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md)<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-AA-4)</sup>, [Elsweyr](Lore_Elsweyr.md)<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-MWat CM-5)</sup>, [Wrothgar](Lore_Wrothgar.md)<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Tof WACT-6)</sup>. She made it clear in her writings her distaste for the province of [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md), describing it as a "seeping pustule on the buttocks of Tamriel".<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-WWo BM-7)</sup> It appears prior to returning to [Auridon](Lore_Auridon.md), Cirantille resided in [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md), for example in *[Moon Worship among the Cat-Men](Lore_Moon_Worship_among_the_Cat-Men.md)* she speaks of the [Mane](Lore_Mane.md) as a "key official to bribe [...] should hostilities occur to our southern border"<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-MWat CM-5)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-Ma Lot H-8)</sup>. At some point after this, Cirantille joined the [Aldmeri Dominion](Lore_Aldmeri_Dominion.md), as her later works relating to the [Bosmer](Lore_Bosmer.md) and [Kwama](Lore_Kwama.md) make mention of this fact.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-Sa S-9)</sup><sup>[[10]](#cite_note-Co K-10)</sup>

In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) she resided in [Skywatch](Lore_Skywatch.md) and held talks and readings of her works for her Altmeri audience.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOCira-2)</sup> During this time period she was commissioned by [King Kurog](Lore_Kurog.md) to provide copies of her books to contribute to the libraries of the newly rebuilt [Orsinium](Lore_Orsinium.md).<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-ESOArzorag-11)</sup> However, the shipment was attacked by [Ogres](Lore_Ogre.md) led by [Urkazbur](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Urkazbur) and her books were defaced and destroyed. In retaliation, she had a contract placed on Urkazbur along with the recovery of the books. The ogre was slain by adventurers and some copies of *[The Ogres of Wrothgar: A Continuing Treatise](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:The_Ogres_of_Wrothgar__A_Continuing_Treatise)* were salvaged and returned to Cirantille.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-ESOSS-12)</sup>
