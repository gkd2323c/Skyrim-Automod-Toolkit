[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/78/SR-npc-Ma%27tasarr.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Ma%27tasarr.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ma%27tasarr.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ma%27tasarr.jpg) Ma'tasarr **Ma'tasarr**, a [Khajiit](Skyrim_Khajiit.md) [citizen](Skyrim_Citizen_(class).md), is one of the hunters who shows up in [Bloated Man's Grotto](Skyrim_Bloated_Man%27s_Grotto.md) hunting [Sinding](Skyrim_Sinding.md). He is clad in [iron armor](Skyrim_Iron_Armor.md), and a pair of [leather bracers](Skyrim_Leather_Bracers.md) and [boots](Skyrim_Leather_Boots.md). An [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md), an [iron greatsword](Skyrim_Iron_Greatsword.md), a leveled [healing potion](Skyrim_Potion_of_Healing.md), and some gold change can be found in his person.

When you arrive, he is already dead at the first campfire, presumably from wounds sustained fighting Sinding.
