Farengar Secret-Fire was the [Nord](Lore_Nord.md) Court Wizard of [Jarl Balgruuf the Greater](Lore_Balgruuf_the_Greater.md) in [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), and assisted Balgruuf with his magical affairs. His duties also required him to assist in the containment of the [Ebony Blade](Lore_Ebony_Blade.md), that would later be found by Balgruuf's son Nelkir.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup> He was uninterested in the [Skyrim Civil War](Lore_Skyrim_Civil_War.md) and did not support the Empire. He was dedicated to the [College of Winterhold](Lore_College_of_Winterhold.md) and focused on furthering the cause of magical research.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-PRIMA-2)</sup><sup>:851</sup> He was known to be reclusive and arrogant, but was also eminent in Daedric studies.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-SRTAG-3)</sup>

Farengar was contacted by one of the few surviving members of the [Blades](Lore_Blades.md), [Delphine](Lore_Delphine.md), about retrieving the [Dragonstone](Lore_Dragonstone.md) from [Bleak Falls Barrow](Lore_Bleak_Falls_Barrow.md). He would receive assistance in this task from the [Last Dragonborn](Lore_Last_Dragonborn.md), who retrieved the stone for him.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Barrow-4)</sup> During one of the Dragonborn's visits to Dragonsreach, Farengar taught them how to use [Arcane Enchanters](Lore_Enchanting.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-EC-5)</sup>

Farengar was also involved in the study of [Dragons](Lore_Dragon.md), and had particular interest in the idea of studying a live Dragon. He would get a brief opportunity to do so when the Last Dragonborn captured [Odahviing](Lore_Odahviing.md) in Dragonsreach, from whom he attempted to take blood or scale samples.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Far-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-7)</sup> Sometime around 4E 201, it is rumored he assisted the [Sheathed Blades](Lore_Sheathed_Blades.md) in locating the hideout or an Oblivion portal opened by Boethian cultists.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-SRTAG-3)</sup>
