Azra Nightwielder was the original Shadowmage, and a pioneer of [Shadow Magic](Lore_Shadow_Magic.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DBNeloth-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-WUSESO-2)</sup> He was the first to discover that shadows were not merely an absence of light, but the reflection of possible worlds created by forces in conflict.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-SOS-3)</sup>

References to Azra are unreliable, as even texts in the [Psijic Order](Lore_Psijic_Order.md)'s archives on [Artaeum](Lore_Artaeum.md) have been tampered with Shadow Magic. However, according to them, Azra was born somewhere in the early Second Era in the [Western Reach](Lore_Western_Reach.md), which lies between [Craglorn](Lore_Craglorn.md), [Bangkorai](Lore_Bangkorai.md), and [the Reach](Lore_The_Reach.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-LATD-4)</sup> Despite the Order's tampered estimate of this date of birth, shadow mages have been seen dating back to the [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-SKCraglorn-5)</sup>

## History

### Second Era
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/be/ON-interior-Xi-Tsei_08.jpg/200px-ON-interior-Xi-Tsei_08.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-interior-Xi-Tsei_08.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-interior-Xi-Tsei_08.jpg) Xi-Tsei Azra sought to refine his skill in Shadow Magic, and one day attempted to manipulate his own shadow to such an extent that all possible versions of himself would become melded into a singular existence. Sometime around the founding of the [Mages Guild](Lore_Mages_Guild.md) in [2E 230](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_230),<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-LATD-4)</sup> [Redguard](Lore_Redguard.md) mercenaries, fearing the power he would command should he succeed, surrounded him in northern [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md). During the fight, Azra lost control of his magic and created a large crater in the ground. People thought he had perished.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-SOS-3)</sup> A small village grew around this crater; it was named [Azra's Crossing](Lore_Azra%27s_Crossing.md) in homage to the wizard.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Shadowkey-6)</sup>

Circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), a [nisswo](Lore_Nisswo.md)'s apprentice in [Blackwood](Lore_Blackwood.md) sought to carry out his master's dream of uncovering the Nightwielder's Tome, as unfinished business led to his soul to stay on the mortal plane. The Tome is an artifact written by Azra Nightwielder in runes that cannot be read by mortal eyes. The nisswo see things without using their eyes, and the Argonian speculated that perhaps Azra used a similar method in its writing. The Argonian tasked an [adventurer](Lore_The_Vestige.md) with retrieving the tome from the [Xi-Tsei](Lore_Xi-Tsei.md) [Xanmeer](Lore_Xanmeer.md), which was taken over by the Sul-Xan, an Argonian tribe of [Dagonites](Lore_Mehrunes_Dagon.md). The apprentice gave Azra's Tome to the [Clutch of Nisswo](Lore_Clutch_of_Nisswo.md) for safekeeping.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ONWUS-7)</sup>

### Third Era
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/2e/SK-place-Fearfrost_Crystal.jpg/200px-SK-place-Fearfrost_Crystal.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SK-place-Fearfrost_Crystal.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SK-place-Fearfrost_Crystal.jpg) The crystal within Fearfrost Caverns [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/95/SK-npc-Azra_Nightwielder.jpg/200px-SK-npc-Azra_Nightwielder.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SK-npc-Azra_Nightwielder.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SK-npc-Azra_Nightwielder.jpg) Azra Nightwielder circa [3E 397](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_397) Many years later, during the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md), an [Umbra' Keth](Lore_Umbra%27_Keth.md) (Shadow of Conflict) formed due to the ongoing [War of the Bend'r-mahk](Lore_War_of_the_Bend%27r-mahk.md). In [3E 397](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_397), an unknown [hero](Lore_Hero.md) (Soul of Conflict) on a quest to destroy the Umbra' Keth discovered a large crystal in the depths of the Fearfrost Caverns. Inside this crystal was Azra Nightwielder, who was suffering from memory loss and couldn't recall his identity. Azra formed an umbric connection with the hero, allowing him to communicate through the hero's shadow. The hero promised to help him uncover his identity. The second incarnation of [Pergan Asuul](Lore_Pergan_Asuul.md), a shadowmage who was trying to strengthen the Umbra' Keth by battling with the hero repeatedly, revealed the mage's name with his dying breath, which helped to restore Azra's memory.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Shadowkey-6)</sup>

Azra and the hero met again in the crater in Azra's Crossing, where he provided vital information pertaining to how the [Star Teeth](Lore_Star_Teeth.md) were needed to defeat the Umbra' Keth. The shadowmage entered the Umbra' Keths lair, the [Crypt of Hearts](Lore_Crypt_of_Hearts.md), to distract the beast to buy the hero some time to prepare, and placed some items throughout the Crypts to aid the hero when they were arrive. First is his powerful mace for the purpose of bludgeoning the beast,<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-SKAzra Mace-8)</sup> and second are scrolls containing the secrets to learning his spells (Azra's Sustenance for healing, and Azra's Wrath for clearing a large area of enemies)<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-SKAzra Sustenance-9)</sup> The time and items Azra provided proved valuable, as the hero managed to defeat the Umbra' Keth. Afterwards, Azra continued his journey, claiming that the shadows called back to him, and has not been sighted since.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Shadowkey-6)</sup>

### Legacy
Although less well known, Azra was also a pioneer in the field of enchanting staffs, and, although his methods grew to be considered rudimentary by the standards of the [Fourth Era](Lore_Fourth_Era.md), he was still considered a genius in his field. Many of his [staves](Lore_Staves.md) can still be found scattered across [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md). Master [Neloth](Lore_Neloth.md) of [House Telvanni](Lore_House_Telvanni.md) is a collector of Azra's work, and has admitted to have learned a lot from studying his staves.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DBNeloth-1)</sup>

The plant [Azra Root](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Azra_Root) was named after him.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-TBOTGT-10)</sup><sup>[[11]](#cite_note-SKAR-11)</sup>

## Artifacts

### Azra's Mace
[Azra's Mace](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Azra%27s_Mace) is a powerful [mace](Lore_Weapons.md) created by Azra Nightwielder that could inflict more damage than weapons of even [Daedric](Lore_Daedric.md) quality. In appearance, it is a dark metal mace engraved with a runic 'A', and a runic 'N', which are the initials of Azra's name. It was found by the [Soul of Conflict](Lore_Soul_of_Conflict.md) in the [Crypt of Hearts](Lore_Crypt_of_Hearts.md) circa [3E 397](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_397).

### Azra's Bandage
[Azra's Bandage](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Azra%27s_Bandage) is a ring with magical healing properties. The ring was kept incased in a shadow-infused crystal by [Pergan Asuul](Lore_Pergan_Asuul.md) within [Earthtear Caverns](Lore_Earthtear_Caverns.md) before being retrieved by the [Soul of Conflict](Lore_Soul_of_Conflict.md) circa [3E 397](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_397). After it was retrieved by the hero, Pergan Asuul lamented that it was the handiwork of Azra and was not meant for hands such as theirs.

### Azra's Staffs
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f2/SR-item-Azra%27s_Staff_of_Daedric_Command.jpg/150px-SR-item-Azra%27s_Staff_of_Daedric_Command.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Azra%27s_Staff_of_Daedric_Command.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Azra%27s_Staff_of_Daedric_Command.jpg) One of Azra's Staffs [Azra's Staffs](Lore_Azra%27s_Staffs.md) are a collection of [staves](Lore_Staff.md) created and [enchanted](Lore_Enchanting.md) by the original Shadowmage Azra Nightwielder, whose advances in [magic](Lore_Magic.md) were legendary. In addition to his role in the development of [Shadow magic](Lore_Shadow_Magic.md), he was also known as a pioneer of staff enchantment. His techniques were considered rudimentary by the [Fourth Era](Lore_Fourth_Era.md), but his genius remained evident throughout the ages. While Azra himself wielded a personal staff, its properties are unknown.

### Nightwielder's Tome
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b1/ON-item-Nightwielder%27s_Tome.jpg/150px-ON-item-Nightwielder%27s_Tome.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-item-Nightwielder%27s_Tome.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-item-Nightwielder%27s_Tome.jpg) Nightwielder's Tome circa 2E 582 The [Nightwielder's Tome](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Nightwielder%27s_Tome) is a book written by Azra Nightwielder in runes that cannot be read by mortal eyes. Attempting to study the contents of the freezing pages will cause your skin to crawl and your vision to worsen. It was theorized that Azra managed to write the tome by being able to see with more than just his eyes.

## Notes
- [1](#note_nb1) While Azra's race is never explicitly written, his skin tone and appearance is consistent with Breton models in Shadowkey. He is far too tan to be a Nord, while his appearance does not match Redguard player or NPC models either.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-Az Comp Bret-12)</sup><sup>[[13]](#cite_note-Az Comp Race Text-13)</sup> Reachmen and Imperials are not present in Shadowkey, making the possibility or intent for these races unlikely.<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-SKRaces-14)</sup> Logistically, with his birthplace being somewhere between Craglorn, Bangkorai and the Reach it is possible for him to be a Breton.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-LATD-4)</sup> In the game manual for Shadowkey, an image of Azra Nightwielder is also shown next to the Breton race entry.<sup>[[15]](#cite_note-SKManual-15)</sup>
