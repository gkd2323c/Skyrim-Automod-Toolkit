# Spiders

This article is about creatures added by Dragonborn. For frostbite spiders in the base game, see [Frostbite Spider](Skyrim_Frostbite_Spider.md). For spider scrolls, see [Spider Scrolls](Skyrim_Spider_Scrolls.md). For the faction, see [Spiders (faction)](Skyrim_Spiders_(faction).md).

## Albino Spiders
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/59/SR-creature-Albino_Spider.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Albino_Spider.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Albino_Spider.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Albino_Spider.jpg) Albino Spider **Albino Spiders** are a species of spiders that are found on the island of Solstheim. They possess various subspecies including [flame spiders](#Flame_Spiders), [frost spiders](#Frost_Spiders), [oil spiders](#Oil_Spiders), [poison spiders](#Poison_Spiders), and [shock spiders](#Shock_Spiders). Albino spiders are physically similar to these spiders with some differences, the main difference being that albino spiders are completely white in color.

Albino spiders can be found in many locations throughout Solstheim, including [Fahlbtharz](Skyrim_Fahlbtharz.md), [Fort Frostmoth](Skyrim_Fort_Frostmoth.md), [Highpoint Tower](Skyrim_Highpoint_Tower.md), [Kolbjorn Barrow](Skyrim_Kolbjorn_Barrow.md), and [White Ridge Barrow](Skyrim_White_Ridge_Barrow.md).

## Flame Spiders
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8a/SR-creature-Jumping_Flame_Spider.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Jumping_Flame_Spider.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Jumping_Flame_Spider.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Jumping_Flame_Spider.jpg) Jumping Flame Spider **Flame Spiders** are a subspecies of the [albino spiders](#Albino_Spider) that live on the island of Solstheim. Flame spiders are physically similar to albino spiders with some differences, the main differences being that flame spiders are red and yellow in color, and possess bony ridges along their carapaces. Flame spiders can be found in [White Ridge Barrow](Skyrim_White_Ridge_Barrow.md) where they spawn inside glowing red egg sacs, which they burst out of when you get too close to them. Flame spiders do not drop anything as they burst upon death. Each variant of flame spiders can be created in the [imbuing chamber](Skyrim_Imbuing_Chamber.md) device in [White Ridge Barrow](Skyrim_White_Ridge_Barrow.md), using the following recipes:

## Frost Spiders
**Frost Spiders** are a subspecies of the [albino spiders](#Albino_Spider) that live on the island of Solstheim. Frost spiders are physically similar to albino spiders with some differences, the main differences being that frost spiders are blue and white in color, and possess icicle-like protrusions along their carapaces. Two frost spiders can be found in a shack near White Ridge Barrow, where they spawn inside glowing white egg sacs. which they burst out of if you get too close to them. Frost spiders do not drop anything as they burst upon death. Frost spiders can be created in the [imbuing chamber](Skyrim_Imbuing_Chamber.md) device in [White Ridge Barrow](Skyrim_White_Ridge_Barrow.md), using the following recipes:

## Oil Spiders
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/ec/SR-creature-Oil_Spider.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Oil_Spider.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Oil_Spider.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Oil_Spider.jpg) An Oil Spider **Oil Spiders** are a subspecies of the [albino spiders](#Albino_Spider) that live on the island of Solstheim. Oil spiders are physically similar to albino spiders but differ in that they are grey with yellow stripes and have large black eyes, much more similar to [Frostbite Spiders](Skyrim_Frostbite_Spider.md). The only oil spiders encountered as enemies are in [Fahlbtharz](Skyrim_Fahlbtharz.md).

While moving, and upon death, oil spiders release a trail of flammable oil that is different in appearance than normal oil but will combust in the same manner if hit by fire. If the oil spider itself is hit by fire, it will explode, which can lead to a chain reaction if dealing with a large group. Oil spiders do not drop anything as they burst upon death.

Oil spiders can be created with the [imbuing chamber](Skyrim_Imbuing_Chamber.md) device in [White Ridge Barrow](Skyrim_White_Ridge_Barrow.md) by placing [Dwarven oil](Skyrim_Dwarven_Oil.md) and [albino spider pods](Skyrim_Albino_Spider_Pod.md) in the machine ([Recipe - Oil Spider](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Recipe_-_Oil_Spider)).

## Mind Control Spiders
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fc/SR-npc-Bandit_29.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Bandit_29.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Bandit_29.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Bandit_29.jpg) A mind controlled bandit Mind Control Spiders are not true creatures in the same sense as other spiders listed on this page. They can only be created by using the [respective Spider Scroll](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mind_Control_Spider). In appearance, they are identical to oil spiders.

As their name implies, these spiders attach to an enemy when thrown and can control the enemy to make them fight for you. When a target is being controlled by the spider, it becomes covered in a network of bright yellow webs. A few of them can be seen within [White Ridge Sanctum](Skyrim_White_Ridge_Barrow.md) attached to bandits who are being experimented on by the sorceress [Merilar Rendas](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Merilar_Rendas), but they disappear when the bandits are killed.

Mind Control Spiders can be created with the [imbuing chamber](Skyrim_Imbuing_Chamber.md) device in [White Ridge Barrow](Skyrim_White_Ridge_Barrow.md) by placing [soul gems](Skyrim_Soul_Gems.md) and [albino spider pods](Skyrim_Albino_Spider_Pod.md) in the machine ([Recipe - Mind Control Spider](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Recipe_-_Mind_Control_Spider)).

## Notes
- There are two additional recipes for creating spiders in game data, but they are not found in the game: [Recipe - Glowing Spider](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Recipe_-_Glowing_Spider) and [Recipe - Pack Spider](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Recipe_-_Pack_Spider).
- Due to game limitations and scripting issues, these spiders do not respawn. In addition, their pods also don't respawn, except for one near a [Shrine of Kynareth](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shrine_of_Kynareth) south of [Bloodskal Barrow](Skyrim_Bloodskal_Barrow.md).
- Although spiders have souls, it is not possible to soul trap them via any means (via the spell, a Soul Trapping staff or a weapons enchanted with the effect).
- Regular, non-imbued spiders have a poison bite attack similar to that of Frostbite spiders, though vastly decreased in power, dealing only 1 poison damage per second for 2 seconds.
- Mind Control Spiders also [appear](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Mind_Spider) in [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Online).
