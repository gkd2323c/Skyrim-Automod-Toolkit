## [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Lighthouse.png) Lighthouses

- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Lighthouse.png) **[Frostflow Lighthouse](Skyrim_Frostflow_Lighthouse.md)** — A lighthouse west-southwest of Winterhold containing Falmer, chaurus, and frostbite spiders. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Frostflow+Lighthouse)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Lighthouse.png) **[Solitude Lighthouse](Skyrim_Solitude_Lighthouse.md)** — A stone building on the northern coast of Skyrim that helps to guide ships into Solitude harbor. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Solitude+Lighthouse)

## Ayleid Ruins

- **[Rielle](Skyrim_Rielle.md)**<sup>[CC](Skyrim_The_Cause.md)</sup> — A medium-sized Ayleid ruin located beneath the Jerall Mountains, just east of the Pale Pass. It straddles the border between the Rift in Skyrim and County Bruma in Cyrodiil. In Skyrim, it is accessed from the north by way of Varlais Cavern, a cave entrance located to the south of Arcwind Point. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Rielle+%28CC%29)
