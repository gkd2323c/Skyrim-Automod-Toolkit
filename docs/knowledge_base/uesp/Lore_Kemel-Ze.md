[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/bb/ON-place-Kemel-Ze.jpg/200px-ON-place-Kemel-Ze.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Kemel-Ze.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Kemel-Ze.jpg) Kemel-Ze entrance during the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md) Kemel-Ze (translated as *"Cliff City"* from [Dwemeris](Lore_Dwemeris.md)) is a [Dwarven ruin](Lore_Dwemer_Ruins.md) found along the [Inner Sea](Lore_Inner_Sea.md) in the coastlines of the [Fungal Lowlands](Lore_Fungal_Lowlands.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Ro KZ-1)</sup> The village of Marog is found nearby.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Ro KZ-1)</sup>

In the First Era, before the Tribunal ascended to the godhood, Nerevar Indoril brokered an alliance with the Dwemer. Dumac Dwarfking gifted Sotha Sil a wing of Kemel-Ze as a sign of that alliance.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Armas-2)</sup> Sotha Sil used this facility to do preliminary work on the Clockwork City. There he studied the flora and fauna of Nirn to learn how to replicate their functions with metal and energy. He built an early factotum, he named Protus.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Protus-3)</sup>

He used Mnemonic Fragments to store some of his memories. He sought to use the memories to create an artificial animus. But all he was able to achieve was the appearance of a fully developed soul,a convincing lie—artificial projection of himself.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Sotha_Sil-4)</sup> In this laboratory, he also created the [Clockwork Probabilis](Lore_Clockwork_Probabilis.md) to manipulate the Intrinsic forces that govern events on Nirn. Some of those forces are constant—like the force which compels objects to fall to the ground, while others defy prediction and categorization. The Clockwork Probabilis is a prototype, a precursor to the Throne Aligned, one of Sotha Sil's great works.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Sotha_Sil-4)</sup>

It was abandoned in [1E 700](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_700) when its original [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md) inhabitants mysteriously disappeared.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-DII-5)</sup> Sotha Sil's wing was also eventually abandoned as his attention shifted to other pursuits. Nonetheless, it remained a sacred site for his followers.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Armas-2)</sup> When he moved to the Clockwork City, Sotha Sil put Protus in charge of overseeing the laboratory and his creations.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-AMOM-6)</sup>

A human [Marukhati](Lore_Marukhati_Selective.md) named Xal visited the ruin in the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md).<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-XG-7)</sup>
