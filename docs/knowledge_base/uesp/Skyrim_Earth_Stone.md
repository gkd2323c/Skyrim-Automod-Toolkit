[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/01/SR-place-Earth_Stone.jpg/200px-SR-place-Earth_Stone.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Earth_Stone.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Earth_Stone.jpg) The Earth Stone The **Earth Stone** is an [All-Maker Stone](Skyrim_All-Maker_Stones.md) southwest of [Raven Rock](Skyrim_Raven_Rock.md).

Once [cleansed](Skyrim_Cleansing_the_Stones.md), this stone gives you the power to [ignore](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bones_of_the_Earth) 80% of all physical damage for 30 seconds. This power is essentially identical to the master-level [Alteration](Skyrim_Alteration.md) spell [Dragonhide](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dragonhide), though without the casting delay, high magicka cost, or skill requirement. Once you use this power, it disappears from the Powers menu, and you must return to the Earth Stone to obtain it again.

## Related Quests
- **[Cleansing the Stones](Skyrim_Cleansing_the_Stones.md)**: Use the [Bend Will](Skyrim_Bend_Will.md) [shout](Skyrim_Dragon_Shouts.md) to cleanse each of the remaining [All-Maker Stones](Skyrim_All-Maker_Stones.md).

## Walkthrough
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/dc/SR-place-Earth_Stone_02.jpg/200px-SR-place-Earth_Stone_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Earth_Stone_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Earth_Stone_02.jpg) The cleansed Earth Stone The stone shows the usual signs of construction, with wooden scaffolding on the southeast side, and [pickaxes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe), [shovels](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shovel), hammers, and carts in the vicinity. Several [nightshade](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nightshade) and [scathecraw](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scathecraw) plants grow around the stone. A path leads northeast to Raven Rock, which is nearby.

Before you cleanse the stone, if [Dragonborn](Skyrim_Dragonborn_(quest).md) has not been completed, [Neloth](Skyrim_Neloth.md) will speak to you as you approach. During the day, [Bralsa Drel](Skyrim_Bralsa_Drel.md), [Rirns Llervu](Skyrim_Rirns_Llervu.md), and three [Redoran guards](Skyrim_Redoran_Guard.md) will be working on and bowing to the stone. During the night, [Cindiri Arano](Skyrim_Cindiri_Arano.md), [Fethis Alor](Skyrim_Fethis_Alor.md), [Garyn Ienth](Skyrim_Garyn_Ienth.md), [Glover Mallory](Skyrim_Glover_Mallory.md), and [Milore Ienth](Skyrim_Milore_Ienth.md) will also be found at the stone. When you cleanse the stone, a leveled [lurker](Skyrim_Lurker.md) will rise from the water around the base of the stone and attack you and the workers, followed by a second leveled lurker approaching from the coast to the west; the Redoran guards will aid in the fight. After the lurkers are defeated, the workers will express their thanks and return to Raven Rock.

## Notes
- The Earth Stone also appears in *[Bloodmoon](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Bloodmoon:Earth_Stone)*, when it was northwest of Raven Rock, not southwest.
