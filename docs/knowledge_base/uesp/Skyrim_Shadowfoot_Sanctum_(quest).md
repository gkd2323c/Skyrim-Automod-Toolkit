## Quick Walkthrough
1. Purchase [Shadowfoot Sanctum](Skyrim_Shadowfoot_Sanctum_(place).md) from [Vekel the Man](Skyrim_Vekel_the_Man.md) for 7,500 gold.
2. Visit your new property.

## Detailed Walkthrough
This short quest will start when you talk to [Vekel the Man](Skyrim_Vekel_the_Man.md) in [The Ragged Flagon](Skyrim_The_Ragged_Flagon.md), an underground tavern in [the Ratway](Skyrim_The_Ratway.md) beneath [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md), and purchase [Shadowfoot Sanctum](Skyrim_Shadowfoot_Sanctum_(place).md) for 7,500 gold.

You will be given a [key](Skyrim_Key_to_Shadowfoot_Sanctum.md) to access the sanctum upon purchasing the property, located a short distance from the Flagon's entrance. It is a fully-furnished player home with a hidden entrance and lots of storage, ideal for members of the [Thieves Guild](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(faction).md). It contains many crafting stations and [shrines](Skyrim_Shrines.md) as well as space for your [spouse](Skyrim_Spouse.md) and [adopted children](Skyrim_Adoption.md). Enter Shadowfoot Sanctum to complete the quest.
