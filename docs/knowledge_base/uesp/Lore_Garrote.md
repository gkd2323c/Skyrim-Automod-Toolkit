# Garrote

[](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-icon-quest-Broken_Garrote.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-icon-quest-Broken_Garrote.png) A garrote Garrote is a type a weapon consisting of a piece of wire or cord used for strangling.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SG-1)</sup>

Garrotes are among the tools employed by the [Morag Tong](Lore_Morag_Tong.md) during their assassinations. One of the skilled practitioners of garrotes within the Morag Tong was [Naryu Virian](Lore_Naryu_Virian.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-MTCNV-2)</sup> The gear utilized by the Morag Tong is tailored specifically to their tasks and responsibilities. Notably, their gloves and gauntlets are designed for delicate work, including garroting, necessitating free finger ends for such tasks.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-CM30-3)</sup>

Garrotes are considered dishonorable among the [Orcs](Lore_Orc.md), and they were among the tools used by Skreg the Undefeatable during fights in [Mor Khazgur](Lore_Mor_Khazgur.md)'s Circle of Champions. Despite his cheating, he eventually met his end thanks to the intervention of [mercenaries](Lore_The_Vestige.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-COC-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-COC2-5)</sup>

## Notes
- Garrote Vines, named after the weapon, are [strangler](Lore_Strangler.md) [plants](Lore_Flora.md) found in the Green Leaf Glade in [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Garrote Vine-6)</sup>
