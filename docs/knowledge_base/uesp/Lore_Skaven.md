[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/1e/AR-place-Skaven.jpg/200px-AR-place-Skaven.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-place-Skaven.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-place-Skaven.jpg) Skaven (Arena) [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d5/LO-map-Hammerfell_%28Anthology%29.jpg/200px-LO-map-Hammerfell_%28Anthology%29.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LO-map-Hammerfell_(Anthology).jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LO-map-Hammerfell_(Anthology).jpg) Map of Hammerfell Skaven (also known as **Ska'vyn**)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONFinemo-1)</sup> is one of the eight major cities in the province of [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md),<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ARMap-2)</sup> situated in the region of [Sunforge](Lore_Sunforge.md) in central Hammerfell. In the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), the city was the seat of power for the eponymous **Kingdom of Skaven**.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-LRPBlue Dawn-3)</sup>

## Layout and Geography
Skaven is found in north-central Hammerfell near the [Dragontail Mountains](Lore_Dragontail_Mountains.md),<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-RGMap-4)</sup> where old [Yokudan](Lore_Yokuda.md) traditions continue to flourish by the [Crowns](Lore_Crown.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-PGE3E-5)</sup> On the northern outskirts of Ska'vyn lies the Caverns of Wry, which contains herds of [Skav Bats](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Skav_Bat) that are bred to produce dairy by the locals. Their signature Bat Cheese is supposedly paired well with [Pomegranate](Lore_Pomegranate.md) wine.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-LHLAYQ-6)</sup> Northwest of the kingdom, across from the [Bjoulsae River](Lore_Bjoulsae_River.md) is the town of Candlemass.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-LRPBlue Dawn-3)</sup> Skaven is neighbored by several settlements, such as [Cliff Keep](Lore_Cliff_Keep.md) to the east, [Heldorn Mount](Lore_Heldorn_Mount.md) to the northeast, and [Verkarth City](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Verkarth_City) to the southwest.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ARMap-2)</sup>

## History
Ska'vyn was active since at least the early-mid [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md), when it and the surrounding region was under the tyrannical reign of the unholy city-state, [Verkarth](Lore_Verkarth_(Hammerfell).md) and their legion, the [Gray Host](Lore_Gray_Host.md). The city had reportedly ruled over Hammerfell's east-central region for many years, between [Elinhir](Lore_Elinhir.md) to the east and [Thorstad Place](Lore_Thorstad_Place.md) to the west. Their power had drawn the ire of Empress [Hestra](Lore_Hestra.md) and her [Empire](Lore_Alessian_Empire.md), who took the initiative and moved west for conquest. The Empire had defeated the Gray Host at the gates of the [Bangkorai Garrison](Lore_Bangkorai_Garrison.md), with the help of the cleric, [Pelin](Lore_Pelin.md). Ska'vyn was liberated from the [vampires](Lore_Vampire.md) after Verkarth was completely razed by the [Imperials](Lore_Imperial.md).<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-TGHAH-10)</sup> At some point in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the Dragonstar Caravan Company, one of the largest trading companies at the time had moved their headquarters from [Craglorn](Lore_Craglorn.md) and into Ska'vyn.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONFinemo-1)</sup>

During the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md) in the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), the city-state of Skaven was an active settlement. It was ruled by Queen Mijurti and it had a rivalry with [Dragonstar](Lore_Dragonstar.md).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-AR-7)</sup> During the [Great War](Lore_Great_War.md), the [Imperial Legion](Lore_Imperial_Legion.md), under General Decianus had a bloody skirmish with the Third [Aldmeri Dominion](Lore_Aldmeri_Dominion.md) outside of Skaven's wall, but the city was captured by the Aldmeri Dominion. However, Lady [Arannelya](Lore_Arannelya.md)'s forces could not move out and continue their campaign. Before the general returned to the [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md) in [4E 174](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_174), he discharged many soldiers that later formed the core of fighters that retook Skaven from the Dominion and drove Arannelya's force back across the [Alik'r Desert](Lore_Alik%27r_Desert.md).<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-TGW-11)</sup>

## Culture and Society
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/4d/ON-hat-Flamebrow_Fire_Veil.jpg/200px-ON-hat-Flamebrow_Fire_Veil.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-hat-Flamebrow_Fire_Veil.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-hat-Flamebrow_Fire_Veil.jpg) Flamebrow Fire Veil, worn by Firedancers
- *For more information, see the: **[Culture](Lore_Redguard.md#Culture)** and **[Society](Lore_Redguard.md#Society)** sections on the Redguard page.*

The First Ska'vyn Camel Scouts were a group that was active sometime in the Second Era. One of their flags was in circulation throughout Tamriel's underworld in 2E 582.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-ONContraband Flag-12)</sup> The Firedancers of central Hammerfell wear scandalously little clothing by local standards, which may be why they protect their modesty—and identities—by hiding their faces behind Flamebrow Fire Veils.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-FFV-13)</sup>
