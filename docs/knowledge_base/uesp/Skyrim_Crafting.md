# Activities

A range of **activities** are possible in Skyrim. Some are obvious parts of gameplay, such as completing [quests](Skyrim_Quests.md) and exploring [dungeons](Skyrim_Dungeons.md). This page lists some of the optional activities that are available.

## Crafting
You are able to make a range of custom items, using the various crafting [skills](Skyrim_Skills.md), specifically:

- [Alchemy](Skyrim_Alchemy.md): Create potions by using two or more ingredients with the same effect. You can also try to discover new effects by combining random ingredients, though this may fail to produce anything.
- [Enchanting](Skyrim_Enchanting.md): Enchant weapons or apparel with magical properties. - [Staff Enchanting](Skyrim_Staff_Enchanter.md)<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup>: Enchant magical staffs with the spells you know.
- [Smithing](Skyrim_Smithing.md): Create and improve weapons and apparel. - [Forges](Skyrim_Forge.md), [grindstones](Skyrim_Grindstone.md), [workbenches](Skyrim_Workbench.md) and [anvils](Skyrim_Anvil.md) are used for the various smithing activities

Other types of crafting are not controlled by any skills:

- [Atronach Forge](Skyrim_Atronach_Forge.md): Create staves, spell scrolls, spell tomes and Daedric items.
- [Baking](Skyrim_Baking.md)<sup>[HF](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md)</sup>: Baked food is more nutritious than raw food.
- [Bone Forge](Skyrim_Bone_Forge.md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Gallows_Hall.md)</sup>: Summon permanently bound undead followers.
- [Construction](Skyrim_Construction.md)<sup>[HF](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md)</sup>: Build your own house from raw materials.
- [Cooking](Skyrim_Cooking.md): Cooked food is more nutritious than raw food.
- [Imbuing Chamber](Skyrim_Imbuing_Chamber.md)<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup>: Create dangerous [magical spiders](Skyrim_Spider_Scrolls.md) for use as weapons.
- [Mining](Skyrim_Mining.md): Mine ore for use in smithing or to sell.
- [Smelting](Skyrim_Smelting.md): Mineral ores need to be smelted before they can be used at weapon forging.
- [Tanning](Skyrim_Tanning.md): Convert hides into leather and leather into leather strips.

## Tasks

### Quests
- **[Chop Wood](Skyrim_Chop_Wood.md)**: Get paid to chop firewood. ([radiant](Skyrim_Radiant.md))
- **[Gather Wheat](Skyrim_Gather_Wheat.md)**: Get paid to gather crops. ([radiant](Skyrim_Radiant.md))
- **[Mine Ore](Skyrim_Mine_Ore.md)**: Get paid to [mine](Skyrim_Mining.md) ore. ([radiant](Skyrim_Radiant.md))

### Other Tasks
- **[Cutting Lumber](Skyrim_Cutting_Lumber.md)**: Serves no purpose except for roleplaying in the original version of the game. If [Hearthfire](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md) is installed, sawn logs can be cut at wood mills, with the permission of the mill owner.
- **[Milling](Skyrim_Milling.md)**: Serves no purpose except for roleplaying in the original version of the game. If Hearthfire is installed, [wheat](Skyrim_Wheat.md) can be used to create [flour](Skyrim_Sack_of_Flour.md).
- **[Fishing](Skyrim_Fishing_(activity).md)**<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Fishing.md)</sup>: Pick up a [fishing rod](Skyrim_Fishing_Rod.md) and reel in rare fish and other items.
