# Amulet of Mephala

The Amulet of Mephala is an artifact associated with the [Daedric Prince](Lore_Daedric_Prince.md) [Mephala](Lore_Mephala.md). The artifact rewards the user with increased vitality in exchange for sacrificing those that trust them, with the effect becoming more potent as more are betrayed. Physically, it is a [silver](Lore_Silver.md) necklace with an [Emerald](Lore_Emerald.md) embedded with it.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup>

### History
Accounts of [Mephala](Lore_Mephala.md)'s involvement differ, but it is said that in [4E 176](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_176) she offered her assistance in killing her former minion Moivva Karnai, a Khajiit in service to the [Thalmor](Lore_Thalmor.md) which she grew tired of. She is rumored to have offered to weaken her minion, or she may have granted her own amulet or the [Ebony Blade](Lore_Ebony_Blade.md) to complete the assassination.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup>
