Reclamation Priest's Journal **by [Reclamation Priest](Skyrim_Reclamation_Priest.md)** *A Reclamation priest complains about Tribunal heretics*
---
The audacity of these [Tribunal](Skyrim_Tribunal_Temple.md) heretics knows no limit. The downright temerity of their priests, to think they can spread their heresy in plain view of the [Reclamation](Lore_Good_Daedra.md), is an affront to the senses.

Yet this arrogance shall be their downfall. I will gather the guard and cleanse this temple in the name of the true gods.
