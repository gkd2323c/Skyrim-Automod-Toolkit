[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/14/SR-place-Dragon_Bridge.jpg/200px-SR-place-Dragon_Bridge.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Dragon_Bridge.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Dragon_Bridge.jpg) Dragon Bridge **Dragon Bridge** is a milling [town](Skyrim_Towns.md) in southern [Haafingar](Skyrim_Haafingar.md).

The town and its iconic bridge are of vital interest to both the [Stormcloak](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md) rebels and the [Imperials](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md) in the [Civil War](Skyrim_Civil_War.md). The townspeople of Dragon Bridge are worried that either side may seek to destroy the bridge as a means of preventing the other from crossing.

There are two families that live in Dragon Bridge: [Horgeir](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Horgeir) and [Olda](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Olda), and the [Lylvieve family](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lylvieve_Family_House_Occupants). The guards have been harassing the Lylvieve family and [Faida](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Faida), the proprietor of the inn by refusing to pay, stealing, and attempting to "have their way" with [Julienne Lylvieve](Skyrim_Julienne_Lylvieve.md). The mill, however, has prospered due to the increased demand for lumber.

The town consists of a main road running northeast from the bridge toward [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md). [Dragon Bridge lumber camp](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Bridge_Lumber_Camp) and sawmill are the first buildings to the left of the road after crossing the bridge into Dragon Bridge. The mill is powered by the [Karth River](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Karth_River), which flows beneath the bridge on its way to the [Sea of Ghosts](Skyrim_Sea_of_Ghosts.md). Next to the lumber camp is the only outpost for the [Penitus Oculatus](Skyrim_Penitus_Oculatus.md), who replaced the [Blades](Skyrim_Blades.md) as the personal security and espionage force for the [Emperor](Skyrim_Emperor_Titus_Mede_II_(real).md) when the Blades disbanded. Northeast of the outpost is [Horgeir's house](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Horgeir%27s_House), home to Horgeir, his wife, and his brother [Lodvar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lodvar).

Opposite his house is the town's inn, the [Four Shields Tavern](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Four_Shields_Tavern), which is the only building on the eastern side of the main road. Behind the inn is a secondary road that starts near the bridge and circles the inn before rejoining the main road at the other end of town. Next door to Horgeir's house is the [Lylvieve family's house](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lylvieve_Family%27s_House), where [Azzada Lylvieve](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Azzada_Lylvieve) lives with his [wife](Skyrim_Michel_Lylvieve.md) and two children.

## Notes
- The treasure of [Treasure Map VIII](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Treasure_Map_VIII) is underwater near the bridge. A [satchel](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Satchel) containing its key can be found a few paces upstream of the bridge.
- A [gold ore vein](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Gold_Ore_Vein) is upstream from the bridge on the bank opposite the town, and an [iron ore vein](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Ore_Vein) is upstream from the bridge on the right next to a [Nirnroot](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nirnroot).
- If *[Hearthfire](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md)* is installed, a [stone quarry](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Stone_Quarry) is behind the lumber camp at the foot of the cliff wall.
- With the [Fishing](Skyrim_Fishing.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md) installed, some [Fishing Supplies](Skyrim_Fishing_Supplies.md) are by the water near the mill. There is also a static copy of the [fishing map for Haafingar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fishing_Map_-_Haafingar) and two [ales](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ale) on top of a barrel, and two [river betties](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:River_Betty), a [brook bass](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Brook_Bass), and an [abecean longfin](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Abecean_Longfin) in a wooden crate on the ground.
- Dragon Bridge also appears in *[Arena](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Arena:Dragon_Bridge)* and [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Dragon_Bridge).

## Bugs
- The [guards](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Guard) in Dragon Bridge are mistakenly named "Imperial Soldier" instead of "Haafingar Guard". - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.7, fixes this bug.
- The Stormcloak guards who occupy Dragon Bridge once Solitude has fallen are not members of the town's guard faction, causing them to drop town specific dialogue they should have. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.2.5, fixes this bug.
- The fast travel arrival point here is farther away from where your [horse](Skyrim_Horses.md) would end up. This is inconsistent with how other locations with horse markers are handled. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.2.6, fixes this bug.
- Female Stormcloak soldiers who occupy Dragon Bridge once Solitude has fallen won't say anything about the Moth Priest if you ask them about it during [Prophet](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Prophet). - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Legendary_Edition_Patch), version 3.0.7, addresses this issue. The female Stormcloak soldiers here had no audio recorded for that. You'll need to ask someone else.
