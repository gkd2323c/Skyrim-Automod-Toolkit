[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/83/ON-crown_store-Varlaisvea_Ayleid_Ruins.jpg/200px-ON-crown_store-Varlaisvea_Ayleid_Ruins.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Varlaisvea_Ayleid_Ruins.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Varlaisvea_Ayleid_Ruins.jpg) The ruins of Varlaisvea circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) Varlaisvea (meaning "Sea of Stars" in [Ayleidoon](Lore_Ayleidoon.md)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MFTS-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-RR-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Ayleidoon-3)</sup>) is a [ruined](Lore_Ayleid_Ruins.md) [Ayleid](Lore_Ayleid.md) complex located at the mouth of the [Brena River](Lore_Brena_River.md), on the coast of the [Abecean Sea](Lore_Abecean_Sea.md). It lies on the [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md) side of the border with the [Gold Coast](Lore_Gold_Coast.md) region of [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-GCG1-4)</sup> It is located south of [Rihad](Lore_Rihad.md) and north of [Anvil](Lore_Anvil.md). During the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md), the ruins were surrounded by cork oak and juniper trees, and were covered by hanging ivy.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-VAR-5)</sup>

Little is known of the original purpose of the Ayleid structures, though some claimed that soaking in the retreat's indoor crystal pool heals the spirit.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-VAR2-6)</sup>

Circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the ruins were available for purchase as a private residence.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-VAR-5)</sup>

By the end of the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), no trace of the ruins remained standing.
