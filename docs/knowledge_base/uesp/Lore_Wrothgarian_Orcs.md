# Wrothgarian Orcs

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/ab/ON-place-Eastern_Wrothgar.jpg/200px-ON-place-Eastern_Wrothgar.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Eastern_Wrothgar.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Eastern_Wrothgar.jpg) The Wrothgarian Mountains The Wrothgarian Orcs, as their name suggests, are [Orcs](Lore_Orcs.md) that inhabit the [Wrothgarian Mountains](Lore_Wrothgarian_Mountains.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Orzorga-1)</sup> Oral traditions within Wrothgarian Orc society claim that they have lived in [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) since the [Dawn Era](Lore_Dawn_Era.md), long before the [Altmer](Lore_Altmer.md) or [Bosmer](Lore_Bosmer.md) settled there.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-MOC-2)</sup> Regardless of whether or not they were always there, the Orcs have eternally felt compelled to return "home" to the Wrothgarian Mountains.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-3)</sup>

## Notes
- Although the Wrothgarian Orcs inhabit mountainous regions, they should not be mistaken for [Mountain Orcs](Lore_Mountain_Orcs.md). Officially, they are known as Wrothgarian Orcs.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Orzorga-1)</sup>
