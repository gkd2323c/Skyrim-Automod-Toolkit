[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/45/ON-interior-Whalefall.jpg/200px-ON-interior-Whalefall.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-interior-Whalefall.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-interior-Whalefall.jpg) Whalefall ca. 2E 582 Whalefall is a small, uninhabited island on the [central strait](Lore_Amenos_Passage.md) of the [Systres Archipelago](Lore_Systres_Archipelago.md), an island region in the westernmost part of the [Abecean Sea](Lore_Abecean_Sea.md). It acquired its name for all of the [whale](Lore_Whale.md) bones that litter it but for a long period of time, it was primarily used by the jailors of nearby Amenos Station, either as a training ground for their personnel or as a hunting ground to kill prisoners for sport.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONLouna-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONLendanor-2)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a7/ON-place-Huntsman%27s_Fortress.jpg/200px-ON-place-Huntsman%27s_Fortress.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Huntsman%27s_Fortress.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Huntsman%27s_Fortress.jpg) Huntsman's Fortress The jails on Amenos were established in [2E 11](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_11) by Potentate [Versidue-Shaie](Lore_Versidue-Shaie.md)<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-SOA-3)</sup> and for the longest time, they used Whalefall as their training ground to subdue prisoners. By the mid- [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md), however, jailors used the island as a hunting ground and dropped selected prisoners to survive a gauntlet to the peak of the island, a wrecked ship fashioned as an arena. Prisoners are given the promise of freedom but it has been reported that no one has ever survived the Whalefall Games.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONLouna-1)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ROTG-4)</sup>

In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), Jailor Tynan Mannick enlisted six hunters and gathered several prisoners for the games. Among those selected was Anton [Motierre](Lore_Motierre.md), a [Breton](Lore_Breton.md) swindler. His partner-in-crime, Louna Jolvanne hatched a plan to rescue him by building a raft made out of trash collected on [Amenos](Lore_Amenos.md) and sail between the islands. She enlisted the help of a [mainlander](Lore_The_Vestige.md) and the two trekked the island as the games ensued. They tracked Anton to the winner's stage, the arena manned by Jailor Mannick, and after a battle with the jailor, they rescued Anton and escaped the island.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ONFreedom-5)</sup>

Huntsman's Fortress was a stronghold located in the northwestern part of the island, where the mysterious baron known as the Huntsman organized gladiatorial contests for the enjoyment of the landed gentry of [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md). He collected unique fauna from all over Tamriel such as [echalettes](Lore_Echatere.md), [Nirnblooded senche](Lore_Nirnblooded.md), and [welwas](Lore_Welwa.md) as pit beasts for the arena. By 2E 582, the [Circle of Champions](Lore_Circle_of_Champions.md) began utilizing the ruins to stage brutal gladiator battles. Fighters from all factions of the Alliance War came to the Huntsman's Fortress arena to compete, fighting for the glory of the Fire Drakes, Pit Daemons, and Storm Lords teams.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-HF-6)</sup>

## Notes
- A [whale fall](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/Whale_fall) is a phenomenon where the carcass of a [whale](Lore_Whale.md) falls to the ocean floor and creates complex localized ecosystems that supply sustenance to deep-sea organisms for decades.
