It is the beast spirit of [Vilkas](Skyrim_Vilkas.md), which can be summoned and slain in [Ysgramor's Tomb](Skyrim_Ysgramor%27s_Tomb.md) to cure him of [lycanthropy](Skyrim_Lycanthropy.md). It manifests as a giant, ghostly [wolf](Skyrim_Wolf.md). It is leveled and is more challenging to fight than a standard wolf. Once killed, it disappears.
