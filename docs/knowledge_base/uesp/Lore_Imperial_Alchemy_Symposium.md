# Imperial Alchemy Symposium

The Imperial Alchemy Symposium is an [Imperial](Lore_Imperial.md) academic institution devoted to the research of [alchemy](Lore_Alchemy.md).

The famous [Altmer](Lore_Altmer.md) alchemist [Sinderion](Lore_Sinderion.md) discovered the recipe of a potion called the "Elixir of Exploration". This potion brought out the huge magical potential of the rare [Nirnroot](Lore_Nirnroot.md). He presented his findings at the Alchemical Symposium in the hopes of funding, but was turned down.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-E-1)</sup> In [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), he hired the [Hero of Kvatch](Lore_Hero_of_Kvatch.md) to collect samples of Nirnroot for him. The Hero provided enough samples to complete Sinderion's research and create the strongest version of the elixir.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Oblivion-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Oblivion2-3)</sup> Sinderion again presented his speech to the Alchemical Symposium in Rain's Hand of [4E 2](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_2).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TNM-4)</sup>

Talan was once a member of the prestigious Imperial Alchemy Symposium, but was removed from its ranks for using the skill of alchemy to perpetuate the mixing of magical and [alcoholic beverages](Lore_Alcoholic_Beverages.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup>
