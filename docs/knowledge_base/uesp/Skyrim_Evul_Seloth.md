[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f9/SR-npc-Evul_Seloth.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Evul_Seloth.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Evul_Seloth.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Evul_Seloth.jpg) Evul Seloth **Evul Seloth** is a [Dark Elf](Skyrim_Dark_Elf.md) [miner](Skyrim_Miner_(class).md) who lives in [Raven Rock Mine](Skyrim_Raven_Rock_Mine.md) after it is reopened. He wears [miner's clothes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Miner%27s_Clothes#00080697) and [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#00080699), and he carries a [steel dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Dagger), a [pickaxe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe), and a selection of [common items and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common).

## Dialogue
He shares dialogue with his fellow miners:

*"It's good to get back to mining after all these years."* *"This mine's amazing! Have you ever seen so much ebony in your life?"* *"I've been out of the business so long, I almost forgot how to swing a pickaxe."* *"Good to know that the mine's safe again. I heard a lot of strange things happened here."* *"Getting your hands dirty, outlander?"* *"Help yourself to as much ore as you like... you've earned it."* *"I haven't set foot in these mines for ages. It's strange being back after so long."* *"There's nothing quite like the sound of metal striking rock."* *"Nice to be making some coin again."* *"Thanks to House Redoran getting these mines reopened, I can provide for my family again."* *"Welcome to Raven Rock Mine, outlander."*
## Notes
- He shares a surname with [Malur Seloth](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Malur_Seloth), who lives in [Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold.md), but there is no indication that they are related.
