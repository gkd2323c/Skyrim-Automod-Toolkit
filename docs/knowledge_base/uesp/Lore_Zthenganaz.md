[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/11/ON-place-Zthenganaz.jpg/200px-ON-place-Zthenganaz.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Zthenganaz.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Zthenganaz.jpg) Zthenganaz entrance circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) Zthenganaz is a buried [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md) ruin, located in the Icy Shore of the [Wrothgarian mountains](Lore_Wrothgarian_Mountains.md), south of [Farrun](Lore_Farrun.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ON-1)</sup>

According to Thelwe Ghelein, it was founded by one of the clans that splintered from the [Rourken](Lore_Rourken.md) after they left [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup> It was defended by an advanced type of [Dwarven Centurion](Lore_Dwarven_Centurion.md) called Ztheng Guardian.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-KN-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup> A dwarven Artifact called the Eye of Zthenganaz, a large gemstone mounted in a gear-like wheel, was, according to legend, crafted by the [Dwarves](Lore_Dwemer.md) to spy on the [Orc](Lore_Orsimer.md) strongholds of the early [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup>

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/88/ON-interior-Zthenganaz.jpg/200px-ON-interior-Zthenganaz.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-interior-Zthenganaz.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-interior-Zthenganaz.jpg) Zthenganaz interior By the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md), the ruins of Zthenganaz were mostly caught in ice.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ON-1)</sup> In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), when [King Kurog](Lore_Kurog.md) called for experts from all over Tamriel to help rebuild the orc nation of [Orsinium](Lore_Orsinium.md), dunmer scholar Raynor Vanos went on a mission to find and explore Zthenganaz.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-KN-3)</sup> While he was searching for the ruin with his sister Kireth, she fell in a hole leading directly into it. Fortunately, he was able to recruit [an adventurer](Lore_The_Vestige.md) passing by to rescue his sister. While in the ruin, Kireth and the adventurer retrieved a power core from a Ztheng Guardian <sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup>. Raynor built a device to control [Dwarven Automatons](Lore_Dwemer_Animunculi.md) with the core, though the device did not work as expected.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-7)</sup> Raynor then sent [the adventurer](Lore_The_Vestige.md) back in the ruin to acquire additonnal data on the constructs and destroy the Ztheng Guardian.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-8)</sup>

## Notes
- The fell monstrosity, that is the Cursebound [Kagouti](Lore_Kagouti.md) inspired [Maestro Efnort](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Maestro_Efnort) of the [Bards College](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Bards_College) to write the poem "The Red-Eyed Terror of Zthenganaz."<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-9)</sup>
- In early drafts, this location was spelled **Zthgnthaz**, which was intended to be the Dwemeris acronym for "time-wizard".<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-SDO-10)</sup>
