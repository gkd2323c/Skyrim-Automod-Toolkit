Travels in High Rock (Excerpt) *An excerpt from a Glenumbra travel guidebook*

[![ON-concept-Glenumbra rocks.png](https://images.uesp.net/6/66/ON-concept-Glenumbra_rocks.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-concept-Glenumbra_rocks.png)
- The countryside of southern and central [Glenumbra](Lore_Glenumbra.md) is a lovely place to travel, especially in the spring. Make sure to avoid the disgusting fens in the eastern regions north of [Aldcroft](Lore_Aldcroft.md). Outside of those, the forests of Glenumbra are verdant, the temperatures more than pleasant, and enjoyable camping is possible almost anywhere.

- I strongly recommend a visit to the woods near [Daggerfall](Lore_Daggerfall.md), where game is plentiful and the local villages offer hearty and inexpensive repasts. Journeying here is a wonderful way to relax and enjoy the beauty of [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md). In [First Seed](Lore_First_Seed.md), you must travel up to [Camlorn](Lore_Camlorn.md). No other city boasts such a lively, colorful, and entertaining festival as Camlorn's celebration of [Flower Day](Lore_Flower_Day.md). It makes a perfect addition to any excursion through the region.
