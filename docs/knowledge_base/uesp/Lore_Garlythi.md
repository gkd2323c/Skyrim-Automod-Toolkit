# Garlythi

Garlythi is one of the [vampire](Lore_Vampire.md) bloodlines of the [Iliac Bay](Lore_Iliac_Bay.md) area. Those of the bloodline are gifted with the ability to magically shield themselves from damage. The bloodline dominates the regions of [Northmoor](Lore_Northmoor.md) and [Phrygias](Lore_Phrygias.md) in [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md). They are the enemies of the [Selenu](Lore_Selenu.md) and [Lyrezi](Lore_Lyrezi.md) bloodlines.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup>
