[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/2c/DF-map-Urvaius.png/200px-DF-map-Urvaius.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-map-Urvaius.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-map-Urvaius.png) Urvaius [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/04/DF-map-Iliac_Bay_%28labeled%29.png/200px-DF-map-Iliac_Bay_%28labeled%29.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-map-Iliac_Bay_(labeled).png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-map-Iliac_Bay_(labeled).png) The area in which Urvaius is found Urvaius is a county in south-central [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md), in the [Iliac Bay](Lore_Iliac_Bay.md) region.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DF-1)</sup> It has existed since at least the late [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2920Book-2)</sup> After the [Warp in the West](Lore_Warp_in_the_West.md), it became a vassal of the [Kingdom of Wayrest](Lore_Wayrest_(kingdom).md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup>

It is bordered by [Ykalon](Lore_Ykalon.md) and [Phrygias](Lore_Phrygias.md) to the north, [Dwynnen](Lore_Dwynnen.md) to the east, [Anticlere](Lore_Anticlere.md) to the south, and [Daenia](Lore_Daenia_(duchy).md) to the west.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup> Its capital is the city of Urvaius. The [Thrafey](Lore_Thrafey.md) clan is the predominant [vampire](Lore_Vampire.md) bloodline, and the regional deity is [Stendarr](Lore_Stendarr.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DF-1)</sup>
