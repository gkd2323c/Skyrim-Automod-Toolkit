# Celestite

Celestite is a kind of blue crystal.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-T1-1)</sup>

One of the various applications of celestite involved crafting intricate drinking glasses, made from the cool, blue-hued geode. Though its name implies a connection to the [Celestials](Lore_Celestial.md), any real relationship between them and the crystal is unknown.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-T1-1)</sup>
