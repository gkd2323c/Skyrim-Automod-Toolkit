[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/7c/SR-npc-Penitus_Oculatus_Agent.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Penitus_Oculatus_Agent.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Penitus_Oculatus_Agent.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Penitus_Oculatus_Agent.jpg) Penitus Oculatus Agent The **Penitus Oculatus Agents** are the security force of the [Penitus Oculatus](Skyrim_Penitus_Oculatus.md) organization. They serve as a personal security and espionage force for the Emperor after the [Blades](Skyrim_Blades.md) disbanded. They are initially only encountered at the [Penitus Oculatus Outpost](Skyrim_Penitus_Oculatus_Outpost.md) in [Dragon Bridge](Skyrim_Dragon_Bridge.md). During the [Dark Brotherhood](Skyrim_Dark_Brotherhood.md) quest [Bound Until Death](Skyrim_Bound_Until_Death.md), a Penitus Oculatus agent is present at [Vittoria Vici](Skyrim_Vittoria_Vici.md)'s wedding to ensure her safety. Two Penitus Oculatus agents are present at the [Emperor Titus Mede II](Skyrim_Emperor_Titus_Mede_II_(decoy).md)'s dinner, and three agents will later ambush you during [To Kill an Empire](Skyrim_To_Kill_an_Empire.md).

## Related Quests
- **[Bound Until Death](Skyrim_Bound_Until_Death.md)**: Assassinate [Vittoria Vici](Skyrim_Vittoria_Vici.md) at her own wedding.
- **[To Kill an Empire](Skyrim_To_Kill_an_Empire.md)**: Attempt to assassinate the [Emperor](Skyrim_Emperor_Titus_Mede_II_(real).md) while disguised as the [Gourmet](Skyrim_Gourmet.md).
- **[Death Incarnate](Skyrim_Death_Incarnate.md)**: Assist [Brotherhood](Skyrim_Dark_Brotherhood.md) members in the fight against the [Penitus Oculatus](Skyrim_Penitus_Oculatus.md) assault.
- **[Hail Sithis!](Skyrim_Hail_Sithis!.md)**: One of the biggest [contracts](Skyrim_Emperor_Titus_Mede_II_(real).md) in [Brotherhood](Skyrim_Dark_Brotherhood.md) history is yours to fulfill.

## Dialogue
If spoken to inside the Outpost:

- *"You must be lost. The tavern is down the road a piece."*
- *"I'm fairly certain you wandered into the wrong building, friend..."*
- *"Citizen?"*

## Quest-Related Dialogue

### Bound Until Death
There is a single Agent at the wedding who can be spoken to:

- *"I'm just here to provide protection for his eminence's cousin. Please, enjoy yourself."*

After the assassination:

- *"She's dead! On my watch! This won't stand!"*

### To Kill an Empire
If you wait before going into the Emperor's Tower you can overhear a conversation between two Penitus Oculatus agents which will differ depending on who controls the city:

If Imperials control Solitude:

- **Penitus Oculatus Agent 1:** *"The city is crawling with Imperial Legion, and you know what? I'm still nervous."*
- **Penitus Oculatus Agent 2:** *"Lazy and useless, the lot of them. They can't even keep the Stormcloaks in line. No way I trust them with the Emperor's life."*
- **Penitus Oculatus Agent 1:** *"You worry too much. The Emperor's never been safer. You think assassination attempts are planned overnight? We discovered the plot. End of story."*
- **Penitus Oculatus Agent 2:** *"I suppose. But what about the old man? Son killed, family name ruined...and he acts as if nothing even happened. Think's he's even fit for duty?"*
- **Penitus Oculatus Agent 1:** *"I'm going to pretend I didn't hear that. Commander Maro is the best that the Penitus Oculatus ever produced. You should be half the man he is."*
- **Penitus Oculatus Agent 2:** *"You misunderstand. Its just...I feel for him, is all. To carry a weight like that. It's got to take its toll. That happened to me, I'd be a wreck."*

If Stormcloaks control Solitude:

- **Penitus Oculatus Agent 1:** *"All these damn Stormcloaks. They're making me nervous. Never in a million years did I imagine they'd gain control of Solitude."*
- **Penitus Oculatus Agent 2:** *"I'm all for diplomacy, but the sooner the Emperor deals with this civil war mess, the sooner we can get back to Cyrodiil."*
- **Penitus Oculatus Agent 1:** *"You worry too much. The Emperor's never been safer. You think assassination attempts are planned overnight? We discovered the plot. End of story."*
- **Penitus Oculatus Agent 2:** *"I suppose. But what about the old man? Son killed, family name ruined...and he acts as if nothing even happened. Think's he's even fit for duty?"*
- **Penitus Oculatus Agent 1:** *"I'm going to pretend I didn't hear that. Commander Maro is the best that the Penitus Oculatus ever produced. You should be half the man he is."*
- **Penitus Oculatus Agent 2:** *"You misunderstand. Its just...I feel for him, is all. To carry a weight like that. It's got to take its toll. That happened to me, I'd be a wreck."*

If you attempt you speak to them:

- *"Please, carry on."*
- *"Move along, citizen."*

While you are acting as the Gourmet you can speak to them:

- *"The Gourmet. This is such an honor."*
- *"The misses and I love your strawberry tarts."*
- *"Hmmm, the Potage le Magnifique. Smells delicious."*

If the decoy Emperor dies from the meal:

- *"By the gods! The Gourmet and the chef have poisoned the Emperor, get them!"*

If you kill the decoy directly:

- *"By the gods! The Gourmet has killed the Emperor! Attack!"*

### Death Incarnate
In the sanctuary:

- **Penitus Oculatus Agent 1:** *"Which one was the rat?"*
- **Penitus Oculatus Agent 2:** *"Dunno. One of these corpses. Does it matter?"*
- **Penitus Oculatus Agent 1:** *"Suppose not. But what's taking the others so long? The sooner we get out of here, the better. Smoke's getting bad. This place'll be raging soon."*
- **Penitus Oculatus Agent 2:** *"Arcturus took some men deeper in, wanted to be sure. We should maybe... assist them."*
- **Penitus Oculatus Agent 1:** *"You assist all you like. And get roasted for your trouble. I'll guard the exit."*

### Hail Sithis!
If you wait near Commander Maro at the Solitude docks, you may overhear a conversation between him and another Penitus Oculatus agent:

- **Penitus Oculatus Agent**: *"And the outpost at Dragon Bridge?"*
- **Commander Maro**: *"It'll be shuttered by the end of the month."*
- **Penitus Oculatus Agent**: *"Very good. And you'll be returning to... Well, if you don't mind me asking, where exactly will you be going now, sir?"*
- **Commander Maro**: *"Hmph. Now there's an excellent question. An excellent question, indeed. Truth is, as soon as the Emperor sets sail, I'm resigning my position."*
- **Penitus Oculatus Agent**: *"Oh... I see. Well then, let me be just say that it's been an honor serving under you Commander Maro."*
- **Commander Maro**: *"The honor has been mine. You should be proud of what we've accomplished here. The Dark Brotherhood is no more. And the Emperor, finally, is safe."*
- **Penitus Oculatus Agent**: *"Yes sir."*

On the Katariah you can overhear agents speaking to each other:

- *"Well, it's all over now. Lieutenant said you could see the smoke from Whiterun."*
- *"Yeah. I'm glad we can finally stand down- getting tired of looking over my shoulder all the time."*
- *"Nothing to do now but sit back, relax, and enjoy the trip home."*

- *"*Sigh*... and that's the last one. All right, enough of this."*
- *"C'mon, let's get something to eat."*

If any Agents spot you they can say:

- *"Well, well. Looks like one of the Dark Brotherhood rats escaped its burning nest. I can fix that."*
- *"To arms, brothers! It's the assassin!"*
- *"The Dark Brotherhood is dead... and so are you!"*
- *"You want the Emperor? You'll have to get through us!"*
- *"You'll never reach the Emperor! Not while I'm standing!"*
- *"It all ends here, assassin!"*
- *"You'll never leave this ship alive, cutthroat!"*
