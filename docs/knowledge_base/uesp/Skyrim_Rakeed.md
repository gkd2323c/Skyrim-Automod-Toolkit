[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f8/SR-npc-Rakeed.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Rakeed.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Rakeed.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Rakeed.jpg) Rakeed [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/6d/SR-npc-Rakeed_02.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Rakeed_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Rakeed_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Rakeed_02.jpg) As a hostage **Rakeed** is a [Redguard](Skyrim_Redguard.md) member of the Remnants and the primary focus of the quest [Interception](Skyrim_Interception.md). During the quest, he is being held hostage by the [Thalmor](Skyrim_Thalmor.md) and [Ismal](Skyrim_Ismal.md). After he is freed, he will travel to [Purewater Run](Skyrim_Purewater_Run.md), where upon the quest's completion, he will disappear from the game along with [Fijeh](Skyrim_Fijeh.md).
