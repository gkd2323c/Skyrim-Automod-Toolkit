[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/06/SR-menu-Alternative_Armors_-_Dwarven_Plate.jpg/200px-SR-menu-Alternative_Armors_-_Dwarven_Plate.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-menu-Alternative_Armors_-_Dwarven_Plate.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-menu-Alternative_Armors_-_Dwarven_Plate.jpg) Dwarven Plate armor **Alternative Armors - Dwarven Plate** is a [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md) that adds a variant of [Dwarven armor](Skyrim_Dwarven.md) that is based on the [Dwarven Plate](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Blades:Dwarven_Plate) armor set from *[Blades](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Blades:Blades)*.

## Official Summary
*Includes two pieces of heavy Dwarven armor as featured in The Elder Scrolls: Blades! Quest by Kris Takahashi. (Quest "[Mightier than the Sword](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mightier_than_the_Sword)" starts by reading [Looter's Note](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Looter%27s_Note) at the [Silver-Blood Inn](Skyrim_Silver-Blood_Inn.md) in [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md).)*
