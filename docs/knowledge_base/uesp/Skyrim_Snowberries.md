## Ingredients
142 guaranteed samples can be found in 66 different locations. Locations with the greatest numbers are:

- 15 in [Yngol Barrow](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Yngol_Barrow) ([Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold_(region).md))
- 12 in the [Solitude Catacombs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Solitude_Catacombs) ([Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md))
- 7 in [Broken Tower Redoubt](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Broken_Tower_Redoubt) ([The Reach](Skyrim_The_Reach.md))
- 5 in [Aeri's House](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Aeri%27s_House) ([The Pale](Skyrim_The_Pale.md))
- 5 in the [Bards College](Skyrim_Bards_College.md) ([Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md))
- 5 in [Brandy-Mug Farm](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Brandy-Mug_Farm) ([Eastmarch](Skyrim_Eastmarch.md))
- 10 outside of [College of Winterhold](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(place).md) ([Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold.md))

It is categorized as a common ingredient, meaning that all apothecary merchants have a 36% chance of carrying 1-5 samples. In addition, it may be randomly found in some barrels and in [apothecary's satchels](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Apothecary%27s_Satchel).

## Notes
- Snowberries are also categorized as [food](Skyrim_Food.md), meaning they can be sold to innkeepers as well as apothecary merchants.
- In addition to alchemy, they are used in [baking](Skyrim_Baking.md) the [Snowberry Crostata](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Snowberry_Crostata)<sup>[HF](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md)</sup> and the [Lavender Dumpling](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lavender_Dumpling)<sup>[HF](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md)</sup>.
- Snowberry bushes actually come in two different versions: one dusted with snow and another that is snow free. Both yield the same ingredient, however.
- Snowberries seem to be based on the real-world Holly tree, such as the leaf shape, berry color and fruit growth formation. However, Holly is a tall tree in real life, and the Snowberry is a bush in the game.
- The snowy, mountainous areas of southern [Falkreath hold](Skyrim_Falkreath_Hold.md) host the largest snowberry plants in the game, particularly around the border of Skyrim and Cyrodiil; they can match or exceed the height of an Altmer character.
- Version 2.0.1 of the [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch) replaces all snowberry bushes in [Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold.md) with the snow-covered version of the same plant.
- Version 2.0.8 of the [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch) replaces all snowberry bushes outside [Driftshade Refuge](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Driftshade_Refuge), [High Gate Ruins](Skyrim_High_Gate_Ruins.md), [Hob's Fall Cave](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hob%27s_Fall_Cave), and [North Cold Rock Pass](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:North_Cold_Rock_Pass) with the snow-covered version of the same plant.
