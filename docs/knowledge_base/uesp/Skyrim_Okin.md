[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/6a/SR-item-Okin.jpg/200px-SR-item-Okin.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Okin.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Okin.jpg) Okin Okin is a [Nord Hero War Axe](Skyrim_Nord_Hero_War_Axe.md) that served as one of favored weapons of the [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) warrior [Kvenel the Tongue](Skyrim_Kvenel_the_Tongue.md), Forged by Haman Forgefire to be used alongside the [Nord Hero Sword](Skyrim_Nord_Hero_Sword.md) [Eduj](Skyrim_Eduj.md). Okin can be found in the possession of its owner within his tomb, [Volunruud](Skyrim_Volunruud.md). The weapon itself comes with an enchantment that does ten points of [frost damage](Skyrim_Frost_Damage.md) and [slows](Skyrim_Slow.md) the target by fifty points for three seconds. [Tempering](Skyrim_Grindstone.md) Okin requires a [steel ingot](Skyrim_Steel_Ingot.md) and the [Arcane Blacksmith](Skyrim_Arcane_Blacksmith.md) perk, with the benefits of the improvements being doubled if the [steel smithing](Skyrim_Steel_Smithing.md) perk is unlocked.

[Heddic Volunnar](Skyrim_Heddic_Volunnar.md), a Nord who went on an expedition to explore Kvenel's tomb, made a mention of his weapons in his [notes on Volunruud](Skyrim_Heddic%27s_Volunruud_Notes.md) before tragically being killed. As the notes reveal, the door to Kvenel's resting place is locked in place, waiting for ceremonial replicas of his signature weapons to open it. Once inside the inner section of the crypt, Volunruud Elder's Cairn, an apparition of Kvenel the Tongue can be found sitting upon a throne. Should he be approached, he will attack, duel wielding the true versions of Eduj and Okin as he presses the assault. If Kvenel's spirit is put to rest again, the two weapons can be recovered from his remains.

## Bugs
- Killing Kvenel while he is sitting on his throne may make it impossible to recover Okin. You can get Eduj, but when you activate his remains again to obtain Okin, it won't be there. Reloading a previous save to re-kill Kvenel off his throne solves the problem. <sup>**?**</sup> - You can kill Kvenel while he is sitting and still get both weapons if you pickpocket Okin first. Eduj is still lootable from his remains.
- You can also loot him twice. The first time Eduj will appear, the second - Okin.
