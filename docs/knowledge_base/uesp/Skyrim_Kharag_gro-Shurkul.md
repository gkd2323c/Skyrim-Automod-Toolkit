[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a8/SR-npc-Kharag_gro-Shurkul.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Kharag_gro-Shurkul.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Kharag_gro-Shurkul.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Kharag_gro-Shurkul.jpg) Kharag gro-Shurkul **Kharag gro-Shurkul** is an [Orc](Skyrim_Orc.md) [lumberjack](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lumberjack) who works for [Hjorunn](Skyrim_Hjorunn.md) at the [Solitude Sawmill](Skyrim_Solitude_Sawmill.md). He will sell [lumber](Skyrim_Sawn_Log.md)<sup>[HF](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md)</sup> to you if [Hearthfire](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md) is installed.

He cooks at the sawmill house from midnight to 1am, then heads to the sawmill for work. At 8am, he goes home to cook for an hour, then resumes his work outside until 4pm. He spends the rest of the afternoon at the house, either sleeping, having a meal, or relaxing.

Kharag wears a [belted tunic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Belted_Tunic) and a pair of [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#0001be1b). He wields an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) and carries his [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Solitude_Sawmill) to the mill along with the [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Kharag%27s_Room) to his room.

## Dialogue
Kharag will come talk about his dislike of going to town when greeting you:

*"What do you want?"* *"Out here at work, Hjorunn treats me like an equal. Better than most in town."* *"Any day is a good one, long as I don't have to go into town. But some days, Hjorunn's too drunk..."* (If Hjorunn is alive) *"Any day is a good one, long as I don't have to go into town. But with Hjorunn gone..."* (If Hjorunn is dead)
## Conversations
Occasionally he will converse with Hjorunn:

**Hjorunn**: *"Kharag, take a break and drink with me! Those trees aren't going anywhere!"* **OR** *"Why so glum, my ugly friend? Did we run out of mead?"*
**Kharag**: *"I have to work twice as hard because you drink up all our profits."* **OR** *"Can you pay attention to work for once, instead of your mug?"*
**Hjorunn**: *"What did you say? I ought to break off one of those tusks and use it as my drinking horn!"* **OR** *"Don't make me to defend the honor of my mead! It's been my friend a lot longer than you have!"*
**Kharag**: *"Hjorunn, the last time you tried anything, your fist broke on my head!"* **OR** *"Just as long as you don't breathe on me, Hjorunn. I'll be out for hours."*
**Hjorunn**: *"Guess we can't afford to miss that much work, can we? Oh well..."* **OR** *"True enough, you cur. Back to work, I suppose..."*
## Notes
- Despite what Kharag says, he can never be found in town, and neither can Hjorunn. Additionally, the townsfolk will never mention having any dislike of the Orc, as no one will ever acknowledge his existence at all.
- Kharag is a potential member of the `Current Follower Faction`, meaning that he could have once been intended to serve you as a follower. The fact that he is set up as a leveled rather than a static NPC seems to further imply this, but there are no other traces of how this was meant to play out, and he lacks the other factions necessary to make it work.
- Kharag's lumber buying dialogue is much shorter than that of all the others you can buy logs from, consisting of *"How much?"* and *"For that price, sure."*
- The base game version of Oblivion's *[Prima Official Game Guide](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Books:The_Elder_Scrolls_IV__Oblivion_Official_Game_Guide)* mentions "Kharag gro-Shurkul" under the male Orcish "Sample Names For Tamriel's Races".

## Bugs
- He never sleeps due to his sleep package being blocked by his work packages. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.2.7, fixes this bug.

- Despite this, he can sometimes be found sleeping in his bed, anyway.
- The key to "his room" unlocks nothing, as there are no doors within the mill. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.1.2, addresses this issue. The key is removed from his inventory.
- Several problems made it difficult for Kharag to actually follow his work schedule. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 3.0.6, fixes this bug.
- Kharag will refer to Hjorunn as dead even if he isn't, due to incorrect conditions attached to the dialogue.
