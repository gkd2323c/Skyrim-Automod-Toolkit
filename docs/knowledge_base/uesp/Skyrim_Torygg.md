[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/56/SR-npc-High_King_Torygg.jpg/200px-SR-npc-High_King_Torygg.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-High_King_Torygg.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-High_King_Torygg.jpg) High King Torygg High King Torygg was the [Jarl](Skyrim_Jarl.md) of [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md) and High King of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) until his death at the hands of [Ulfric Stormcloak](Skyrim_Ulfric_Stormcloak.md) in [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), signaling the start of the [Stormcloak Rebellion](Lore_Stormcloak_Rebellion.md). For more information, see the [main lore article](Lore_Torygg.md).

He wears the unique [embroidered garment](Skyrim_Embroidered_Garment.md), a pair of [cuffed boots](Skyrim_Cuffed_Boots.md), and a [gold and ruby circlet](Skyrim_Gold_and_Ruby_Circlet.md). He is armed with a [steel sword](Skyrim_Steel_Sword.md).

## Related Quests
- **[Sovngarde](Skyrim_Sovngarde_(quest).md)**: Seek out [Alduin](Skyrim_Alduin.md) within [Sovngarde](Skyrim_Sovngarde_(place).md).

## Dialogue
If you encounter him in [Sovngarde](Skyrim_Sovngarde_(place).md):

- *"When Ulfric Stormcloak, with savage Shout, sent me here, my sole regret was fair Elisif, left forlorn and weeping. I faced him fearlessly - my fate inescapable, yet my honor is unstained - can Ulfric say the same?"*

If you encounter him before [Alduin](Skyrim_Alduin.md) has been defeated, Torygg may say:

- *"Beware! The World-Eater waits within the mist!"*
- *"Do you know the way? I'm weary and lost."*
- *"Turn back! Before he finds you!"*
- *"There is no escape. Courage is useless."*

If you encounter him after Alduin is defeated:

- *"The mist is gone! A glorious day!"*
- *"The World-Eater is dead! The way is now clear!"*
- *"All hail the Dragonborn! Hail him/her with great praise!"*
- *"You saved us all. We will sing of you forever."*
