*Enchanting description:* **Absorb <mag> points of health.**

**Absorb Health** (a.k.a. **Drain Health**) transfers <mag> points of [Health](Skyrim_Health.md) from the target to the person wielding the weapon. On the target, the effect is comparable to [Damage Health](Skyrim_Damage_Health.md); on the attacker, the effect is comparable to [Restore Health](Skyrim_Restore_Health.md).

## Enchanting

## Notes
- In Skyrim, there appears to be no distinction between "Drain Health" and "Absorb Health". [Ravage Health](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ravage_Health) replaces the "Drain Health" effect from previous games.
- For those enchanting for profit, absorb health provides the third most lucrative profitable enchantment in the game, after [Paralysis](Skyrim_Paralyze_(effect).md) and [Banish](Skyrim_Banish.md). It is, however, the most lucrative weapon enchantment that is available at the beginning of the game.
- Absorb Health will not work against [Dwarven Automatons](Skyrim_Dwarven_Automatons.md); you can't leech their health in battle to heal you.
- The Ebony Blade has a unique form of this enchantment, which varies in magnitude as the blade is powered up.
- The book *[Notes On The Lunar Forge](Skyrim_Notes_On_The_Lunar_Forge.md)* incorrectly states that Lunar weapons give you a [vampire-like](Skyrim_Vampirism.md) effect that transfers health from the victim to the user like this one, when their [real enchantment](Skyrim_Light_Damage.md) doesn't absorb health at all.
- The Lord's Mail Creation Club armor has a constant absorb health effect that will absorb the health of foes by 5 pts per second within a certain radius.

## Bugs
- Combining Absorb Health with Frost Damage will result in fewer charges than the enchanting altar indicates. The magnitude of problem gets worse when fortify enchanting potions are used. <sup>**?**</sup>
- The Absorb Health shader (Ench Red FXShader) has incorrect UV scale settings which can lead to the effect causing distortions on weapons it was applied to. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Legendary_Edition_Patch), version 3.0.4, fixes this bug.
