## Notes
- The Vampire Gauntlets are the only piece of vampire-specific, non-unique equipment to be worn by only the randomly encountered Traveler. All other vampires (both named and generic) do not wear them. Similarly, the gloves, boots and hood are the only vampire equipment worn by the Advisor, who is only encountered if you join the Dawnguard.

- The Vampire Hood can be found in the Bloodchill Manor, along with the Vampire Leather Hood.

## Bugs
- Wearing the red armor and being in first person will show your arms with the sleeves of the light grey armor, instead of the proper red and black ones. <sup>**?**</sup>
- The sleeves of vampire armor will clip notably through most close-fitting gauntlets and bracers, such as Elven Gauntlets, Leather Bracers, Glass Gauntlets, etc. <sup>**?**</sup>
