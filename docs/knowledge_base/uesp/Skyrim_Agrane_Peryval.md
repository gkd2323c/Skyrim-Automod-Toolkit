[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a5/SR-npc-Agrane_Peryval.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Agrane_Peryval.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Agrane_Peryval.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Agrane_Peryval.jpg) Agrane Peryval Agrane Peryval is a [Breton](Skyrim_Breton.md) merchant who can be found at his campsite north of the [Windward Ruins](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Windward_Ruins), just outside [Dawnstar](Skyrim_Dawnstar.md). You will be directed to him as part of the related quest, although you do not need to purchase anything from him.

He wears a set of [Saturalia clothing](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Saturalia_Clothes), which consists of boots, gloves, clothes, and a hat, and wields an [elven](Skyrim_Elven.md) mace. He also carries a [woodcutter's axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Woodcutter%27s_Axe) and a selection of [common loot and gold](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Common). Additionally, he knows the spell [Fast Healing](Skyrim_Fast_Healing.md).
