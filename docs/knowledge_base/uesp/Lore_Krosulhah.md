[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f2/SR-quest-The_Gardener_of_Men_03.jpg/200px-SR-quest-The_Gardener_of_Men_03.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-The_Gardener_of_Men_03.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-The_Gardener_of_Men_03.jpg) Krosulhah outside Nchardak Krosulhah was a [dragon](Lore_Dragon.md) whose name meant **Sorcerer-Day-Mind** in the [Dragon Language](Lore_Dragon_Language.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Shout-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Prima-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Alduin-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-SOS-4)</sup> At a certain point in history, Krosulhah swore allegiance to [Miraak](Lore_Miraak.md) and considered him the only true [Dovahkiin](Lore_Dragonborn.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Krosulhah-5)</sup>

In [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), the [Last Dragonborn](Lore_Hero.md#Last_Dragonborn) and [Neloth](Lore_Neloth.md) traveled to [Nchardak](Lore_Nchardak.md) to retrieve the [Black Book](Lore_Black_Books.md) that was kept there. After the successful retrieval of the artifact, they were attacked by Krosulhah, who was sent to kill the Last Dragonborn in the name of Miraak. They did battle at the entrance to Nchardak, and the Dragonborn, aided by Neloth, absorbed the dragon's soul.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Krosulhah-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TGOM-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-DBPath-7)</sup> Having had his soul devoured by another dovah, Krosulhah was killed permanently and cannot be raised again.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-LAD-8)</sup><sup>[[9]](#cite_note-Arngeir-9)</sup>
