# Fox

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c9/SR-creature-Fox.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Fox.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Fox.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Fox.jpg) A fox (Skyrim) Foxes are small, non-aggressive creatures that inhabit the wilderness. They prey on [rabbits](Lore_Rabbit.md) and flee from larger predators and people. Hunters target foxes mainly for their pelts, and although less common, some people of [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) keep foxes as pets. Some species are unusually large, and can be ridden as mounts.

**[Fennec Foxes](Lore_Fennec_Fox.md)** are a breed of fox native to [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md). Adapted to the desert, these foxes prey on [scorpions](Lore_Scorpion.md). They can be difficult to hunt as their large ears grant them excellent hearing, but hunters can also utilize a poison made of garlic and onion paste. Fennec fox meat is more tender and less gamey than that of [wolves](Lore_Wolf.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AAIT-1)</sup> Many desert people have domesticated fennec foxes into loyal and alert pets.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup>

## Culture
According to [druid](Lore_Druids.md) legend, the first foxes swam from [Y'ffelon](Lore_Y%27ffelon.md) shortly before [Mount Firesong](Lore_Mount_Firesong.md) first erupted.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup>

[Shor](Lore_Shor.md) is often depicted as a fox,<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ESOEFT-4)</sup> and carvings of foxes are often seen in Nordic ruins.

Members of [House Hlaalu](Lore_House_Hlaalu.md) are known to play a game called "Foxes & Felines".<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-FAF-5)</sup>

Some foxes are sacred to the [Reachfolk](Lore_Reachfolk.md). The Reach leatherworkers of [Markarth](Lore_Markarth.md) are known to set aside sacred fox hides for the swiftest and most cunning hunters.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-LOTQF-6)</sup> One of the aspects of the [Daedric Prince](Lore_Daedric_Prince.md) [Hircine](Lore_Hircine.md), Gulibeg, is known as the Quick Fox and shares characteristics with some cultural interpretations of [Lorkhan](Lore_Lorkhan.md).<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ONGulibeg-7)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-VOF-8)</sup><sup>[[9]](#cite_note-ESOFAHM-9)</sup><sup>[[10]](#cite_note-ONHSG-10)</sup>

## Species
- **Arctic Fennec Fox** — The Arctic Fennec is native to the snowy shores of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md)'s [Sea of Ghosts](Lore_Sea_of_Ghosts.md). When local [Nords](Lore_Nord.md) spot one romping in the drifts, they respond with the traditional oath of *"Shor's Ears!"*.
- **Bergama Fennec Fox** — [Ash'abah](Lore_Ash%27abah.md) revere fennec foxes for leading lost children back to the tribe. Scholars say that it is more likely the foxes are seeking out the nearest watering hole.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-11)</sup>
- **Dusky Fennec Fox** — Also called the **Midnight Fennec Fox**. They are native to the high savannah of northern [Elsweyr](Lore_Elsweyr.md), and are often domesticated by nomadic tribes of [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md).
- **Flint-Pelt Fox** — Legend tells of a [Breton](Lore_Breton.md) hunter who freed a Flint-Pelt Fox from a [hagraven](Lore_Hagraven.md)'s snare. Now these creatures seek out any warrior that attacks hagravens, often lingering around areas terrorized by their hated enemies.
- **Linchal Titan Fox** — Though shy, this orange and black fox fiercely defends its den, a trait which builders of [Fort Linchal](Lore_Fort_Linchal.md) noted in their records. They tamed those they displaced during construction, then took them home to the [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md), where they were popular with nobles.
- **Snow Foxes** — A small, white-furred variety of fox known for their speed that can be encountered in the colder regions of Skyrim. [Rabbits](Lore_Rabbit.md) are one of their favorite prey. Some are kept as pets by Skyrim's inhabitants. Hunters often sell their pelts, which are considered more valuable than those of regular foxes.
- **Swamp-Foxes** — A species native to [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md).
