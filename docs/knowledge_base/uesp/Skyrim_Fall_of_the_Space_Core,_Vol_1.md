It was made available exclusively on the PC through Steam on February 7, 2012 alongside the [Creation Kit](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Creation_Kit) and [High Resolution Texture Pack](Skyrim_High_Resolution_Texture_Pack.md). It can be downloaded for free from the [Steam Workshop](http://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=7375). The mod was not officially made available for the [Special Edition](Skyrim_Special_Edition.md).

## Overview
The add-on adds the titular [Space Core](http://theportalwiki.com/wiki/Space_Sphere) to the game. To find the [Space Core](#Space_Core), go to the ruined house west and a little north of the [Western Watchtower](Skyrim_Western_Watchtower.md), outside of [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md) [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=-4648&y=-8009). The best way to find it is by starting at [Whiterun Stables](Skyrim_Whiterun.md) and going directly west from there. When you've passed the Western Watchtower on your left, the Space Core will suddenly plunge from the sky and crash into the ruins, setting them on fire. The player may then carry the Space Core, or place it in their inventory. The Space Core will say a single line of dialogue each time it is activated, or when idle, with many lines referring to the land and events of Skyrim.

The Space Core can be taken to the [Atronach Forge](Skyrim_Atronach_Forge.md) in [The Midden](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Midden) and placed on the pedestal to power the forge. If an ingot of [Refined Moonstone](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Refined_Moonstone) or a piece of [Moonstone Ore](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Moonstone_Ore) and an [Iron Helmet](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Helmet) are placed in the offering box, a [Dovahcore Helmet](#Dovahcore_Helmet) will be created. The Dovahcore Helmet can then be improved at the workbench through [Smithing](Skyrim_Smithing.md) by characters with the [Arcane Blacksmith](Skyrim_Arcane_Blacksmith.md) perk using a Refined Moonstone ingot.

The add-on also adds two new loading screens featuring the Space Core, and adds [Wheatley](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wheatley_(Portal)), who flies around the [Skills](Skyrim_Skills.md) menu.

## Notes
- There are two new [loading screens](Skyrim_Loading_Screens.md) added by the add-on. One reads:

*Space Spheres, also known as the Spheriphem, are natives of space, and will cajole adventurers to help them return to that comforting void.* The other reads: *Space is the thing you see above Tamriel when you're walking around outside with your head tilted back.* - The Space Core weighs 3.14, which is a simplified version of [pi](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pi). This is rounded down to 3.1 in game.
- The item values are powers of 2 commonly used in computing.

## Bugs
- Attempting to place the Space Core on the Atronach Forge while in possession of the [Sigil Stone](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sigil_Stone) will duplicate the latter. <sup>**?**</sup> - To place the Space Core, you will need to drop all Sigil Stones from your inventory.
- After a prolonged period outside the inventory, the Space Core may stop idly talking. It will still talk upon interaction. - This can be fixed by placing it on the Atronach Forge pedestal.
