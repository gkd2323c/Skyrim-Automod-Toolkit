Curator's List of Sought-After Relics **by [Curator Umutha](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Curator_Umutha)** *A list of various prized artifacts and their last known location*
---
The First Charter: Lost somewhere in the western mountains.

Dwarf Light: Legend has it the lantern was most recently used to explore Nyzchaleft Falls.

Uzdabikh's Helm: Disappeared during the ancient battle of Fharun Pass.

Trinimac House Idol: A tiny, ancient statue used for personal worship, last seen near Paragon's Remembrance.

Guthrag's Mask: Last possessed by the Chief's Eye of [Fharun Stronghold](Lore_Fharun_Stronghold.md).

[Frostbreak Chalice](Lore_Frostbreak_Chalice_(item).md): Rumored to have been reclaimed by one of [Urfon](Lore_Urfon_Ice-Heart.md)'s underlings and hidden in one of the [fortress](Lore_Frostbreak_Fortress.md)'s towers.

Agra Crun: The blood shield of [Malacath](Lore_Malacath.md)'s faithful, lost in the wilds of [Wrothgar](Lore_Wrothgar.md).

[Nuzava's Anvil](Lore_Nuzava%27s_Anvil_(item).md): Owned by the fabled smith who defined the [Morkul](Lore_Morkul_Clan.md) style, it disappeared on the Icy Shore as [Nuzava](Lore_Nuzava.md) traveled home.

Thugbo's Map of Clans: Recovered from a destroyed historical text but lost on the road to [Orsinium](Lore_Orsinium.md).

Heart of Zandadunoz: A titan's phylactery, lost near [Honor's Rest](Lore_Honor%27s_Rest.md).

Torc of Baloth Bloodtusk: A heavy necklace worn by the [Wild Boar](Lore_Baloth_Bloodtusk.md), it disappeared from history near what is now known as [Honor's Rest](Lore_Honor%27s_Rest.md).

Horn of Beasts: Broken, its remains probably lie in or near the ruins of [Old Orsinium](Lore_Old_Orsinium.md).

Centurion's Signet: Lost near Paragon's Rememberance *[[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)]*.

Thukhozod's Bracers: Prized by the necromancer and thought to be hidden in his sanctum.

Liquid Silver: A rare metal once mined from the depths of [Argent Mine](Lore_Argent_Mine.md).

Black Quill: Fabled bardic writing instrument, connected by legend to Coldperch Cavern.

Eye of Zthenganaz: A cursed [Dwarven](Lore_Dwemer.md) device, lost near the ruins of Rkindaleft.

Hammer of Glass: Carved from a single crystal and lost in the ruins of [Old Orsinium](Lore_Old_Orsinium.md).

Scepter of the King-Chief: An [Orc](Lore_Orc.md) relic currently possessed by a lowly but powerful [Riekr](Lore_Riekr.md).

Mad Urkazbur's Ice-Effigy: History in the making from an [ogre](Lore_Ogre.md) elder.

Note: The greatest relic of King Torug's companion, lost to the ages, can only be recovered by a true Hero of Wrothgar. Only the most worthy will be contacted by the curator when they are ready.
