[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a3/ON-place-Temple_of_the_Crescent_Moons.jpg/200px-ON-place-Temple_of_the_Crescent_Moons.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Temple_of_the_Crescent_Moons.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Temple_of_the_Crescent_Moons.jpg) A temple on Khenarthi's Roost Khenarthi's Roost, the ** Isle of Three Temples**, is a small island off the coast of [Elsweyr](Lore_Elsweyr.md) inhabited by both [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md) and [Maormer](Lore_Maormer.md), named for the Khajiiti goddess [Khenarthi](Lore_Khenarthi.md). The island is dotted with [moon sugar](Lore_Moon_Sugar.md) plantations and is a location of both active Khajiiti worship and Maormer magic, as the Khajiit signed a peace treaty with the Maormer which allowed them to settle on the island.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-To KR-1)</sup> The port city of [Mistral](Lore_Mistral.md) functions as the capital of the island and the center of commerce. Khenarthi's Roost once contained the ancient Kingdom of **Khenarthia**, one of the original sixteen kingdoms of Elsweyr, known for their sugar production and expert fishermen.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TSK-2)</sup>

According to legend, the hawk goddess [Khenarthi](Lore_Khenarthi.md) rested upon a tree on the island during her first journey across the heavens. This tree would later become the Great Tree, found along the coast west of Mistral.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESOBenieth-3)</sup> Many of the island's Khajiit therefore worship Khenarthi, although traditional worship of [Jone](Lore_Jone.md) and [Jode](Lore_Jode.md) is also prominent. Khenarthi's Roost is known for the three Khajiiti temples to the Crescent Moons, the [Dark Moon](Lore_Dark_Moon.md), and the [Two-Moons Dance](Lore_Two-Moons_Dance.md). The island is lush with vegetation, and beasts brought by trade vessels from all over [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) have come to call Khenarthi's Roost home. Among these creatures are the [skeevers](Lore_Skeever.md) of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md), the [alits](Lore_Alit.md) of [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md), the [thunderbugs](Lore_Thunderbug.md) of [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md), and [giant snakes](Lore_Giant_Snake.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup> The rare [Pyandonean Ray](Lore_Pyandonean_Ray.md) can be caught off the coast, and [harpies](Lore_Harpy.md) and [senche](Lore_Senche-Cat.md) can also be encountered.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ESOFauna-5)</sup>

Viper Island is a somewhat large island west of Khenarthi's Roost.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-CAESO-6)</sup>

## History
Maormer and Khajiit coexisted on Khenarthi's Roost, sometimes on friendly terms, sometimes not, since as early as [Merethic Era](Lore_Merethic_Era.md). [Anequina Sharp-Tongue](Lore_Anequina_Sharp-Tongue.md) and [Khunzar-ri](Lore_Khunzar-ri.md) visited the isle during the time of conflict. One of the Sea Elf captains, Linvalor, kidnapped the daughter of a Moon-Priest. Anequina rescued her by winning the game of Deceiver's Disks.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-KR-7)</sup>

The island was once ruled by a powerful Maormer mage named [Uldor](Lore_Uldor.md), who used his magic to steal and wear the bodies of his victims, entrapping their spirits forever. Among his victims were a crew of [Nord](Lore_Nord.md) raiders from [Windhelm](Lore_Windhelm.md) who attempted to loot the island.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-Jakidi ESO-8)</sup> Uldor was eventually sealed within the Temple of the Mourning Springs along with his victims when the Khajiit received an [artifact](Lore_Artifacts.md) known as the Mourning Stone from the Maormer who opposed Uldor.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-Tot TMESO-9)</sup>

The Khajiit of the island eventually signed the [Treaty of Khenarthi's Roost](Lore_Treaty_of_Khenarthi%27s_Roost.md) with the Maormer, wherein an Ambassador of King [Orgnum](Lore_Orgnum.md) would share power with the Mayor of Mistral. The treaty sought to end Maormer piracy in the region in return for fifteen percent of all registry fees and excises. The usually seafaring Maormer would occupy a full-time embassy in Mistral, and the Maormer festival of Serpent's Glory would be celebrated with a feast every year.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-To KR-1)</sup> However, the wording of this treaty unfairly favored the Maormer, and acts of piracy continued.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-TPo DESO-10)</sup><sup>[[11]](#cite_note-11)</sup>

In [2E 580](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_580),<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-12)</sup><sup>[[13]](#cite_note-AUQ-13)</sup> the first [Aldmeri Dominion](Lore_Aldmeri_Dominion.md) was formed by an alliance between the [Altmer](Lore_Altmer.md) of [Summerset](Lore_Summerset_Isles.md) and the [Bosmer](Lore_Bosmer.md) of Valenwood. The Khajiit of [Elsweyr](Lore_Elsweyr.md) were soon invited to join, although the Maormer presence on Khenarthi's Roost delayed the island's membership invitation until [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), when a Dominion delegation was sent to the island. The Dominion quickly began spreading propaganda among the Khajiiti islanders,<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-Thalmor Rep ESO-14)</sup> and each moon sugar plantation came under inspection in an attempt to weed out illegal [skooma](Lore_Skooma.md) production and end the smuggling of the island's Crosstree bandit gang.<sup>[[15]](#cite_note-TFBESO-15)</sup><sup>[[16]](#cite_note-APo SESO-16)</sup><sup>[[17]](#cite_note-MSMESO-17)</sup> The delegation was headed by [the Silvenar](Lore_The_Silvenar.md) [Edhelorn](Lore_Edhelorn.md) and the [Green Lady](Lore_Green_Lady.md) [Finoriell](Lore_Finoriell.md), the embodiment of the Bosmer people. However, the Dominion fleet was wrecked on the coast by a mysterious storm, and several crewmembers were abducted by the Sea Vipers, Maormer pirates.<sup>[[18]](#cite_note-Sot HESO-18)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-CAESO-6)</sup> The Great Tree became infested with [kwama](Lore_Kwama.md) after this storm when a trading ship was wrecked nearby, although the tree's [spriggan](Lore_Spriggan.md) guardians managed to tame the kwama to nurture the tree's roots.<sup>[[19]](#cite_note-TRot PESO-19)</sup> Dominion researchers also foolishly removed the Mourning Stone from Uldor's tomb, causing his spirit to briefly plague the island with undead until the temple was resealed.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-Tot TMESO-9)</sup>

Viewing Khenarthi's Roost as Maormer territorial waters, Ambassador Ulondil was justifiably outraged at the Altmer proposition of the island joining the Dominion. The embassy refused to allow the delegates to view their treaty, forcing Dominion agents to steal it. Once the bias was revealed to Mayor Harrani, the Khajiit began to consider the Dominion's offer.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-TPo DESO-10)</sup> Seeing that all hope was lost, the Maormer assassinated the Silvenar and began a ritual to summon Storm-Slave, a powerful [storm atronach](Lore_Storm_Atronach.md), with the intention to destroy Mistral. The Dominion prevented this and drove the Maormer off, allowing the grateful Khajiit to join the alliance.<sup>[[20]](#cite_note-TTUESO-20)</sup> The Maormer paid for their treachery soon afterwards, when the dying Finoriell swam the whole way to [Pyandonea](Lore_Pyandonea.md) and spent her remaining life killing indiscriminately.<sup>[[21]](#cite_note-ONFinoriell-21)</sup>
