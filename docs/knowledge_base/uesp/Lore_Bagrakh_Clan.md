# Bagrakh Clan

[![ON-misc-Seal of Clan Bagrakh.png](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/1c/ON-misc-Seal_of_Clan_Bagrakh.png/200px-ON-misc-Seal_of_Clan_Bagrakh.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-misc-Seal_of_Clan_Bagrakh.png) The Bagrakh Clan (or **Clan Bagrakh**) is an [Orsimer](Lore_Orc.md) clan known to have existed since the [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md) and resided in [Wrothgar](Lore_Wrothgar.md). They were one of the founding Orc clans of [Old Orsinium](Lore_Orsinium.md) in the First Era.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-FOFAF-1)</sup> [Malacath](Lore_Malacath.md) described them as warriors of Old Orsinium, but criticized them for grow to enjoy raiding the weak for cheap thrills, instead of doing it to draw out worthy opposition.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Malacath-2)</sup> [King Kurog](Lore_Kurog_gro-Orsinium.md) was a famous member of the clan, noted for founding the [Second Orsinium](Lore_Orsinium.md).

## History
During the early tenth century of the First Era, Clan Bagrakh committed the Bjoulsae Raids against [Breton](Lore_Breton.md) and [Redguard](Lore_Redguard.md) cities along the [river](Lore_Bjoulsae_River.md). [Golkarr](Lore_Golkarr.md) and [Clan Igrun](Lore_Igrun_Clan.md) praised the raids, possibly even taking credit for them, though in the eyes of [Men](Lore_Men.md), the Orcs of Orsinium were mainly responsible.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-FOFAF-1)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Ushutha Journal-3)</sup> In [1E 948](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_948), [King Joile](Lore_Joile.md) of [Daggerfall](Lore_Daggerfall.md) sent a message to [Gaiden Shinji](Lore_Gaiden_Shinji.md) regarding the raids, and as a result the [Siege of Orsinium](Lore_Siege_of_Orsinium.md) began two years later.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-WJot B-4)</sup>

After the Siege, the [Malacath Devoted](Lore_Wrathful_Flame.md) performed a ritual every decade cursing Clan Bagrakh and a number of others under Malacath's orders, for the reason that Bagrakh's raids were the buildup for the invasion of Orsinium.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-FOFAF-1)</sup>

In his youth, [Kurog gro-Bagrakh](Lore_Kurog_gro-Orsinium.md), the future King of the Second Orsinium, was born into a Bagrakh stronghold located in Wrothgar. When he returned to the stronghold a few years prior to [King Ranser's War](Lore_Ranser%27s_War.md) in [2E 566](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_566), he challenged Chief [Bolazgar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Bolazgar) to a duel. Kurog killed Bolazgar and was subsequently made the chief of Clan Bagrakh,<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TCo KK-5)</sup> though this ended when he was killed in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-BOAKH-6)</sup><sup>[[nb 1]](#intnote_nb1)</sup>
