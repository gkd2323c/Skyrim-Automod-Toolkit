# Beryl

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9b/ON-furnishing-Fabricant_Shrubs%2C_Beryl.jpg/200px-ON-furnishing-Fabricant_Shrubs%2C_Beryl.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-furnishing-Fabricant_Shrubs,_Beryl.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-furnishing-Fabricant_Shrubs,_Beryl.jpg) Fabricant Shrubs, Beryl Beryl is a crystalline material. It is integrated into the mechanical flora of the [Clockwork City](Lore_Clockwork_City.md), often found as a key component in shrubs and [cypress](Lore_Cypress.md) trees within the realm.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-CC1-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-CC2-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-CC3-3)</sup> A specific type of blue dye, known as Abyssal Beryl is named after beryls.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-AB-4)</sup> Additionally, beryl-inspired names are given to pets,<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Nixad-5)</sup> used as nicknames,<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-STBC-6)</sup> and even applied to formal names of variants of species, like the Berylglow [Nix-Ox Steed](Lore_Nix-Ox.md).<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-BNOS-7)</sup> Beryl gemstones, sometimes adorned with ancestral runes, were used to craft coat buttons.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-Treasuresberyl-8)</sup>

[Chrysoberyl](Lore_Chrysoberyl.md) is used by [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md) crafters.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-Treasures Alfiq-9)</sup>
