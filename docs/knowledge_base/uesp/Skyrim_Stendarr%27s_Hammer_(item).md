[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/bb/SR-item-Stendarr%27s_Hammer.jpg/200px-SR-item-Stendarr%27s_Hammer.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Stendarr%27s_Hammer.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Stendarr%27s_Hammer.jpg) Stendarr's Hammer Stendarr's Hammer is a unique [Warhammer](Skyrim_Warhammer.md) associated with the deity [Stendarr](Lore_Stendarr.md). The hammer is acquired through a [quest](Skyrim_If_I_had_a_Hammer.md) which involves stealing it from the [Dwemer Museum](Skyrim_Dwemer_Museum.md) in [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md). Its enchantment causes respectable damage to the target, but drains stamina from its wielder.

[Tempering](Skyrim_Grindstone.md) Stendarr's Hammer requires an [ebony ingot](Skyrim_Ebony_Ingot.md), a [flawless sapphire](Skyrim_Flawless_Sapphire.md), and the [Arcane Blacksmithing](Skyrim_Arcane_Blacksmith.md) perk. It is particularly notable for its extreme weight (slightly more than three times the weight of a [Dragonbone Warhammer](Skyrim_Dragonbone_Warhammer.md),<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup> which has the same base damage), giving it an incredible power attack stamina cost in addition to its base Stamina cost enchantment.

## Notes
- Stendarr's Hammer previously [appeared](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Tribunal:Stendarr%27s_Hammer) in *[Tribunal](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Tribunal:Tribunal)*, where it was too heavy and fragile to be used as a feasible weapon.
- Simply having the hammer equipped is enough to slow you down, and will drain stamina faster when sprinting.
