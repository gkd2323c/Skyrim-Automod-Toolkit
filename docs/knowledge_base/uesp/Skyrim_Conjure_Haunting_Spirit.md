- *Summons a Haunting Spirit for 60 seconds. When killed, the spirit steals 10 points from the skills of its killer and transfers them to the caster for a short time.*

**Conjure Haunting Spirit** is an Apprentice level [Conjuration](Skyrim_Conjuration.md) spell added by the [Necromantic Grimoire](Skyrim_Necromantic_Grimoire.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md).

## Effects
- [Conjure Haunting Spirit](Skyrim_Summon.md), for 60 secs
- Upon getting killed, Haunting Gift +10 to all skills for 60 seconds.

## Perks
- [Summoner](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Summoner), increase range to 48 ft at first rank or 72 ft at second rank.
- [Necromancy](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Necromancy), Greater duration for spell.
- [Dark Souls](Skyrim_Dark_Souls.md), increases Health by 100.

## Notes
- **Conjure Haunting Spirit** is unaffected by [Spell Absorption](Skyrim_Spell_Absorption.md).
- The Haunting Gift effect can stack if separate enemies kill different Haunting Spirits.
- All stats are boosted by Haunting Gift, including crafting skills. Thus the spell can be used to brew strong potions, make stronger enchantments, and temper weapons and armor to higher levels.
