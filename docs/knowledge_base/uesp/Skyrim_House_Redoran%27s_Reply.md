House Redoran's Reply **by Dralis Rorlen** *Redoran's response to the issues brought up by Adril's Survey*
---
[Councilor Morvayn](Skyrim_Lleril_Morvayn.md),

While I appreciate the urgency of the situation on [Solstheim](Lore_Solstheim.md), I'm afraid that I can't approve the request for supplies and funds at this time. This is nothing personal, Lleril. Your island is one of seventy-four outlying settlements of [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md) at this time. [House Redoran](Lore_House_Redoran.md) must rank these outposts in terms of economic and strategic importance. We have limited coin in our coffers to send to these settlements, so we have to decide which ones take precedence. Unfortunately, Solstheim is very low on the list. I'm doing everything I can for you. All I ask is that you hang on a little longer.

Dralis Rorlen
Councilmember, House Redoran
