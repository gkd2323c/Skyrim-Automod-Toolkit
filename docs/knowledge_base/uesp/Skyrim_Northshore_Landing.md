## Walkthrough
The dock itself consists of a T-shaped jetty, with the down leg pointing north and falling apart at the end. There are several [mudcrabs](Skyrim_Mudcrab.md) in and around the docks. On the jetty are five fish barrels, several crates, and four sacks, along with a novice-locked [chest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chest). The docks are by the mouth of a stream that is the runoff from a nearby waterfall in the mountains. A [nirnroot](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nirnroot) is by the stream near the base of the waterfall, and behind the waterfall is an unlocked wooden chest. Along the shore and under the docks are several [pearl oysters](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pearl_Oyster), [clams](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Clam), and stacks of crates, barrels, and chests. There is a partially flooded rowboat pulled up on shore to the west of the docks. On the hull is a [Nordic barnacle](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nordic_Barnacle) cluster. On a crate to the immediate right of the stairs up to the docks is another barnacle cluster. Opposite the stairs, to the south, is a novice-locked chest. Due north of the rowboat, under the water, is an apprentice-locked chest beside a sunken boat with two Nordic barnacle clusters on the hull and a patch of [slaughterfish eggs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Slaughterfish_Egg) nearby. Under the docks, level with the end of the damaged jetty, are a couple crates with a [silver ingot](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Silver_Ingot) on the seabed between them.

On the east shore of the stream is an empty chest, with an [ebony ore vein](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ebony_Ore_Vein) farther east against the cliffs, while to the north is a derelict shack with the doors and windows missing and holes in the roof and walls. The shack is home to a large, unique mudcrab, named [Old Salty](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Old_Salty). In front of the shack is an open box containing two pieces of [mudcrab chitin](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mudcrab_Chitin) beside two fish barrels, a food sack, and a [tanning rack](Skyrim_Tanning_Rack.md). Inside, against the south wall, is a bookcase holding an expert-locked [East Empire Company strongbox](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:East_Empire_Company_Strongbox) and many [ruined books](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ruined_Book). To the right of the bookcase is a bucket containing a piece of mudcrab chitin. Against the north wall are two fish barrels, an unlocked chest, and a long wooden table with a map of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) on top. There are three loose [septims](Skyrim_Gold.md) placed over the capitals of three of the [holds](Skyrim_Holds.md), with a further thirteen loose septims, a [gold ring](Skyrim_Gold_Ring.md), and an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) to the right, and a piece of mudcrab chitin to the left. If you look through the window at the table, there is also a [silver amethyst ring](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Silver_Amethyst_Ring) behind a crate that is difficult to see from inside.

If you come to Northshore Landing during the quest [A New Source of Stalhrim](Skyrim_A_New_Source_of_Stalhrim.md), a large ship will be present at the dock. There will be a few [Thalmor soldiers](Skyrim_Thalmor.md#Dragonborn) standing guard or patrolling the area under the leadership of [Ancarion](Skyrim_Ancarion.md).

## Notes
- A small [unmarked island](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Unmarked_Places_Solstheim#Horker_Iceberg) lies to the north, visible from Northshore Landing. There are two [stalhrim](Skyrim_Stalhrim.md) deposits and a leveled enchanted [battleaxe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Battleaxe) there guarded by two or three [horkers](Skyrim_Horker.md). Once the stalhrim has been mined, one of the sarcophagi contains a dead [draugr](Skyrim_Containers.md#Draugr) that can be looted.
- A novice-locked [random chest](Skyrim_Unmarked_Places.md#Another_Random_Chest) surrounded by riekling spears can be found to the southwest against the cliff face, near two horkers.
- During the quest, Old Salty and the rest of the mudcrabs won't be present once Ancarion and his men arrive at the dock. This is especially true if you convince them to become merchants for Stalhrim equipment.
- This wiki's current Skyrim satellite map displays Northshore Landing without Ancarion's ship; the previous Solstheim satellite map displayed the area with the ship present.
