## Bits and Pieces
The entrance leads directly into the shop, with a wooden bench and small round table on the left. The counter is at the far end of the room in front of the south wall. All of the items on display around the shop are [Displayed Merchandise](#Displayed_Merchandise), with the exception of any items in [containers](Skyrim_Containers.md) or in the display cabinets, the herbs hanging from the ceiling, a loose [septim](Skyrim_Gold.md) under the counter, and another loose septim on the shelves behind the counter. The herbs hanging from the ceiling are a total of seven [garlic](Skyrim_Garlic.md) braids, and three bunches of dried [elves ear](Skyrim_Elves_Ear.md). Against the west wall is an expert-locked display cabinet containing a [Dwarven dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dwarven_Dagger), a [steel dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Dagger) and a leveled [warhammer](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Warhammer). There is a second expert-locked display cabinet containing a leveled [greatsword](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Greatsword) (up to [Daedric](Skyrim_Daedric.md)) (see [bugs](#Bugs)) and a leveled [dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dagger). There is a chair between these two cabinets, where you will often find [Sayma](Skyrim_Sayma.md) sitting upon entering the store. There is a central pillar supporting the vaunted ceiling and in the northwestern corner is a cooking fire at eye level with an empty metal pot in front of it and two more hanging above it inside, with a set of double doors leading to the residence.

Through the doors into the residence there is a wooden bench under two wall shelves on the left, on the south wall, with a couple of pieces of cheese on them and a common book on the bench. In the northeastern corner is a cupboard holding two [sweet rolls](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sweet_Roll), a bowl containing [tomatoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tomato), a haunch of raw [venison](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Venison), three loaves of [bread](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bread) and several pieces of cheese, with a wall shelf on the east wall to the right of it holding crockery. To the left of the cupboard, against the north wall, is a bookcase holding six common books, a [flute](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Flute) and a [potion of health](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Health), while in the northwestern corner is a long wooden table and two chairs, with a potion of regeneration, a bunch of tundra cotton, a [baked potato](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Baked_Potatoes), a [salmon steak](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Steak), a [seared slaughterfish](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Seared_Slaughterfish), a loaf of bread, a couple of wheels of cheese and two loose septims on top. To the left of this, along the west wall, is a wooden bench with a small unit holding a bottle of [alto wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine) separating the table and bench. To the south is a stairwell with a flight up to the top floor and a flight down to the cellar.

Halfway up there is a large vase with a [snowberry](Skyrim_Snowberries.md) bush planted in it. At the top of the stairs is a L-shaped balcony with two doors off of it on the west wall. In front of you is a small seating area with a low chair, square wooden table and small round table in the corner with an iron dagger on top. On the square table is a plate of bread and cheese with a bottle of [wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wine) and a [goblet](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Goblet). Around the corner is a narrow table with a [bowl](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bowl) of bread and a plate with two tomatoes and an [eidar cheese wedge](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wedge) on top. Above this is a wall shelf holding a bunch of elves ear. At the northern end of the balcony is a bookcase holding eleven common books, a potion of health and a flute, with a [drum](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Drum) beside it and a chair in front.

The first door opens into a bedroom containing a single bed in the northwestern corner with an open crate at the foot of it containing a bunch of tundra cotton. There is also a small round table with the [business ledger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Business_Ledger) on top. To the right against the east wall is a small wardrobe with a set of [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md) on top and a washbasin on a stand beside it. In the southwestern corner is a tall wardrobe containing fine clothes, with a wall shelf, chair and small wardrobe along the south wall, while opposite the small wardrobe is a small cupboard with an apprentice-locked [strongbox](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Strongbox) containing random items on top of it.

The second door leads to another bedroom with a single bed in the southeastern corner, and small square table beside it with three loose septims on top. At the foot of the bed is another open crate containing a bunch of tundra cotton. To the right of the bed, against the south wall is a set of shelves holding three [iron ingots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Ingot), a bunch of [lavender](Skyrim_Lavender.md), with a potion of regeneration on top and a pair of [iron boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Boots) beside it. Against the north wall is a small wardrobe, with a tall one against the west wall to the left. Opposite the door is a small cupboard containing clutter and with a narrow table to the left, behind the door. On this is an iron dagger and a bowl containing a bunch of tundra cotton and above is a wall shelf holding a set of clothes.

Descending to the cellar leads to a storage area with food barrels, an archery target with a square wooden table with a set of [banded iron armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Banded_Iron_Armor) and a [banded iron shield](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Banded_Iron_Shield) on top in the northwestern corner, while in the southeastern corner is a square wooden table with a [goat hide](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Hide) (see [bugs](#Bugs)) on it and a cupboard with a couple of loaves of bread, several pieces of cheese and a [rock warbler egg](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Rock_Warbler_Egg) on the shelves and an [iron sword](Skyrim_Iron_Sword.md) on top. To the left along the east wall is an unlocked [chest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chest) containing leveled items.

## Notes
- There is a [](Skyrim_Shadowmarks_(Thieves_Guild).md) Loot [shadowmark](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(faction).md#Shadowmarks) to the bottom left of the entrance door.
- Aside from some barrels and sacks, all containers in the area are [non-respawning](Skyrim_Respawning.md) and safe to store items in.
- This building appears in [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Online) as [Bountiful Goods](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Bountiful_Goods).
- If you arrive for the first time at Solitude during the day (open store hours), Bits and Pieces will be the only store (aside from the inn) that will be open, as the others will be closed so the owners or employees can witness Roggvir's execution.
