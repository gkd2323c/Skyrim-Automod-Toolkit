# Dwarven Adamant

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e2/ON-crown_store-Adamant_Dwarven_Senche.jpg/200px-ON-crown_store-Adamant_Dwarven_Senche.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Adamant_Dwarven_Senche.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Adamant_Dwarven_Senche.jpg) A vamidium made of adamant Dwarven Adamant, also known as **Adamant**,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-VDH-1)</sup> is a white-hued alloy of ores of unknown origin, believed to be the most durable material ever made. It is among the rarest materials on [Nirn](Lore_Nirn.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ADW-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ADH-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ADS-4)</sup>

The Dwemer used adamant, along with other materials, such as [ebony](Lore_Ebony.md), [vitrine](Lore_Vitrine.md), and [dwarven metal](Lore_Dwarven_Metal.md), for constructing certain [vamidiums](Lore_Vamidium.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-VDH-1)</sup>

## Notes
- The word Adamant is occasionally used to refer to [Adamantine Tower](Lore_Adamantine_Tower.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TGOA-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TOA-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-TROQA-7)</sup> The tower is made of [Adamantine](Lore_Adamantine.md), however.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TOA-6)</sup>
- The [Dragonguard](Lore_Dragonguard.md) of Dov-Vahl Shrine mention Adamant in one of their inscriptions: "CLAWS OF ADAMANT, VOICE OF END."<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-FTF-8)</sup>
- One of the magical anomalies that occurred in [Craglorn](Lore_Craglorn.md) in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) was known as the Adamant Anomaly.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-OAA-9)</sup>
