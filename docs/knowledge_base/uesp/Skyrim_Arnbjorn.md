[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/22/SR-npc-Arnbjorn.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Arnbjorn.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Arnbjorn.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Arnbjorn.jpg) Arnbjorn sharpening a blade [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/91/SR-npc-Arnbjorn_2.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Arnbjorn_2.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Arnbjorn_2.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Arnbjorn_2.jpg) Werewolf Arnbjorn **Arnbjorn** is a [werewolf](Skyrim_Werewolf.md) [assassin](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Combat Assassin), former [Companion](Skyrim_Companions.md), and a member of the [Dark Brotherhood](Skyrim_Dark_Brotherhood.md). He is also [Astrid](Skyrim_Astrid.md)'s husband.

He wears a special set of [Shrouded Armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shrouded_Armor) with elbow-length sleeves instead of the usual wrist length, and fights with an [Iron Battleaxe of Chills](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Battleaxe_of_Chills).

Arnbjorn lives a very relaxed life at the Sanctuary. He gets up at 8AM then spends the rest of the day usually sharpening weapons or [talking to the other family members](Skyrim_Dark_Brotherhood.md#Radiant_Conversations). Then, at midnight, he'll head to his bed to finally get some sleep.

When you greet him, he'll reveal his marital status first, saying, *"Let me guess - Astrid failed to mention I'm her husband? She has a tendency to do that. Doesn't want the others thinking she plays favorites."* He also uses a lot of food-related nicknames, *"What do you want, beef roast?"*, *"You're part of a family now, hamshank. I trust you... you trust me. Got it?"*, and *"Hello, tidbit."* He'll also explain these by saying how he thinks of you as food *"Don't take offense to the nicknames, morsel. I'm a werewolf. Hard not to think of you as a snack."* If he catches you [sneaking](Skyrim_Sneak.md) by him, he'll say, *"You're putting your hand... in my pocket? Have you gone complete cracked?"* If you ask him to tell something about himself, he will give you a small summary of information. *"Look. If you're trying to get to know me - don't. I don't like you, and I never will. It's nothing personal. But... My wife trusts you, and I trust her judgment. So you can't be all bad. Here's all you need to know: I'm a werewolf. I like killing things. I love Astrid. I hate annoying people. And the color blue gives me a headache."* This response, however, changes drastically if you are a member of [The Companions](Skyrim_The_Companions.md); *"I might ask the same of you. For you are more than mortal. You are moon-born. You are wolf. So you're a Companion, then? I can't imagine you got your gift anywhere else. I hope you have better luck with them than I did. Oh yes, I was once brother to the Companions. Let's just say they found my methods "unsettling." The Dark Brotherhood, obviously, feels differently."* If you ask him what he thinks about [Cicero](Skyrim_Cicero.md) and the [Night Mother](Skyrim_Night_Mother.md), he will boldly state, *"The witless fool and his pet corpse? Take a guess."* When exiting conversation, Arnbjorn will use more nicknames, saying either *"Take care of yourself, beef roast."*, *"Later, ham hock."*, or a more out-of-character *"Yeah, good luck."*

## Related Quests

- **[Destroy the Dark Brotherhood!](Skyrim_Destroy_the_Dark_Brotherhood!.md)**: Finish the [Dark Brotherhood](Skyrim_Dark_Brotherhood.md) and rid Skyrim of their kind.

### The Cure for Madness
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e4/SR-quest-The_Cure_for_Madness.jpg/200px-SR-quest-The_Cure_for_Madness.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-The_Cure_for_Madness.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-The_Cure_for_Madness.jpg) *"I gave as good as I got."* When you return to the Sanctuary, you'll find Veezara wounded on the ground. Neither Cicero or Arnbjorn are anywhere to be found. Speaking to Astrid will fill you in on the details, *"I want you to find that miserable little fool and end his life! But first... find my husband. Make sure he's all right. After the attack, Arnbjorn flew into a rage. When Cicero left... Arnbjorn went after him. They disappeared into the wild."* Once you find evidence pointing to the location that they were headed, Astrid once again urges you to make sure Arnbjorn is in good health, *"Every moment counts, so I want you to take my horse. His name is [Shadowmere](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shadowmere). You'll find him outside, by the pool. Let's just say he's... one of us. Find Arnbjorn. Make sure my husband's all right."*

While Astrid is clearly worried, Nazir seems to have more confidence in Arnbjorn, stating *"Going after Cicero now would be a fool's errand. That little jester is currently being digested in Arnbjorn's stomach, mark my words."*

Once you arrive at the [Dawnstar Sanctuary](Skyrim_Dawnstar_Sanctuary.md), you will immediately spot Arnbjorn sitting on the ground, clearly in pain. When he notices you, he will start dialogue:

*"Should have figured Astrid would send you."* **You're hurt.** *"What gave it away? Ha ha ha ha ha. Yeah, got to admit that little jester is good with that butter knife. But don't worry, I gave as good as I got."* **Where's Cicero now?** *"In there! Through the door. Some old Sanctuary, by the looks of it. I would have followed him, but I don't know the phrase."* **I know the phrase. I'll get Cicero - you go home.** *"All right, you convinced me. Doubt I'd be much good to you, anyway. The little fop cut me pretty deep. But I slashed him good. Pretty sure I severed an artery. Don't know what you're going to find in there... but you can probably just follow the blood."* He will end the conversation with a request:

*"Do me a favor and kill that little jester twice. Just to make sure."* After this he leaves with the words: *"Good hunting. I'll see you back at the Sanctuary."*

If you exited the conversation at any point, he will say, *"Gee, thanks for the help."*

When you return to the Sanctuary after finishing business with Cicero, Arnbjorn will be happy to see you and say, *"There you are. Look, I... I just wanted to say thanks for coming to find me. Back in Dawnstar. Crazy jester finally met his match, am I right?"*

### Death Incarnate
When you finish [To Kill an Empire](Skyrim_To_Kill_an_Empire.md), and return to Sanctuary, you will find Arnbjorn in Werewolf form fending off members of the [Penitus Oculatus](Skyrim_Penitus_Oculatus.md). While doing so, he will meet his demise.

### Destroy the Dark Brotherhood!
During Destroy the Dark Brotherhood!, you will be sent to kill every Dark Brotherhood member in the [Dark Brotherhood Sanctuary](Skyrim_Dark_Brotherhood_Sanctuary.md), Arnbjorn will be one of the required people to kill. During this quest, he gains a leveled battleaxe as well as leveled gold and two leveled healing potions.
