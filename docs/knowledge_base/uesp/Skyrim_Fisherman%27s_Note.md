Fisherman's Note *The personal log of an ill-fated fisherman*
---
The nights are long [here](Skyrim_Giant%27s_Tooth.md). The frost nips at your scales. But I've always been stubborn. Thick-shelled, as they say. So I've stuck to it.

The first days were the hardest. I dared not try and fish, with the [giants](Skyrim_Giant.md) lurking. But hunger makes brave hatchlings of us all, so I dove in, and found the waters calm.

As for the actual job, it has proven a tough egg to crack.

The [ferryman](Skyrim_Ferryman.md) did not lie when he said ironwood bark was as tough as a mammoth tusk. Ours is to wait for the smaller [fruit](Skyrim_Ironwood_Fruit.md) to harvest.
