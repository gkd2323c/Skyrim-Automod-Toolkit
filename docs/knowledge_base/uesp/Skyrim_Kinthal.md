[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/7a/SR-npc-Kinthal.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Kinthal.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Kinthal.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Kinthal.jpg) Kinthal Kinthal is the gang leader of the [Saints bandit](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Saints_Bandit) group.

He wears a [golden saint helmet](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Golden_Saint_Helmet), golden saint gauntlets, fur armor and hide boots. He wields a [golden warhammer](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Golden_Saint_Warhammer) but also carries a [golden dagger](Skyrim_Golden_Dagger.md), he also carries two [scrolls of conjure golden saint warriors](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Conjure_Golden_Saint_Warrior) and 50 [gold](Skyrim_Gold.md). Kinthal can also summon a Golden Saint Warrior while fighting.

He can be found at his camp, just east of [Karthwasten](Skyrim_Karthwasten.md), up on a hill.
