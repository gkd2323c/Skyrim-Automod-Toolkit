# Darumzu

**Darumzu** is one of the two favored [stars](Lore_Stars.md) in the [Atmoran](Lore_Atmora.md) sky. The ship of [Ylgar](Lore_Ylgar.md), of the original [Companions](Lore_Companions.md), was named after it, while the ship of his brother [Yngol](Lore_Yngol.md) was named after [Harakk](Lore_Harakk.md), another of the stars favored by the Atmorans. While the crew of the *Harakk* perished in the Storm of Separation during the crossing of the Atmoran Strait, the Darumzu arrived late and survived the voyage.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup>
