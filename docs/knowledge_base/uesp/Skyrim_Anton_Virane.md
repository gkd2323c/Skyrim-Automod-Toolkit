[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e0/SR-npc-Anton_Virane.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Anton_Virane.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Anton_Virane.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Anton_Virane.jpg) Anton Virane **Anton Virane** is a [Breton](Skyrim_Breton.md) [citizen](Skyrim_Classes.md#Citizen) living in [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md). He was born in [Daggerfall](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Daggerfall) in [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md), which is where he learned to cook. He is the master chef working in [Understone Keep](Skyrim_Understone_Keep.md) with his two assistants [Rondach](Skyrim_Rondach.md) and [Voada](Skyrim_Voada.md), although he considers them both to be worthless. He is often assumed to be a native of [the Reach](Skyrim_The_Reach.md), an accusation which he finds offensive. He is also a close friend of [Balagog gro-Nolob](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Balagog_gro-Nolob).

Anton wears a [chef's tunic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chef%27s_Tunic) along with a [chef's hat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chef%27s_Hat) and a pair of [shoes](Skyrim_Clothing.md#00018801). He wields an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) and carries a selection of [common loot and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common).

## Quest-Related Events

### Recipe for Disaster
During [Recipe for Disaster](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Recipe_for_Disaster), you will be sent to Anton to discover the identity of the Gourmet, then your task will be to kill him when you're done. When you greet Anton, he'll say:

*"Yes, yes, for the hundredth time, I am a Breton. I was born in High Rock. And then I came here. I am not a Reachman!"* *"Who are you? What do you want?"* **The Gourmet. Who is he? Where is he?** *"The... the Gourmet? Never! I don't know what led you here, but nothing will betray my trust. I'll take the secret of the Gourmet's identity to my grave."* **For the Dark Brotherhood, that can be easily arranged.** *"The Dark Brotherhood? Now... now wait a minute. Let's not get hasty. I mean, surely my friend wouldn't want me to endanger my own life. Right? Look, his name is Balagog gro-Nolob. He's an Orc! The Gourmet's an Orc! He's staying at the [Nightgate Inn](Skyrim_Nightgate_Inn.md). That's all I know! Now... now you'll let me go. Right?"* You'll now have a few options:

| Topic | Dialogue | Response |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Of course, Anton. Thank you. (Lie)** | *"Okay. All right. Wonderful. You're welcome! I'll just be on my way, then..."* | Anton walks away |
| **I'm afraid not. Loose ends, and all that.** | *"No. Oh, please no..."* | Anton runs away |
| **(Remain Silent)** | *"So... we're done?"* | Anton walks away |

## Dialogue
When you approach him for the first time, Anton will make it clear to you that he is not a native of the Reach:

*"Before you say anything, no, I am not from the Reach."* **You're not from the Reach?** *"No. I'm a Breton from High Rock, and I refuse to be mistaken for a filthy native. I don't care if we share the same ancestors, these people are savages. Half of them are [Forsworn](Skyrim_Forsworn.md) barbarians, and have you seen what they eat? What have I done for the gods to take me so far from my beloved Daggerfall?"* **So you're from Daggerfall, then?** *"Yes. The finest court in all of High Rock. You think politics here are something? Well, we Bretons invented politics. That is where I learned to cook, and where I belong. Not here, with all these dogs."* His greetings can be:

*"I have meals to cook for the entire castle and almost no help."* *"The Jarl gives me two incompetent assistants. Worthless."*

## Notes
- If you spare Anton Virane during Recipe for Disaster, [Festus Krex](Skyrim_Festus_Krex.md) will be disappointed, and say *"Good, good. And you've got the Writ of Passage too, I see, so that's done. But why? Why, pray tell, have I received word that Anton Virane is still walking around Markarth, hale and healthy as the day he was born? Back to Markarth with you! Virane must be silenced! He's a potential witness, you witless dagger jabber!"*
