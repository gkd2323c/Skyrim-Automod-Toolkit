[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/4d/ON-place-Bogmother.jpg/200px-ON-place-Bogmother.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Bogmother.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Bogmother.jpg) The ruins of Bogmother circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) Bogmother is an ancient [Argonian](Lore_Argonian.md) ruin, located deep in the bogs of [Shadowfen](Lore_Shadowfen.md), southwest of [Stormhold](Lore_Stormhold.md). It was once a temple village, and some of the intricate carvings can still be seen on its towers and the monumental stepped pyramid (or *Xanmeer*).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-EGTBlack Marsh-1)</sup> The area is still sacred to the Argonians, and hosts several venerable relics.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DAR-2)</sup> Open-bottomed spoons made of Bogmother [dreugh](Lore_Dreugh.md) -shell are used in the [Argonian](Lore_Argonian.md) ritual of the Empty Feast of Nullity.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESOSpoon-3)</sup>
