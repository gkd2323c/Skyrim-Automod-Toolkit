[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/94/OB-place-Howling_Cave.jpg/200px-OB-place-Howling_Cave.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-place-Howling_Cave.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-place-Howling_Cave.jpg) Howling Cave in [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433) Howling Cave is a cavern system found in the [West Weald](Lore_West_Weald.md) of Cyrodiil.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Oblivion-1)</sup> It was occupied by the necromancer Atirion in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), he was resurrecting undead from the nearby graveyard to terrorize [Vlastarus](Lore_Vlastarus.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESO-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Jurana-3)</sup> In [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), a band of necromancers took up residence in the caves and were killed by the Hero of Kvatch in service of [Meridia](Lore_Meridia.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Oblivion-1)</sup>

Circa [4E 180](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_180), Varro Matius' cousin Nazarius reported seeing walking skeletons at an Ironwood patch near **the Howling Caves**,<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Varro Matius-4)</sup> prompting [the Warrior](Lore_Hero.md) to rescue townsfolk and investigate it as the lair of [Celemaril](Lore_Celemaril.md) to no avail.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Blades-5)</sup> The exact location of these caves and their relation to Howling Cave is unknown.
