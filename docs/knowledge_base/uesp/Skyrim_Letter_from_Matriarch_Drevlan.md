Letter from Matriarch Drevlan **by [Matriarch Drevlan](Skyrim_Matriarch_Drevlan.md)** *The Matriarch rewards service*
---
*There are three versions of this letter. Their IDs are "xx000E6F", "xx000EA2", and "xx000FEE". The first has the Matriarch congratulate you for converting the innkeeper, the second has her congratulate you for converting shopkeepers, and the third has her mildly disappointed in you for targeting random people.*

---
Word of the Goddess' return has spread throughout Tamriel. It was wise of you to enlist the help of the innkeeper, who deals with many patrons in need of spiritual guidance. As a result of these efforts, new soldiers and priests have pledged their lives in service. Take this gold as reward for your efforts.
