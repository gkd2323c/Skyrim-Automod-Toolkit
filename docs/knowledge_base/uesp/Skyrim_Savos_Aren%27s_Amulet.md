[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/1d/SR-item-Savos_Aren%27s_Amulet.jpg/200px-SR-item-Savos_Aren%27s_Amulet.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Savos_Aren%27s_Amulet.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Savos_Aren%27s_Amulet.jpg) Savos Aren's Amulet Savos Aren's Amulet is an [amulet](Skyrim_Amulet.md) that is received from [Mirabelle Ervine](Skyrim_Mirabelle_Ervine.md) as a reward for the quest [Containment](Skyrim_Containment.md). It once belonged to [its eponymous owner](Skyrim_Savos_Aren.md), though he is never seen wearing it. In appearance, this amulet is identical to a [silver sapphire necklace](Skyrim_Silver_Sapphire_Necklace.md), though it weighs more. The amulet's enchantment increases your [magicka](Skyrim_Magicka.md) by 50 points.

## Related Quests
- **[Containment](Skyrim_Containment.md)**: Deal with the aftermath of the Eye's power surge.
