[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/ae/SR-npc-Hadvar.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Hadvar.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Hadvar.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Hadvar.jpg) Hadvar **Hadvar** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [warrior](Skyrim_CombatWarrior1H.md), a soldier of the [Imperial Legion](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md), and an early companion. As one of the garrisoned troops defending [Helgen](Skyrim_Helgen.md), he tries to assist civilians, including [Torolf](Skyrim_Torolf.md)'s son [Haming](Skyrim_Haming.md), while escaping the [dragon](Skyrim_Dragon.md) raid.

Despite aiding in your execution, he is apologetic that you are to be executed. Shortly after noticing you running for shelter from the attack, he commands you to follow him to [Helgen Keep](Skyrim_Helgen_Keep.md). Once there, you must decide to either have him escort you through the keep, or to have a fellow prisoner, [Ralof](Skyrim_Ralof.md), rejoin you for your escape. If you follow him into the keep, you will engage in battle against [Stormcloaks](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md). After escaping from Helgen, he will take you to [Riverwood](Skyrim_Riverwood.md), where his uncle, [Alvor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Alvor), lives. He will also invite you to join the Imperial Legion in [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md). He will be present at the battle for [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md), and, after a promotion, lead the rescue mission at [Fort Kastav](Skyrim_Fort_Kastav.md) and will participate in many of the Legion missions. Following the conclusion of the civil war, he can be found at the [Sleeping Giant Inn](Skyrim_Sleeping_Giant_Inn.md) in Riverwood, but cannot be engaged in conversation.

Hadvar is clad in a set of Imperial light armor. This includes a [studded Imperial armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Studded_Imperial_Armor), a pair of [Imperial light bracers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Light_Bracers), and a pair of [Imperial light boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Light_Boots). He is equipped with an [Imperial sword](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Sword) and an [iron shield](Skyrim_Iron_Shield.md) for close-range combat, and uses a [long bow](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Long_Bow) supplemented with twelve [iron arrows](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Arrow) when the need arises. He carries three [potions of minor healing](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Healing). After the escape from Helgen, he will change to a set of [Imperial heavy armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Armor), along with [Imperial boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Boots) and [bracers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Bracers).

### [Before the Storm](Skyrim_Before_the_Storm.md)

## Notes
- He is voiced by Jonas Fisch. - Before Hadvar had a unique voice, he used the Male Young Eager voicetype. In the game's voice files, his dialogue still exists in this voicetype, as well as other lines that weren't re-recorded by Jonas Fisch.
- If you followed Ralof out of Helgen, but you joined the Imperials, Hadvar will not be present during the Civil War quests. Instead, he is disabled from the game.
- Although he initially wears light armor, he is actually more proficient in the Heavy Armor skill.
