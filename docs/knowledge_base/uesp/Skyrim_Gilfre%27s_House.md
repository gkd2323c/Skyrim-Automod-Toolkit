[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9b/SR-place-Gilfre%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-place-Gilfre%27s_House.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Gilfre%27s_House.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Gilfre%27s_House.jpg) Gilfre's House next to Mixwater Mill **Gilfre's House** is the home owned by [Gilfre](Skyrim_Gilfre.md) at [Mixwater Mill](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mixwater_Mill).

Located southeast of [Gallows Rock](Skyrim_Gallows_Rock.md) and southwest of [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md), [Gilfre](Skyrim_Gilfre.md) has lived here alone since her five workers left to fight in the civil war.

## Walkthrough

### Exterior
It is on the banks of the [White River](Skyrim_White_River.md), which is used to power the mill via a water wheel. The house, a [worker's house](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Worker%27s_House) and the sawmill itself are all part of [Mixwater Mill](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mixwater_Mill). Everything on the deck in front of the house is owned and taking anything is considered stealing, though there is a [woodcutter's axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Woodcutter%27s_Axe) that you can take for free. There is a rack holding four [salmon](Skyrim_Salmon_(dead).md) and a crate with a [food](Skyrim_Food.md) sack on top and the aforementioned woodcutter's axe leaning against it. There is a chicken coop with three [nests](Skyrim_Chicken%27s_Egg.md) for the four [chickens](Skyrim_Chicken.md) that wander the site between this and the worker's house. Also there are several [blue](Skyrim_Blue_Mountain_Flower.md), [red](Skyrim_Red_Mountain_Flower.md) and [purple mountain flowers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Purple_Mountain_Flower) and at least one [snowberry bush](Skyrim_Snowberries.md) around the house and site. On the bank by the sawmill is a [wood chopping block](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wood_Chopping_Block). The front door is novice-locked most of the day and all night.

### Gilfre's House
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9b/SR-interior-Gilfre%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Gilfre%27s_House.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Gilfre%27s_House.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Gilfre%27s_House.jpg) Gilfre's bedroom It is a single-roomed dwelling containing a double bed coming out from the east wall, with a set of shelves to the left, against the north wall. These hold six common books, two [leeks](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leek), a bulb of [garlic](Skyrim_Garlic.md) and several [potatoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potato), with several pieces of cheese and a plate containing two [pine thrush eggs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pine_Thrush_Egg) on top. Hanging from the ceiling above the shelves are two garlic braids, three bunches of dried [elves ear](Skyrim_Elves_Ear.md) and a bunch of dried [frost mirriam](Skyrim_Frost_Mirriam.md). To the left of the shelves is a flower basket containing three [chicken's eggs](Skyrim_Chicken%27s_Egg.md) and to the right is a food barrel. There is an unlocked [chest](Skyrim_Chest.md) at the foot of the bed and to the right, against the south wall is a round wooden table with two bottles of [Black-Briar mead](Skyrim_Black-Briar_Mead.md) on top, while next to the table is a tall wardrobe containing [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md). To the right of this is a rack hanging from the ceiling holding three [rabbits](Skyrim_Rabbit_(dead).md).

Opposite the entrance is a fireplace, on the south wall, with a [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking_Pot.md) hanging in front and a pair of [shoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shoes) by the hearth. There are eight loose [septims](Skyrim_Gold.md) on the mantelpiece. To the right of the fireplace, also against the south wall is a long wooden table and bench, with two bottles of [alto wine](Skyrim_Alto_Wine.md), a bottle of [wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wine), two bottles of [Nord mead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead), a sprig of snowberries, a [salmon steak](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Steak), a potato, a [cabbage](Skyrim_Cabbage.md), several loaves of [bread](Skyrim_Bread.md) and a [eidar cheese wedge](Skyrim_Eidar_Cheese_Wedge.md) on top of the table, with a [potion of vigor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Vigor) on the floor underneath. In the northeastern corner is a square wooden table and two chairs. On this table is a copy of the [Archery](Skyrim_Archery.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[The Marksmanship Lesson](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Marksmanship_Lesson)*, a random [healing](Skyrim_Healing.md) [potion](Skyrim_Potions.md), a random [stamina](Skyrim_Stamina.md) potion and five loose septims.

## Notes
- You may choose to live here if you [marry](Skyrim_Marriage.md) Gilfe. Like at [Volkihar Keep](Skyrim_Volkihar_Keep.md) and [Fort Dawnguard](Skyrim_Fort_Dawnguard.md) after completing [Bloodline](Skyrim_Bloodline.md) quest, everything on the deck in front of the house except for the woodcutter's axe will still be owned, though, and must be stolen.

## Bugs
