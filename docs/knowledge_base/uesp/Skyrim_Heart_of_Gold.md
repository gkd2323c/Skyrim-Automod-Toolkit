## Quick Walkthrough
1. Read *[Bounty: Goldfish!](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bounty__Goldfish!)*.
2. Obtain a goldfish for [Mila Valentia](Skyrim_Mila_Valentia.md) in Whiterun.
3. Speak to Mila Valentia.
4. Follow Mila Valentia.

## Detailed Walkthrough
This quest can be started after [Clear-Headed](Skyrim_Clear-Headed.md) has been completed by talking to Viriya:

- **Know of anyone in need of fishing work around Skyrim?**
- *"You're headed to Whiterun. It's all in this note."*

She will then hand you a [bounty](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bounty__Goldfish!) from Mila requesting a goldfish. Once you have obtained a goldfish, head to Whiterun and speak to Mila:

- **I heard that you'd like a pet goldfish. What do you think of this one?**
- *"Could I keep him? Please?"*
- **Sure. Here you go.**
- *"You're the best! Thanks!"*

You will then have to follow Mila to her house, where she will place the goldfish in a trough of water. She will then reward you with 1 gold.

## Notes
- Making a full loop around [Mara's Eye Pond](Skyrim_Mara%27s_Eye_Pond.md), southwest of Windhelm, can net you 3-6 Goldfish. If none are present, wait an hour and dive back in.
- Three Goldfish spawn in the pond located outside of [Fellglow Keep](Skyrim_Fellglow_Keep.md) which is only a short distance from Whiterun, making this an easy option for completing the quest.
- After you give Mila the Goldfish, it's possible that she will wander around Whiterun, following her mother all day. Or it could happen that she doesn't snap out of her playing, if you told her about the Goldfish while she was playing catch with the other kids. If you don't want to wait use the console: `setstage cc BGSSSE001_Misc_Whiterun Part One 40` then talk to her to finish the quest. On PS4, try disabling immersive citizen AI mods and reloading.
- If the quest does not move forward once you give the goldfish, you might want to attack Mila with the Bow of Shadows equipped to help advance the quest.
