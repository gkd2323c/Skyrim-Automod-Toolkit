It can be dropped by Arch Necromancers and Necromancer bosses, such as the one in [Boulderfall Cave](Skyrim_Boulderfall_Cave.md).

## Notes
- This item can be disenchanted and is listed as Dark Moon in an [Arcane Enchanter](Skyrim_Arcane_Enchanters.md).
- The enchantment can be placed on any item that can hold a custom [Fortify Magicka](Skyrim_Fortify_Magicka.md) enchant. (Head, Hand, Neck and Finger)
- The Fortify Magicka effect is 2 times as strong as a normal Fortify Magicka when Enchanting. - A normal Fortify Magicka and Dark Moon can be placed on the same item.
- The decrease in health regeneration is always 30%.
- The enchanted version will start dropping after level 40.
- The Fortify Magicka effect on the hood only occurs at night (7pm-5am). Custom enchantments do not have this limitation.
