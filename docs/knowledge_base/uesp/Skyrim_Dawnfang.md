[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/52/SR-item-Dawnfang_and_Duskfang_%28Inactive%29.jpg/200px-SR-item-Dawnfang_and_Duskfang_%28Inactive%29.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Dawnfang_and_Duskfang_(Inactive).jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Dawnfang_and_Duskfang_(Inactive).jpg) An inactive Dawnfang/Duskfang Dawnfang and Duskfang is an [Akaviri](Lore_Akaviri.md) artifact created by the [Tsaesci](Lore_Tsaesci_(race).md). During the day, it is Dawnfang and deals fire damage, at night it transforms to Duskfang and deals frost damage. It is acquired through the [A Soul Divided](Skyrim_A_Soul_Divided.md) quest, which has you claim the weapon from [the Guardian Vault](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Guardian_Vault) in Riften's Ratways. Tempering Dawnfang/Duskfang requires an [Ebony Ingot](Skyrim_Ebony_Ingot.md) and the [Arcane Blacksmith](Skyrim_Arcane_Blacksmith.md) perk: however, although not itself a Daedric item, it is considered to be of Daedric quality and requires that perk for the maximum improvement to its tempering.
