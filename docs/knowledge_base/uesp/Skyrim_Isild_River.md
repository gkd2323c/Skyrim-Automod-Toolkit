[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/66/SR-place-Isild_River.jpg/200px-SR-place-Isild_River.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Isild_River.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Isild_River.jpg) The Isild River **Isild River** is a river in northern [Solstheim](Skyrim_Solstheim.md) on the [Felsaad Coast](Lore_Felsaad_Coast.md).

Before the Red Year of [4E 5](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_5), the Isild River ran northwest from the frozen [Lake Fjalding](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lake_Fjalding) until it merged with the [Harstrad River](Skyrim_Harstrad_River.md) at the foot of the Moesring Mountains. The river then continued to its mouth on the icy Felsaad Coast, west of [Skaal Village](Skyrim_Skaal_Village.md). It was reportedly a beautiful part of the island, at the right time of year.

The cataclysms of the Red Year substantially changed the landscape of Solstheim. Many of the landmarks remained the same; the river is still west of Skaal Village and flows from Lake Fjalding to the coast. However, the Isild and Harstrad rivers no longer meet. Instead, each empties separately into a small bay on the northern coast.

## Notes
- The name *isild* comes from Danish and Norwegian words *is* meaning *ice* and *ild* meaning *fire*, translating it to *Icefire River*.
