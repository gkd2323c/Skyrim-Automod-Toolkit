[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f1/SR-npc-Spellsword_%28NPC%29.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Spellsword_%28NPC%29.jpg)](File_SR-npc-Spellsword_(NPC).jpg.md) [](File_SR-npc-Spellsword_(NPC).jpg.md) Spellsword **Spellswords** are a group of [conjurers](Skyrim_Conjurer_(class).md) found in [Southfringe Sanctum](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Southfringe_Sanctum) led by [Bashnag](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bashnag). They wear a leveled set of [light armor](Skyrim_Light_Armor_(item).md), including the cuirass, boots, and a 50% chance of the gauntlets, all of which can be up to [scaled](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scaled) in quality. They have a small chance of carrying some leveled [potions](Skyrim_Potions.md) and a [gem](Skyrim_Gems.md) along with a small amount of gold. In combat, they rely on a leveled [one-handed weapon](Skyrim_One-Handed_Weapons.md) that can be up to [elven](Skyrim_Elven.md) in quality along with a small chance of carrying a leveled [staff](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Staff). Aside from these weapons, they can also cast a variety of spells in combat, including [Frost Cloak](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Cloak), [Healing](Skyrim_Healing.md), [Ice Storm](Skyrim_Ice_Storm.md), [Lightning Bolt](Skyrim_Lightning_Bolt.md), [Oakflesh](Skyrim_Oakflesh.md), and [Reanimate Corpse](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Reanimate_Corpse).

## Related Quests
- **[The Savior of Selveni Nethri](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Savior_of_Selveni_Nethri)**: Help a necromancer escape from [Southfringe Sanctum](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Southfringe_Sanctum).

## Notes
- Dunmer spellswords seem to be spawned far more than other races; Altmer are the rarest, especially Altmer men.
- Spellswords do not respawn once slain.
- Their equipment is nearly identical as to what [bandits](Skyrim_Bandit.md) wear and use. Their dialogue is also the same as bandits' dialogue, with the sole exception of Altmer; their lines instead mirror what hostile mages use (necromancers, elemental mages, etc.).
