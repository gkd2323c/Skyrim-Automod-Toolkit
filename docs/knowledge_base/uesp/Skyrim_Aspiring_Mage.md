[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/23/SR-npc-Aspiring_Mage.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Aspiring_Mage.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Aspiring_Mage.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Aspiring_Mage.jpg) The Aspiring Mage The **Aspiring Mage** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [warrior](Skyrim_CombatWarrior1H.md) who is [randomly encountered](Skyrim_World_Interactions.md#College_Application_Denied) in the wilderness. He can be seen attempting to [reanimate the corpse](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Reanimate_Corpse) of a woodland creature. He had wanted to follow in his grandfather's footsteps and become a mage, but his application to join the [College of Winterhold](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(faction).md) was denied. It turns out he is only able to cast magic because of his grandfather's [leveled staff of reanimation](Skyrim_Staves.md#Reanimate). You may persuade him to give you the staff, intimidate him, or just pay for it.

The Aspiring Mage wears a set of farm [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#0006c1d9) and a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md#000209a5). Apart from his staff, he also carries a leveled [soul gem](Skyrim_Soul_Gems.md).

## Dialogue
As he tries to reanimate the creature, he will intone:

- *"Rise up and live again!"*
- *"I grant you a new life!"*
- *"Come on, get up!"*
- *"This should work. I've done it before."*
- *"Gah! This staff is useless... I'm useless. The whole world is useless!"*

You can approach him to ask what the problem is:

- **What's the problem?**
- *"Oh... hello there. Didn't notice you come up. I was focusing my magicka. Or at least, I thought I was. I don't really know what I'm doing."*

You can reply in two ways:

- **Yes. It's painfully obvious you don't know what you're doing.**
- *"You don't have to tell me that."*

- **That's quite a staff you have there.**
- *"I used to think so, too."*

Either way, he will continue by explaining:

- *"See, my grandfather was a wizard. My father wouldn't talk about him much. But I found some of his things in the attic. Including this staff. After that I thought I was turning into a wizard too, because dead things near me would come to life again for while. I even went to the [College of Winterhold](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(place).md). But they just laughed at me. The magic is in the staff, not in me they said."*

At this point, you can try to acquire the staff from him.

| Player Choice | Response |
| --- | --- |
| **Give the staff to me before someone gets hurt.** <br> *(Persuade)* | **Passed:** *"Okay, okay. You've talked me into it."* <br> **Failed:** *"Nah. I don't think so."* |
| **Hand over the staff, or else.** <br> *(Intimidate)* | **Passed:** *"No need to get rough!"* <br> **Failed:** *"I don't have to take that from you!"* |
| **Seems like coin would be worth more to you.** <br> *(<Bribe Cost> gold)* | **Passed:** *"Gold solves most problems, doesn't it?"* |

A successful attempt will have him say:

- *"In fact. Keep it. I must have used up the magic in the staff or something. It's just a dumb stick of wood now."*
