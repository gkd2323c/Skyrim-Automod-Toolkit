[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/bc/SR-npc-Butcher.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Butcher.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Butcher.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Butcher.jpg) The Butcher The **Butcher** is the [bandit](Skyrim_Bandit.md) leader of [Cragslane Cavern](Skyrim_Cragslane_Cavern.md).

He wears a full set of heavy armor, excluding the helmet, which can be up to [steel plate](Skyrim_Steel_Plate_Armor.md) ([Nordic](Skyrim_Nordic.md) if the [Dragonborn add-on](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md) is installed) in quality. He also carries a shield, which can be up to [ebony](Skyrim_Ebony.md) in quality, but doesn't use it unless you disarm him. The Butcher wields a two handed weapon which can be up to ebony quality, and carries a leveled amount of [gold](Skyrim_Gold.md).
