## Ingredients
369 guaranteed samples found in 140 different locations. Locations with the greatest numbers are:

- 24 in the [Thalmor Embassy](Skyrim_Thalmor_Embassy.md) ([Haafingar](Skyrim_Haafingar.md))
- 16 in [Boulderfall Cave](Skyrim_Boulderfall_Cave.md) ([The Rift](Skyrim_The_Rift.md))
- 11 in [House Gray-Mane](Skyrim_House_Gray-Mane.md) ([Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md))
- 9 in the [Blue Palace](Skyrim_Blue_Palace.md) ([Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md))

It is categorized as a common ingredient, meaning that all apothecary merchants have a 36% chance of carrying 1-5 samples. In addition, it may be randomly found in some barrels and in [apothecary's satchels](Skyrim_Apothecary%27s_Satchel.md).

## Notes
- Garlic is also used in [cooking](Skyrim_Cooking.md), where it can be combined with other raw food to create [beef stew](Skyrim_Beef_Stew.md), [horker stew](Skyrim_Horker_Stew.md), and [tomato soup](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tomato_Soup).
- With the addition of the [Dragonborn](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md) add-on, garlic can be combined with other raw ingredients to create [horker and ash yam stew](Skyrim_Horker_and_Ash_Yam_Stew.md).
- With the addition of the [Fishing](Skyrim_Fishing.md) Creation, garlic can be combined with other raw ingredients to create [crab stew](Skyrim_Crab_Stew.md).
- With the addition of [Survival Mode](Skyrim_Survival_Mode.md), garlic can be combined with other raw ingredients to create [hot beef stew](Skyrim_Hot_Beef_Stew.md), [hot crab stew](Skyrim_Hot_Crab_Stew.md), [hot horker stew](Skyrim_Hot_Horker_Stew.md), [hot horker and ash yam stew](Skyrim_Hot_Horker_and_Ash_Yam_Stew.md), and [hot tomato soup](Skyrim_Hot_Tomato_Soup.md).
- With the addition of [baking](Skyrim_Baking.md) in the [Hearthfire](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md) add-on, garlic can be combined with other raw ingredients to create [chicken dumpling](Skyrim_Chicken_Dumpling.md) and [garlic bread](Skyrim_Garlic_Bread.md).
- Because it is used in cooking, it is also categorized as [raw food](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Raw_Food), and therefore can be sold to innkeepers as well as apothecary merchants.
- [Vampires](Skyrim_Vampire.md) aren't affected by garlic. In addition, vampire alchemists, such as [Feran Sadri](Skyrim_Feran_Sadri.md), will occasionally have it for sale.
