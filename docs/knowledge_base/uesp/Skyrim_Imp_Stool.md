## Ingredients
20 guaranteed samples can be found in 15 different locations. Locations with multiple samples are:

- 2 in [Angeline's Aromatics](Skyrim_Angeline%27s_Aromatics.md) ([Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md))
- 2 in [Chillwind Depths](Skyrim_Chillwind_Depths.md) ([Hjaalmarch](Skyrim_Hjaalmarch.md))
- 2 in [the Hag's Cure](Skyrim_The_Hag%27s_Cure.md) ([Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md))
- 2 in [Tolvald's Gap](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tolvald%27s_Gap) ([The Rift](Skyrim_The_Rift.md))
- 2 in the [Understone Keep](Skyrim_Understone_Keep.md) ([Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md))

It is categorized as a rare ingredient, meaning that most apothecary merchants have a 21% chance of carrying 1-4 samples. In addition, it may be randomly found only in rare-type [apothecary's satchels](Skyrim_Apothecary%27s_Satchel.md).

## Notes
- The base magnitude of the [Restore Health](Skyrim_Restore_Health.md) effect for this ingredient is 3 instead of 5. This is only relevant if [blisterwort](Skyrim_Blisterwort.md) and imp stool are combined, in which case the resulting potion will have a 40% smaller magnitude than a typical custom Restore Health potion.
