[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/be/ON-place-Hew%27s_Bane_02.jpg/200px-ON-place-Hew%27s_Bane_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Hew%27s_Bane_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Hew%27s_Bane_02.jpg) The Hew's Bane peninsula in Khefrem circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fd/LO-map-Hammerfell_%28Oblivion_Codex%29.jpg/200px-LO-map-Hammerfell_%28Oblivion_Codex%29.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LO-map-Hammerfell_(Oblivion_Codex).jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LO-map-Hammerfell_(Oblivion_Codex).jpg) A map of Hammerfell Khefrem is a coastal region in southwestern [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md), meeting the waters of the [Eltheric Ocean](Lore_Eltheric_Ocean.md) and [Hubalajad's Bay](Lore_Hubalajad%27s_Bay.md). The city of [Gilane](Lore_Gilane.md) and ruins of the [Rourken](Lore_Rourken.md) capital are part of the region. It contains the peninsula of [Hew's Bane](Lore_Hew%27s_Bane.md), which was originally known as Khefrem's Boot.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ON-1)</sup>

The region is also home to the Khefrem Academy of Yokudan Heritage.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-THFo S-2)</sup>

## History
In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), two [Daggerfall Covenant](Lore_Daggerfall_Covenant.md) officers, Lakhazga gro-Rimat and Morothmash gro-Uzug, defected and fled from [Glenumbra](Lore_Glenumbra.md) to the wilds of Khefrem, where they contacted the [Aldmeri Dominion](Lore_Aldmeri_Dominion.md) through a [Baandari](Lore_Baandari.md) peddler. The Covenant learned about this and pursued them across the [Abecean Sea](Lore_Abecean_Sea.md) to [Auridon](Lore_Auridon.md), where the [Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md) prevented their capture.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESO-3)</sup> In the same year, Madam Firilanya, a famous clothier, made landfall in Khefrem during her trip from [Anvil](Lore_Anvil.md) to the [Alik'r Desert](Lore_Alik%27r_Desert.md). She noted that the western waters were refreshing.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ESOFirilanya-4)</sup>
