[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/12/SR-place-Druadach_Mountains.jpg/200px-SR-place-Druadach_Mountains.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Druadach_Mountains.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Druadach_Mountains.jpg) The Druadach Mountains The **Druadach Mountains** are a mountain range in western [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) serving as a natural barrier to separate [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) from [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md) and [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md).

The range curves from the northeast around [Dragon Bridge](Skyrim_Dragon_Bridge.md) to [Falkreath Hold](Skyrim_Falkreath_Hold.md). The misty peaks tower over [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md) and [Karthwasten](Skyrim_Karthwasten.md) in [the Reach](Skyrim_The_Reach.md), though they gradually diminish closer to [Haafingar](Skyrim_Haafingar.md) and [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md). The [Karth River](Skyrim_Karth_River.md) originates in the range and drops down many waterfalls as it flows to the northeast following the range to the [Sea of Ghosts](Skyrim_Sea_of_Ghosts.md). Since the [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md), the [Reachmen](Lore_Reachmen.md), now known as the [Forsworn](Skyrim_Forsworn.md), have been taking advantage of the cliffs, caves, and rugged terrain of the Druadach Mountains in guerrilla warfare campaigns against invading armies. The range is home to many ancient [Dwemer ruins](Skyrim_Dwemer_Ruins.md), [Nord fortresses](Skyrim_Forts.md), and [Orc strongholds](Skyrim_Orc_Strongholds.md).
