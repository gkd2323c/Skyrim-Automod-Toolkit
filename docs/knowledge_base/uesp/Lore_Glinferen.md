[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/50/ON-place-Atatar.jpg/200px-ON-place-Atatar.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Atatar.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Atatar.jpg) Atatar, the seat of Glinferen's power King Glinferen was the last known monarch of [Atatar](Lore_Atatar.md), an [Ayleid](Lore_Ayleid.md) [city-state](Lore_Ayleid_Empire.md) in northern [Blackwood](Lore_Blackwood.md). Glinferen was a [Daedraphile](Lore_Daedraphile.md) and fought against the [Aedra](Lore_Aedra.md) -worshipping [Barsaebics](Lore_Barsaebic.md) in what would become known as the [Narfinsel Schism](Lore_Narfinsel_Schism.md). This conflict culminated in the Scouring of [Wendelbek](Lore_Wendelbek.md) in [1E 198](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_198), when King Glinferen led a combined force of Daedraphile warriors against the traditionalist Barsaebics. His victory drove the Barsaebics out of the [Heartlands](Lore_Heartlands.md) and into inhospitable [Argonia](Lore_Argonia.md), effectively ending organized Ayleid opposition to [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md) worship in [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup>

In [1E 242](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_242)<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup> the [Alessian Slave Rebellion](Lore_Alessian_Slave_Rebellion.md) began when [Alessia](Lore_Alessia.md) made Covenant with [Akatosh](Lore_Akatosh.md) to gain the favor of the Aedra.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TAo K-3)</sup> In desperation, King Glinferen sent an envoy to the Barsaebic city of [Twyllbek](Lore_Twyllbek.md)<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONTwyllbek-4)</sup> in Argonia seeking aid against the Alessians. The Barsaebic king, still stininging from the humiliation of exile, bluntly refused to help.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup> These events ultimately led to the downfall of Ayleid civilization<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TAo K-3)</sup> and the abandonment of Atatar.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup>
