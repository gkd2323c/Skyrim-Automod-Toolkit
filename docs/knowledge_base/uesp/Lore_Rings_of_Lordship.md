# Rings of Lordship

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b1/SI-item-Rings_of_Lordship.jpg/200px-SI-item-Rings_of_Lordship.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SI-item-Rings_of_Lordship.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SI-item-Rings_of_Lordship.jpg) The Rings of Lordship *And here is the Ring of Lordship, a symbol of your new station. Symbols are important. They carry weight in this Realm, and others. You would be well served to remember that.*
— [Sheogorath](Lore_Sheogorath.md)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SISheo-1)</sup> The Rings of Lordship are two little-known enchanted rings, each one held by the respective rulers of [Mania](Lore_Mania.md) and [Dementia](Lore_Dementia_(place).md), the two halves of the [Shivering Isles](Lore_Shivering_Isles.md). The rings act as a symbol of office, something which carries great weight in the Isles.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SISheo-1)</sup> The Manic ring is purple with yellow and pink gemstones shaped into a flower, and possesses a powerful enchantment which fortifies the wearer's personality and [disease](Lore_Disease.md) resistance while also granting a magical shield. The Demented ring is dark and spiked with a purple gem, and grants its wearer a strong chameleon effect while also fortifying endurance and poison resistance.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup> One of the rings may also sometimes be silver and spiked in appearance, with yellow gemstones.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup>

Circa [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433) the [Hero of Kvatch](Lore_Hero_of_Kvatch.md) received one of the rings as a reward from [Sheogorath](Lore_Sheogorath.md) after dispatching either the [Duke of Mania](Lore_Thadon.md) or the [Duchess of Dementia](Lore_Syl.md) and assuming their title.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-quest-4)</sup>
