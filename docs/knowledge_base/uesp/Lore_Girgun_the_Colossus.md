[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9f/SR-place-Thirsk_Mead_Hall.jpg/200px-SR-place-Thirsk_Mead_Hall.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Thirsk_Mead_Hall.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Thirsk_Mead_Hall.jpg) Thirsk Mead Hall Girgun the Colossus was a [Nord](Lore_Nord.md) chieftain of [Thirsk](Lore_Thirsk.md) who lived in the second century of the [Fourth Era](Lore_Fourth_Era.md). He became Chieftain of Thirsk after slaying Thjold the Ill-Minded and presenting his brains as a battle trophy. He ruled Thirsk for six years.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TAH-1)</sup>
