*Every day is Saint Plenty's Day in the Legion.*
—Attributed to Imperials of Fort Frostmoth, ca. 3E 427<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Morrowind-1)</sup> **Saint** Plenty was an [Imperial saint](Lore_Imperial_Saints.md) known to the [Imperial Legion](Lore_Imperial_Legion.md). They are the namesake of [Saint Plenty's Day](Lore_Holidays.md#Saint_Plenty.27s_Day), a holiday celebrated by the Imperial Legion, though little else is known about its traditions or significance. Little is known about Plenty, except that they were already known by [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427). [Imperial](Lore_Imperial.md) members of the Imperial Legion stationed at [Fort Frostmoth](Lore_Fort_Frostmoth.md) on the isle of [Solstheim](Lore_Solstheim.md) during the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md) were known for the saying, "Every day is Saint Plenty's Day in the Legion."<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Morrowind-1)</sup>

It was believed that saints were granted [second sight](Lore_Sight.md) by the [Divines](Lore_Divines.md), allowing them to perceive the world in a way that differs from other mortals.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-FE-2)</sup> Many of the Imperial Saints were typically interred along the [Green Emperor Way](Lore_Green_Emperor_Way.md) among other prominent figures of [the Empire](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:The_Empire).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-GTTIC-3)</sup> However, it is unknown whether Plenty possessed the sight or was ever buried there.
