[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/84/SR-npc-Geimund.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Geimund.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Geimund.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Geimund.jpg) Geimund **Geimund**, a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [citizen](Skyrim_Citizen_(class).md), is the proprietor of [Solitude Stables](Skyrim_Solitude_Stables.md), where he lives with his wife [Fridrika](Skyrim_Fridrika.md) and son [Horm](Skyrim_Horm.md), and sells [horses](Skyrim_Horse.md). He used to be a mercenary, but retired after a nasty encounter with [hagravens](Skyrim_Hagraven.md) at [Ravenscar Hollow](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ravenscar_Hollow).

He spends most of his time outside the stables, either tending to the horses or working the crops, from when he wakes up at 8am until he heads back inside the stable building at 8pm. The evenings are spent with his family until he goes to bed at midnight.

He wears a set of farmer's [clothes](Skyrim_Clothing.md#0006c1d9) with [boots](Skyrim_Clothing.md#000209a5). He carries an [iron mace](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Mace), a spare [belted tunic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Belted_Tunic), and an assortment of [lower-class items and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Poor).

## Dialogue
When greeting you, he might say:

*"You got business at the stables?"* *"Work hard for the future and leave the past be. That's my motto."* *"With a wife like Fridrika and a son like Horm, I'm the luckiest man in Skyrim."* (If both are alive) *"You wouldn't know it, but before my family got killed? I was the luckiest man in Skyrim."* (If both are dead) When exiting conversation, he might also say:

*"If you're heading up to Solitude you should stop to check out the view on your way. It's a good one."* *"Carriages to all the cities in Skyrim. Don't forget us."* You can also ask him about his job:

**How long have you been a stablemaster?** *"Been about eight years now. I used to be a mercenary, but one trip to Ravenscar Hollow cured me of that. There's hagravens in that cave with talons like daggers. They'll open your bowels quick as a wink if you let 'em get close. Creature like that'll make a man think long and hard about what he wants out of life."*
## Notes
- If Geimund dies, his son Horm inherits the stables and sells its horses.
- There is also a Nord man in [Oblivion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Oblivion) with the name [Geimund](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Geimund).
- In the Creation Kit, he is marked as Friends with both [Fihada](Skyrim_Fihada.md) and [Legate Rikke](Skyrim_Legate_Rikke.md), both of whom he never meets and neither relationship is ever explained.

## Bugs
- If you buy a horse from Katla's Farm and the horse is killed, attempting to purchase a new horse from the same stable results in 1000 gold being deducted, and Geimund stating that your horse is "the one with the saddle". However, the saddled horse may not be available for taking, or may not be present at all. Instead, your new horse spawns at the [Riften Stables](Skyrim_Riften_Stables.md). - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.5, fixes this bug.
