*Heals the caster 100 points.* **Close Wounds** is an adept level [Restoration](Skyrim_Restoration.md) spell, which will instantly [restore the health](Skyrim_Restore_Health.md) of the caster by 100 pts.

## Effects
- [Restore Health](Skyrim_Restore_Health.md), 100 pts
- [Restore Stamina](Skyrim_Restore_Stamina.md), 100 pts; if the [Respite](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Respite) perk is unlocked.

## Perks
- [Regeneration](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Regeneration), increases health (and stamina with the Respite perk) restoration to 150.

## Notes
- From a pure health per magicka standpoint, Close Wounds is generally the most efficient healing spell once magicka not regenerating while a spell is being cast is accounted for. It becomes more efficient than [Healing](Skyrim_Healing.md) once you have about 70 magicka. Obviously, for smaller amounts of health, Healing may be more efficient regardless of level.
- Can be a reward for completing [Pilgrimage](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pilgrimage) if Restoration skill is at least 40.
