[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/86/SR-place-Pelagia_Farm.jpg/200px-SR-place-Pelagia_Farm.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Pelagia_Farm.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Pelagia_Farm.jpg) Pelagia Farm **Pelagia Farm** is a large farm south of [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md) owned by [Severio Pelagia](Skyrim_Severio_Pelagia.md).

It is a sprawling farm, with a small single-story building maintained by [Nimriel](Skyrim_Nimriel.md) and [Gloth](Skyrim_Gloth.md). They live at the farm, while Severio lives in his [house](Skyrim_Severio_Pelagia%27s_House.md) in Whiterun.

When approaching Pelagia Farm for the first time, you will come across [Aela the Huntress](Skyrim_Aela_the_Huntress.md), [Farkas](Skyrim_Farkas.md), and [Ria](Skyrim_Ria.md) battling a [giant](Skyrim_Giant.md). The group of [Companions](Skyrim_Companions.md) will always win the fight.

## Walkthrough

### Exterior
There are at least three fields of [vegetables](Skyrim_Food.md#Fruit.2FVegetables) with either stone walls or fences segregating them. During the day, [Severio Pelagia](Skyrim_Severio_Pelagia.md) is in the fields with his workers. He will gladly buy any [cabbages](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage) or [potatoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potato) you hold. There is a [grain mill](Skyrim_Milling.md) to the east of the house and several other outbuildings and storage sheds around the property. To the west of the house is a pen for a [cow](Skyrim_Cow.md) and a few paces in front of the entrance are a couple of [chickens' nests](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chicken%27s_Egg).

### Pelagia Farm
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d4/SR-interior-Pelagia_Farm.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Pelagia_Farm.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Pelagia_Farm.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Pelagia_Farm.jpg) Farmstead interior Upon entering there is a long wooden table and bench on your left against the west wall, with an [apple pie](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Apple_Pie), a few [carrots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Carrot), three [boiled creme treats](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boiled_Creme_Treat), a [green apple](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Green_Apple), a [bowl](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bowl) containing several loaves of [bread](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bread), and an [eidar cheese wedge](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wedge) on the table, with a chair at either end and a wall shelf above holding crockery. On your right as you enter, against the east wall are a stack of [food](Skyrim_Food.md) barrels and sacks next to a bookcase holding fourteen common books, a copy of *[Songs of Skyrim: Revised](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Songs_of_Skyrim__Revised)* and a bowl containing a cabbage. Next to this is a doorway to a bedroom. In the northwest corner of the room is a cupboard holding a cabbage, a [goat cheese wedge](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wedge) and a [leather strip](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Strips), among piles of crockery. There is a chair next to the cupboard with a bottle of [ale](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ale) and a couple of [plates](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Plate) on the floor and two more wall shelves above holding clutter. There is a second doorway on the east wall with a large fire pit between the two of them with a [cooking spit](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Spit) hanging over it.

The first bedroom contains a single bed against the north wall and a dresser beside it with a bottle of [alto wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine), a set of [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md), a [gourd](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Gourd) in a bowl, and a copy of *[Rising Threat, Vol. IV](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Rising_Threat,_Vol._IV)* on top. There is a chair in the southeastern corner. The second bedroom contains a single bed against the south wall, with an end table beside it, holding a bottle of ale, a [silver ingot](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Silver_Ingot), and copies of *[Yngol and the Sea-Ghosts](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Yngol_and_the_Sea-Ghosts)* and *[The Book of Daedra](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Book_of_Daedra)*. A narrow table against the north wall holds a bottle of ale, a [salmon steak](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Steak) on a plate, two common books, and a [draught of health](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Draught_of_Health). Underneath it is a pair of [iron boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Boots).

## Notes
- Though you can find and activate a [grain mill](Skyrim_Milling.md) here, it will only lead to an animation, with no actual function in the game even with *[Hearthfire](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md)* installed.
- Even if you help Severio Pelagia, everything in the farmhouse may still be considered owned, and entering may still count as trespassing.
- [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) Version 2.1.1 of the [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch) marks the barrels outside the building as owned by the farm.
