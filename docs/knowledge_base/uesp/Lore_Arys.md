# Arys

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/0c/ON-place-Arys_Ancestral_Tomb.jpg/200px-ON-place-Arys_Ancestral_Tomb.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Arys_Ancestral_Tomb.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Arys_Ancestral_Tomb.jpg) Arys Ancestral Tomb ca. 2E 582 [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5f/MW-place-Arys_Ancestral_Tomb.jpg/200px-MW-place-Arys_Ancestral_Tomb.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-place-Arys_Ancestral_Tomb.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-place-Arys_Ancestral_Tomb.jpg) Arys Ancestral Tomb ca. 3E 427 Arys family is an ancient [Dunmeri](Lore_Dunmer.md) lineage that lived in the province of [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md). They were affiliated with the [Great House](Lore_Great_Houses.md) [Telvanni](Lore_Telvanni.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-WRTP-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DA-2)</sup>

They had ties to [Tel Branora](Lore_Tel_Branora.md), and some family members were also known to inhabit the settlement.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DA-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TA-3)</sup>

Moreover, **Arys Ancestral Tomb** was located in close proximity to the settlement, to the west of Tel Branora, near [Azura's Coast](Lore_Azura%27s_Coast.md). In the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md), the tomb was situated on the southern part of a small peninsula, but by the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), it had become separated from mainland [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md) and was located on a small islet instead.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-MAAT-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-OAAT-5)</sup>

## History

### Second Era
At some point during the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md), before [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) at least one member of the Arys family had already ascended to the rank of Mistress. A Telvanni Mistress from the Arys family was among the twelve influential members of House Telvanni who signed the official refusal to join the [Ebonheart Pact](Lore_Ebonheart_Pact.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-WRTP-1)</sup> By that time the Arys family had already constructed their family tomb west of Tel Branora.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-OAAT-5)</sup> Dralosa Arys was a Telvanni mage, and Tolendos Arys was a sailor. Both inhabited Tel Branora as of 2E 582.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DA-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TA-3)</sup>

Idren Arys, also active in 2E 582, was a member of the [Urshilaku Tribe](Lore_Urshilaku_Tribe.md) of [Ashlanders](Lore_Ashlanders.md) and could be found in [Ald'ruhn](Lore_Ald%27ruhn.md).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-IA-6)</sup>

An individual named Arys was a member of the [Fighters Guild](Lore_Fighters_Guild.md) who died while attempting to destroy one of the [Dark Anchors](Lore_Dark_Anchor.md) at [Gil-Var-Delle](Lore_Gil-Var-Delle.md).<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-MN-7)</sup>

### Third Era
The status of House Arys in the Third Era is unknown, but their ancestral tomb remained near Tel Branora as of [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427). By this time, it was inhabited by the [undead](Lore_Undead.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-MAAT-4)</sup>
