**Ash Rune** is an expert level alteration spell that creates a [rune](Skyrim_Rune_(effect).md) which will explode when enemies come near, immobilizing them in ash for 30 seconds. They will be unable to attack or be attacked in any way until the spell wears off. The spell must be cast on a valid surface within the range of your casting ability. When you press the cast button, the cross-hair will let you know if you are pointing at such a surface. If it is red, then you can't cast the spell there.

## Effects
- Ash Rune, 50 pts for 1 sec - Immobilizes enemy in hardened ash for 30 seconds

## Perks
- [Rune Master](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Rune_Master) increases range to 100 feet.
- [Stability](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Stability) increases the duration of the paralysis effect to 45 seconds.

## Notes
- You can set off ash runes (your own or others) using any Alteration projectile spell from a distance.
- Some enemies are immune to this spell, notably all [dragons](Skyrim_Dragon.md), [Ash Spawn](Skyrim_Ash_Spawn.md), and anything else that is immune to regular [paralysis](Skyrim_Paralysis.md). Note that though they may not be immobilized in ash, they *will* still set off the rune if they touch it.
- Paralysis Runes were originally developed as part of the [Game Jam](Skyrim_Game_Jam.md) and were later added as a [distinct rune type](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Paralysis_Rune) by the [Arcane Accessories](Skyrim_Arcane_Accessories.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md).
