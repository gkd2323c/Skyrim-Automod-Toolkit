[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/7d/AR-place-Cliff_Keep.jpg/200px-AR-place-Cliff_Keep.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-place-Cliff_Keep.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-place-Cliff_Keep.jpg) Cliff Keep (Arena) Cliff Keep is a location found in the [Sunforge](Lore_Sunforge.md) region of north-central [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md), along the [Dragontail Mountains](Lore_Dragontail_Mountains.md) and east of [Skaven](Lore_Skaven.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RGMap-1)</sup>

The town has been in existence since at least the [Tiber War](Lore_Tiber_War.md), around [2E 862](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_862).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RGMap-1)</sup> During the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md) in the late- [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), the village of Cliff Keep was an active settlement. It was ruled by Lady Lhan-i and had a rivalry with Dragon Gate. Taverns in the village included the Dancing Guard, Devil's Cup, Flying Golem, Gold Giants, Green Castle, and Lucky Golem inns, with religious groups of the [Conclave of Baal](Lore_Conclave_of_Baal.md), Order of the Golden Tomb, and Order of the One Prophet had temples in the town.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-AR-2)</sup> Cliff Keep is neighbored by several settlements, including Skaven to the west, [Belkarth](Lore_Belkarth.md) and Nimbel Moor to the east, and Vulkneu Town to the south.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ARMap-3)</sup>
