# Nord

| --- |

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/6b/SR-race-Nord.jpg/200px-SR-race-Nord.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-race-Nord.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-race-Nord.jpg) A compilation of the preset Nord faces available in Skyrim [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8d/SR-concept-6877541105.jpg/200px-SR-concept-6877541105.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-concept-6877541105.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-concept-6877541105.jpg) Female concept art [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/04/SR-concept-6877541355.jpg/200px-SR-concept-6877541355.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-concept-6877541355.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-concept-6877541355.jpg) Male concept art The **Nords** are the children of the sky, a race of tall and fair-haired humans from [Skyrim](Skyrim_Skyrim.md) who are known for their incredible resistance to cold and magical frost. They are fierce, strong and enthusiastic warriors, and many become renowned warriors, soldiers and mercenaries all over [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md). Eager to augment their martial skills beyond the traditional methods of Skyrim, they excel in all manner of warfare, and are known as a militant people by their neighbors. Nords were known for a time for their nautical prowess when they migrated from [Atmora](Lore_Atmora.md).

For more information, see the [main lore article](Lore_Nord.md).

Nords are the most common race encountered in Skyrim's cities, and can be encountered in nearly every aspect - among others, guards, merchants, bandits and Jarls. [Stormcloaks](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md) encountered consist almost entirely of Nords, with the Nords also making up the majority in the [Imperial Legion](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md) as both officers and soldiers.

## In-Game Description
*Citizens of Skyrim, they are a tall and fair-haired people. Strong and hardy, Nords are famous for their resistance to cold and their talent as warriors. They can use a Battlecry to make opponents flee.*

## Traits
- *Resist Frost* ability (Form ID `000aa020`): Your Nord blood gives you 50% [resistance to Frost](Skyrim_Resist_Frost.md), constant.
- *Battle Cry* greater power (Form ID `000e40c3`): Targets flee for 30 seconds, once per day. - The power consists of the following effects: - *Battle Cry*, 99 pts in 60 ft for 30 secs on Self
- *[Stagger Push](Skyrim_Stagger.md)*, 0.05 pts in 40 ft on Self
- Be warned that this power treats everyone in range as a target, and is considered an attack, so you may want to avoid using it in situations where you have non-follower allies or in the presence of civilians.
- Despite its similarity to the [Illusion](Skyrim_Illusion.md) [Fear](Skyrim_Fear_(effect).md) effect, it does not benefit from any Illusion perks. It also does not affect undead, Daedra, or machines.
- [*Fortify Warmth*](Skyrim_Fortify_Warmth.md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Survival_Mode.md)</sup> (Form ID `xx00088b`): Your Nord blood improves your [resistance to cold environments](Skyrim_Warmth.md) by 25 points.
- Height: 1.03.

## Gameplay
Nord skill bonuses lean toward a more combat-oriented style of play. With both one and two-handed weapons receiving boosts, players are able to choose their favorite weapon(s) with a bit more ease, and boosts to both light armor and blocking provides players both mobility and defense.

The Nord [Battle Cry](#Battle_Cry) power can be vital at earlier levels, causing enemies to flee long enough for the player to potentially recover.

Their innate resistance to Frost provides them a significant advantage against foes such as the draugr, frost dragons, ice wraiths, and mages who employ Frost magic. If a Nord player decides to [become a vampire](Skyrim_Vampirism.md), they will reach the maximum frost resistance possible. (85%)
