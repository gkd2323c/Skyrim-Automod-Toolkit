*Alchemy description:* **Restore <mag> Stamina.**
**Restore Stamina** is an effect that refills the target's [stamina](Skyrim_Stamina.md). It can be used to replenish stamina lost by sprinting or [power attacks](Skyrim_Power_Attack.md), or to repair damage caused by a [Damage Stamina](Skyrim_Damage_Stamina.md) effect. Restore Stamina will not increase stamina past its base value.

All characters naturally [regenerate stamina](Skyrim_Regenerate_Stamina.md) over time. Stamina is also restored when you [sleep](Skyrim_Sleeping.md) or wait.

## Spells
The following spells use the Restore Stamina group of effects, provided the [Respite](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Respite) perk is unlocked in the [Restoration](Skyrim_Restoration.md) skill tree. Following each spell is the specific variant of the effect that it uses:

- [Close Wounds](Skyrim_Close_Wounds.md): *Restore Stamina* (`0004250e`; `Perk Restore Stamina FFSelf`)
- [Fast Healing](Skyrim_Fast_Healing.md): *Restore Stamina* (`0004250e`; `Perk Restore Stamina FFSelf`)
- [Grand Healing](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Grand_Healing): - *Restore Stamina* (`001031d5`; `Grand Healing Restore Stamina`)
- *Restore Stamina Area* (`000b62f2`; `Perk Restore Stamina FFSelf Area`)
- [Heal Other](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Heal_Other): *Restore Stamina* (`0004e93f`; `Perk Restore Stamina FFActor`)
- [Healing](Skyrim_Healing.md): *Restore Stamina* (`0004250d`; `Perk Restore Stamina Conc Self`)
- [Healing Hands](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Healing_Hands): *Restore Stamina* (`0004e93e`; `Perk Restore Stamina Conc Actor`)

## Notes
- Some [food](Skyrim_Food.md), particularly [vegetable soup](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Vegetable_Soup) and [venison stew](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Venison_Stew), will grant a small stamina regeneration effect which will allow you to perform endless [power attacks](Skyrim_Combat.md#Power_Attacks) until the Restore Stamina effect wears off.
- Several [alcoholic beverages](Skyrim_Food.md#Beverages) like meads, wines, and brandies typically restore a small amount of stamina, but at the cost of reduced stamina regeneration for a period of time.
