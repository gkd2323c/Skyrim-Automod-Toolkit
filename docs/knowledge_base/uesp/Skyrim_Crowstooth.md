[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/25/SR-npc-Crowstooth.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Crowstooth.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Crowstooth.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Crowstooth.jpg) Crowstooth **Crowstooth** is a [Forsworn](Skyrim_Forsworn.md) who is encountered during the quest [Bones for a Crow](Skyrim_Bones_for_a_Crow.md).

Crowstooth wears a set of [Forsworn Armor](Skyrim_Forsworn_Armor.md). She has a [Forsworn Bow](Skyrim_Forsworn_Bow.md), a unique [Forsworn Sword](Skyrim_Forsworn_Sword.md) and the unique Forsworn axe [Tyrant's Bane](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tyrant%27s_Bane), and carries a number of [Forsworn Arrows](Skyrim_Forsworn_Arrow.md), in addition to a random selection of loot. She is accompanied by [Alvasorr the Rat](Skyrim_Alvasorr_the_Rat.md), who will back her up in the final fight of the quest.

She and Alvasorr appear at the northern tower in [Arcwind Point](Skyrim_Arcwind_Point.md) after the player has killed her second-in-command [Linelle](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Linelle) and followed the hints from [Linelle's Note](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Linelle%27s_Note). One of the other bounty hunters who went after Crowstooth, [Bjormund Wind-Strider](Skyrim_Bjormund_Wind-Strider.md), lies dead at the top of the tower and can be looted for his unique Dragonplate Armor.

Be prepared for a difficult battle before you even get to Crowstooth; high-level [Draugr](Skyrim_Draugr.md) will be present at Arcwind Point and are difficult to avoid as you take the path north to reach the tower. If [Dragon Rising](Skyrim_Dragon_Rising.md) is complete and [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md) is also installed, a leveled [Dragon](Skyrim_Dragon.md) appears, harassing the Draugr who will be [Shouting](Skyrim_Dragon_Shouts.md) at it when you arrive.
