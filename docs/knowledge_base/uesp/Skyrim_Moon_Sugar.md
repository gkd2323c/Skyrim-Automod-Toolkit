## Ingredients
10 guaranteed samples can be found in 4 different locations, with five more samples in one location added by [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md) and two more in a location added by [Dragonborn](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md):

- 6 in [Riften Warehouse](Skyrim_Riften_Warehouse.md) ([Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md))
- 5 in [Redwater Den](Skyrim_Redwater_Den.md)<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup> ([The Rift](Skyrim_The_Rift.md))
- 2 in [Cragslane Cavern](Skyrim_Cragslane_Cavern.md) ([Eastmarch](Skyrim_Eastmarch.md))
- 2 in [The Retching Netch](Skyrim_The_Retching_Netch.md)<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup> ([Raven Rock](Skyrim_Raven_Rock.md))
- 1 in [Sinderion's Field Laboratory](Skyrim_Sinderion%27s_Field_Laboratory.md) ([Blackreach](Skyrim_Blackreach.md))
- 1 in [Sunderstone Gorge](Skyrim_Sunderstone_Gorge.md) ([Falkreath Hold](Skyrim_Falkreath_Hold.md))

It is categorized as an uncommon ingredient, meaning that all apothecary merchants have a 15% chance of carrying 1-5 samples. In addition, it may be randomly found in uncommon- and rare-type [apothecary's satchels](Skyrim_Apothecary%27s_Satchel.md). Other possible locations for already-harvested samples include:

- 1-6 guaranteed samples are sold by the [Khajiit traders](Skyrim_Khajiit_Traders.md).
- 3 guaranteed samples are sold by a [skooma dealer](Skyrim_Skooma_Dealer_(NPC).md) who may be [randomly encountered](Skyrim_World_Interactions.md#Skooma_Dealer) on the roads.

## Notes
- Moon sugar is one of 3 items needed to [cook](Skyrim_Cooking.md) an [Elsweyr fondue](Skyrim_Elsweyr_Fondue.md).
- Moon sugar is also an ingredient used to [bake](Skyrim_Baking.md) a [Lavender Dumpling](Skyrim_Lavender_Dumpling.md)<sup>[HF](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md)</sup>.
