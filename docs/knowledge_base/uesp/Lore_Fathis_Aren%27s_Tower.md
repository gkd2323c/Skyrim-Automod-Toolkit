[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/1b/OBR-place-Fathis_Aren%27s_Tower.jpg/200px-OBR-place-Fathis_Aren%27s_Tower.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OBR-place-Fathis_Aren%27s_Tower.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OBR-place-Fathis_Aren%27s_Tower.jpg) Fathis Aren's Tower circa [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433) [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d9/LG-cardart-Giant_Slaughterfish.png/200px-LG-cardart-Giant_Slaughterfish.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LG-cardart-Giant_Slaughterfish.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LG-cardart-Giant_Slaughterfish.png) A Giant Slaughterfish Fathis Aren's Tower, also known as the **Temple of the Emperor Zero**,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Prima-1)</sup> is a ruined fort along the [Green Road](Lore_Green_Road.md) south of [Bravil](Lore_Bravil.md), in the [Nibenay Valley](Lore_Nibenay_Valley.md) region of [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-OB-2)</sup>

The tower possesses an extensive underground complex, connected to a grotto that extends deep under [Niben Bay](Lore_Niben_Bay.md). Some parts of the grotto are flooded and provide access to the bottom of the bay. These flooded passages are home to [slaughterfish](Lore_Slaughterfish.md), including the rare [giant slaughterfish](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Giant_Slaughterfish). The grotto extends all the way to Bravil, allowing access to the tower from a secret passage in Castle Bravil.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-OB-2)</sup>

## History
Based on its name,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Prima-1)</sup> the tower seems to have originally been dedicated to the [Cult of Emperor Zero](Lore_Cult_of_Emperor_Zero.md), a popular cult in [Nibenay](Lore_Nibenay.md) dedicated to the worship of [Cuhlecain](Lore_Cuhlecain.md) and founded at the end of the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md) by [Tiber Septim](Lore_Tiber_Septim.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-PGE1-3)</sup>

By the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), the ruins of the temple had been converted into the private wizard's tower of Fathis Aren, the Court Wizard of Bravil and an Associate of the [Mages Guild](Lore_Mages_Guild.md). A proficient [conjurer](Lore_Conjuration.md), Aren converted the underground complex into his lair, guarded by [Dremora](Lore_Dremora.md) and other bound [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md). The tunnels were also inhabited by his fellow conjurers who were not associated with the Mages Guild.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-OB-2)</sup>

In [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), the [Gray Fox](Lore_The_Gray_Fox.md), [Corvus Umbranox](Lore_Corvus_Umbranox.md), used a crystal ball known as [Savilla's Stone](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Savilla%27s_Stone) to divine the items needed to break into the [Imperial Palace](Lore_Imperial_Palace.md) and steal an [Elder Scroll](Lore_Elder_Scrolls.md). He learned that Fathis Aren had come into possession of the Arrow of Extrication, a key-shaped arrowhead. This arrow was needed to unlock a passage in the Hall of Epochs, an ancient [Ayleid](Lore_Ayleid.md) ruin that allowed access to the Palace via a secret route known as the Old Way. The Gray Fox tasked the [Hero of Kvatch](Lore_Hero_of_Kvatch.md) with recovering the arrow from Fathis Aren's Tower, which required breaking into Castle Bravil and traversing the underground passages. The arrowhead was successfully stolen; it is unknown if Aren survived the robbery.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup>
