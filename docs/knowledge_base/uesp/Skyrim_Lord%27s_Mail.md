It was released on November 30, 2017 for 300 Creation Credits. Before its addition, the Lord's Mail last [appeared](Morrowind_Lord%27s_Mail_(artifact).md) in *[Morrowind](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Morrowind)*.

## Official Summary
*The legendary Imperial relic known as The Lord's Mail is rumored to be in the possession of Redguard mercenaries. The Imperial Legion has ordered you to retrieve it by any means. (Quest "Gift of Kynareth" starts by reading "Letter to General Tullius" at Castle Dour in Solitude.)*
## Overview
The quest **[Gift of Kynareth](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Gift_of_Kynareth)** will start when you read a [letter to General Tullius](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Letter_to_General_Tullius) in [Castle Dour](Skyrim_Castle_Dour.md) in [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md). It'll direct you to a camp of [Alik'r mercenaries](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Alik%27r_Mercenary) in [Whiterun Hold](Skyrim_Whiterun_Hold.md). However, the Lord's Mail is cursed and you will need to return to Solitude to seek the blessing of [Kynareth](Lore_Kynareth.md) at the [Temple of the Divines](Skyrim_Temple_of_the_Divines.md). The armor itself is heavy [ebony](Skyrim_Ebony.md) with a constant health drain effect for enemies who get into range.

## Notes
- This Creation received an update in February 2019 which modified the Lord's Mail armor texture and made the new Redguard outfits available at [Radiant Raiment](Skyrim_Radiant_Raiment.md).
- The Redguard set worn by Majid The Mad is unique and must be looted before leaving the area or he'll be despawned upon return. This variant is visually identical to [Nazir](Skyrim_Nazir.md)'s set, differing only in ID, making Majid the only non-glitch option to obtain the set without [destroying the](Skyrim_Destroy_the_Dark_Brotherhood!.md) [Dark Brotherhood](Skyrim_Dark_Brotherhood.md).
