[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/ca/SR-npc-Haelga.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Haelga.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Haelga.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Haelga.jpg) Haelga browsing the stalls at the Riften market **Haelga** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [citizen](Skyrim_Citizen_(class).md) and the promiscuous owner of [Haelga's Bunkhouse](Skyrim_Haelga%27s_Bunkhouse.md) in [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md). She saved her niece [Svana Far-Shield](Skyrim_Svana_Far-Shield.md) from the [orphanage](Skyrim_Honorhall_Orphanage.md) when her parents died, and now has her working as a maid in the Bunkhouse. Relations between the two are rather strained, as Svana believes she is being worked like a slave and that Haelga takes her worship of [Dibella](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Dibella) too seriously. Haelga's room shows her dedication to her Dibellan Arts: she has a shrine to Dibella, [stamina potions](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Stamina), [honey](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Honey), [leather strips](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Strips), a [horker tusk](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Horker_Tusk) hidden under the bed, [Falmer blood elixir](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Falmer_Blood_Elixir) ("make love like a sabre cat") and even a set of shackles on her bed. She is known to give a [mark of Dibella](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mark_of_Dibella) to every one she practices her Dibellan Arts with. The marks are made specially by [Madesi](Skyrim_Madesi.md), who does not know what they are used for. She's worried that Riften's people would kick her out of the city if her worship of Dibella became public knowledge.

Haelga wakes up at 6am to have breakfast, before starting work at 7am in her bunkhouse, where she tends the front desk. At 2pm she goes out shopping in the Riften market before returning to work at 6pm. She finishes work at 7pm and enjoys dinner at the bunkhouse, and then goes out at 8pm to meet [Bolli](Skyrim_Bolli.md) for drinks at [the Bee and Barb](Skyrim_The_Bee_and_Barb.md). She returns home at 12am to sleep.

She wears some [shopkeeper clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#0006ff45), a pair of [fine boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fine_Boots), and an [amulet of Dibella](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Amulet_of_Dibella). She also carries the [key to the bunkhouse](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Haelga%27s_Bunkhouse_Key), a [belted tunic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Belted_Tunic), and a [selection of upper-class items and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Rich). She is armed with a [dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dagger) that can be up to [elven](Skyrim_Elven.md) in quality at level 20.

## Related Quests

## Quest-Related Dialogue

### Caught Red Handed
During the quest [Caught Red Handed](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Caught_Red_Handed), Svana will ask you to collect three Marks of Dibella from Haelga's lovers: [Bolli](Skyrim_Bolli.md), [Indaryn](Skyrim_Indaryn.md), and [Hofgrir Horse-Crusher](Skyrim_Hofgrir_Horse-Crusher.md).

Once you've gathered the Marks, confront Haelga by saying:

**I believe these are yours?** *"What? How... where did you get these? No, don't tell me. I don't want to know. Look, we need to keep this quiet... between you and me, okay. No one else needs to know about it. If word got out that I'm practicing my Dibellan Arts in Riften, they'll run me out of town. Here, take this and don't mention a word of this to anyone, especially Svana!"* She'll give you a [magic scroll](Skyrim_Scrolls.md).

### Taking Care of Business
**I have a message from Brynjolf.** *"What does he want now? I already explained to him that you can't get blood from a stone."* **This isn't about the money anymore.** *"Look, I can't make the coin appear out of thin air. Please, be reasonable. I'll... I'll pay next month."* **We've run out of patience.** *"And so have I. What's the point of paying anyway? Your outfit can't even fend for itself. I could do better tossing the gold into the sewer. You can't scare me with your tough talk. I'm not paying you people a single coin."* **Then you'll pay in more than just coin. (Brawl)** *"Ha! We'll see about that!"* If you win the brawl:

*"Here... take your damn coin and get out of here!"* If you take her statue of Dibella:

*"Please! Don't take the statue! It's the only thing of value I have left!"* **I have a message from Brynjolf.** *"You... you have my statue! What are you going to do with it?"* **So, should I drop this statue down a well?** *"Not Lady Dibella! No, please! I can't lose her! I get the message. Here, take your gold. I hope you choke on it!"*
### Spread the Love
When you are [handing out](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Spread_the_Love) [religious pamphlets](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Warmth_of_Mara) to the citizens of Riften, you may give Haelga one. She doesn't seem interested, though:

*"Huh? Oh. Sure, whatever. I prefer Dibella's teachings myself."*
