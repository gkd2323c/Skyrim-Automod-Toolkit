## Wildburn Seeds
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/ef/ON-item-Wildburn_Seed.jpg/200px-ON-item-Wildburn_Seed.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-item-Wildburn_Seed.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-item-Wildburn_Seed.jpg) A Wildburn Seed [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-icon-quest-Wildburn_Seed.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-icon-quest-Wildburn_Seed.png) A Wildburn Seed **Wildburn Seeds** are greenspring seeds infused with corrupting daedric magic.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Beragon-4)</sup><sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Darolith-1)</sup> They were used by the [Recollection](Lore_Recollection.md) in the region of [Strid Vale](Lore_Strid_Vale.md) to grow the Dawnwood at an incredible pace, but also caused corrupting "Wildburn" effects on its outskirts that withers all nature. Their ultimate goal was to cover the entire West Weald in forest.

New Wildburn Seeds naturally sprout around the epicenter of the original Wildburn ritual that infused a Greenspring Seed with Daedric magic, thus causing the Wildburn to spread further.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Orders-5)</sup> The Wildburn corruption twists flora and drives fauna wild and feral.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Amaurel-6)</sup> [Tharrikers](Lore_Tharriker.md) appeared in West Weald shortly after the creation of Dawnwood and Wildburn regions, possibly because of it.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Salerius Tharriker-7)</sup>

The Recollection created the Wildburn Seeds through a ritual using the [Staff of Many Paths](Lore_Staff_of_Many_Paths.md), using them to cover much of the [West Weald](Lore_West_Weald.md) in thick jungle overnight. When [King Nantharion](Lore_Nantharion.md)'s involvement with the Recollection was discovered by the [Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md), he attempted to use the Staff to create a new Wildburn Seed, but the interruption of the ritual caused the Seed's energies to be released prematurely, destroying the Recollection's living fortress of Hoperoot. At [Miscarcand](Lore_Miscarcand.md) Nantharion attempted to use the Staff to drain the energies of the great [Ayleid Well](Lore_Ayleid_Wells.md) in order to restore the [Daedric Prince](Lore_Daedric_Prince.md) [Ithelia](Lore_Ithelia.md) to her ancient power and create a Wildburn that'd cover all of the West Weald, however he was slain by the Vestige before he could unleash the Wildburn.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Beragon-4)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-Seeds Suspicion-8)</sup><sup>[[9]](#cite_note-Nantharion-9)</sup><sup>[[10]](#cite_note-Gambit-10)</sup>
