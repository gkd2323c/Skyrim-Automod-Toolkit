# Arcane Knot

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b1/ON-item-Arcane_Knot.jpg/200px-ON-item-Arcane_Knot.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-item-Arcane_Knot.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-item-Arcane_Knot.jpg) The Arcane Knot The Arcane Knot is a dangerous and deeply unstable mystical object of incredible power. It is said that if reality can be thought of as a great glass mirror then, when that mirror breaks, the result is "many mirrors" and therefore [many realities](Lore_Many_Paths.md). The Arcane Knot is said to possess the power to treat the mirror as if it never broke, to connect the different realities and even allow one to cross over from one to the next.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Keshargo-1)</sup>

The Knot's influence can have various effects such as granting the one holding it incredible power, causing the size of their physical form to increase, or spawning [Glass Atronachs](Lore_Glass_Atronach.md), but it is deeply volatile and will eventually consume the holder and everyone around them, infusing them with Temporal Splinters that build up into a destructive Arcane Fracture, and draining and eating away at them until they disappear into the Knot itself.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Keshargo-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Neletai Notes-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Seeker Expedition-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Arcane Knot-4)</sup> It is said that even momentary exposure to the Knot can cause one's very [animus](Lore_Souls.md) to shatter and thus surviving in its presence for any period of time is impressive.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Scribe Nairoline-5)</sup>

For a long time the Arcane Knot was held deep within the Lucent Citadel in [Fargrave](Lore_Fargrave.md). Its origin and intended function were completely unknown,<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Scribe Nairoline-5)</sup> and even among Fargrave's [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md) there were only a select few that could remember the citadel.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Journal Melus-6)</sup> In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) an expedition of [Mirrormoor](Lore_Mirrormoor.md) Daedra attempted to retrieve the Arcane Knot, as their leader believed it belonged to them. A group of adventurers defeated the Daedra and retrieved the Arcane Knot, surviving its influence long enough to contain its chaotic power within the Null Arca, a Daedric device created to suppress dangerous magical objects. With the potential calamity the Mirrormoor Daedra could've caused with the Knot prevented, the contained object was taken away for study by Scribes of [Hermaeus Mora](Lore_Hermaeus_Mora.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Keshargo-1)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Neletai-7)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Scribe Nairoline-5)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-Knot Awaits-8)</sup>

## Notes
- The Arcane Knot and the Lucent Citadel are associated with the [Daedric Prince](Lore_Daedric_Prince.md) [Ithelia](Lore_Ithelia.md). Hermaeus Mora wiped her existence from memory, which is why the Lucent Citadel and the Arcane Knot became a mystery. When she was freed, the defenders of the Lucent Citadel were reawakened.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-9)</sup> - Furthermore, the Lucent Guardians and associated aesthetic are associated with Ithelia.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-10YACL-10)</sup>
