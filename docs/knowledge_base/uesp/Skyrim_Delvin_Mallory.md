[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/73/SR-npc-Delvin_Mallory.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Delvin_Mallory.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Delvin_Mallory.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Delvin_Mallory.jpg) Delvin Mallory **Delvin Mallory** is a [Breton](Skyrim_Breton.md) [thief](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Trainer Sneak Master) and an important member of the [Thieves Guild](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(faction).md) in [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md). He is always found in [the Ragged Flagon](Skyrim_The_Ragged_Flagon.md), located inside [the Ratway](Skyrim_The_Ratway.md). Like other patrons of the bar, he does not sleep. He is also an old friend of [Astrid](Skyrim_Astrid.md), the leader of the [Dark Brotherhood](Skyrim_Dark_Brotherhood.md).

Delvin's outfit consists of a set of [Guild Master's armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Guild_Master%27s_Armor), which includes matching pairs of [gloves](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Guild_Master%27s_Gloves) and [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Guild_Master%27s_Boots). He also carries a leveled [dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dagger) (up to [elven](Skyrim_Elven.md) quality), a leveled [poison](Skyrim_Poison.md), and a small amount of [gold](Skyrim_Gold.md).

Delvin is the Master [trainer](Skyrim_Trainers.md) for the [Sneak](Skyrim_Sneak.md) skill. After joining the Thieves Guild, he will tell you that he handles all the [fishin', numbers, and bedlam jobs](#Related_Quests.23Radiant_Quests) - *"the ones with the more personal touch"*.

If you also bring to him certain [collectible items](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Litany_of_Larceny) that you may encounter throughout the Thieves Guild questline, Delvin will also give a monetary reward. These items will then appear on the shelves located behind the guildmaster's desk in the [Cistern](Skyrim_The_Ragged_Flagon_-_Cistern.md).

*"Well, well. I was looking for this little beauty. If you happen across any other unusual trinkets like this, be sure and bring them to me. Returning to Delvin with the item, he will say:

*"I think you'll find this payment to be more than fair."* With other quests, you may come across an [East Empire Shipping Map](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:East_Empire_Shipping_Map) found within the [East Empire Company Warehouse](Skyrim_East_Empire_Company_Warehouse.md) in [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md). Delvin will reward you for bringing the item to him and say:

*"This should compensate you for your find."* Another item that can be found is the [Model Ship](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Model_Ship) located in the [Snow Veil Sanctum](Skyrim_Snow_Veil_Sanctum.md). Delvin will then exclaim:

*"What a delightful find! This should cover it."* Also during the quest [Hard Answers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hard_Answers), the [Dwemer Puzzle Cube](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dwemer_Puzzle_Cube) can be found just before the door in [Calcelmo's Laboratory](Skyrim_Understone_Keep.md#Calcelmo.27s_Laboratory) leading onto the [Wizard's Balcony](Skyrim_Understone_Keep.md#Markarth_Wizard.27s_Balcony). Here you go, this should make you smile."* When you search Mercer's house [Riftweald Manor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Riftweald_Manor) for clues about Mercer's whereabouts, you will find the [Bust of the Gray Fox](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bust_of_the_Gray_Fox) alongside [Mercer's plans](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mercer%27s_Plans). *"By the Eight... you actually got your hands on it! This alone is worth more than some thieves earn in a lifetime."* However, you may not be able to turn in this item due to a [bug](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Litany_of_Larceny#Bugs).

**Brynjolf:** *"No tricks, Karliah or I'll cut you down where you stand. Now what's this so-called proof you speak of?"*
**Karliah:** *"I have [Gallus's journal](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Gallus%27s_Encoded_Journal). I think you'll find its contents disturbing."*
**Brynjolf:** *"Let me see. No, it... can't be. This can't be true. I've known Mercer too long..."*

**Brynjolf:** *"It says Mercer's been stealing from our vault for years. Gallus was looking into it before he was murdered."*

**Brynjolf:** *"Delvin, Vex... watch the Flagon. If you see Mercer, come tell me right away."*

*"Stabbed in the back. It's like the Dark Brotherhood all over again."* *"Can't talk long, got to keep my eyes open for Mercer."* *"Hold your thoughts 'til after we untangle this mess."*

## Notes
- Delvin Mallory is voiced by [Enn Reitel](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/Enn_Reitel).
- The dialogue Delvin and Astrid use concerning each other suggest that they may have been more than friends at some point. The *[Prima Official Game Guide](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Books:The_Elder_Scrolls_V__Skyrim__Prima_Official_Game_Guide)* confirms this. - The guide also states that Delvin grew up in [Honorhall Orphanage](Skyrim_Honorhall_Orphanage.md).
- He is the author of the book *[Shadowmarks](Skyrim_Shadowmarks_(book).md)*.
- There is a [warning note](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:A_Warning) addressed to Delvin from [Vex](Skyrim_Vex.md), found in [the Ragged Flagon](Skyrim_The_Ragged_Flagon.md). Another [note](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Timely_Offer) addressed to him is found beside it.
- Delvin is evidently somewhat infatuated with Vex, as evidenced by the aforementioned note, and also by [another one](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Give_Me_A_Chance) he wrote to her. She clearly does not return his affections.
- Delvin's written dialogue deviates from his audio dialogue significantly. When his line of dialogue reads: *"Talk to me again if you prove your worth to Brynjolf."*, the audio actually says: *"Talk to me again when you've proven yourself to Brynjolf."* - Another line of dialogue reads: *"Do what he says, Vex. This isn't helpin' right now."* The audio actually says: *"This ain't helpin' right now."* There are many other inconsistencies between written and audio dialogue when speaking to him.
- In the [Dragonborn](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md) expansion, it is revealed that Delvin is the brother of Raven Rock blacksmith [Glover Mallory](Skyrim_Glover_Mallory.md), whose daughter is [Sapphire](Skyrim_Sapphire_(person).md). This would also make him Sapphire's uncle. - It is possible that one of Delvin's Fishing Jobs will direct you to steal from Glover, despite the fact that he is also in the Thieves Guild, which is theoretically forbidden.
- Delvin Mallory also appeared in *[Legends](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Legends:Legends)* as the card [Smuggler Underboss](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Legends:Smuggler Underboss) and as a [quest giver](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Legends:Characters#Brynjolf). - For more information on recurring characters, please [see the main article](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/General:Recurring_Characters).

## Bugs
- [Official Skyrim Patch](Skyrim_Patch.md) 1.7 broke the functionality of the "Say Once" dialogue flag. This allows Delvin's special jobs to be turned in more than once because his dialogue is set up to flag each one this way. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.3.1, addresses this issue. Additional conditions have been added to the 4 dialogue options to prevent them from showing up a second time.
