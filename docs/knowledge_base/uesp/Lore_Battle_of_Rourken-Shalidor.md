# Battle of Rourken-Shalidor

The Battle of Rourken-Shalidor was a climactic battle in a war that took place sometime before the [Disappearance of the Dwarves](Lore_Disappearance_of_the_Dwarves.md),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TL-1)</sup> in the [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md),<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SS-2)</sup> in which [Archmage Shalidor](Lore_Shalidor.md) single-handedly fought against the Dwemer legions of clan [Rourken](Lore_Rourken.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-AMM-3)</sup> During the war, [King Rourken](Lore_Rourken_(person).md) had the [Spellbreaker](Lore_Spellbreaker.md) artifact forged to be used against Shalidor.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ARTIL-4)</sup><sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TL-1)</sup> The high honor guard of Rourken also may have used gauntlets imbued with [tonal magics](Lore_Tonal_Architecture.md) derived from Spellbreaker during this conflict.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ONRSG-5)</sup>

Following the final battle, Spellbreaker was lost for some time.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ARTIL-4)</sup><sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TL-1)</sup> Shalidor vaguely mentioned bearing his [spellmirror](Lore_Spellmirror.md) into a battle.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Shalidor-6)</sup> A staff enchanted by Shalidor himself after his legendary First Era battle against the Rourken clan of Dwemer was named the [Spellbreaker's Staff](Lore_Spellbreaker%27s_Staff.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SS-2)</sup>
