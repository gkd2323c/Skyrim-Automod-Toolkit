## Waning Lily
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/6c/ON-flora-Waning_Lily.jpg/200px-ON-flora-Waning_Lily.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-flora-Waning_Lily.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-flora-Waning_Lily.jpg) Waning Lily A delicate aquatic flower that is often planted by [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md) in remembrance of beloved family members who have passed to the Sands Behind the Stars. They only blossom during Jone and Jode's waning.

## [Wickwheat](Lore_Wickwheat.md)
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/23/MW-flora-Wickwheat.jpg/200px-MW-flora-Wickwheat.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-flora-Wickwheat.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-flora-Wickwheat.jpg) Wickwheat [Wickwheat](Lore_Wickwheat.md) is a common wild [ashland grain](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Grain) found in the province of [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md). It typically grows in the province's plains like the [Grazelands](Lore_Grazelands.md) of [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md), and its grains contain some modest magical properties. For the native [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md) of Morrowind, it is a cheap and easy to come by ingredient for [cooking](Lore_Dunmer_Cuisine.md) and [alchemy](Lore_Alchemy.md).

**Found in:** - [Morrowind](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Wickwheat)

**Harvested Ingredient:** [Wickwheat](Lore_Wickwheat_(alchemy).md)

## Wrathberry Bush
[](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-icon-food-White_Grapes.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-icon-food-White_Grapes.png) Wraithberries Wrathberry bushes grow in the snowy regions of [Wrothgar](Lore_Wrothgar.md). The berries that grow from these bushes have a filmy white skin and red center. Wrathberries are said to be similar in taste to snowberries, but sharper. Wrathberries are sometimes used in Orcish cuisine. Eaten raw they stain the teeth the color of blood, which Orcs use an intimidation tactic before battle.

## Wyrd Tree
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e2/ON-place-Beldama_Wyrd_Tree.jpg/200px-ON-place-Beldama_Wyrd_Tree.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Beldama_Wyrd_Tree.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Beldama_Wyrd_Tree.jpg) The Wyrd Tree The Wyrd Tree is a giant tree that is the source of all life in Glenumbra. The tree lies within the center of a secluded glade deep in the forests of [Daenia](Lore_Daenia.md) in [Glenumbra](Lore_Glenumbra.md). The [Beldama Wyrd](Lore_Beldama_Wyrd.md) make their home here. The tree supposedly has a connection to the bones of the earth.

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Beldama_Wyrd_Tree)
