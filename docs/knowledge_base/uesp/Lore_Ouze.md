[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/74/ON-place-Ouze.jpg/200px-ON-place-Ouze.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Ouze.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Ouze.jpg) Ouze circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/01/ON-place-Ouze_02.jpg/200px-ON-place-Ouze_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Ouze_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Ouze_02.jpg) Altar in Ouze with a triskelion symbol **Ouze** (otherwise known as **Ooze**<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup>) is a sprawled-out ruin adorned with engravings of [triskelions](Lore_Triskelion.md), situated along the eastern banks of the [Xylo River](Lore_Xylo_River.md), near the city-state of [Silvenar](Lore_Silvenar.md) in [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md). Ouze houses the spirits of [Oathbreakers](Lore_Changeling.md), those who have broken the sacred laws of the [Green Pact](Lore_Green_Pact.md), as well as those who would not give up their shapeshifting abilities. In Ouze's tar-pits, the Oathbreakers are stripped of their life and left in eternal slumber. The strongest of the Oathbreakers was Guardian [Celonron](Lore_Celonron.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONSpinner Indinael-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-GC-3)</sup> [Voice of Ouze](Lore_Voice_of_Ouze.md) was another prominent Oathbreaker who acted as mediator between [Yffre](Lore_Y%27ffre.md)'s faithful and their kin.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Vo O-4)</sup> In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the [Order of the Black Worm](Lore_Order_of_the_Black_Worm.md) defiled the grounds of Ouze, attempting to resurrect the dead that were buried there. Their plot was foiled by Ouze's guardians.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-7)</sup>
