The Fall of Queen Nurnhilde **by [Helgreir Lute-Voice](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Helgreir_Lute-Voice), Bard of [Windhelm](Lore_Windhelm.md)** *An ode to a fallen queen*
---
Sing the glory of [Sovngarde](Lore_Sovngarde.md)! Sing the story of [Nurnhilde](Lore_Nurnhilde.md)!

- - - - Oh queen dear, beloved of [Nord](Lore_Nord.md) and gods,
- You stood against the [Akaviri](Lore_Akavir.md), turning back the vile invaders,
- We wept like weans when your fair head fell,
- But we know you watch over us from Sovngarde above!
- Sing the glory of Sovngarde! Sing the story of Nurnhilde!
- Weep for our beloved queen!
- - - - — Helgreir Lute-Voice, Bard of Windhelm
