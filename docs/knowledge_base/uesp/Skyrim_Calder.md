[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/26/SR-npc-Calder.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Calder.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Calder.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Calder.jpg) Calder **Calder** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [warrior](Skyrim_CombatWarrior1H.md) from [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md).

Once you have purchased the home in Windhelm, [Hjerim](Skyrim_Hjerim.md), and attained the rank of [thane](Skyrim_Thane.md) of Windhelm, Calder is appointed your [housecarl](Skyrim_Housecarl.md) and will automatically appear in Hjerim.

Calder wears a set of [steel armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Armor), a pair of [steel Nordic gauntlets](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Nordic_Gauntlets), and a pair of [steel cuffed boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Cuffed_Boots). He is equipped with a [steel war axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_War_Axe) and a [steel shield](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Shield).

He will wait in the home until he is ordered to [follow](Skyrim_Followers.md) you. If he is dismissed he will return to Hjerim and resume waiting.

He is also a potential candidate for [marriage](Skyrim_Marriage.md).
