[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/ed/SR-npc-Ghost_Knight.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Ghost_Knight.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ghost_Knight.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ghost_Knight.jpg) Ghost Knight Simon Rodayne is a ghost [Breton](Skyrim_Breton.md) [spellsword](Skyrim_Spellsword_(class).md), who in life was a member of the [Order of the Crypt](Lore_Crypt_of_Hearts.md#Fourth_Era), before he fell in love with (or was tricked by) a witch of the [Beldama Wyrd](Lore_Beldama_Wyrd.md) named [Daenella](Skyrim_Daenella.md) and consequently killed. He is said to haunt the banks of the [Karth River](Skyrim_Karth_River.md) at night, below his hilltop grave. You will find out during the quest [Crypt of the Heart](Skyrim_Crypt_of_the_Heart.md) that his heart has been taken by the Beldama coven to be corrupted, thereby damning his soul.

He first appears as the **Ghost Knight** during Crypt of the Heart when you approach the [red mountain flower](Skyrim_Red_Mountain_Flower.md) on the bridge near the [Reachcliff Secret Entrance](Skyrim_Reachcliff_Secret_Entrance.md). He will lead you to his grave if you follow him, fading in and out of sight.

He is clad in a full set of [ebony Spell Knight armor](Skyrim_Ebony_Spell_Knight_Armor.md) and carries a [steel sword](Skyrim_Steel_Sword.md). In addition, he knows the spells [Bound Sword](Skyrim_Bound_Sword.md), [Chain Lightning](Skyrim_Chain_Lightning.md), [Firebolt](Skyrim_Firebolt.md), [Healing](Skyrim_Healing.md), and [Ironflesh](Skyrim_Ironflesh.md).
