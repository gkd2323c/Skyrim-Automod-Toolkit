[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a4/SR-npc-Torolf.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Torolf.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Torolf.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Torolf.jpg) Torolf **Torolf** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [lumberjack](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lumberjack) who appears at the beginning of the game. He is the son of [Froki Whetted-Blade](Skyrim_Froki_Whetted-Blade.md), the husband of [Matlara](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Matlara), and the father of [Haming](Skyrim_Haming.md). He is first seen talking to his son about the prisoners and the soldiers that are coming into town, telling him to go inside. He is killed during the dragon attack on Helgen.

He wears a set of [hide armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hide_Armor) with a matching pair of [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hide_Boots), and carries an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md).
