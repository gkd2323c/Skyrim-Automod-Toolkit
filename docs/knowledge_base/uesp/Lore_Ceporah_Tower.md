[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c1/ON-place-Ceporah_Tower.jpg/200px-ON-place-Ceporah_Tower.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Ceporah_Tower.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Ceporah_Tower.jpg) Ceporah Tower [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e8/ON-tribute-psijic-Ceporah%27s_Insight.png/200px-ON-tribute-psijic-Ceporah%27s_Insight.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-tribute-psijic-Ceporah%27s_Insight.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-tribute-psijic-Ceporah%27s_Insight.png) Ceporah Tower The Ceporah Tower is an ancient structure on the Isle of [Artaeum](Lore_Artaeum.md), an island originally in the [Summerset Archipelago](Lore_Summerset_Isles.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-FOA-1)</sup> The Tower serves as the headquarters of the monastic [Psijic Order](Lore_Psijic_Order.md), who call Artaeum home.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOSummerset-2)</sup> While parts of the Tower were constructed by the Psijics, its foundations are far older, having an entirely unique architecture, and was built by unknown ancient beings.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESOTemelin-3)</sup> Directly beneath Ceporah Tower lies the **Dreaming Cave**, which houses a portal that can be used to travel to any realm of [Oblivion](Lore_Oblivion.md) and beyond, and is described as the place where all the [magicka](Lore_Magicka.md) and other energies in Artaeum pool, like water dripping from a chain,<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ESOIachesis-4)</sup> it has been used for this purpose by many, including [Sotha Sil](Lore_Sotha_Sil.md) —a member of the [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md) [Tribunal](Lore_Tribunal.md) —who made a compact with many of the [Daedric Princes](Lore_Daedric_Princes.md) in the late [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-2920TLYFR-5)</sup>

Ceporah Tower, like Artaeum itself, normally resides in a [plane](Lore_Planes_of_Existence.md) outside [Nirn](Lore_Nirn.md) where it was transported by the Psijics, it is said that this plane exists in a place harder to reach than even [Aetherius](Lore_Aetherius.md) or even realms "beyond" it, where even the magic of the [Sea Sload](Lore_Sea_Sload.md) can't reach, thus allowing for the Order to research the truths of [Aurbis](Lore_Aurbis.md) without interruption.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ONLil-6)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ESOIachesis-4)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ESOValsirenn-7)</sup> It is said that even the [Daedric Princes](Lore_Daedric_Princes.md) do not know where the plane where Ceporah Tower resides is located, though the Psijics maintain telepathic contact with certain oracles of the [Wyrd](Lore_Wyrd.md) to gain information on Tamriel's affairs, and the location can be discerned if one finds such an oracle and traces the connection back to the island itself, even allowing for a portal to it to be opened.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-ONMarieve-8)</sup> It is used by the Psijics in many of their rituals,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-FOA-1)</sup> and is known as the location beneath which [Vanus Galerion](Lore_Vanus_Galerion.md) confronted [Mannimarco](Lore_Mannimarco.md) about the latter's increasing fascination with [necromancy](Lore_Necromancy.md) and soul-trapping.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-MKOW-9)</sup> It was this confrontation that led directly to the founding of the [Mages Guild](Lore_Mages_Guild.md).

## Architecture
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/58/ON-interior-The_Dreaming_Cave.jpg/200px-ON-interior-The_Dreaming_Cave.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-interior-The_Dreaming_Cave.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-interior-The_Dreaming_Cave.jpg) The Dreaming Cave Many of the persistent enchantments in the Ceporah Tower can be modulated by Control Globes if one has the years of experience and delicate touch required.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-10)</sup> The material the tower is constructed from is unknown but suspected to be [coral](Lore_Coral.md) or something similar.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-Matty-11)</sup>

## History
The bottom base structure of the tower was already present when Artaeum was first discovered. It is unknown who built it,<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESOTemelin-3)</sup> but it was certainly not the elves,<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-Matty-11)</sup> and is presumed to have been made by ancient beings as it isn't known to match any other ruins found within Summerset.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESOTemelin-3)</sup> It is possible these beings were the [Sload](Lore_Sload.md), who were known to inhabit Summeret prior to the Aldmer<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-PGE3S-12)</sup> and lay claim to Summerset.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ESOValsirenn-7)</sup> The tower was said to resemble Sload structures,<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-13)</sup> and the oldest ruins on the island are made of coral, suggesting they had inhabited the area long ago.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-PGE3S-12)</sup>
