**[Ansei](Lore_Ansei.md)** Satameh, the **Tigress of Dragonstar** was an ancient [Yokudan](Lore_Yokudan.md) sword singer warrior and mother of two other Ansei warriors known as the twins of sword and spear, Ra Hasa and Ra Huzar,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AS-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SS-2)</sup> whose teachings inspired generations of [Redguard](Lore_Redguard.md) swordmasters.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-CO6C-3)</sup>

## History
Little is known about her life, but it is known that she gave birth to twins: Ra Huzar, a master of the spear,<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-RHU-4)</sup> and Ra Hasa, a skilled swordswoman,<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-RHA-5)</sup> both revered as Ansei themselves. She also earned the moniker of the [Tigress](Lore_Senche-Cat.md#Tigers) of [Dragonstar](Lore_Dragonstar.md). Following her passing, she was laid to rest in the Valley of Blades alongside her children, with each of them honored by a commemorative statue.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AS-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SS-2)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-VOB-6)</sup>

The teachings of Satameh remained highly esteemed and were followed by Redguard swordmasters as late as [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582). They were listed alongside the teachings and principles of other Ansei such as [Divad Hunding](Lore_Divad_Hunding.md), [Ra Abah](Lore_Ra_Abah.md), and [Kalam](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Kalam). According to these teachings, while a warrior may refine their skills to excellence and sharpen their blade to cut through stone and steel effortlessly, the measure of a true swordsman lies in the caliber of adversaries they attract.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-CO6C-3)</sup>

Amid the [Planemeld](Lore_Planemeld.md) crisis, [Sai Sahan](Lore_Sai_Sahan.md) journeyed to the sword-school in the Valley of Blades to obtain the Ring of Stendarr's Mercy but faced [Mannimarco](Lore_Mannimarco.md) and his Daedric followers. Mannimarco attempted to halt Sai Sahan by resurrecting the remains of Satameh and her children, but they were ultimately vanquished.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-VOB-6)</sup>
