[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f5/SR-item-Bolar%27s_Oathblade.jpg/200px-SR-item-Bolar%27s_Oathblade.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Bolar%27s_Oathblade.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Bolar%27s_Oathblade.jpg) Bolar's Oathblade [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a8/SR-place-Bloated_Man%27s_Grotto_02.jpg/200px-SR-place-Bloated_Man%27s_Grotto_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Bloated_Man%27s_Grotto_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Bloated_Man%27s_Grotto_02.jpg) The Statue of Talos where this weapon rests Bolar's Oathblade is the [Akaviri](Lore_Akavir.md) katana of the dead [Blade](Skyrim_Blades.md) Acilius Bolar. Its enchantment causes 25 points of [stamina damage](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Stamina_Damage) and twelve points of [fear](Skyrim_Fear_(effect).md) for thirty seconds. [Tempering](Skyrim_Grindstone.md) this weapon requires one [quicksilver ingot](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Quicksilver_Ingot) and the [Arcane Blacksmith](Skyrim_Arcane_Blacksmith.md) perk. The improvements are doubled if you have the [Steel Smithing](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Smithing) perk.

Bolar, being the sole survivor of the fall of [Cloud Ruler Temple](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Cloud_Ruler_Temple) to the [Thalmor](Lore_Thalmor.md), retreated to a shrine of [Talos](Lore_Talos.md) hidden inside [Bloated Man's Grotto](Skyrim_Bloated_Man%27s_Grotto.md). However, the Thalmor eventually tracked him down to his resting place to continue the systematic eradication of the Blades. Choosing to defend this ancient site and die with honor, Acilius confronted them after leaving behind his [last writ](Skyrim_Bolar%27s_Writ.md) and this blade.

## Notes
- The blade will not spawn during the hunt in [Ill Met By Moonlight](Skyrim_Ill_Met_By_Moonlight.md) as the inside of Bloated Man's Grotto is completely altered. - If you haven't retrieved the blade before starting the quest, the only way to get it is to kill [Sinding](Skyrim_Sinding.md) and not enter the grotto for an in-game month. After that time period, the cave will reset to its pre-quest contents.

## Bugs
- Bolar's Oathblade doesn't fully recharge due to the enchantment value not being an evenly divisible number of the enchantment cost. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Legendary_Edition_Patch), version 3.0.9, addresses this issue. The enchantment value is now 575 so that it will charge fully as a multiple of 115.
