[](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-weapon-Nordic_Sword.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-weapon-Nordic_Sword.png) Ancestral Sword of Clan Ice-Blade The Ancestral Sword of Clan Ice-Blade is a unique sword found in [Silverdrift Lair](Skyrim_Silverdrift_Lair.md). It deals no damage and cannot stagger opponents, even with power attacks or bashing. It also has a feeble [Turn Undead](Skyrim_Turn_Undead_(effect).md) enchantment, making it useless outside of the related quest.

## Related Quests
- **[The Gray Cowl of Nocturnal](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Gray_Cowl_of_Nocturnal)**<sup>[CC](Skyrim_The_Gray_Cowl_Returns!.md)</sup>: Investigate the legend of the Gray Fox.

## Notes
- [Poisons](Skyrim_Poison.md) can be used to deal damage with the Ancestral Sword.
- [Followers](Skyrim_Followers.md) and [Raised Undead](Skyrim_Reanimate.md) will not use this weapon, as their fists deal more damage.

## Bugs
- The Ancestral Sword can be disenchanted, and the resulting Turn Undead enchantment has no name or description. - The [Official Skyrim Special Edition Patch](Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch.md) fixes this bug.
- The Turn Undead enchantment does not wear off, reducing the target's [Confidence](Skyrim_Confidence.md) permanently. **Mod Notes**: The effect does not have the "Recover" flag set.
