[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/4e/SR-menu-Civil_War_Champions.jpg/200px-SR-menu-Civil_War_Champions.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-menu-Civil_War_Champions.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-menu-Civil_War_Champions.jpg) Civil War Champions **Civil War Champions** is a [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md) that adds new armor sets based on the [Imperial Legion](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md) and [Stormcloaks](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md), as well as a quest in which you duel a champion of the opposing faction. It was released on December 6, 2018 for 600 Creation Credits.

The Creation was primarily made by [Borx25](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/users/2531318), while the quest was created by [Kris Takahashi](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/users/1102501).

## Official Summary
*In the midst of an unending war, the Stormcloaks and Imperials have agreed to a duel. Two champions are to meet on the field of battle, to decide once and for all whose warriors are the fiercest. Choose a side, don the armor of the champion, and prove your mettle. Created by Borja "Borx25" Franco. Quest created by Kris Takahashi. (Quest "Battle of the Champions" starts by reading Battle of the Champions at The Drunken Huntsman in Whiterun or waiting for a letter from a commanding officer.)*
## Content
21 items are included with the Creation, with a full set of champion's armor and weapons for each of the warring factions.

## Notes
- The Creation also includes two unique items, the [Imperial Dragon Cloak](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Dragon_Cloak) and [Storm-Bear Cloak](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Storm-Bear_Cloak), both of which are unobtainable. The Unofficial Skyrim Creation Club Content Patch resolves this.
- This Creation received an update in February 2019 which modified the textures for both sets of armor.
