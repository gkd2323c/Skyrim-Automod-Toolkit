[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/37/SR-npc-Imperial_Courier.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Imperial_Courier.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Imperial_Courier.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Imperial_Courier.jpg) An Imperial Courier **Imperial Couriers** can be encountered on a few occasions within Skyrim. If you did not receive a suggestion from [Hadvar](Skyrim_Hadvar.md) to join the [Imperial Legion](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md) after completing [Unbound](Skyrim_Unbound.md), and haven't yet joined either side in the [civil war](Skyrim_Civil_War.md), it's possible to encounter the Imperial Courier randomly. He can also be encountered during [A False Front](Skyrim_A_False_Front_(Stormcloaks).md), when you must retrieve their [documents](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Documents) for [Galmar Stone-Fist](Skyrim_Galmar_Stone-Fist.md) if you've joined the [Stormcloaks](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md). Attacking him outside the related quest is considered a [crime](Skyrim_Crime.md).

He wears a full set of [Imperial Light Armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Light_Armor) and is armed with an [Imperial sword](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Sword), supplemented by an [Imperial bow](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Bow) with thirteen [steel arrows](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Arrow). He also carries a backup [steel dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Dagger) and a [belted tunic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Belted_Tunic).

## Related Quests
- **[A False Front](Skyrim_A_False_Front_(Stormcloaks).md)**: Find and deliver some forged documents to the [Imperials](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md).
- **[Joining the Legion](Skyrim_Joining_the_Legion.md)**: Prove yourself worthy to join the [Imperial Legion](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md).

## Notes
- If the Imperial Courier is encountered in a hold where the guards are loyal to the Stormcloaks, they will attack the Courier.
