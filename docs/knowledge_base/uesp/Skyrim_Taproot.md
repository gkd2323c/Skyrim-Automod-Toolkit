## Ingredients
28-33<sup>([DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md))</sup> guaranteed samples found in 22-24<sup>([DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md))</sup> different locations. Locations with multiple samples are:

- 3 in [Ienth Farm](Skyrim_Ienth_Farm.md) ([Raven Rock](Skyrim_Raven_Rock.md))<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup>
- 2 in [Severin Manor](Skyrim_Severin_Manor.md) ([Raven Rock](Skyrim_Raven_Rock.md))<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup>
- 2 in [Ansilvund Excavation](Skyrim_Ansilvund_Excavation.md) ([Eastmarch](Skyrim_Eastmarch.md))
- 2 around [Clearpine Pond](Skyrim_Clearpine_Pond.md) ([Haafingar](Skyrim_Haafingar.md))
- 2 in [Fort Snowhawk](Skyrim_Fort_Snowhawk.md) ([Hjaalmarch](Skyrim_Hjaalmarch.md))
- 2 in [Honeyside](Skyrim_Honeyside.md) ([Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md))
- 2 in [Nightcaller Temple](Skyrim_Nightcaller_Temple.md) ([The Pale](Skyrim_The_Pale.md))
- 2 in [Proudspire Manor](Skyrim_Proudspire_Manor.md) ([Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md))

It is categorized as an uncommon ingredient, meaning that all apothecary merchants have a 15% chance of carrying 1-5 samples. In addition, it may be randomly found in uncommon- and rare-type [apothecary's satchels](Skyrim_Apothecary%27s_Satchel.md).

## Creatures
Taproots are dropped by all varieties of [spriggan](Skyrim_Spriggan.md), including [spriggan matrons](Skyrim_Spriggan_Matron.md) and [spriggan earth mothers](Skyrim_Spriggan_Earth_Mother.md)<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup>.

## Related Quests
- **[Healing a House](Skyrim_Healing_a_House.md)**<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup>: Help [Elynea Mothren](Skyrim_Elynea_Mothren.md) repair the tower of [Tel Mithryn](Skyrim_Tel_Mithryn_(settlement).md). - Three samples are needed for this quest.

## Notes
- Contrary to what is stated in the official Game Guide, spriggans only drop taproot, and never drop [spriggan sap](Skyrim_Spriggan_Sap.md). - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.0, addresses this issue. Both ingredients can be dropped when spriggans die.
- Objects which look exactly like Taproots in netting can be found in witches' lairs, [Hagraven](Skyrim_Hagraven.md) nests and [Forsworn](Skyrim_Forsworn.md) camps and are apparently used as light sources by the inhabitants. You cannot harvest taproot from these objects, however.
- Although all varieties of spriggan are different colors, the harvested taproot of each one is always the brown and green of the base type of spriggan.
