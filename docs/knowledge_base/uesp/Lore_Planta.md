# Planta

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a9/OBR-flora-Letifer_Orca_Planta.jpg/200px-OBR-flora-Letifer_Orca_Planta.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OBR-flora-Letifer_Orca_Planta.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OBR-flora-Letifer_Orca_Planta.jpg) Letifer Orca Planta [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/da/ON-quest-Sanguine%27s_Solace.jpg/200px-ON-quest-Sanguine%27s_Solace.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-quest-Sanguine%27s_Solace.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-quest-Sanguine%27s_Solace.jpg) Sanguine's Solace Planta is the proper term for plant-like beings that possess a degree of consciousness and exhibit [magical](Lore_Magic.md) properties. Despite their sentience, they are still classified as [flora](Lore_Flora.md). Notable examples include Sanguine's Solace, native to [Sanguine](Lore_Sanguine.md)'s realm of [Revelry](Lore_Revelry.md) and known to be invasive on [Nirn](Lore_Nirn.md),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-LV-1)</sup> and the [Letifer Orca](Lore_Letifer_Orca_Planta.md), a carnivorous species found in the [Shivering Isles](Lore_Shivering_Isles.md) of [Sheogorath](Lore_Sheogorath.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-LOP-2)</sup> They are not to be confused with plant-like [Nature Spirits](Lore_Nature_Spirit.md).
