[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/bd/SR-npc-Hafjorg.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Hafjorg.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Hafjorg.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Hafjorg.jpg) Hafjorg **Hafjorg** is an [elderly](Skyrim_Elder.md) [apothecary](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Vendor Apothecary) who helps her husband [Elgrim](Skyrim_Elgrim.md) run his [alchemy](Skyrim_Alchemy.md) shop, [Elgrim's Elixirs](Skyrim_Elgrim%27s_Elixirs.md). She is generally warm towards her husband and you, but does provide slight nagging in reference to the location of the shop along the [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md) canal waterways. She is typically the one behind the counter, although she will also offer services while sitting at their dinner table. She needs help [retrieving](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Truth_Ore_Consequences) an [ore sample](Skyrim_Quest_Items.md#Quicksilver_Ore) from [Filnjar](Skyrim_Filnjar.md) at [Shor's Stone](Skyrim_Shor%27s_Stone.md), and will reward you with random potions if you retrieve the ore for her.

Elgrim's Elixirs is open 24 hours a day, so you can trade with Hafjorg at any time. She wakes up at 6am, then wanders around the shop until 9am for a one-hour breakfast. She wanders around again until 11am, then she starts her job as shopkeeper. She ends her workday at 5pm, spending an hour relaxing before she has a one-hour dinner at 6pm. She stays up until midnight when she goes to bed.

She wears a set of merchant [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#0006ff45), [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#0004223d), and a [hat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hat#0004223b). She carries a [dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dagger) (up to [elven](Skyrim_Elven.md) quality at level 20), the [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Elgrim%27s_Elixirs_Key) to the shop, a [belted tunic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Belted_Tunic), along with a selection of [middle-class items and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common).

## Dialogue
Her greetings:

*"This place has seen better days, but it's not my fault."* *"Where's my head? I never even greeted you properly."* *"I apologize in advance for my husband's crankiness; I promise you he means well."* *"Thanks again for delivering that ore sample."* When spoken to:

**Are you an alchemist as well?** *"I know how to mix a few basic potions, but I'm nowhere near as skilled as a master alchemist. I've picked up a few tricks by reading the tomes scattered all over the shop, but I don't have the knack to pull half of the formulas off. I wish Elgrim had spent more time tutoring me then that [Ingun](Skyrim_Ingun_Black-Briar.md) girl."* **Are you jealous of her?** *"Oh! No, I didn't mean it that way. Elgrim can barely put his * [[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)] * on his own shoes let alone have the energy to pursue a young girl. He's been gushing about how talented Ingun is at alchemy. He'd never say it to her face; that's not his way. I just think Elgrim's taken it upon himself to mentor the young thing so he focuses a lot of attention on her."* **Does it bother you living underground?** *"When Elgrim said we were setting up shop down here, I told him he would catch his death. The dirt, the moisture... it's disgusting. Who wants to spend their life living like a skeever? But that old bellyacher just fed me some nonsense about light ruining his mixtures and being close to the water... agh."* Upon ending conversation:

*"Don't forget us if you need supplies for your journey."*
## Quest-Related Dialogue

### Truth Ore Consequences
To start her quest, you need to speak to her about her plights:

**You've got your hands full, eh?** *"I swear, if I wasn't around, Elgrim would forget to eat his meals. How we make any coin at all is beyond me. In fact, I need to arrange for someone to pick up an ore sample for me in Shor's Stone... goodness knows Elgrim won't lift a finger to do it."* Telling her that you could get it for her will have her say:

**I could get it for you.** *"You would? Oh, that would be wonderful. Speak to Filnjar in Shor's Stone. He's got some sort of ore sample he wants us to identify and was willing to pay us well for it."* **Good luck with that.** *"The job pays well, so I hope I can make arrangements soon. We could really use the gold."* Upon ending the conversation:

*"Be careful, the road to Shor's Stone could be dangerous."* She will be grateful once you bring her the requested ore sample:

**I've brought the ore sample from Filnjar.** *"Thank you. I'll be sure to get Elgrim to examine this as soon as his... busy schedule allows. Here, I had him make you a few things for all that travel."* Approaching her if you have not finished her quest will have her ask you:

*"Have you managed to make it out to Shor's Stone?"*
### Spread the Love
Offering her a [pamphlet](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Warmth_of_Mara) from the [local temple of Mara](Skyrim_Temple_of_Mara.md) during [Spread the Love](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Spread_the_Love) will have her say:

**Here, sister. Blessings of Mara upon you.** *"Hmmm. Never read anything from the temple before. I suppose there wouldn't be any harm in reading it."*
## Conversations
At random times, Hafjorg may be seen having small clashes with her husband:

**Hafjorg**: *"Elgrim, honey. I'm concerned about your new pupil. She seems very... odd."*
**Elgrim**: *"Ingun? She's brilliant; a natural!"*
**Hafjorg**: *"Oh dear, I didn't mean to say she isn't bright. It's the things she makes here, the things she says and all those poor little animals she tests them on. It's just not right."*
**Elgrim**: *"Damn it, woman! Ingun is the most promising alchemist I've ever seen and I won't have you scaring her off!"* **Elgrim**: *"Hafjorg! Where did you put it woman?!"*
**Hafjorg**: *"Sigh. What is it now, dear?"*
**Elgrim**: *"Don't you 'dear' me! I had a bowl of Vampire Dust on my worktable not an hour ago and after you swept in there it's gone!"*
**Hafjorg**: *"Oh my. Elgrim, it's on the shelf to the right of the table, as always."*
