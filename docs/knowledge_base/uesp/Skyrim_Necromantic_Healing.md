- *Heals the undead target 10 points per second, but not the living, atronachs or machines.*

**Necromantic Healing** is an apprentice level [Restoration](Skyrim_Restoration.md) spell, which will slowly [restore the health](Skyrim_Restore_Health_to_Undead.md) of an undead target.

## Effects
- [Restore Health to Undead](Skyrim_Restore_Health_to_Undead.md), 10 pts for 1 sec
- [Restore Stamina](Skyrim_Restore_Stamina.md), 10 pts for 1 sec; if the [Respite](Skyrim_Respite.md) perk is unlocked.

## Perks
- [Regeneration](Skyrim_Regeneration.md), increases health (and stamina with the Respite perk) restoration to 15/sec.

## Notes
- This spell is normally unavailable if you join the Dawnguard, as [Ronthil](Skyrim_Ronthil.md) will be hostile to you. However, if you use a [Pacify](Skyrim_Pacify_(effect).md) spell or item, you can calm him down so that he'll sell it to you. You must do this before you complete quest [Touching the Sky](Skyrim_Touching_the_Sky.md) on Dawnguard side.
