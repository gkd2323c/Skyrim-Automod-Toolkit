[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b8/SR-npc-Sirkjorg.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Sirkjorg.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Sirkjorg.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Sirkjorg.jpg) Sirkjorg **Sirkjorg** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) who can be found working at the [Temple of Miraak](Skyrim_Temple_of_Miraak.md) until it is cleansed, at which point he and [Herkja](Skyrim_Herkja.md) relocate to [Bujold's Retreat](Skyrim_Bujold%27s_Retreat.md), or else to [Thirsk Mead Hall](Skyrim_Thirsk_Mead_Hall.md) if the quest [Retaking Thirsk](Skyrim_Retaking_Thirsk.md) is completed.

He wears [banded iron armor](Skyrim_Banded_Iron_Armor.md) with iron [gauntlets](Skyrim_Iron_Gauntlets.md), [boots](Skyrim_Iron_Boots.md), and [helmet](Skyrim_Iron_Helmet.md). Sirkjorg wields a [Nordic mace](Skyrim_Nordic_Mace.md) in combat.

## Dialogue
When Sirkjorg returns to the Mead Hall, he will make several comments:

- *"It's colder down here. I don't like the cold."'*
- *"The food is better up here, too."*
- *"Glad to be back."*

## Notes
- Sirkjorg is a member of the [Dremora](Skyrim_Dremora_(class).md) class, which determines the level of his skills.
