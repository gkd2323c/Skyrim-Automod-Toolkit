[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/75/SR-npc-Odvan.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Odvan.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Odvan.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Odvan.jpg) Odvan **Odvan** is a [Breton](Skyrim_Breton.md) [witchblade](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Witchblade) who lives in [Cidhna Mine](Skyrim_Cidhna_Mine.md).

He wears a standard [roughspun tunic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Roughspun_Tunic), like many of his fellow prisoners, and a pair of [footwraps](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Footwraps). He carries a bottle of [skooma](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Skooma) and a single [shiv](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shiv). He knows the [Alteration](Skyrim_Alteration.md) spell [Oakflesh](Skyrim_Oakflesh.md), the [Conjuration](Skyrim_Conjuration.md) spell [Raise Zombie](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Raise_Zombie), and the [Destruction](Skyrim_Destruction.md) spell [Flames](Skyrim_Flames.md).

## Scenes
Throughout the day, Odvan can be seen talking to Duach:

**Odvan**: *"Think maybe we should jump the guards when they come down next?"* **Duach**: *"They keep the doors locked when they come down. You kill them, you're trapping us here with no food."*
## Rumors
His aunt, Bothela, may mention him when her apprentice asks if she has any family:

*"I have one. A nephew. He's in prison for being a member of the Forsworn."*
## Notes
- This scene, as shown by internal names, was originally meant to play out between Odvan and Duach instead:

**Braig**: *"Have any skooma? I need to get something from Grisvar."* **Duach**: *"You make one move towards my skooma, and I shiv you."* - There is some confusion about his relationship to Bothela. In all of her dialogue, she refers to him as her nephew. But in Odvan's dialogue, he once refers to her as his grandma and once implies her to be his aunt. Meanwhile, their relationship data has them set up as grandchild and grandparent, adding confusion.
