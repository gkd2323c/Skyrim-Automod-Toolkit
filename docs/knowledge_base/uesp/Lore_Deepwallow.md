[3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433) Deepwallow is a village in the [Dementia](Lore_Dementia_(place).md) region of the [Shivering Isles](Lore_Shivering_Isles.md). It is located on the [Heretic's Horn](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Heretic%27s_Horn), along the coast of a marshy lagoon south of [New Sheoth](Lore_New_Sheoth.md). It is connected by bridge to the Low Road, diverging from it at the gates of [Crucible](Lore_Crucible.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SI-1)</sup>

Circa [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), the settlement was home to only three individuals, following the suicide of Gadeneri Ralvel. She was survived by her brother, Erver Devani, who became embitter over his sister's fate, her [soul](Lore_Souls.md) doomed to haunt the [Hill of Suicides](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Hill_of_Suicides).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup> The [Hero of Kvatch](Lore_Hero_of_Kvatch.md) recovered Gadeneri's scattered skull from [Knifepoint Hollow](Lore_Knifepoint_Hollow.md) and brought it to her on the Hill of Suicides, freeing her from that fate.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup>
