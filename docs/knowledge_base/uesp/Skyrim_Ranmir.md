[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d3/SR-npc-Ranmir.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Ranmir.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ranmir.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ranmir.jpg) Ranmir **Ranmir** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [citizen](Skyrim_Citizen_(class).md) of [Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold.md). He lives with his sister [Birna](Skyrim_Birna.md) in [Birna's Oddments](Skyrim_Birna%27s_Oddments.md). He spends his days drinking away his sorrows in [The Frozen Hearth](Skyrim_The_Frozen_Hearth.md). You can learn why by speaking to either [Haran](Skyrim_Haran.md) or [Dagur](Skyrim_Dagur.md). He's been drunk since the disappearance of his [lover](Skyrim_Isabelle_Rolaine.md), convinced she left him for someone else. He also hasn't been paying for his drinks recently, and Haran could use some help getting Ranmir to catch up on his tab.

Ranmir wears a set of farm [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#0003452e) along with a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md#0003452f). He carries his [house key](Skyrim_Key_to_Ranmir%27s_House.md) along with a selection of [lower-class items and gold](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Poor). In combat, he relies on an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md).

## Quest-Related Dialogue

### A Few Words with You

- **You need to pay Haran back for all your drinks.**
- *"Who are you to say what I should do? I'll pay her back when I'm ready."*

You will then have 3 options:

| Option | Dialogue | Reaction |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **You owe her gold. Pay it.** **(Persuade)** | **Passed**: *"You're right. What would my forefathers think if they knew I wasn't paying my debts? Tell Haran I'll bring her the gold I owe."* <br> **Failed**: *"And I told you I'd pay it when I'm good and ready."* | **Passed**: Return to Haran <br> **Failed**: Nothing |
| **What if I pay it?** **(Bribe)** | **Passed**: *"I won't say no to that."* <br> **Failed**: *"I'm not about to take on debts from some milk drinker I don't know."* | **Passed**: Return to Haran <br> **Failed**: Nothing |
| **Do it, or things get ugly.** **(Intimidate)** | **Passed**: *"Calm down there, friend. I'll pay! Tell Haran that she'll have her gold."* <br> **Failed**: (Start a brawl) - **Victory**: *"What was that? Some kind of foreign move?"* - **You ready to pay up now?** <br> - *"You got me, fair and square. Tell Haran I'll pay."* <br> - **Failure**: (Nothing) | **Passed**: Return to Haran <br> **Failed**: Brawl |
| **Fine. Forget I asked.** | *"That's what I thought."* | Nothing |

### Drowned Sorrows
When asking him about Isabelle:

- **I understand you knew Isabelle Rolaine.**
- *"What? Don't you... Don't you say that name to me. Don't you dare. Ever."*

Speaking to him after you have found [Isabelle's letter](Skyrim_Isabelle%27s_Letter.md):

- **I have something for you.**
- *"Unless it's gold for another round of drinks, I don't wanna hear it."*
- **It's a letter from Isabelle. You should read it.**
- *"Don't you go pulling my leg. Give it here. This... This is really from her, isn't it? Did she really run off and do this? For me? By the Eight. She's dead, isn't she? And it's all my fault. I... I have some thinking to do. Thank you for this."*

If you try to speak to him as he leaves:

- *"Please, leave me alone. I need to be by myself."*

He will then leave and disappears from the game.

## Conversations
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/2f/SR-quest-Yngol%27s_Barrow.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Yngol%27s_Barrow.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Yngol%27s_Barrow.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Yngol%27s_Barrow.jpg) Birna and Ranmir, arguing The first time you enter into Winterhold, you may encounter Birna and Ranmir arguing on the road:

**Birna**: *"We need coin, Ranmir, and you're not bringing home any."*
**Ranmir**: *"And you'd have me do what, join the [College](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(faction).md) and prance about casting spells all day?"*
**Birna**: *"Don't just walk away from me! Where do you think you're going?"*
**Ranmir**: *"To the [Inn](Skyrim_The_Frozen_Hearth.md) for a drink, of course! Where else could I even go in this gods-forsaken town?"*
**Birna**: *"And what, you think that'll solve all your problems?"*
**Ranmir**: *"Probably not, but it's worth a try!"*

Should Ranmir meet with his sister inside The Frozen Hearth, she will either angrily ask about their money or desperately try to get him to return home:

**Birna**: *"Well, isn't this a surprise!"*
**Birna**: *"How much of our money have you spent today, Ranmir? Anything left, or is it all gone?"*
**Ranmir**: *"If I wanted to get yelled at, I'd be home. Can't you leave me be?"*
**Birna**: *"Oh, I'm sorry. Am I bothering you? Am I making your life more difficult?"*

**Birna**: *"Please, come home. Just... Just come home."*
**Ranmir**: *"Can't. Got at least two rounds to go."*
**Ranmir**: *"Why don't you go... sell something."*
**Birna**: *"Why? Because I'd rather be here trying to keep my only brother from drinking himself to death."*
