# Black-Tongue Tribe

The **Black-Tongue Tribe**, or **Kota-Vimleel** in [Jel](Lore_Jel.md), is an Argonian tribe residing within [Murkmire](Lore_Murkmire.md). They live in the dark and shadowy corners of the mire, but do not share the same negative reputation as the similar [Ghost People Tribe](Lore_Ghost_People_Tribe.md). They tend to be soft-spoken unless caught off-guard, wherein they will not hesitate to kill those who trespass upon their lands. <sup>[[1]](#cite_note-To MBT-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-To MGP-2)</sup>

The tribe, dedicated to [Sithis](Lore_Sithis.md), has been known to actively attempt to influence their clutches to produce as many [Shadowscales](Lore_Shadowscales.md) as they can. A large portion of the tribe consists of alchemists, who use their vast knowledge of the plants of Black Marsh to produce a powerful contraceptive known as "Gloom Nectar", which allows the Black-Tongues to synchronize their egg-laying cycles and ensure that many of their clutches are born under the sign of the Shadow.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-To MBT-1)</sup>

The tribe knows of fourteen xal-fek or "sacred plants", one of which is the [Leviathan Pizzle](Lore_Leviathan_Pizzle.md). Often mistaken for various poisonous mud blossoms, it is known to have invigorating effects and is one of the ingredients in their unique "Dread Fathering Elixir" used during Bonding Rituals as another tool to maximize the number of hatchlings born under the Shadow.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-WMD-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-BGT-4)</sup>

A majority of the Tribe's alchemical ingredients are grown by other tribes, such as the [Hee-Tepsleel](Lore_Hee-Tepsleel.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-To MTC-5)</sup>
