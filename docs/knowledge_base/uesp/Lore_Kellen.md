Kellen was an old [Moth Priest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Moth_Priests) and a storyteller whom has read the [Elder Scrolls](Lore_Elder_Scrolls.md), who was active in the time after the [Great War](Lore_Great_War.md) and during the [Return of the Dragons](Lore_Return_of_the_Dragons.md) in the [Fourth Era](Lore_Fourth_Era.md). Kellen and travelled with a [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md) companion named Nagh, as well as a pet [owl](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Owl).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-FPrologue-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-CWCOffer From AFriend-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TESLCIC-3)</sup> He recited stories forgotten to memory and now only known through legend,<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Legends-4)</sup> the veracity of his tales however remains ambiguous.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-5)</sup>

## History

### The Forgotten Hero
In the time after the Great War, Kellen and his companion Nagh sought after and recovered an Elder Scroll from a place where rival forces were clashing. Kellen stayed with one side before the enemy's next attack and recited the story of the [Forgotten Hero](Lore_Forgotten_Hero.md) to a warrioress of that side named Isha, whose warchief was dying after being wounded in battle. After a long-winded explanation from Kellen, Isha realized that the intended moral of Kellen's tale was that she could give hope of victory to the soldiery by bearing her warchief's ferocious mask and lead the men into battle as if she were their warchief.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-FPrologue-1)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-FEpilogue-6)</sup>
