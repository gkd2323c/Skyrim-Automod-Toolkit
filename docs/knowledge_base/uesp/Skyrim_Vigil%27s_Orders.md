Vigil's Orders *The Vigil prepares to battle Oblivion* *
---
The Vigil is the last line of defense between the light and darkness. If the defilers manage to open the gate, then we are bound by Stendarr to not let a single abomination pass through it.

Find the gate, and secure it. Whatever it takes, we must hold the line.
