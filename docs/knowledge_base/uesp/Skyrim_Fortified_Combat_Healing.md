*Enchanting description:* **Slow health regen during combat.**

**Fortified Combat Healing** (not to be confused with [fortify healing](Skyrim_Regenerate_Health.md)) increases the rate at which the target regenerates health while in combat.

## Enchanting
The only item that uses this enchantment is [Chrysamere](Skyrim_Chrysamere_(item).md), added by the eponymous [Chrysamere](Skyrim_Chrysamere.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Chrysamere.md)</sup>.

As it cannot be disenchanted, this effect is unavailable for enchanting.

## Effect
See the [Health attribute](Skyrim_Health.md) page for a basic overview of health regeneration both in combat and out of combat works.

- The default combat multiplier for health regeneration is 0.7, which reduces passive health gains to 70% of out-of-combat values.
- [Chrysamere](Skyrim_Chrysamere_(item).md) increases this value by 1.0, bringing the total to 1.7. This means that while in combat, health regenerates at 170% of its out-of-combat rate, actually increasing your health regeneration significantly while fighting.

### Example
A character with 500 health and no other effects would by default regenerate:

- `500 * 0.7% = 3.5 health` while not in combat.
- `500 * 0.7% * 0.7 = 2.45 health` while in combat.

By wielding [Chrysamere](Skyrim_Chrysamere.md) this would change to:

- `500 * 0.7% * 1.7 = 5.95 health` while in combat.
