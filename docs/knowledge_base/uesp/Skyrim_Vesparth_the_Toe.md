[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/41/SR-npc-Vesparth_the_Toe.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Vesparth_the_Toe.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Vesparth_the_Toe.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Vesparth_the_Toe.jpg) Vesparth the Toe **Vesparth the Toe** is a [Dark Elf](Skyrim_Dark_Elf.md) member of the [Tribunal Temple](Skyrim_Tribunal_Temple.md). She follows what are considered heretical doctrines by the New Temple. She is the descendant of a family of [Buoyant Armigers](Lore_Buoyant_Armigers.md), and wields the [Cleaver of St. Felms](Skyrim_Cleaver_of_St._Felms.md), a holy artifact of the old Tribunal Temple. Along with others, she forms a circle around the inner sanctum of [Ashfall's Tear](Skyrim_Ashfall%27s_Tear.md), akin to that of the [Hands of Almalexia](Lore_Hands_of_Almalexia.md). She can later be recruited as a follower if you side with the heretics. She is one of the few followers who are able to dual-wield any of the given one-handed weapons. Although her default armor is light [Chitin Armor](Skyrim_Chitin_Armor.md), she specializes in heavy armor. Vesparth can also become a [personal steward](Skyrim_Personal_Steward.md).

Vesparth wears Chitin Armor, Chitin Boots, and Chitin Bracers, and carries both the legendary Cleaver of St. Felms and a Steel War axe as her weapons. She also carries several food items, 2 Lockpicks, 33 Gold, and her Journal. She does not carry any arrows that you can trade.
