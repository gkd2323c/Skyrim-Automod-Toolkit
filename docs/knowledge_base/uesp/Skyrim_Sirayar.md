He is a spy for the Remnants.

## Related Quests
- **[Interception](Skyrim_Interception.md)**<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Redguard_Elite_Armaments.md)</sup>: Aid the Hammerfell resistance.

## Quest-Related Dialogue
Sirayar will need to be spoken to during Interception:

- **"The ravens take flight once more."**
- *"I understand. Anyway, it's done. Time to move on."*

He will then hand you a [note](Skyrim_Sirayar%27s_Note.md) which states that the wagon will be passing by [Sunderstone Gorge](Skyrim_Sunderstone_Gorge.md).
