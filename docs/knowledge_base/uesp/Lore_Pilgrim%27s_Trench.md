**Pilgrim's Trench** is an underwater location off the coast of [Winterhold](Lore_Winterhold_(region).md), [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md), where a number of shipwrecks have settled.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SK-1)</sup> It is one of the largest ship graveyards in [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md), along with the [Shattered Shoals](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Shattered_Shoals) off [Khenarthi's Roost](Lore_Khenarthi%27s_Roost.md) and Shipwreck Strand in [Stonefalls](Lore_Stonefalls.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESO-2)</sup>

The infamous pirate lord [Velehk Sain](Lore_Velehk_Sain.md) once sank an Imperial galleon in the vicinity of Pilgrim's Trench, and buried its treasure on a nearby island, hidden by a magical ward.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup> In [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), the [East Empire Company](Lore_East_Empire_Company.md) ship *Sea Stallion* went down in a storm, and now lies at the bottom of the Trench.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-LSS-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-HJ-5)</sup>

The depths of Pilgrim's Trench are home to the elusive [anglerfish](Skyrim_Passive_Creatures.md#Anglerfish), as well as the more common [arctic char](Skyrim_Passive_Creatures.md#Arctic_Char) and [arctic grayling](Skyrim_Passive_Creatures.md#Arctic_Grayling).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-FS-6)</sup>
