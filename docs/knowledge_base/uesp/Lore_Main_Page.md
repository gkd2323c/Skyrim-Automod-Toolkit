# The Elder Scrolls Lore

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/6b/MER-art-Arena_Anthology_Game_Map.jpg/400px-MER-art-Arena_Anthology_Game_Map.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MER-art-Arena_Anthology_Game_Map.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MER-art-Arena_Anthology_Game_Map.jpg) The most recent and authoritative map of Tamriel, from *[The Elder Scrolls Anthology](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/General:The_Elder_Scrolls_Anthology)*
- *For guidelines on the Lore section, see [UESPWiki:Lore](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Lore).*

The events of *[The Elder Scrolls](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/General:The_Elder_Scrolls)* unfold on the vast continent of [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md), located on the planet of [Nirn](Lore_Nirn.md) (one of the [Planes of Existence](Lore_Planes_of_Existence.md)) in the realm of [Mundus](Lore_Mundus.md). [Aldmeris](Lore_Aldmeris_(language).md) for "*Dawn's Beauty*", Tamriel is home to nine distinct provinces: [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md), [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md), [Elsweyr](Lore_Elsweyr.md), [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md), [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md), [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md), [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md), [Summerset Isle](Lore_Summerset_Isle.md), and [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md).
