# Spode

[![](https://images.uesp.net/6/67/DF-npc-Lord_Bertram_Spode_%28face%29.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-npc-Lord_Bertram_Spode_(face).png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-npc-Lord_Bertram_Spode_(face).png) Lord Bertram Spode Spode is a noble [Breton](Lore_Breton.md) family associated with the March of [Glenpoint](Lore_Glenpoint.md). Little is known about the Spodes' history, except that Lord Bertram Spode was an associate of the [Thieves Guild](Lore_Thieves_Guild.md) around circa [3E 405](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_405).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-LBS-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DC-2)</sup><sup>:35</sup> Their exact status within the region is unclear, as Glenpoint was ruled by an unknown marquis during that time.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Glenpoint-3)</sup>
