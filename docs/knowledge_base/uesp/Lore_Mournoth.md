[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/31/ON-place-Mournoth.jpg/200px-ON-place-Mournoth.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Mournoth.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Mournoth.jpg) Mournoth circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/17/ON-map-Mournoth.jpg/200px-ON-map-Mournoth.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-map-Mournoth.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-map-Mournoth.jpg) Map of Mournoth Mournoth is a region in eastern [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md), within the [Bangkorai](Lore_Bangkorai.md) region. It borders [Gavaudon](Lore_Gavaudon.md) to the west, [Ephesus](Lore_Ephesus.md) to the south, [Wrothgar](Lore_Wrothgar.md) to the north, and [the Reach](Lore_The_Reach.md) of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) to the east. The region contains the city of [Evermore](Lore_Evermore.md). The most important landmarks are the [Halcyon Lake](Lore_Halcyon_Lake.md) on the [Bjoulsae River](Lore_Bjoulsae_River.md), [Murcien's Hamlet](Lore_Murcien%27s_Hamlet.md), Jackdaw Cove, the Viridian Woods, and the [Viridian Lake](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Viridian_Lake).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ON-1)</sup>

Mournoth used to belong to the [Reachmen](Lore_Reachmen.md) as part of the [Western Reach](Lore_Western_Reach.md), and they often tried to reclaim it, along with the city of Evermore and settlement of Murcien's Hamlet, which had mixed Breton and Reachfolk population.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ON-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-MZ-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Brinarch-3)</sup> One such attempt happened in [2E 541](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_541), when [Durcorach the Black Drake](Lore_Durcorach.md) captured the whole of Bangkorai.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Toa M-4)</sup> Another attempt took place in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), when the armies of Reachmen under the [Hagraven](Lore_Hagraven.md) Uela captured the northeastern Mournoth and threatened to attack Evermore. The Reachmen were eventually driven away, and Uela was killed.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ON-1)</sup>
