**Regenerate Magicka** (also known as **Fortify Magicka Rate**) increases the rate at which the target naturally regenerates [magicka](Skyrim_Magicka.md).

## Blessings and Powers
- The [Blessing of Akatosh](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Blessing_of_Akatosh) provides 10% faster magicka regeneration.
- [The Apprentice Stone](Skyrim_The_Apprentice_Stone.md) provides 100% faster magicka regeneration out of combat at the expense of a 100% weakness to magic.
- The [Highborn](Skyrim_Highborn.md) greater power allows [Altmer](Skyrim_High_Elf.md) to regenerate magicka faster for 60 seconds, once per day.
- [The Atronach Stone](Skyrim_The_Atronach_Stone.md) comes with a 50% slower magicka regeneration penalty.

## Food
The following food items provide a temporary version of this effect:

## Notes
- The [Ring of the Erudite](Skyrim_Ring_of_the_Erudite.md) in the [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md) [add-on](Skyrim_Add-ons.md) provides a unique version of the Regenerate Magicka effect that modifies the base value of the wearer's magicka regeneration instead of the percent modification seen in the above discussed effects and enchantments. See that item's page for more information.
- Multiple sources of magicka regeneration bonuses and penalties are additive. For instance, two +100% bonuses and one -50% penalty add up to +150%.
- By default, magicka regenerates at 3% of your total per second out of combat, and 1% per second in combat.
- When custom enchanting equipment with the enchantment that both fortifies a magic school and magicka regeneration, note that only the magic school being fortified (such as Restoration) will benefit from a high Enchantment skill and Enchantment-boosting potions; the magicka regeneration rate stays at 10%, even though found robes that carry these enchantments have a static 50% magicka regen rate. However, with the perk that allows two enchantments on one item, you can enchant the item with one of the aforementioned enchantments and a standard Regenerate Magicka enchantment, resulting in a 10% boost to the apparel you otherwise would not have. Note also that only chest apparel will accept this specific enchantment combining magic school fortification and magicka regeneration, not headwear or rings.
- If [Survival Mode](Skyrim_Survival_Mode.md) is active, higher [Fatigue](Skyrim_Fatigue.md) levels will cause your magicka to regenerate more slowly.
