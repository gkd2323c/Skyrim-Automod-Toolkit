[](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-armor-Lord%27s_Mail(m)_02.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-armor-Lord%27s_Mail(m)_02.png) Lord's Mail (male) The Lord's Mail is an artifact created by [Kynareth](Lore_Kynareth.md). It is [heavy armor](Skyrim_Heavy_Armor_(item).md) and enchanted with [poison resistance](Skyrim_Resist_Poison.md), [magic resistance](Skyrim_Resist_Magic.md), as well as a unique area effect that inflicts 5 points of [health absorption](Skyrim_Absorb_Health.md) per second on nearby opponents. It can be [improved](Skyrim_Workbench.md) with an [ebony ingot](Skyrim_Ebony_Ingot.md). It counts as [Imperial armor](Skyrim_Imperial_Armor.md) for the purpose of the [Matching Set](Skyrim_Heavy_Armor.md#Matching_Set) perk. It can be obtained by completing the quest [Gift of Kynareth](Skyrim_Gift_of_Kynareth.md).

## Related Quests
- **[Gift of Kynareth](Skyrim_Gift_of_Kynareth.md)**<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Lord%27s_Mail.md)</sup>: Recover a legendary [Legion](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md) artifact and obtain the blessing of [Kynareth](Lore_Kynareth.md).

## Notes
- This can be a useful item to wear while swimming as it will damage underwater enemies such as [slaughterfish](Skyrim_Slaughterfish.md).
- [Brawling](Skyrim_Brawl.md) while wearing the Lord's Mail will violate the rules of the brawl and will give you a [bounty](Skyrim_Crime.md#Bounties) for assault.
