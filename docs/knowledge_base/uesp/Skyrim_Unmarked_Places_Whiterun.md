### Big Log Bridge
[![SR-place-Big Log Bridge.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/37/SR-place-Big_Log_Bridge.jpg/200px-SR-place-Big_Log_Bridge.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Big_Log_Bridge.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Big_Log_Bridge.jpg) [![SR-place-Big Log Bridge 02.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/6f/SR-place-Big_Log_Bridge_02.jpg/200px-SR-place-Big_Log_Bridge_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Big_Log_Bridge_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Big_Log_Bridge_02.jpg) South of [Hillgrund's Tomb](Skyrim_Hillgrund%27s_Tomb.md) is a large fallen tree bridging a tributary of the [White River](Skyrim_White_River.md) that runs between two sheer cliffs. Standing guard on this log is a bandit, who will only attack if you get too close. The corpse of another bandit lies on a small beach below the bridge, with a dagger, necklace, and coin purse lying next to it. The body of a third bandit can be found on the northern cliff, next to the remains of a tree which conceals a locked chest and a copy of the Illusion skill book *[The Black Arts On Trial](Skyrim_The_Black_Arts_On_Trial.md)*. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=82938&y=-24767&zoom=17&legacy=true)

### Fetid Pond
[![SR-place-Fetid Pond.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/dc/SR-place-Fetid_Pond.jpg/200px-SR-place-Fetid_Pond.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Fetid_Pond.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Fetid_Pond.jpg) - POITundra29

Located southwest of Swindler's Den. It is a small pond in a depression with a mammoth skeleton and a dead bandit who carries the skill book *[Beggar](Skyrim_Beggar.md)*. A sabre cat lurks closeby. There is also a nirnroot growing by the pond.

### Hunter and Hunted
[![SR-place-Hunter and Hunted.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d3/SR-place-Hunter_and_Hunted.jpg/200px-SR-place-Hunter_and_Hunted.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Hunter_and_Hunted.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Hunter_and_Hunted.jpg) - POITundra27

On a mountain ridge west of Drelas' Cottage is a dead hunter and the beast that bested him. The hunter has two-three types of arrows on him, of varied quantities, up to 48 each. He also carries the skill book *[The Refugees](Skyrim_The_Refugees.md)*.

### King of the Mudcrabs
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b8/SR-place-Giant_Mudcrab_Pond.jpg/200px-SR-place-Giant_Mudcrab_Pond.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Giant_Mudcrab_Pond.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Giant_Mudcrab_Pond.jpg) Mudcrab pond southeast of Gjukar's Monument - POITundra10

Southeast of [Gjukar's Monument](Skyrim_Gjukar%27s_Monument.md) is a small pool containing the shell of a gigantic [mudcrab](Skyrim_Mudcrab.md), surrounded by smaller living mudcrabs. On the shore of the pool, beneath a large rock, is a skeleton with a leveled sword and shield lying next to it. This location is the spawn point of the [mudcrab guardian spirit](Skyrim_Mudcrab_Guardian_Spirit.md) during [Kyne's Sacred Trials](Skyrim_Kyne%27s_Sacred_Trials.md). [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=-58960&y=-14208&zoom=17&legacy=true)

### Necromancer's Bluff
[![SR-place-Necromancer's Bluff.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/10/SR-place-Necromancer%27s_Bluff.jpg/200px-SR-place-Necromancer%27s_Bluff.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Necromancer%27s_Bluff.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Necromancer%27s_Bluff.jpg) [![SR-place-Necromancer's Bluff 02.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/04/SR-place-Necromancer%27s_Bluff_02.jpg/200px-SR-place-Necromancer%27s_Bluff_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Necromancer%27s_Bluff_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Necromancer%27s_Bluff_02.jpg) - POITundra11

Located on a small rocky hill east of Sleeping Tree Camp are some ancient Nordic ruins with an altar in the center. Lying on this altar is a deceased [conjurer](Skyrim_Conjurer.md), some alchemy ingredients, and a copy of the [Conjuration](Skyrim_Conjuration.md) skill book *[Liminal Bridges](Skyrim_Liminal_Bridges.md)*. Two hostile [skeletons](Skyrim_Skeleton.md) and a [bone wolf](Skyrim_Bone_Wolf.md) (depending on what Creations you have installed) inhabit these ruins, while a unique third skeleton wearing a [Black Mage Hood](Skyrim_Black_Mage_Hood.md) stands by three mysterious graves nearby. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=-28563&y=-18361&zoom=17&legacy=true)

### Puzzling Pillar Ruins
[![SR-place-Puzzling Pillars.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f4/SR-place-Puzzling_Pillars.jpg/200px-SR-place-Puzzling_Pillars.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Puzzling_Pillars.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Puzzling_Pillars.jpg) - POITundra14

Southeast of [Sleeping Tree Camp](Skyrim_Sleeping_Tree_Camp.md) are some ancient Nordic ruins which are all that remains of an incomplete location referred to in the Creation Kit as "Barleydark Farm". These ruins consist of some monumental stones and [rotatable pillars](Skyrim_Spinning_Symbol.md) displaying snake, whale and eagle images. After rotating to the correct symbols (the monumental stones will help you solve the puzzle), activating the switch at the center of the ruin will open a nearby grate. At the bottom of a small spiral staircase is a skeleton lying next to a chest which contains a leveled armor piece and a copy of the [Destruction](Skyrim_Destruction.md) skill book *[A Hypothetical Treachery](Skyrim_A_Hypothetical_Treachery.md)*. If you get the combination wrong and pull the lever, you will have to wait for 30 in-game days for the location to reset and try it again. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=-31712&y=-23920&zoom=17&legacy=true)

### Ruined Toll and Wispmother's Well
[![SR-place-Ruined Toll.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/11/SR-place-Ruined_Toll.jpg/200px-SR-place-Ruined_Toll.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Ruined_Toll.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Ruined_Toll.jpg) [![SR-place-Ruined Toll 02.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/67/SR-place-Ruined_Toll_02.jpg/200px-SR-place-Ruined_Toll_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Ruined_Toll_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Ruined_Toll_02.jpg) - POIFall Forest25

Northeast of [Pinepeak Cavern](Skyrim_Pinepeak_Cavern.md), across the river and up a steep winding path, a small abandoned well can be found. Upon approaching it for the first time, a [wispmother](Skyrim_Wispmother.md) will rise up out of the well and attack you, along with her three attendant [wisps](Skyrim_Wisp.md). A nearby broken section of wall hides a basket with a copy of the Conjuration skill book *[The Warrior's Charge](Skyrim_The_Warrior%27s_Charge.md)* resting atop it. Hidden underneath the basket is a locked strongbox. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=83896&y=-44446&zoom=17&legacy=true)

### Smuggler's Den: Whiterun
[![SR-place-Smuggler's Den Whiterun.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/75/SR-place-Smuggler%27s_Den_Whiterun.jpg/200px-SR-place-Smuggler%27s_Den_Whiterun.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Smuggler%27s_Den_Whiterun.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Smuggler%27s_Den_Whiterun.jpg) - POITundra01

West of [Dragonsreach](Skyrim_Dragonsreach.md) there is a smuggler's den under a cliff. A [horse](Skyrim_Horse.md) with a carriage is located here along with a chest, potions, [skooma](Skyrim_Skooma.md), and a copy of the [Pickpocket](Skyrim_Pickpocket.md) skill book *[Aevar Stone-Singer](Skyrim_Aevar_Stone-Singer.md)*. Attempting to open the chest will trigger the appearance of more bandits. You will be brought here during the quest [The Staff of Hasedoki](Skyrim_The_Staff_of_Hasedoki.md), as part of the [Staff of Hasedoki](Skyrim_Staff_of_Hasedoki.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md). [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=22032&y=3456&zoom=17&legacy=true)

### The Lady of the Lake
[![SR-place-Lady of the Lake.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d7/SR-place-Lady_of_the_Lake.jpg/200px-SR-place-Lady_of_the_Lake.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Lady_of_the_Lake.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Lady_of_the_Lake.jpg) - POITundra05

Southeast of [Silent Moons Camp](Skyrim_Silent_Moons_Camp.md) and northeast of [Bleakwind Basin](Skyrim_Bleakwind_Basin.md), protruding from a small pool, is a skeletal arm clutching a leveled sword. This is a reference to [The Lady of the Lake](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lady_of_the_Lake) from Arthurian folklore. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=6640&y=5424&zoom=17&legacy=true)

### The Skeleton's Strong Box: Greymoor
[![SR-place-Bloodied Box Sleeping Tree Camp.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/96/SR-place-Bloodied_Box_Sleeping_Tree_Camp.jpg/200px-SR-place-Bloodied_Box_Sleeping_Tree_Camp.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Bloodied_Box_Sleeping_Tree_Camp.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Bloodied_Box_Sleeping_Tree_Camp.jpg) - POITundra36

### Whiterun's NE Stone Pillar
Located between [Battle-Born Farm](Skyrim_Battle-Born_Farm.md) and [Whitewatch Tower](Skyrim_Whitewatch_Tower.md), NE of Whiterun (city), there is a slope which leads to a stone pillar, locked chest and a gold ore vein. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=31189&y=2975&zoom=8&legacy=true)

### White River Cape's chest
Located between [Battle-Born Farm](Skyrim_Battle-Born_Farm.md) and [Graywinter Watch](Skyrim_Graywinter_Watch.md), there is a small cape on White River with some deadwood and novice-locked chest amid the deadwood. A sabre cat may or may not spawn there also. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=43084&y=-3383&zoom=8&legacy=true)
