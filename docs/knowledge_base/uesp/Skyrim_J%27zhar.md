[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d6/SR-npc-J%27zhar.jpg/200px-SR-npc-J%27zhar.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-J%27zhar.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-J%27zhar.jpg) J'zhar **J'zhar** is a dead [Khajiit](Skyrim_Khajiit.md) [witchblade](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Witchblade) found in the [Alftand Glacial Ruins](Skyrim_Alftand_Glacial_Ruins.md) near [Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold.md). His brother [J'darr](Skyrim_J%27darr.md) is nearby and still alive. Reading his [journal](Skyrim_J%27zhar%27s_Journal.md), it appears that J'darr is most likely to blame; however, some *[Research Notes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Research_Notes)* found in the previous room reveals that the Khajiit brothers were attacked by [Dwarven automata](Skyrim_Dwarven_Automatons.md). After entering Alftand Glacial Ruins, a delusional J'darr can be heard speaking to his deceased brother, looking for the skooma that he believes J'zhar had hidden, unable to find any hidden skooma, his behavior becomes frenzied, convinced that the dead J'zhar is in fact trying to steal his skooma.

J'zhar wears a randomly determined set of farm [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md) (7 variants) or a [belted tunic](Skyrim_Belted_Tunic.md), [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md) (4 variants), and has a 50% chance of wearing a [hat](Skyrim_Hat.md) (3 variants). There's a small possibility of him carrying a small amount of gold and a [lockpick](Skyrim_Lockpick.md).

## Notes
- J'zhar was likely created with [J'zargo](Skyrim_J%27zargo.md) as a base, as he still retains some of the latter's factions. This also results in a number of oddities, as seen below.

## Bugs
- [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) If you have an old version of the [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch) installed J'zhar will incorrectly trigger [Rejoining the College](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Rejoining_the_College) if raised and you kill him. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Legendary_Edition_Patch), version 3.0.6, fixes this bug. ([details](https://afktrack.afkmods.com/index.php?a=issues&i=20995))
- Due to the fact that he's part of the [Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold_(region).md) crime faction, should you resurrect and re-kill him, then you could theoretically receive a bounty in that hold. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Legendary_Edition_Patch), version 3.0.6, fixes this bug.
