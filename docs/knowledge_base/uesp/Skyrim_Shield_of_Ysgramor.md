[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8f/SR-item-Shield_of_Ysgramor.jpg/200px-SR-item-Shield_of_Ysgramor.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Shield_of_Ysgramor.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Shield_of_Ysgramor.jpg) The Shield of Ysgramor The Shield of Ysgramor is an artifact found in [Ysgramor's Tomb](Skyrim_Ysgramor%27s_Tomb.md), both being named in honor of [Ysgramor](Skyrim_Ysgramor.md). Besides its uses as a shield, the artifact also confers its enchantment while equipped. Its enchantment grants the user 20 point [Resist Magic](Skyrim_Resist_Magic.md) and [Fortify Health](Skyrim_Fortify_Health.md) effects.

## Related Quests
- **[Glory of the Dead](Skyrim_Glory_of_the_Dead.md)**: Cure [Kodlak](Skyrim_Kodlak_Whitemane.md)'s spirit and become Harbinger of the [Companions](Skyrim_Companions.md).

## Notes
- [Myrwatch](Skyrim_Myrwatch_(place).md)'s gallery<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Myrwatch.md)</sup> contains a special holder for this item that displays it alongside [Wuuthrad](Skyrim_Wuuthrad.md).
- Blocking while having this shield equipped in the first person view can blot out over a third of the screen, potentially making combat difficult, as you can't quite see what the enemy is doing.
- This shield can only be obtained by finishing the Companions' questline during [Glory of the Dead](Skyrim_Glory_of_the_Dead.md), whereupon it will be in the boss chest near the end of the tomb, before the hallways leading back up to the entrance. If you enter the ruin before the quest (or even being unaffiliated with the Companions at all) by entering the room with Ysgramor's statue and using a wooden plate to glitch through the sealed doorway in front of you, the shield will not be in the chest by the time you fight your way to the end of the tomb.

## Bugs
† This item has no tempering options.

- The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch) addresses this issue. Patch sets tempering ingredient to [Ebony Ingot](Skyrim_Ebony_Ingot.md) and smithing perk to [Ebony Smithing](Skyrim_Ebony_Smithing.md).
