## Falkreath Stormcloak Camp
It is a typical Stormcloak camp, consisting of a central campfire with a [charred skeever](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Charred_Skeever_Hide) cooking over it, around which the rest of the camp is arranged. Around the campfire are three small tents containing unowned hay piles. Northeast of the campfire is the command tent, containing a lit brazier, an end table, and a large table covered with a map of Skyrim showing the progress of the civil war and the various forts and cities held by each side. This is where the commander spends most of their time. To the southeast are two picketed [horses](Skyrim_Horses.md). They are owned, and taking them is considered stealing. South of the campfire is a [wood chopping block](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wood_Chopping_Block), while to the north is the hospital tent, which contains four hay piles occupied by wounded soldiers. Outside the hospital tent is a low table holding a random [potion of health](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Health) and a random [potion of healing](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Healing), next to another table with a built-in [alchemy lab](Skyrim_Alchemy_Labs.md). Northeast of the hospital tent is the commander's tent, containing an owned bedroll, an unlocked [chest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chest), and an end table. Between the hospital tent and the campfire is the workstation for the [Stormcloak quartermaster](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Stormcloak_Quartermaster), consisting of an [anvil](Skyrim_Anvil.md), a [grindstone](Skyrim_Grindstone.md), and a [workbench](Skyrim_Workbench.md). A long wooden table holds various [iron](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Weapons) and [steel weapons](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Weapons) and [armor](Skyrim_Armor.md). To the left of the workbench are some barrels.

Any time [Falkreath Hold](Skyrim_Falkreath_Hold.md) is controlled by the Stormcloaks, this camp will not be present. As long as you haven't completed [Joining the Legion](Skyrim_Joining_the_Legion.md), you are free to sleep in the small tents and trade with the quartermaster.

## Notes
- When the camp is enabled for the first time it permanently disables an unmarked place of a [Khajiit Caravan Massacre](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Unmarked_Places_Falkreath#Khajiit_Caravan_Massacre) south-southwest with a skill book [The Buying Game](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Buying_Game).
- East of the camp is a den guarding by two wolfs with human and animal skeletal remains and a coin purse among them, when Falkreath Stormcloak Camp is enabled for the first time it permanently disables a Nord's corpse carrying a skill book [Hallgerd's Tale](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hallgerd%27s_Tale). Behind the cave, up the hill farther east, is a [silver ore vein](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Silver_Ore_Vein).
- This wiki's current Skyrim satellite map only displays the camp's location; the previous one displayed the camp as well.
