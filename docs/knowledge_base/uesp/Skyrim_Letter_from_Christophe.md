Letter from Christophe **by Christophe Bartlet**
---
Dearest [Maven](Skyrim_Maven_Black-Briar.md),

After our disagreement a fortnight ago, I feared that business had finally gotten the best of our friendship. Since I still have yet to receive a reply to my last letter, it seems my suspicions are correct. I realize my mistake has cost you a lot and all I hope to do is make amends. I'll be staying at the [Bannered Mare](Skyrim_The_Bannered_Mare.md) in [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md) should you wish to speak with me.

Regards,

Christophe Bartlet
