# Kollopi

This article is about the rodent. For the mollusk, see [Kollop](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Kollop).

The Kollopi are arboreal [rodents](Lore_Mammal.md) that live in the province of [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md), mainly in the [Graht-oak](Lore_Graht-oak.md) trees such as [Elden Root](Lore_Elden_Root.md) and [Falinesti](Lore_Falinesti.md). They feed off the tender, albeit slim branches of the graht-oak, which is why they are harvested by the local [Imga](Lore_Imga.md). They are experts in collecting these creatures from the ends of the canopy, using their talented dexterity.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TRKR-1)</sup>

The Kollopi is considered a simple peasant dish, served as delectable and savory balls of flesh marinated in spices and juices.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TRKR-1)</sup>

They share a name with the Shimmering Kollopi Tree, small willows found in the [Wilderking Court](Lore_Wilderking_Court.md), in southeast [Greenshade](Lore_Greenshade.md). The tree's essence can be used as camouflage that coats the wielder in forest moths. The seeds can be planted and turn into vengeful [lurchers](Lore_Lurcher.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOFrighten-2)</sup> Unlike the dish, the roots have an unpleasant taste but can absorb various odors and is an ideal mouth freshener.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESOContraband-3)</sup>
