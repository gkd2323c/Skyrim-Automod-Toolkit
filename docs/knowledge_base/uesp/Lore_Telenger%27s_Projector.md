# Telenger's Projector

Telenger's Projector (also called **the Projector**, real name unknown) is a powerful magical device created by [Telenger the Artificer](Lore_Telenger_the_Artificer.md) for the purposes of [teleportation](Lore_Teleportation.md). When used in conjunction with the [Hyperagonal Locational Determinator](Lore_Hyperagonal_Locational_Determinator.md), it allows its wielder to teleport to virtually any location they desired, including places protected by powerful magical defenses such as [Artaeum](Lore_Artaeum.md) and the [Clockwork City](Lore_Clockwork_City.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RDJ-1)</sup>

## History
In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), Telenger became involved in uncovering a conspiracy against Proxy Queen [Alwinarwe](Lore_Alwinarwe.md), orchestrated by [Rinwaray](Lore_Rinwaray.md). While speaking with [Razum-dar](Lore_Razum-dar.md), leader of the [Eyes of the Queen](Lore_Eyes_of_the_Queen.md), in [Alinor](Lore_Alinor.md), Telenger lamented that despite considering himself the greatest living expert in teleportation magic, he had been unable to obtain the prestigious position of [Sapiarch](Lore_College_of_Sapiarchs.md) of Transliminal Travel, which was still held by [Camilonwe of Alinor](Lore_Camilonwe_of_Alinor.md). Determined to prove his superiority, Telenger devised an ambitious magical experiment. After a month of work, he constructed a device called the Hyperagonal Locational Determinator, capable of locating any place in the [Gray Maybe](Lore_Gray_Maybe.md), even those concealed by powerful wards. By using this device alongside the Projector, whose exact date of creation is unclear, Telenger succeeded in transporting himself to the vanished island of Artaeum. There he infiltrated [Sotha Sil](Lore_Sotha_Sil.md)'s chambers in [Ceporah Tower](Lore_Ceporah_Tower.md), retrieving research notes belonging to the Clockwork God before the [Psijic Order](Lore_Psijic_Order.md) could discover his intrusion.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RDJ-1)</sup>

Because this feat involved breaking numerous laws, Telenger could not publicly reveal what he had done. Instead, he confided in an advisor named Penewen. At her suggestion, he used the same devices to send her to the Clockwork City, so that she could later testify to his magical prowess. Penewen eventually returned and praised his abilities, but soon afterward departed from his life. Some time later, Telenger used the same device to transport Razum-dar to the Clockwork City so that he could investigate the matter further. Razum-dar's investigation took him through several locations both within the Clockwork City and beyond it, including the Shadow Cleft of the [Evergloam](Lore_Evergloam.md). Eventually he returned to Alinor, arriving at Telenger's workshop, where he discovered Rinwaray posing as Penewen attempting to steal the Projector. She escaped by using the device itself. After spinning one of its dials and activating the knob, she turned the Projector on Razum-dar and teleported him to Artaeum, the same location Telenger had previously infiltrated using the device. Following a series of events involving the [Sload](Lore_Sload.md), the Psijics sent Razum-dar back to Alinor, where he found Telenger's workshop ransacked and the Projector missing. Razum-dar eventually located Telenger and freed him from imprisonment. Using another of Telenger's inventions, the Clairvoyeur, he was able to track Penewen's location. He ultimately foiled her plan before she could use [Seht's Affect Inducer](Lore_Seht%27s_Affect_Inducer.md), which she had obtained during her mission, to magically manipulate the Proxy Queen and seize power for herself.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RDJ-1)</sup>

## Notes
- The terminology used to describe the Projector, originates from Razum-dar, who often gave his own names to items rather than using those assigned by their creator, Telenger. For instance, he referred to another of Teleger's creations, Hyperagonal Locational Determinator simply as "the Finder." He also referred to the Projector as "awkwardly named device".<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RDJ-1)</sup>
