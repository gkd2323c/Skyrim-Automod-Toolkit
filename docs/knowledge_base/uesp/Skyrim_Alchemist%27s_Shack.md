## Walkthrough
The shack is a dilapidated single-story building with its doors and windows missing. It can be seen from the road between [Helgen](Skyrim_Helgen.md) and [Ivarstead](Skyrim_Ivarstead.md). There is a wattle-fenced garden to the rear, with an [alchemy lab](Skyrim_Alchemy_Labs.md) to the right of the rear doorway. A barrel containing random ingredients is between the shack and the lab, with two samples of [hawk feathers](Skyrim_Hawk_Feathers.md) and a [red mountain flower](Skyrim_Red_Mountain_Flower.md) on top. A [blue mountain flower](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Blue_Mountain_Flower), three [deathbell](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Deathbell), three [jazbay grapes](Skyrim_Jazbay_Grapes.md), a [nightshade](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nightshade), two [scaly pholiota](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scaly_Pholiota), and two [spiky grass](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Grass_Pod) grow in the garden, and a [birds' nest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Rock_Warbler_Egg) is in front to the west. This is a good area to find [blue butterflies](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Blue_Butterfly) and [butterflies](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Butterfly) during the day and [luna moths](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Luna_Moth) at night.

With the [Pets of Skyrim](Skyrim_Pets_of_Skyrim.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creations.md) installed, the rabbit [Thistle](Skyrim_Thistle_(creature).md) can be found in the garden and can be tamed with carrots.

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a4/SR-interior-Alchemist%27s_Shack.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Alchemist%27s_Shack.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Alchemist%27s_Shack.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Alchemist%27s_Shack.jpg) The shack's interior As you enter through the front doorway you will find two shelving units; the one on your right contains a [Potion of Health](Skyrim_Potions.md#Potion_of_Health), two books, and some clutter. The shelving unit in front of you contains a [gourd](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Gourd), a sample of [glow dust](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Glow_Dust), two [carrots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Carrot), three [red apples](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Apple), and a [butterfly in a jar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Butterfly_in_a_Jar). The jar can only be found in this shack and respawns with the rest of the shack. Between the two shelving units is a barrel containing [uncommon ingredients](Skyrim_Ingredients.md) and a basket containing two [leeks](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leek). To your left as you enter is a bed (unowned). Next to this is an end table holding an [alchemist's journal](Skyrim_Alchemist%27s_Journal_(shack).md) and an [apothecary's satchel](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Apothecary%27s_Satchel) containing [common ingredients](Skyrim_Ingredients.md). This end table does not respawn, and can be useful as a storage option. To the right of the rear exit is a bucket containing a sample of [blisterwort](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Blisterwort) and hanging above are a [garlic braid](Skyrim_Garlic.md) and a sample of [frost mirriam](Skyrim_Frost_Mirriam.md).

## Notes
- The items will respawn over time, except for the end table beside the bed.
- Two [wolves](Skyrim_Wolf.md) often spawn on the road shortly before the shack, as well as some [goats](Skyrim_Goat.md) further up the road from Helgen, who often attract the attention of the wolves. Up the mountain to the left of the shack is a trail which is full of [snowberries](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Snowberry) and an Iron Ore vein. Be careful up the mountain pass, as a [Snow Bear](Skyrim_Bear.md) makes its home there, regardless of your character level. This is the only place a Snow Bear will always spawn.
