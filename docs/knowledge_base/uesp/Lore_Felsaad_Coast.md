[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5f/SR-place-Felsaad_Coast.jpg/200px-SR-place-Felsaad_Coast.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Felsaad_Coast.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Felsaad_Coast.jpg) Felsaad Coast The Felsaad Coast is the snowy, lightly forested northeastern region on the island of [Solstheim](Lore_Solstheim.md). The [Isild River](Lore_Isild_River.md) separates it from the [Moesring Mountains](Lore_Moesring_Mountains.md) region to the west, and it fades into forest to the south. The area is relatively rich in wildlife; many [horkers](Lore_Horker.md), [bristlebacks](Lore_Bristleback.md), [snow wolves](Lore_Snow_Wolf.md), and other creatures roam the land. [Skaal Village](Lore_Skaal_Village.md) and [Thirsk](Lore_Thirsk.md) are both located here, as are many barrows and ice caves.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Bloodmoon-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Dragonborn-2)</sup>

Before the [Red Year](Lore_Red_Year.md) of [4E 5](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_5),<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-HORR-3)</sup> the Felsaad Coast was bordered by the [Isinfier Plains](Lore_Isinfier_Plains.md) to the southwest and [Hirstaang Forest](Lore_Hirstaang_Forest.md) to the south. The area was prone to blizzards, and like the rest of Solstheim, it was home to pine trees as well as shrubs like [belladonna](Lore_Belladonna.md) and [holly](Lore_Holly_Bush.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Bloodmoon-1)</sup>

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/7d/BM-place-Felsaad_Coast.jpg/200px-BM-place-Felsaad_Coast.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:BM-place-Felsaad_Coast.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:BM-place-Felsaad_Coast.jpg) The Felsaad Coast circa [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427) The cataclysms of the Red Year substantially changed the landscape of Solstheim.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-HORR-3)</sup> The Felsaad Coast was evidently shattered, as numerous large islands sprung up along the coast and the size of the region shrunk significantly. The coastline itself is substantially different, as a bay has formed to the north, and a peninsula now sticks out from the northeastern tip of the island. What remains of the Felsaad Coast is still largely reminiscent of its pre-Red Year state in terms of its climate, wildlife, and populace, though the regions bordering it have drastically changed.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Dragonborn-2)</sup>
