[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/90/SR-place-Frostflow_Lighthouse.jpg/200px-SR-place-Frostflow_Lighthouse.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Frostflow_Lighthouse.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Frostflow_Lighthouse.jpg) Frostflow Lighthouse **Frostflow Lighthouse** is a lighthouse west-southwest of [Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold.md) containing [Falmer](Skyrim_Falmer.md), [chaurus](Skyrim_Chaurus.md), and [frostbite spiders](Skyrim_Frostbite_Spider.md). It contains two zones: *[Frostflow Lighthouse](#Frostflow_Lighthouse)* and *[Frostflow Abyss](#Frostflow_Abyss)*.

A [Redguard](Lore_Redguard.md) family recently bought the lighthouse. The purchase was a long-held dream for [Habd](Skyrim_Habd.md) and his wife [Ramati](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ramati); however, their children [Sudi](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sudi) and [Mani](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mani) weren't as keen on the move.

## Related Quests
- **[Frostflow Abyss](Skyrim_Frostflow_Abyss.md)**: Uncover the tragic events inside Frostflow Lighthouse.

## Walkthrough

### Exterior
It is on a headland overlooking the coastline between [Dawnstar](Skyrim_Dawnstar.md) and [Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold.md). It is southeast of an [Imperial camp](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Winterhold_Imperial_Camp) and southwest of [Hob's Fall Cave](Skyrim_Hob%27s_Fall_Cave.md). As you approach the lighthouse, you will encounter a dead [horse](Skyrim_Horse.md) lying on the path near the entrance, while in front of the entrance is a cart holding a [weak poison](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Weak_Poison) and a [lavender](Skyrim_Lavender.md) branch with two [bear traps](Skyrim_Bear_Trap.md) concealed underneath it. There are also a couple [snowberry](Skyrim_Snowberries.md) bushes around the building, a [blue mountain flower](Skyrim_Blue_Mountain_Flower.md) bush next to one of the snowberry bushes, a covered well near a single-stalled stable, and a small vegetable patch containing six [leeks](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leek) and four [potato](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potato) plants. There is a small novice-locked shed on the side of the lighthouse with a [bucket](Skyrim_Bucket.md) next to it. The shed itself contains a [pickaxe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe), a [broom](Skyrim_Broom.md), and a [pitchfork](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pitchfork) on the left and a set of shelves on the right holding a [potion of minor stamina](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Stamina), two [leather strips](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Strips), a [basket](Skyrim_Basket.md), a [potion of minor healing](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Healing), a [potion of strength](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Strength), a quiver of [iron arrows](Skyrim_Iron_Arrow.md) on the bottom, and a medium [coin purse](Skyrim_Coin_Purse.md) on top. There is also a [corundum ore vein](Skyrim_Corundum_Ore_Vein.md) just northeast of the vegetable garden.

### Frostflow Lighthouse
As soon as you enter the lighthouse, you will see that something is very wrong. There are blood splatters on the floor, and the circular room in front of you has been turned upside-down, with a human corpse and the body of a dead [chaurus](Skyrim_Chaurus.md). On either side of you are wooden doors; one is expert-locked, while the other is master-locked. You can hear another chaurus moving around nearby. As you approach the body of [Ramati](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ramati), a [miscellaneous quest](Skyrim_Frostflow_Abyss.md) will initiate directing you to find the source of the murders. Ramati is carrying [Habd's journal](Skyrim_Habd%27s_Journal.md) and has a [Falmer war axe](Skyrim_Falmer_War_Axe.md) sticking out of her. There are six bottles of [Nord mead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead) on the floor next to a chair. Against the wall to the northwest is a large fireplace with a [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking_Pot.md) in front and the body of a chaurus beside it with a [woodcutter's axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Woodcutter%27s_Axe) sticking out of it. There is a bottle of Nord mead on the floor, while on the mantlepiece is a [torchbug in a jar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Torchbug_in_a_Jar), a [burial urn](Skyrim_Burial_Urn.md), and a [steel dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Dagger). Inside the urn is [the key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mani%27s_Cellar_Key), which opens the master-locked cellar door. If you read the family's journals (see notes), they will lead you to this key.

In the middle of the room near Ramati's body is a broken table and three overturned chairs with three [baked potatoes](Skyrim_Baked_Potatoes.md) and three [salmon steaks](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Steak) now on the floor. Against the east wall is a sideboard with a basket containing [potatoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potato) underneath and a salmon steak, two [salt piles](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Salt_Pile), and three fillets of [salmon meat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Meat) on top. Above the sideboard, hanging from the ceiling are two racks holding a [rabbit](Skyrim_Rabbit_(dead).md), three [salmon](Skyrim_Salmon_(dead).md), a bunch of [dried elves ear](Skyrim_Dried_Elves_Ear.md), and a [garlic braid](Skyrim_Garlic_Braid.md). To the right is a wooden table with three [gold](Skyrim_Gold.md) coins on top. Against the wall to the southwest is a cupboard holding clutter with a bottle of Nord mead on top.

There are two open doors leading to two bedrooms from the circular room. The bedroom on the left contains two unowned single beds, one in the western corner that appears to have been Sudi's, and the other in the eastern corner that must have been Mani's. Between these are two sets of shelves back-to-back. Under Sudi's bed is a medium coin purse and a copy of *[Immortal Blood](Skyrim_Immortal_Blood.md)*. On the shelves facing the bed are ten [purple mountain flowers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Purple_Mountain_Flower), a set of [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md), two pairs of [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md), a small coin purse, a [silver necklace](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Silver_Necklace), an [amethyst](Skyrim_Amethyst.md), a copy of *[The Legend of Red Eagle](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Legend_of_Red_Eagle)* and another book. Beside the shelves is a small square wooden table with [Sudi's journal](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sudi%27s_Journal) on top. Under Mani's bed is a [knapsack](Skyrim_Knapsack.md) and a bottle of Nord mead. Inside the knapsack are a few [potions of minor healing](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Healing), a [letter](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mani%27s_Letter) to his sister explaining that he is leaving as soon as father returns, a random [ring](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ring), some raw food, a set of [Hammerfell garb](Skyrim_Hammerfell_Garb.md), and a common book. On his set of shelves is a set of common clothes, a pair of boots, an [iron war axe](Skyrim_Iron_War_Axe.md), and a [troll skull](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Troll_Skull). There is also a dresser against the southeast wall containing more clothes.

The bedroom on the right contains an unowned double bed coming out from the northeast wall, with an end table on either side. The one on the left has [Ramati's journal](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ramati%27s_Journal) on top, while the one on the right has two common books on top. Behind the door, against the southeast wall is a dresser with a copy of the [Restoration](Skyrim_Restoration.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[Mystery of Talara, v 2](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mystery_of_Talara,_v_2)* along with a common book on top. Against the northwest wall is a table with two common books and seven gold coins on top, and beside this is a cupboard holding some clothes, a [potion of healing](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Healing), two [potions of resist cold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Resist_Cold), and a bottle of [Honningbrew mead](Skyrim_Honningbrew_Mead.md). To the right, against the northeast wall is a small set of shelves holding various clothes, [hats](Skyrim_Hat.md), and boots.

The expert-locked door to the northwest opens to reveal a flight of stairs leading up. Halfway up, a small coin purse sits on the small shelf by the wall. At the top, there are several bales of straw, a large pile of firewood, and a ladder leading to the lighthouse's exterior and brazier. Outside, there is a large locked chest that cannot be picked (it requires a key) and another ladder leading up to the side of the brazier to allow it to be lit. Back inside, the master-locked door to the east opens at the top of stairs leading down to the cellar. There are two bear traps on the stairs, the latter halfway down beside a pile of food sacks. At the bottom there are plenty more blood splatters on the floor and a large pile of firewood with a woodcutter's axe beside it. Two chaurus are wandering around the cellar, and by a stone pillar are two fish barrels with an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) on one of them and a pole suspended between this and the next pillar holding four whole salmon. Against the outer wall to the southwest is a pile of crates and a couple barrels, with a [potion of minor healing](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Healing) and an iron dagger under a basket on a crate and a [pickaxe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe) on the ground beside them. On the far side of the second pillar is an adept-locked chest containing minor loot. Continuing around the cellar's outer wall, to the south is a [tanning rack](Skyrim_Tanning_Rack.md), and to the left is a set of shelves holding a [bear pelt](Skyrim_Bear_Pelt.md) on top, some clothes, and a sack of food. Farther left, past a food barrel is a second set of shelves holding more clothes, more food sacks, and a [potion of minor magicka](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Magicka). To the left of this is a pile of crates, and left again are a couple knocked-over cupboards with a hole where the Falmer broke through. To the left of the hole, against the internal wall are a pile of straw bales, some crates with two [lockpicks](Skyrim_Lockpick.md) and a food sack on top, and two more suspended poles holding a total of eight whole salmon.

Exiting the cellar through the hole dug by the Falmer, there is a [Falmer war axe](Skyrim_Falmer_War_Axe.md) on the ground to the right. After a few paces, you proceed to *Frostflow Abyss*.

### Frostflow Abyss
The abyss contains Falmer, chaurus, and frostbite spiders, and if you have *[Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)* installed, you will also have to deal with a few leveled [chaurus hunters](Skyrim_Chaurus_Hunter.md) that are hidden in [cocoons](Skyrim_Chaurus_Cocoon.md). The abyss has high numbers of both [chaurus egg sacs](Skyrim_Chaurus_Eggs.md) and [glowing mushrooms](Skyrim_Glowing_Mushroom.md), totaling up to 516 egg sacs and 93 mushrooms that can be harvested. You continue descending an icy tunnel. At the bottom, blood splatters are on the ground, and the passage opens into a cavern with two Falmer tents opposite, with a leveled Falmer magic-user in the one on the right. To either side of the tunnel you are exiting are stacks of crates, an empty chest, and barrels. On a crate to the left are five gold coins with a random [potion of stamina](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Stamina) beside it. Between the stacks is a bear pelt on the ground, while off to the right is a chaurus near a gated exit, a [Falmer sword](Skyrim_Falmer_Sword.md) on the ground, and a knapsack in the corner. Around the corner to the left is a third Falmer tent, which contains a random [potion of magicka](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Magicka). The next tent is empty, while between the first two tents is the body of [Mani](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mani) pierced by a Falmer sword and lying on a flat-topped rock with fires by his head and feet. The third tent has a small fire in front and only contains the Falmer magic-user. The exit is through the gate to the right.

Beyond the gate, the passage climbs and turns to the northeast at the top, with nine patches of [white cap](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:White_Cap) fungus at the corner. It then opens into a cave with a Falmer and a chaurus. In this area, there are several flat-topped rocks with various items on top. On the first rock on the left are one random [poison](Skyrim_Poison.md), two loose chaurus eggs, a bowl of [troll fat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Troll_Fat), a sample of [ice wraith teeth](Skyrim_Ice_Wraith_Teeth.md), a sample of glowing mushroom, a [nightshade](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nightshade) flower, and two random potions of healing. On a rock by the east wall are two Falmer war axes, a [Falmer shield](Skyrim_Falmer_Shield.md), a [Falmer bow](Skyrim_Falmer_Bow.md), and two pieces of [chaurus chitin](Skyrim_Chaurus_Chitin.md). On a rock beside the wall to the southeast is a dead chaurus and a piece of chaurus chitin. To the southwest is a passage marked by a lit [torch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Torch) thrust into the snow on the right. Take care, as there is a [tripwire](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tripwire) that triggers a [claw](Skyrim_Claw.md) trap from above.

At the next corner, where a sample of glowing mushroom is on the ground, the passage turns northwest and opens into a cave with a Falmer tent opposite and cobwebs and spider egg sacs to the right. Inside the tent is a leveled Falmer, and two [frostbite spiders](Skyrim_Frostbite_Spider.md) are off to the right. The passage exits this cave to the southwest, in front of the tent. The next turn is to the southwest, where there is a two-tiered cavern ahead containing two leveled Falmer and a leveled chaurus. One of the Falmer is standing at the front of the ledge above, while the other is farther back out of sight, and the chaurus is on the ground level behind the pillar in front. There is a Falmer tent on the left with a couple loose chaurus eggs on the ground in front and a novice-locked Falmer chest and two loose [skeever tails](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Skeever_Tail) inside. Opposite the tent against the west wall is a pen with a closed gate, behind which is the body of [Sudi](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sudi) with a [scrawled page](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scrawled_page) and a [bloodstained note](Skyrim_Bloodstained_note.md) on the ground beside her along with a lit torch. It is implied by the note that she used her [dagger](Skyrim_Dagger.md) to kill herself rather than let herself be eaten alive.

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/1f/SR-interior-Frostflow_Abyss.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Frostflow_Abyss.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Frostflow_Abyss.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Frostflow_Abyss.jpg) Chaurus lair Under the ledge is a pillar with a chaurus hidden behind it. On the right is a caved-in section with gaps you can look through into a cavern farther ahead. The passage continues to the southeast. As it climbs, you pass through another mushroom farm with several patches of white cap fungus. The passage brings you out on the ledge above, where there are three Falmer tents. The first tent on the right contains an apprentice-locked Falmer chest. The second tent is empty, and has several more white cap fungus patches and a Falmer war axe and a poison by a rock to the left. The third tent is to the left, and contains four loose chaurus eggs and a Falmer sword and shield. To the west is a ledge you will need to drop down to continue. Your [follower](Skyrim_Followers.md) (if you have one with you) may not jump down, but instead wait for you on the higher level. To prevent this, you can nudge them over the edge by running into them or using the [Unrelenting Force](Skyrim_Unrelenting_Force.md) [dragon shout](Skyrim_Dragon_Shouts.md).

There is another Falmer tent on the right and a leveled chaurus in front. Once you descend you will have no way of returning back the way you came and will have no choice but to continue. The tent contains a Falmer bow, two random potions of healing and two random poisons. This is the area you could see through the cave-in. There is an unlocked chest beside the tent, and the passage continues to the northwest. After a short distance, the passage turns again to the southwest by some more white cap fungus patches, and after a few paces ends at another ledge with two chaurus below you among many chaurus egg sacs. The cavern at the bottom has shallow water covering the floor and a passage leading northwest.

Follow this passage to the next cavern, where two chaurus, at least one of which is leveled, and several chaurus cocoons are among many more chaurus egg sacs. There is a ledge at the rear of the cavern, and the passage exits to the north. At the end is a ledge you have to drop down again into a final huge cavern with three chaurus nearby and several more coccoons around the area. There is a torch and a [quicksilver ore vein](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Quicksilver_Ore_Vein) in this room. To the northeast is the biggest chaurus reaper you will have seen, along with a couple smaller ones. This is the final battle. Inside the reaper are [Habd's remains](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Habd%27s_Remains) and [a key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Habd%27s_Lighthouse_Key) which unlocks the chest found at the top of the lighthouse. There is an earthen ramp climbing up to the left, which then follows the cavern walls and loops around the cavern counterclockwise. Near the top are two more empty Falmer tents and an unlocked boss leveled chest to the right of a final passage heading northeast to a drop that brings you back to the first cavern by the knapsack. Take Habd's remains up to the brazier at the top of the lighthouse to gain the active effect [Sailor's Repose](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sailor%27s_Repose), which makes healing spells stronger by curing 10% more.

## Notes
- There is a [corundum ore vein](Skyrim_Corundum_Ore_Vein.md) northeast of the lighthouse.
- Three journals can be found within the first floor of the lighthouse: - [Habd's journal](Skyrim_Habd%27s_Journal.md) can be found on the corpse of his wife, Ramati.
- [Sudi's journal](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sudi%27s_Journal) can be found on a table in the room to the left.
- [Ramati's journal](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ramati%27s_Journal) can be found on an end table in the room to the right.
- [Mani's letter](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mani%27s_Letter) can be found in the knapsack in the room to the left, mentioning his intention to leave.
- If you have a follower with you, they may give the "approaching a cave" dialogue due to the lighthouse having the keyword "dungeon".
- In [Survival Mode](Skyrim_Survival_Mode.md), this dungeon will not warm you up, unlike most interior locations.

## Bugs
- On approaching the lighthouse, some followers may refer to it as a cave, saying *"Hmm. Caves. Wonder what's below."*
- Followers won't go past chaurus gates in the cave. - Bumping them (running into them to push them in a direction) or the [Unrelenting Force](Skyrim_Unrelenting_Force.md) shout to push them past the gates works. Also, closing and re-opening the gates often gets followers to try again and to go through.
- The quest's Sailor's Repose reward has an incorrect taper duration set, in addition to also having an incorrect magnitude set. Its abilities are applied by perk and the effect entries are merely to make it show up in the UI when you get it. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.3, fixes this bug.
- Some Chaurus Egg Sacs may be difficult or impossible to harvest in Frostflow Abyss. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.8, fixes this bug.
- Due to the fact that Ramati, Mani, and Sudi are part of [the Reach](Skyrim_The_Reach.md) crime faction, should you resurrect and re-kill them, then you could theoretically receive a bounty in that hold. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Legendary_Edition_Patch), version 3.0.6, fixes this bug.
- The quest may break if you pick the lock on the basement door before you've been given the notification that the quest is active. - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch), version 4.3.2, addresses this issue. The door will now require the key in order to proceed so that the quest doesn't completely break.
- Two Falmer may be stuck in their tents in Frostfall Abyss. - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch), version 4.3.7, fixes this bug.
