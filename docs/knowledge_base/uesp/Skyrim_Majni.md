[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/51/SR-npc-Majni.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Majni.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Majni.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Majni.jpg) Majni **Majni** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [werewolf](Skyrim_EncClassWerewolf.md) living under [Frostmoon Crag](Skyrim_Frostmoon_Crag.md). He is the leader of the Frostmoon Pack and [Akar](Skyrim_Akar.md)'s brother. He wears a set of [fur armor](Skyrim_Fur_Armor.md) with the matching [helmet](Skyrim_Fur_Helmet.md), [bracers](Skyrim_Fur_Bracers.md), and [shoes](Skyrim_Fur_Shoes.md). He carries a [dagger](Skyrim_Dagger.md), another [one-handed weapon](Skyrim_One-Handed_Weapons.md), and some gold.

He sells 4 unique werewolf rings; [Ring of Bloodlust](Skyrim_Ring_of_Bloodlust.md), [Ring of Instinct](Skyrim_Ring_of_Instinct.md), [Ring of the Hunt](Skyrim_Ring_of_the_Hunt.md), [Ring of the Moon](Skyrim_Ring_of_the_Moon.md).

## Dialogue
When approached if you are not a werewolf:

- *"You have no business with our pack. Begone."*

If you are a [werewolf](Skyrim_Werewolf.md) he will be more welcoming:

- *"Welcome, sister/brother. It's been too long since we met another who shares our blood. I am Majni, Alpha of the Frostmoon Pack. You are welcome here, so long as you respect our law."*
- **Are you all werewolves?**
- *"Aye. The blood still runs true among some on Solstheim, as it has for centuries. We have chosen to embrace Lord Hircine's blessing. You know it well. The speed and strength of the beast. The thrill of the hunt. The triumph of the kill. This is our purpose, our way of life. So it was for my father, and his father before him. And I welcome any who walk that path."*
- **Are there other werewolves on Solstheim?**
- *"You might find a few others. Strays. Lone hunters. But ours is the last true pack that remains."*
- **Do you have anything to trade?**
- *"Meat. Furs. Bone. Trophies of the hunt. But those are of little use to you. There is one more thing... Since the days of the Great Hunt, our pack has kept a set of rings, blessed by Lord Hircine himself. I sense his favor on you. For you... I will part with them, if you can pay my price."*

When asked to trade:

- *"Here, take a look."*

His greetings:

- *"Well met, sister/brother."*
- *"Greetings, blood-kin."*
- *"How goes your hunt?"*

His goodbyes:

- *"Huntsman guide you."*
- *"Farewell."*
- *"Hunt well, sister/brother."*
