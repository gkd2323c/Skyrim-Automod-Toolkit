Sirollus Saccus was a master [blacksmith](Lore_Smithing.md) for the [Imperial Legion](Lore_Imperial_Legion.md) who is among the longest-lived [men](Lore_Man.md) in recorded history.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MW-1)</sup>

During the reign of Empress [Katariah](Lore_Katariah.md) he was hailed as the greatest smith in the [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md), and was thus chosen to represent the [Elder Council](Lore_Elder_Council.md) in an armorer's challenge between Katariah and the Council.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TAC-2)</sup> The chosen smith of Katariah was an [Argonian](Lore_Argonian.md) named [Hazadir](Lore_Hazadir.md) who was formerly a slave in Morrowind, despite using much more inexpensive material and more mundane methods, Hazadir's equipment was able to lead Katariah's champion [Nandor Beraid](Lore_Nandor_Beraid.md) to victory over the Elder Council's chosen champion Raphalas Eul, who donned Sirollus' marvelous equipment.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TAC-2)</sup> This was because what Hazadir lacked over Sirollus in mastery and assets, he made up for in sheer knowledge over the rugged environment of Black Marsh in which the battle was simulated after.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TAC-2)</sup> Defeated yet determined, Sirollus set off to Morrowind to learn whatever it was that Hazadir had learned there<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TAC-2)</sup> and as of [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427) still remained there at the Ebonheart garrison on Vvardenfell.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MW-1)</sup>
