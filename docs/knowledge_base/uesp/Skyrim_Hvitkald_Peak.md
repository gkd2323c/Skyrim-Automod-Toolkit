[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8f/SR-place-Hvitkald_Peak.jpg/200px-SR-place-Hvitkald_Peak.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Hvitkald_Peak.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Hvitkald_Peak.jpg) Hvitkald Peak **Hvitkald Peak** is one of the highest points on Solstheim, west of the [Abandoned Lodge](Skyrim_Abandoned_Lodge.md). The marker for this peak does not appear on the compass until you have [climbed](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mountain_Climbing) nearly to the top.

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c1/SR-place-Hvitkald_Peak-Top_To_Bloodskal_Barrow.jpg/200px-SR-place-Hvitkald_Peak-Top_To_Bloodskal_Barrow.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Hvitkald_Peak-Top_To_Bloodskal_Barrow.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Hvitkald_Peak-Top_To_Bloodskal_Barrow.jpg) Looking down at Bloodskal Barrow from Hvitkald Peak Of the four marked mountain peaks on Solstheim, this is the easiest to reach. The ascent is best started from the peak's northern side, northwest of the Abandoned Lodge, at the end of the ridge just behind the exit from [Fahlbtharz](Skyrim_Fahlbtharz.md). Simply jump along the ridge in a southerly direction until you reach the marker.

From the top, you can see most of the Solstheim ash wastes. To the south, beyond the end of the ridge, lies the [Wreck of the Strident Squall](Skyrim_Wreck_of_the_Strident_Squall.md) and the [Bulwark](Skyrim_The_Bulwark.md) of [Raven Rock](Skyrim_Raven_Rock.md), while southwest is [Bloodskal Barrow](Skyrim_Bloodskal_Barrow.md). To the north, you can see Fahlbtharz, and behind that, [Mortrag Peak](Skyrim_Mortrag_Peak.md). The Moesring Mountains extend away to the northeast, and on the eastern horizon are the steps to the [Temple of Miraak](Skyrim_Temple_of_Miraak.md). On a hill to the southeast sits [Highpoint Tower](Skyrim_Highpoint_Tower.md), with [Tel Mithryn](Skyrim_Tel_Mithryn_(settlement).md) visible to its left, and [Fort Frostmoth](Skyrim_Fort_Frostmoth.md) on the coast to its right. The Abandoned Lodge can be seen below you, at the foot of the mountain.

## Notes
- [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) The `tmm` [console](Skyrim_Console.md) command won't make the location's marker visible on the map.
- *Hvitkald* derives from the Norwegian words *hvit* meaning *white* and *kald* meaning *cold*, translating to *White Cold*.
- Hvitkald Peak also [appears](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Bloodmoon:Hvitkald_Peak) in *[Bloodmoon](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Bloodmoon:Bloodmoon)*.
