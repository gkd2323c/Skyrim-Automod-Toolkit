Hastily Scribbled Note **by [Gaston Bellefort](Skyrim_Gaston_Bellefort.md)** *About a foolish attempt to infiltrate the Dark Brotherhood Sanctuary*
---

- My scribing tools are lost, and I've no time for a lengthy entry, anyway. It's taken weeks, but I've finally found it! The [Sanctuary of the Dark Brotherhood](Skyrim_Dark_Brotherhood_Sanctuary.md)! One of them, anyway. In [Skyrim](Skyrim_Skyrim.md), under a forest road.

- I've been watching them, the [assassins](Skyrim_Assassin.md). Their comings and goings. The fools have no idea they're being observed. My next goal is to somehow make it past the sinister black door, into the Sanctuary itself.

- I don't have time to even think about the dangers. The truth must be known!
