[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/07/SR-place-Ironback_Hideout.jpg/200px-SR-place-Ironback_Hideout.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Ironback_Hideout.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Ironback_Hideout.jpg) Ironback Hideout **Ironback Hideout** is a dilapidated house to the northwest of [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md), a short distance east of [The Steed Stone](Skyrim_The_Steed_Stone.md). It is the remains of a house without a roof, windows, or doors. Two [bandits](Skyrim_Bandit.md) and a bandit chief have set up camp within the ruined structure.

It can be reached by following the road towards the [Thalmor Embassy](Skyrim_Thalmor_Embassy.md) until you are east of the house and then turning west. Any other route takes a difficult climb over and around cliffs.

The [Elite Crossbows](Skyrim_Elite_Crossbows.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md) adds a trapdoor beneath a tent, leading to **[Ironback Hideout Cellar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ironback_Hideout_Cellar)**. This entrance is initially locked and must be entered as part of the related quest.

## Ironback Hideout
What remains of the house are some of the walls, the fireplace, and floor. There is a broken front porch with a [wood chopping block](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wood_Chopping_Block) at the end, next to a doorway into the shell. Two tents have been erected inside, one of which contains a medium [coin purse](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse). The fireplace is opposite the doorway and is lit, with an empty pot in front of it. With the Elite Crossbows Creation installed, *[Kragrash's Letter](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Kragrash%27s_Letter)* can be found atop the mantel. There is a [bandit](Skyrim_Bandit.md) and a bandit chief sitting on a wooden block and crate pulled up in front of the fire. To the right of the fireplace is a long wooden table with an empty metal pot, a portion of [grilled leeks](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Grilled_Leeks), a [baked potato](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Baked_Potatoes), and a copy of the [Pickpocket](Skyrim_Pickpocket.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[Beggar](Skyrim_Beggar_(book).md)* on the table, and a novice-locked [chest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chest) underneath. To the left of the fireplace is a [tanning rack](Skyrim_Tanning_Rack.md) and a missing section of wall that leads to where one of the bandits is on lookout duty on a ledge and also a [corundum ore vein](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Corundum_Ore_Vein) against the cliff face.

## Notes
- A second corundum ore vein can be found under the house at the base of the cliff.

## Bugs
- It may only become labeled as "cleared" after leaving the area.
- The two regular bandits here are also marked as bosses when only the Bandit Chief should be. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Legendary_Edition_Patch), version 3.0.3, fixes this bug.
- The chest will respawn extremely quickly if you travel away from the area and then travel back. - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch), version 4.1.2, addresses this issue. ([details](https://afktrack.afkmods.com/index.php?a=issues&i=23107)) The chest's encounter zone setting was incorrectly set, which caused it to reset so quickly. The chest's encounter zone has been corrected, and it now properly resets with the rest of the dungeon.
