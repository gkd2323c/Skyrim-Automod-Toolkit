[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/2c/SR-menu-Alternative_Armors_-_Daedric_Mail.jpg/200px-SR-menu-Alternative_Armors_-_Daedric_Mail.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-menu-Alternative_Armors_-_Daedric_Mail.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-menu-Alternative_Armors_-_Daedric_Mail.jpg) Daedric Mail Armor **Alternative Armors - Daedric Mail** is a [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md) that adds an alternative variant of [Daedric armor](Skyrim_Daedric.md) that is based on the [Daedric Mail](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Blades:Daedric_Mail) armor set from *[Blades](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Blades:Blades)*.

## Official Summary
*Includes three pieces of light Daedric armor as featured in The Elder Scrolls: Blades! Quest by Kris Takahashi. (Quest "Missing Merchant" starts by speaking to the innkeeper of Candlehearth Hall in Windhelm.)*
## Overview
This Creation adds the quest [Missing Merchant](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Missing_Merchant), in which you will be tasked with investigating the disappearance of merchant Gunther Frost-Foot, last seen in the area of [Traitor's Post](Skyrim_Traitor%27s_Post.md) east of Windhelm. Gunther has unfortunately been killed by the bandits there, but in killing them you will learn that the Khajiit caravaneer [Ma'dran](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ma%27dran) has acquired a full set of Daedric Mail from the bandits and is offering it for sale.
