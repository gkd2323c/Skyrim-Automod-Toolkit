## Dab

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Fishing#Rare_Fish)

## Dace
The **Dace** is a type of river fish.

- The **Speckled Dace** can be found in the rivers of [Anequina](Lore_Anequina.md).
- The **Toothy Dace** can be found in the rivers of eastern [Solstice](Lore_Solstice_(island).md).

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Fishing#Rare_Fish)

## Daggerfish

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Fishing#Rare_Fish)

## [Dakoi](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Dakoi)
[Dakoi](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Dakoi) is a legendary [fish](Lore_Fish.md) said to surface only during the [New Life](Lore_New_Life_Festival.md) season. It was sought in the waters of [Stormhold](Lore_Stormhold.md), within the [Shadowfen](Lore_Shadowfen.md) region of [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md), as many believed that catching one could ensure a year of good fortune.

**Found in:** - Lore

## Darter
The **Darter** is a type of fish.

- The **Glimmertarn Darter** can be found in the lakes of [Galen](Lore_Galen.md).
- The **Ivory Darter** can be found in the rivers of [Blackwood](Lore_Blackwood.md).
- The **Plasm Darter** can be found in the foul waters of [Coldharbour](Lore_Coldharbour.md).
- The **Shaveskin Darter** can be found in the rivers of [Pellitine](Lore_Pellitine.md).

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Fishing#Rare_Fish)
