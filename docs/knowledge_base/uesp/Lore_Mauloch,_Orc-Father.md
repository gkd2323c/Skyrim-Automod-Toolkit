Mauloch, Orc-Father **by [Ramurbak gro-Abamath](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Ramurbak_gro-Abamath)** *The origins of Mauloch and his laws*
---
Know [Mauloch](Lore_Mauloch.md), spurned of [Boethiah](Lore_Boethiah.md) and King of Curses.

An age ago, a [cult of Elves](Lore_Chimer.md) left the [Summerset Isles](Lore_Summerset_Isles.md), abandoning their kin to follow [Veloth](Lore_Veloth.md), a pathetic tool of Boethiah. [Trinimac](Lore_Trinimac.md) confronted Boethiah for this trespass and was challenged to battle. Trinimac was about to strike a mighty blow when [Mephala](Lore_Mephala.md) appeared and stabbed him in the back. As Trinimac kneeled, wounded by Mephala's treachery, Boethiah gloated and cast a terrible ritual to scar and twist his appearance, then cast him to a place of choking air and ash.

Trinimac, enraged by his failure, was reborn in blood as he sliced open his own chest, tearing the shame from his spirit. Mauloch, the God of Curses, rose from the ash and cursed Boethiah for his malice.

On that day, Mauloch spoke the [Code](Lore_Code_of_Malacath.md) to his faithful, and swore vengeance against those who would break it.

"Do not steal.

Do not kill your kin.

Do not attack without cause.

Those who break these rules must pay the Blood Price."

And thus, the [Orcs](Lore_Orc.md) were born of blood: the Blood of Mauloch.
