[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/49/SR-npc-Durian.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Durian.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Durian.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Durian.jpg) Durian Durian (also called **Dawnguard**) is an [Orc](Skyrim_Orc.md) [Ranger](Skyrim_Ranger_(class).md) who can be found inside [Bloodchill Cavern](Skyrim_Bloodchill_Cavern.md), attending the banquet during [Guests for Dinner](Skyrim_Guests_for_Dinner.md). He's secretly a member of the [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard_(faction).md).

## Related Quests
- **[Guests for Dinner](Skyrim_Guests_for_Dinner.md)**<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Bloodchill_Manor.md)</sup>: Attend a feast.

## Quest-Related Dialogue

### Guests for Dinner

- **Are you here for the dinner party?**
- *"Yep."*

- **Are all the guests present?**
- *"It would seem so."*

After the vampires are killed (Player as a Non-Vampire):

- **The vampires are all dead. And the will says the house belongs to me.**
- *"Very good. But you've created a debt between us. Here. Take this. We are even now."*

He will then hand you the [Bloodchill Manor Key](Skyrim_Bloodchill_Manor_Key.md).

## Notes
- He retains the changed name "Dawnguard" and can be found at [Candlehearth Hall](Skyrim_Candlehearth_Hall.md) in [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md) after the completion of [Guests for Dinner](Skyrim_Guests_for_Dinner.md) as a Non-Vampire.
- Durian will not appear if you complete the quest [Guests for Dinner](Skyrim_Guests_for_Dinner.md) as a [Vampire](Skyrim_Vampirism.md).

## Bugs
- Leaving the cavern before finishing the quest may cause Durian to be a permanent follower that is impossible to get rid of.
