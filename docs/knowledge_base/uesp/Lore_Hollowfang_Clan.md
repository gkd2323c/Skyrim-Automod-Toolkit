# Hollowfang Clan

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/30/ON-npc-Nisaazda.jpg/200px-ON-npc-Nisaazda.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Nisaazda.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Nisaazda.jpg) Clan Mother Nisaazda The Hollowfang Clan was a [Khajiit](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Khajiit) [vampire](Lore_Vampire.md) clan that resided in northern [Elsweyr](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Elsweyr) in the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md). They give thanks to [Sangiin](Lore_Sangiin.md), the blood god.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-NJ-1)</sup> The members of the clan are learned in the arts of blood magic and alchemy.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-AD-2)</sup>

When they struck down the [Dragon](Lore_Dragon.md) Sahrotnax from the sky and he crash-landed in their den at Moongrave Fane,<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-DUP-3)</sup> they held it captive and planned to strengthen themselves by drinking its blood. At this time, a Nord vampire and member of the [Dragonguard](Lore_Dragonguard.md) named [Grundwulf](Lore_Grundwulf.md) was convinced by the clan to carry out this task, and dubbed "The Devourer" by the Clan Mother, Nisaazda. Their endeavors were stopped by the [Undaunted](Lore_Undaunted.md) and [Chevalier Renald](Lore_Chevalier_Renald.md). They killed the Clan Mother and Grundwulf, but not after he drained the dragon of his power.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup>
