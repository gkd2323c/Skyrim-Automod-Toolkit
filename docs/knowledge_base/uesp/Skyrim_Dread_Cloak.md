**Dread Cloak** is a greater power that surrounds the caster in frost, causing [frost damage](Skyrim_Frost_Damage.md) to the health and stamina of any enemies within melee range. It was set up to be available to all stage 3 or 4 vampires, but it is not actually implemented in the game.

## Effects
- [Dread Cloak](Skyrim_Frost_Damage.md), 8 pts in 10 ft for 60 sec

## Notes
- This power is essentially identical to the [Frost Cloak](Skyrim_Frost_Cloak.md) spell, with the exception of its zero casting-cost and once-per-day limitation.
- You can only have one Cloak spell of any kind active at a time. Casting a second will cancel the first.
- Like any cloak spell, the effect will continue to the end of its duration even if you sheathe your weapons, and even operates underwater.
