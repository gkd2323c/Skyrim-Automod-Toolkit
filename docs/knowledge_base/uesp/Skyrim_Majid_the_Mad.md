[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/88/SR-npc-Majid_the_Mad.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Majid_the_Mad.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Majid_the_Mad.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Majid_the_Mad.jpg) Majid the Mad **Majid the Mad** is a [Redguard](Skyrim_Redguard.md) mercenary who is encountered at a camp southeast of [Fort Greymoor](Skyrim_Fort_Greymoor.md) during the quest [Gift of Kynareth](Skyrim_Gift_of_Kynareth.md). He is the leader of a band of [Alik'r mercenaries](Skyrim_Alik%27r_Mercenary.md) that stole the [Lord's Mail](Skyrim_Lord%27s_Mail_(item).md). He is hostile and will attack on sight.

Majid wears a set of red [Redguard Clothes](Skyrim_Redguard_Clothes.md) and a unique red [Redguard Hood](Skyrim_Redguard_Hood.md), as well as a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md). He wields two [scimitars](Skyrim_Scimitar.md), carries a random amount of [gold](Skyrim_Gold.md), the [Key to Alik'r Mercenary Chest](Skyrim_Key_to_Alik%27r_Mercenary_Chest.md), and has a 25% chance to carry a [lockpick](Skyrim_Lockpick.md). The key is needed for the related quest to unlock the chest containing the Lord's Mail and a set of instructions named *[Majid - Please Read](Skyrim_Majid_-_Please_Read.md)*.

He is accompanied by two Alik'r mercenaries and can be a tough fight, so prepare accordingly.

## Related Quests
- **[Gift of Kynareth](Skyrim_Gift_of_Kynareth.md)**<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Lord%27s_Mail.md)</sup>: Recover a legendary [Legion](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md) [artifact](Skyrim_Lord%27s_Mail_(item).md) and obtain the blessing of [Kynareth](Lore_Kynareth.md).

## Notes
- The Redguard Hood item worn by Majid is identical in appearance to that worn by [Nazir](Skyrim_Nazir.md), but is actually a different item; the only difference is that this hood covers long hair. The Redguard Clothes item is the same one worn by Nazir.
