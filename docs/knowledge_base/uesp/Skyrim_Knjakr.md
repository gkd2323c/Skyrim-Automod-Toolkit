[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/77/SR-npc-Knjakr.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Knjakr.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Knjakr.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Knjakr.jpg) Knjakr **Knjakr** is an [elderly](Skyrim_Elder.md) cook living in [The Ratway Warrens](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Ratway_Warrens) of [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md). He is implied to be a cannibal, and when approached will try to entice you into his cell.

If you decide to enter Knjakr's cell, he will immediately turn hostile and attack. His apparel consists of a [chef's tunic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chef%27s_Tunic), a [chef's hat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chef%27s_Hat), and a pair of [shoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shoes#00018801). He is armed with an [iron war axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_War_Axe). A [grindstone](Skyrim_Grindstone.md) for improving weapons can be found in his room.
