Vitraen was the founder and first Count of [Vitharn](Lore_Vitharn.md) in the [Dementia](Lore_Dementia_(place).md) region of the [Shivering Isles](Lore_Shivering_Isles.md). Vitraen built Vitharn from the mud of Dementia. He gathered those who would pledge fealty to him as their liege lord and reached an agreement with the nearby tribes of Fanatics, uniting them as vassals to protect his lands and line.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-FOV-1)</sup>

Vitraen's wife was Countess Mawean, and together they had two children: a son named [Csaran](Lore_Csaran.md) and a daughter named Nweala, who were the first son and daughter of Vitharn. Vitraen and Mawean believed that with the proper political influence, Csaran could usurp [Sheogorath](Lore_Sheogorath.md) and lead the Shivering Isles into a prosperous age. Vitraen refused even to acknowledge Sheogorath, thinking himself and his heirs to be the irrefutable rulers of the Isles, which amused the Madgod to no end.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-FOV-1)</sup>

Vitraen lived out the rest of his days in the Shivering Isles. Csaran succeeded him as Count of Vitharn.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-FOV-1)</sup> Vitraen was entombed beside Mawean in Vitharn's Mausoleum.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-VM-2)</sup>
