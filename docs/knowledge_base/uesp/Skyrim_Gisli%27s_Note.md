Gisli's Note **by [Gisli](Skyrim_Gisli.md)**
---
<Alias=Player>,

Praise is something I seldom offer, but I found my meal to be a fine display of culinary skill and service. Quite delectable!

I shall have to tell [Viarmo](Skyrim_Viarmo.md) all about this delightful meal and the accomplished fisherman who prepared it. Perhaps he will write a song in your honor. Please accept this coin along with my thanks.

-- [Gisli](Skyrim_Gisli.md)
