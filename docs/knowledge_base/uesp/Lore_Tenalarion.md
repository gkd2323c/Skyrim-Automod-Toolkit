**High King** Tenalarion was an [Altmer](Lore_Altmer.md) ruler of the [Summerset Isles](Lore_Summerset_Isles.md), active during the early years of the [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md) and a contemporary of [Alessia](Lore_Alessia.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-HOCC-1)</sup>

## History
Little is known about Tenalarion's life and reign, except that he was actively ruling as of [1E 243](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_243) and in the years that followed. He held the throne to the [Kingdom of Alinor](Lore_Kingdom_of_Alinor.md) in the period after the [Alessian Slave Rebellion](Lore_Alessian_Slave_Rebellion.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-HOCC-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TLKOTA-2)</sup> Faced with the rise of a new power in [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md), one that distrusted [Elves](Lore_Mer.md) and reviled [Daedric](Lore_Daedra.md) worship, High King Tenalarion imposed a strict ban on [Velothi](Lore_Veloth.md) practices.

Refusing to accept the seizure of their property and the imprisonment of their leaders, [Clan Corelanya](Lore_Clan_Corelanya.md) chose to leave the Summerset archipelago. This led to the founding of the separate kingdom on the shores of [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md) in [1E 522](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_522), established by [Sundavar Corelanya](Lore_Sundavar_Corelanya.md). The new nation sought to maintain neutrality with the High Kings and Queens of Summerset, including Tenalarion and his successors.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-HOCC-1)</sup>
