[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b4/SR-item-Wuuthrad.jpg/200px-SR-item-Wuuthrad.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Wuuthrad.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Wuuthrad.jpg) Wuuthrad Wuuthrad is an ancient battleaxe once wielded by the founder of the [Companions](Skyrim_Companions.md), [Ysgramor](Skyrim_Ysgramor.md). Ysgramor carried the axe when he and the Five Hundred Companions drove the elves from [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) and [Solstheim](Lore_Solstheim.md).

For historical information about Wuuthrad, see the [lore article](Lore_Wuuthrad.md).

Its primary effect is causing 1.2x damage to elves, namely [High Elves](Skyrim_High_Elf.md), [Wood Elves](Skyrim_Wood_Elf.md), [Dark Elves](Skyrim_Dark_Elf.md), and [Falmer](Skyrim_Falmer.md). Accordingly, the visage of a screaming elf decorates the axe. At the start of the game, Wuuthrad is in several [fragments](Skyrim_Fragment_of_Wuuthrad.md), four of which are inside the Companions headquarters [Jorrvaskr](Skyrim_Jorrvaskr.md). During the course of the Companions quest line, the weapon is reforged and is used to gain access to Ysgramor's tomb.

After placing Wuuthrad into the hands of Ysgramor's statue at the entrance of his tomb, you can take it back straight away and continue along without affecting the rest of the quest. You can use it as you see fit, though it is no longer a quest item and so has a weight. Wuuthrad cannot be improved by smithing, though it can be enchanted. Despite this, the effect text doesn't change, which means you cannot see how much damage the enchantment does. You can still modify the amount.

## Notes
- The "enchantment" on Wuuthrad is actually a script that adds the hidden C06Blade Of Ysgramor Perk when equipped.
- The lack of a proper enchantment allows the axe to be affected by the [Elemental Fury](Skyrim_Elemental_Fury.md) Shout, for as long as it is never enchanted.
- [Hendraheim](Skyrim_Hendraheim_(place).md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Hendraheim.md)</sup> and [Myrwatch](Skyrim_Myrwatch_(place).md)'s gallery<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Myrwatch.md)</sup> both contain special holders for this item that display it near the [Shield of Ysgramor](Skyrim_Shield_of_Ysgramor.md).
- Ysgramor can be found armed with this axe while in Sovngarde, even if the player has it equipped as well.

## Bugs
- Wuuthrad's bonus does not apply to elven (High Elf, Wood Elf, and Dark Elf) vampires, as vampires are given a separate race in the game data. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.3.2, fixes this bug.
- Wuuthrad's bonus against elves does not apply to [Orcs](Skyrim_Orc.md) (considered elves in some lore) or Orc [vampires](Skyrim_Vampire.md). - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.3.2, addresses this issue. It sets up the artifact to affect both.
- Wuuthrad's bonus against elves does not apply to [Snow Elves](Skyrim_Snow_Elf.md) or to the [frozen Falmer](Skyrim_Frozen_Falmer.md). - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Dawnguard Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Dawnguard_Patch), version 2.0.2, fixes this bug.
- Wuuthrad cannot be improved at a grindstone. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.0, addresses this issue. Wuuthrad can be upgraded using an ebony ingot.
- This weapon is not affected by any smithing perks. - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch) addresses this issue. It causes the weapon to benefit from the [Ebony Smithing](Skyrim_Ebony_Smithing.md) perk.
- Wuuthrad can be enchanted in addition to its existing enchantment. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.0, fixes this bug.
- In [Ysgramor's Tomb](Skyrim_Ysgramor%27s_Tomb.md) during the [Purity](Skyrim_Purity.md) quest for [Farkas](Skyrim_Farkas.md), when you drop the Glenmoril Witch Head into the fire, Wuuthrad may disappear, and cannot be regained after this.
- Placing Wuuthrad on the statue in Ysgramor's Tomb and then taking it back results in you getting a new copy of the axe rather than the existing one you just had and might have tempered. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.3.3, fixes this bug.

- Be warned, however, that if you've already put it on the statue, this fix will not take effect.
