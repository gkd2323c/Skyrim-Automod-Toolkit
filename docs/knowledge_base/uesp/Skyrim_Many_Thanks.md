Many Thanks **by "R"** *A dubious thank you to Mercer Frey for stealing an item*

- [M](Skyrim_Mercer_Frey.md),

- Not certain how you managed to get your hands on that item we discussed, but I'm more than pleased. I thought you'd simply wait until it was being transported from [Castle Dour](Skyrim_Castle_Dour.md) to its final destination, but according to what I've heard, it vanished right from their armory. I wish I knew how you were able to slip by the guards, bypass the portcullis, unlock the armory door and break into that [dwarven](Lore_Dwemer.md) puzzle-locked chest... you need to teach me that little trick some time. I've left your cut in the usual drop spot and might have another job for you soon.

- R
