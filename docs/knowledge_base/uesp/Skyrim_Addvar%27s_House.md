[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/26/SR-place-Addvar%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-place-Addvar%27s_House.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Addvar%27s_House.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Addvar%27s_House.jpg) Addvar's House **Addvar's House** is a small house in the residential district of [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md), next to [Evette San's House](Skyrim_Evette_San%27s_House.md).

It is the home of town fishmonger [Addvar](Skyrim_Addvar.md), his wife [Greta](Skyrim_Greta.md), and their daughter [Svari](Skyrim_Svari.md).

It contains only one zone, *[Addvar's House](#Walkthrough)*.

## Walkthrough
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5f/SR-interior-Addvar%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Addvar%27s_House.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Addvar%27s_House.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Addvar%27s_House.jpg) Addvar's House interior This house is a comfortably lit wooden building. To the left of the rug by the entrance is a basket holding a [woodcutter's axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Woodcutter%27s_Axe), along with a small crate containing a [gourd](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Gourd), an [orange dartwing](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Orange_Dartwing), a [rock warbler egg](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Rock_Warbler_Egg), and a [fly amanita](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fly_Amanita), as well as a nearby bucket holding a whole [pheasant](Skyrim_Pheasant.md). Hanging above the half-wall to the left are three rings, which hold a total of six [rabbits](Skyrim_Rabbit_(dead).md) and one [salmon](Skyrim_Salmon_(dead).md).

In the central area, there is a table with two chairs, above which are three hanging [garlic](Skyrim_Garlic.md) braids. On the table are two tankards, a loaf of [bread](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bread), garlic, an [Eidar cheese wedge](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wedge), [Nord mead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead), and two identical meals on wooden plates, which consist of four [grilled leeks](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Grilled_Leeks) and [seared slaughterfish](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Seared_Slaughterfish). In front of the fireplace is a [cooking](Skyrim_Cooking.md) pot and a cast iron pot holding a [cabbage](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage). Mounted above the fireplace is a goat head.

To the left is a small area set aside for food preparation. Beneath an elk head mounted on the wall is a table with four tankards, a barrel of mead, a horn candle, two [potatoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potato), [beef stew](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Beef_Stew), a [pheasant breast](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pheasant_Breast), a [raw rabbit leg](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Raw_Rabbit_Leg), two and a half loaves of bread, two [baked potatoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Baked_Potatoes), four grilled leeks, a [salmon steak](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Steak), seared slaughterfish, and a number of cheese wheels and wedges.

To the right are two rooms. The one next to the basket of [tundra cotton](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tundra_Cotton) contains a bed belonging to Svari. In this room are two respawning food barrels, a basket, a bucket, a non-respawning wardrobe, and a non-respawning end table with [Alto wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine) and a horn candle on it. The next room is the pantry, with seven [goat cheese wheels](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wheel), seven [Eidar cheese wheels](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wheel), six potatoes, three cabbages, and two [carrots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Carrot). On top of one shelving unit is a [potion of regeneration](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Regeneration), and on the other is a copy of the book *[Uncommon Taste](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Uncommon_Taste)*. There are also three respawning food barrels stacked behind the shelving units, and a few clutter items. Next to this room, under the stairs, is a non-respawning chest containing a small amount of gold, with a high chance for an [animal part](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Animal_Parts) or other miscellaneous item to appear, and a very small chance of a torch appearing.

The upper level has a number of small items, along with a few pieces of furniture to sit on. On a long crate there rests a non-respawning large sack and a small [coin purse](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse). The nearby chest (non-respawning) only has loot if you have [Imperial Luck](Skyrim_Imperial_Luck.md), [Prowler's Profit](Skyrim_Prowler%27s_Profit.md), or [Golden Touch](Skyrim_Golden_Touch_(perk).md). On the adjacent cupboard (non-respawning) are a gourd, a gold piece, [fine clothes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fine_Clothes#000cee80), [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#000209a5), a [troll skull](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Troll_Skull), three pieces of garlic, some tundra cotton, a clothes iron, and a human skull. On a thin table near this cupboard are an [iron helmet](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Helmet), a roll of paper, a tankard, and a horn candle. In a corner is a double bed belonging to Addvar. Near this bed are a non-respawning end table and a table with a chair next to it. On the table are two tankards, some bread, a bottle of [wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wine), and a melted candle.

## Notes
- The front door has a novice lock, the [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Addvar%27s_House) to which is carried by Greta and Svari.
- There is a "Loot" [shadowmark](Skyrim_Shadowmarks_(Thieves_Guild).md) to the bottom left of the entrance door.
- Sometimes skeleton sounds can be heard near the door. When using the `tcl` command, a chest flagged "Do Not Delete" can be found on basement level.

## Bugs
- The double bed is only owned by Addvar, which results in Greta being unable to use it, and therefore she isn't able to sleep at all. - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch), version 4.2.4, fixes this bug.
