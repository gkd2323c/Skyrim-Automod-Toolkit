[](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-weapon-Steel Greatsword.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-weapon-Steel Greatsword.png) Stormfang Stormfang is a [steel greatsword](Skyrim_Steel_Greatsword.md) wielded by a [reaver lord](Skyrim_Reaver_Lord.md) found in [Brodir Grove](Skyrim_Brodir_Grove.md). It is identical to a normal steel greatsword in all respects. Stormfang's enchantment deals thirty points of [shock damage](Skyrim_Shock_Damage.md) to the target. It can be tempered with a [steel ingot](Skyrim_Steel_Ingot.md), with the [Steel Smithing perk](Skyrim_Steel_Smithing.md) doubling improvement.

## Notes
- Stormfang appeared in the [Bloodmoon](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Bloodmoon:Bloodmoon) expansion for [Morrowind](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Morrowind), in the possession of [Ulfgar the Unending](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Bloodmoon:Ulfgar_the_Unending).
- Despite being classified as a "unique" weapon, you can disenchant Stormfang to learn its enchantment.
- Stormfang can be obtained multiple times. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Dragonborn Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Dragonborn_Patch), version 1.0.5, fixes this bug.
