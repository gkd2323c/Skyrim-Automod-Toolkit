From the record of Endelion, Aldmeri Dominion Cartographer **by [Endelion](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Endelion)** *A cartographer's notes on charting the coastline of Khenarthi's Roost*
---
[![ON-concept-Khenarthis Roost.png](https://images.uesp.net/7/7f/ON-concept-Khenarthis_Roost.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-concept-Khenarthis_Roost.png)
- Charting the coastline of [Khenarthi's Roost](Lore_Khenarthi%27s_Roost.md) and its nearby waters has proved to be quite difficult, owing to inclement weather and interference from [Sea Elf](Lore_Maormer.md) "enforcers" who waylaid the trade ship I secured passage on. Luckily, they took no interest in me; we surely would have been delayed further (or worse, judging by the behavior of these brutes) if there was any suspicion of an agent of the [Aldmeri Dominion](Lore_Aldmeri_Dominion.md) aboard. When we finally docked at the Port of [Mistral](Lore_Mistral.md), I parted ways with the traders—I'll need some time on the island to perfect and detail my maps, as the most recent records we have are quite crude and incomplete. My superiors will certainly be pleased with the results, which will be well worth my commission.
