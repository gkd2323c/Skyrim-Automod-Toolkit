# Netch Leather


Netch Leather is added by the [Dragonborn](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md) add-on. It can be used in tanning and crafting [bonemold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bonemold) and [chitin](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chitin) armors as well as the [Netch Leather Armor](Skyrim_Netch_Leather_Armor.md) if the [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md) is installed.

## Netch Leather Smithing
Netch Leather Smithing requires the completion of the quest [More Than You Can Chew](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:More_Than_You_Can_Chew). A free set of painted netch leather gear can be collected from the chest in the [Riekling](Skyrim_Riekling.md) camp. A full set of regular netch leather gear can be collected by completing the additional objective from reading the [Crafting with Netch Leather](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Crafting_with_Netch_Leather) book and traveling to [Fort Hraggstad](Skyrim_Fort_Hraggstad.md). Doing this will also get you the netch leather boots artifact known as the [Boots of Blinding Speed](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots_of_Blinding_Speed).

Netch leather armor can be [tempered](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tempering) with a single [tanned netch leather](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tanned_Netch_Leather). One netch leather can be converted into one tanned netch leather at a [tanning rack](Skyrim_Tanning.md). The armor material is considered [leather](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Animal_Hides#Leather_Armor) for the [Matching Set perk](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Matching_Set).

### Netch Leather Armor
The cuirass is on par with [scaled armor](Skyrim_Scaled_Armor.md). The regular shield sits between a [leather](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Animal_Hides#Leather_Armor) and an [elven shield](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Elven_Shield) in terms of armor stats, while the tower shield scores a bit worse than a [glass shield](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Glass_Armor) on that regard, and in terms of weight sits between [dragonscale](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dragonscale_Armor) and [stalhrim](Skyrim_Stalhrim.md). However, the other pieces of armor are equivalent to glass.

### Shadowed Netch Leather Armor
Shadowed Netch leather armor is the same as the regular version, except for the need of a piece of [charcoal](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Charcoal) to create each piece of armor, surpassing the regular version with 1 point of base armor rating. That makes for instance, the boots, gauntlets and helmet equivalent to [dragonscale armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dragonscale).

### Shadowed Boiled Netch Leather Armor
Similar to the regular versions of the boiled armor and helmet, the shadowed boiled netch leather pieces have superior stats to the regular shadowed pieces. The cuirass scores a little worse than [glass armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Glass_Armor) in terms of armor rating, though also weighing slightly less. The helmet has a whopping score of 22, the [dragonscale](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dragonscale) helmet pales in comparison with a rating of 17.
