[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d8/SR-npc-Olava_the_Feeble.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Olava_the_Feeble.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Olava_the_Feeble.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Olava_the_Feeble.jpg) Olava the Feeble **Olava the Feeble** is an [elderly](Skyrim_Elder.md) [citizen](Skyrim_Citizen_(class).md) and a seer who lives in [her house](Skyrim_Olava_the_Feeble%27s_House.md) behind [Breezehome](Skyrim_Breezehome.md) in [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md)'s Plains District. She has a [dark secret](Skyrim_Dark_Brotherhood.md) and will provide you with a very special reading, pointing you to an [assassin of old](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Locate_the_Assassin_of_Old).

Olava gets out of bed every morning at 6am and immediately heads up to the [Bannered Mare](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Bannered_Mare) for a three-hour breakfast. At 9am she heads back to her house and sits down on the porch where she relaxes for ten hours. At 7pm she enters the house where she strolls around until her bedtime at midnight.

She wears a [belted tunic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Belted_Tunic), a pair of [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#0001be1b), and a [hat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hat#000209aa). She is equipped with an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md), and carries a [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Olava_the_Feeble%27s_House) to her house, as well as a selection of [lower-class items and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Poor).

## Quest-Related Events

### Locate the Assassin of Old
When giving you the [Dark Brotherhood](Skyrim_Dark_Brotherhood.md) quest [Breaching Security](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Breaching_Security), [Gabriella](Skyrim_Gabriella.md) will promise you [Olava's Token](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Olava%27s_Token) as a bonus. Talking to Olava when you have her token will have her say:

**I have this token for you.** *"Token, you say? Let me see. Oh, goodness me. You're a friend of Gabriella's, then. Well, I guess we both know why you're here."* **I was told you could see my future.** *"Hmph. Yes. Yes, I surely can. It's not something I do lightly, mind you, and it's not as specific as you might want. But yes. I will do this for you. Please, relax. Free your mind. Yes, that's it... There's a cave. No, not a cave. A... home? A place you feel secure. You will find safety there... [sanctuary](Skyrim_Dawnstar_Sanctuary.md). I see snow, lit by the [star of dawn](Skyrim_Dawnstar.md). And you are not alone. There are others. A [child of night](Skyrim_Babette.md)... a [stalker of the sands](Skyrim_Nazir.md)? Oh, but before you are family, there will be [blood](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Death_Incarnate). Such blood. Wait! There's something else... A potential for adventure, and wealth. It is a ruin, ripe for the plunder. [Deepwood Redoubt](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Deepwood_Redoubt). Far to the northwest... Through there is... [Hag's End](Skyrim_Hag%27s_End.md). The last resting place of an assassin of old. A Dark Brother, who bequeaths his ancient earthly possessions... to you."* **Is there anything else?** *"No... no, that is all. Now... now please. I find myself very weary all of sudden."* The quest [Locate the Assassin of Old](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Locate_the_Assassin_of_Old) will then initiate. If you approach her again, she will say:

*"By Sithis, but you are a persistent one. Haven't I told you enough?"*
## Notes
- The future she reveals hints at an [event](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Death_Incarnate) that occurs in the Dark Brotherhood. If that event has already occurred, she will instead say: *"Your destiny is dark. Oh yes, so dark. But much of it has been fulfilled. You have found sanctuary, and the comforting words of a mother's love. And you have companionship. A child of night, and a stalker of the sands. Together, you are a family."*, before starting the quest.
- According to an unused line of dialogue in game data, Olava was able to give you a reading for 5 gold, saying: *"Oh, very well. But my energies are low. I warn you - I won't be able to see much. Give me... five gold, and I'll do what I can."* However, it is not possible to get a reading this way, so her farewells are unused as well: *"May all your possible futures be bright."* and *"Please, leave me to recover my... mental energies."*
- According to the *[Smugglers' Note](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Smugglers%27_Note)*, Olava is apparently in league with the [Khajiit](Skyrim_Khajiit.md) [trade caravans](Skyrim_Khajiit_Traders.md) (specifically [Ahkari](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ahkari)), and her status as seer is being used as a cover of some kind.
- There is a soul gem in a holder on a table in her house, opposite two chairs, which suggests she does "crystal ball" readings for potential customers.

## Bugs
- On Tirdas and Turdas, Olava was supposed to listen to the ramblings of [Heimskr](Skyrim_Heimskr.md) between 3pm and 7pm. However, her bench package always takes precedence. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.1, fixes this bug.
- Olava's greetings don't make sense in the order they're given. It seems odd for her to say "maybe some other time" before she actually asks if you're there for a reading. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.1.3, addresses this issue. Olava now gives her "Come to Olava for a reading, did you? Sorry, pup, but I'm not quite in the mood." greeting first, then her "Oh, I tell the future, all right. When I feel up to the task, that is. But I'm afraid my energies haven't quite peaked yet today." greeting, and saves her "Maybe some other time I'll reveal your destiny, hmm? Tea leaves, palm reading, crystal scrying... Oooh! Maybe trepanning? Ha ha ha ha." greeting until the end.
- Clearing out Hag's End and then starting Locate the Assassin of Old may cause the Dark Brotherhood Assassin's corpse to disappear. - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch), version 4.3.7, fixes this bug.
- In [Breaching Security](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Breaching_Security), [Olava's Token](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Olava%27s_Token) is required to receive a fortune reading from her. However, it is not a true quest item and may have been discarded or sold by accident. - The [Official Skyrim Patch](Skyrim_Patch.md), version 1.4, fixes this bug.
