**Mass Paralysis** is a master level [Alteration](Skyrim_Alteration.md) spell which causes [Paralysis](Skyrim_Paralyze_(effect).md) on all targets within 40 ft of the caster.

## Effects
- [Paralyze](Skyrim_Paralyze_(effect).md), for 15 secs in 40 ft
- [Stagger Area Attack](Skyrim_Stagger.md), 0.05 pts in 40 ft

## Perks
- [Stability](Skyrim_Stability.md), raises duration to 22 secs

## Notes
- This spell affects friends as well as foes, and may earn you a bounty for multiple assaults depending on who's affected by it. For this reason, it's best if it is used in situations where you only have [followers](Skyrim_Followers.md) around, as they have a higher threshold against attacks from you before they become hostile and/or report it.
- [Atronachs](Skyrim_Daedra.md), [automatons](Skyrim_Dwarven_Automatons.md), [dragons](Skyrim_Dragon.md), [dragon priests](Skyrim_Dragon_Priest.md), [horses](Skyrim_Horse.md), [ice wraiths](Skyrim_Ice_Wraith.md), [mammoths](Skyrim_Mammoth.md), [skeletons](Skyrim_Skeleton.md), [wisps](Skyrim_Wisp.md), and [wispmothers](Skyrim_Wispmother.md) are immune to paralysis. These targets may still be affected by the stagger effect, however.
- This spell is very useful against enemies who tend to swarm you, such as [falmer](Skyrim_Falmer.md), [draugr](Skyrim_Draugr.md) and [forsworn](Skyrim_Forsworn.md), especially in close quarters.
