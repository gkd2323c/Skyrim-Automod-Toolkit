[The Journal of Ralis Sedarys](Skyrim_The_Journal_of_Ralis_Sedarys.md), Volume 20 **by [Ralis Sedarys](Skyrim_Ralis_Sedarys.md)**
---
This excavation is going to be one of the harder ones, I can tell. Hired some diggers out of [Raven Rock](Skyrim_Raven_Rock.md). They all said they were experienced miners, but I guess they're not used to running into trouble as they dig. In a place like this, you never know what was buried long ago, and have to be ready for whatever you come across.

In any case, there were some unfortunate losses when the sleeping dead weren't quite sleeping and weren't quite dead. Operation is stalled unless my friendly partner is able to find enough coin for us to hire some replacements. Maybe it's time to take up a collection from the constant spectators who gather to mock me.
