[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f0/SR-item-Ring_of_Hircine.jpg/200px-SR-item-Ring_of_Hircine.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Ring_of_Hircine.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Ring_of_Hircine.jpg) Ring of Hircine The Ring of Hircine is a Daedric Artifact given to you by [Hircine](Lore_Hircine.md) as one potential reward for completing [Ill Met By Moonlight](Skyrim_Ill_Met_By_Moonlight.md). Originally discovered as a cursed ring, you are given the ring by an imprisoned [werewolf](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Werewolves) named [Sinding](Skyrim_Sinding.md). After acquiring the ring, it will be automatically equipped, not being able to be removed until you complete the quest. As another downside, werewolves will discover that the ring causes them to randomly transform into their beast form. The ring can either be restored or returned to Hircine depending on your actions. If you kill Sinding, you will lose the Ring of Hircine, only to receive [Savior's Hide](Skyrim_Savior%27s_Hide.md) in its place. If you help Sinding kill the hunters pursuing him, you will be rewarded by Hircine by being allowed to keep the ring, with the curse removed. (Note: the cursed ring can be unequipped by using Beast Form power to become a werewolf which will unequip the ring.)

The restored version of the artifact allows werewolves additional transformations into their beast form per day via the Ring of Hircine power accessible from the Active Effects menu. This does not affect the original Beast Form power, which only grants one transformation per day. When using the ring of hircine to become a werewolf, your health will regenerate if your character has this ability as a base effect unlike the Beast Form power, which requires the Ring of the Hunt for health to regenerate as a werewolf.
