Goldenglow Bill of Sale
---
This deed signifies ownership of [Goldenglow Estate](Skyrim_Goldenglow_Estate.md) to the undersigned. This includes all property, livestock, resources, buildings and staff. Ownership may only be transferred with an accompanying Bill of Sale.
