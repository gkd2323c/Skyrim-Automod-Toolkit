[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/70/SR-npc-Alik%27r_Mercenary_01.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Alik%27r_Mercenary_01.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Alik%27r_Mercenary_01.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Alik%27r_Mercenary_01.jpg) The yellow-hooded Alik'r Mercenary An Alik'r Mercenary is a [Redguard](Skyrim_Redguard.md) mercenary who is encountered at a camp southeast of [Fort Greymoor](Skyrim_Fort_Greymoor.md) during the quest [Gift of Kynareth](Skyrim_Gift_of_Kynareth.md). He is a member of a band of the Alik'r following [Majid the Mad](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Majid_the_Mad) that stole the [Lord's Mail](Skyrim_Lord%27s_Mail_(item).md). He is hostile and will attack on sight.

The Mercenary wears a set of yellow [Redguard Clothes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Redguard_Clothes) and a unique yellow [Redguard Hood](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Redguard_Hood), as well as a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md). He wields a bow and arrows, carries a random amount of [gold](Skyrim_Gold.md), and has a 25% chance to carry a [lockpick](Skyrim_Lockpick.md). He must be killed to safely access the chest containing the Lord's Mail.

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fb/SR-npc-Alik%27r_Mercenary_02.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Alik%27r_Mercenary_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Alik%27r_Mercenary_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Alik%27r_Mercenary_02.jpg) The green-hooded Alik'r Mercenary An Alik'r Mercenary is a [Redguard](Skyrim_Redguard.md) mercenary who is encountered at a camp southeast of [Fort Greymoor](Skyrim_Fort_Greymoor.md) during the quest [Gift of Kynareth](Skyrim_Gift_of_Kynareth.md). He is a member of a band of the Alik'r following [Majid the Mad](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Majid_the_Mad) that stole the [Lord's Mail](Skyrim_Lord%27s_Mail_(item).md). He is hostile and will attack on sight.

The Mercenary wears a set of green [Redguard Clothes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Redguard_Clothes) and a unique green [Redguard Hood](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Redguard_Hood), as well as a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md). He uses the spells [Steadfast Ward](Skyrim_Steadfast_Ward.md), [Fast Healing](Skyrim_Fast_Healing.md) and either [Firebolt](Skyrim_Firebolt.md), [Ice Spike](Skyrim_Ice_Spike.md) or [Lightning Bolt](Skyrim_Lightning_Bolt.md), carries a random amount of [gold](Skyrim_Gold.md), and has a 25% chance to carry a [lockpick](Skyrim_Lockpick.md). He must be killed to safely access the chest containing the Lord's Mail.

The mercenaries and Majid can be a tough fight, so prepare accordingly.
