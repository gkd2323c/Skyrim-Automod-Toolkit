[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/36/OB-place-Topal_Bay.jpg/200px-OB-place-Topal_Bay.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-place-Topal_Bay.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-place-Topal_Bay.jpg) Looking across Topal Bay towards Elsweyr Topal Bay (also known as **Topal Sea**) is a large, placid body of water south of Tamriel.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-PGE1C-1)</sup> It is enclosed by the provinces of [Elsweyr](Lore_Elsweyr.md), [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md), and [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md). In Cyrodiil, the city of [Leyawiin](Lore_Leyawiin.md) and the [Blackwood](Lore_Blackwood.md) region lie on Topal Bay, at the delta of the [Niben River](Lore_Niben_River.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-GTL-2)</sup> The bay's western shores are defined by Elsweyr's [Quin'rawl Peninsula](Lore_Quin%27rawl_Peninsula.md), where a great deal of smuggling occurs.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-PGE1E-3)</sup> Topal Bay empties into the [Southern Sea](Lore_Southern_Sea.md).

The bay is named after [Topal the Pilot](Lore_Topal_the_Pilot.md), the [Aldmer](Lore_Aldmer.md) explorer who discovered the Niben River and charted Tamriel's sea lanes.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-FOTN-4)</sup> The bay's coastline to the east with Black Marsh has long been notorious for rampant piracy.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-PGE3A-5)</sup> The bay is also fed by the [Onkobra River](Lore_Onkobra_River.md), which supposedly flows from the heart of Black Marsh.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TAA3-6)</sup>

The [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md) believe that [moon sugar](Lore_Moon_Sugar.md) is "crystallized moonlight" caught by the waters of the bay and brought to the sugarcane groves of the [Tenmar Forest](Lore_Tenmar_Forest.md) by the tidal forces of [Masser](Lore_Masser.md) and [Secunda](Lore_Secunda.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-PGE1E-3)</sup> Khajiit Moon-Priests tend to the sacred waters of the Bay that flow into [Moonlit Cove](Lore_Moonlit_Cove.md), natural caverns where this moons-blessed water is sheltered from the sun's rays.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-7)</sup>

Access to the [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md) and many other settlements make the Topal Bay a place of vital strategic importance, and the [Aldmeri Dominion](Lore_Aldmeri_Dominion.md) has been known to threaten the bay with their navy many times throughout history.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-PGE1A-8)</sup><sup>[[9]](#cite_note-TGW-9)</sup>

By the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md), a species of [durzog](Lore_Durzog.md) had spread to the shores of Topal Bay. Known as Topal Durzogs, these aquatic hunters were thought to have been originally bred by the [Coral-Splitter Tribe](Lore_Goblin.md#Coral-Splitter_Tribe) of [goblins](Lore_Goblin.md).<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-10)</sup>

Circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the Topal Bay region and Blackwood were governed as independent entities.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-ONKeshu-11)</sup>

- **[Tideholm](Lore_Tideholm.md)** — An island east of the Quin'rawl Peninsula.
- **[Topal Island](Lore_Topal_Island.md)** — The largest island in Topal Bay.
