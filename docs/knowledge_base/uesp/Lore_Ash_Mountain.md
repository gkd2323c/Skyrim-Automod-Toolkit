[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b4/ON-place-Ash_Mountain_02.jpg/200px-ON-place-Ash_Mountain_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Ash_Mountain_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Ash_Mountain_02.jpg) Ash Mountain circa 2E 582 Ash Mountain is a volcano in southeastern [Stonefalls](Lore_Stonefalls.md), in the [Daen Seeth](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Daen_Seeth) region, west of the town of [Senie](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Senie) and east of [Ebonheart](Lore_Ebonheart.md). The mountain is surrounded by the [Daedric](Lore_Daedra.md) ruins of the Great Temple of [Boethiah](Lore_Boethiah.md), constructed by the [Chimer](Lore_Chimer.md) prior to the ascension of the [Tribunal](Lore_Tribunal.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOCTo B-1)</sup> It also houses a small ruined shrine to [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md). Large numbers of [scamps](Lore_Scamp.md) and [clannfear](Lore_Clannfear.md) can be found in the ruins, along with [shalk](Lore_Shalk.md) near the lava flows.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESO-2)</sup>

The volcano served as a prison for [Balreth](Lore_Balreth.md), one of the [Brothers of Strife](Lore_Brothers_of_Strife_(creature).md). In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), [Tanval Indoril](Lore_Tanval_Indoril.md), the grandmaster of [House Indoril](Lore_House_Indoril.md), summoned Balreth and ordered him to destroy the [Daggerfall Covenant](Lore_Daggerfall_Covenant.md) army attacking the city of [Davon's Watch](Lore_Davon%27s_Watch.md). Balreth routed the invading army before they entered the city, but he was deemed too dangerous to remain unbound. With the help of the [Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md) and the spirit of [Mavos Siloreth](Lore_Mavos_Siloreth.md), a Chimer who had originally bound Balreth in the [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md), Balreth was defeated beneath the Ash Mountain and his spirit was bound again.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESO-2)</sup>
