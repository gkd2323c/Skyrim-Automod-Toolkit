Guaranteed samples can be found in the following locations:

- 5 in [Severin Manor](Skyrim_Severin_Manor.md) ([Raven Rock](Skyrim_Raven_Rock.md)), three on a shelf next to the alchemy table downstairs, two more in a basket next to it
- 2 at a [shrine to Kynareth](Skyrim_Unmarked_Places.md#Shrine_of_Kynareth) on a hill north of the [Earth Stone](Skyrim_Earth_Stone.md) [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=solstheim&x=23823&y=39381&zoom=16&legacy=true)
- 1 in [Shaman's Hut](Skyrim_Shaman%27s_Hut.md) ([Skaal Village](Skyrim_Skaal_Village.md)), in a bucket
