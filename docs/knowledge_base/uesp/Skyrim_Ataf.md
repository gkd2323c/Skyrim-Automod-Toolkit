[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d4/SR-npc-Ataf.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Ataf.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ataf.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ataf.jpg) Ataf **Ataf**, a [Redguard](Skyrim_Redguard.md) [bard](Skyrim_Bard_(class).md), is a student at the [Bards College](Skyrim_Bards_College.md) in [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md). Being the youngest member of the college, he spends his days in classes to improve his talents at his chosen profession. He is eager to please both his teachers and peers, and will greet new acquaintances rather nicely.

Ataf wears a set of [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#0003452e) and a pair of [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#0003452f). He also keeps a [belted tunic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Belted_Tunic) on his person, but won't equip it. As befitting for a bard, Ataf has a [drum](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Drum), [flute](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Flute), and [lute](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lute) with him. To defend himself he wields an [iron sword](Skyrim_Iron_Sword.md). He also carries a [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Bards_College) to the Bards College and a selection of [lower-class items and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Poor).

Ataf will perform a variety of activities throughout the day at the Bards College. Very few activities in his schedule are set, but he will attend the many classes the college offers. At 4am he will go to the southwestern lecture hall on the top floor to take part in [Inge Six Fingers](Skyrim_Inge_Six_Fingers.md)' class. After the class breaks at 8am, he will go to the lecture hall opposite of it for [Pantea Ateia](Skyrim_Pantea_Ateia.md)'s lesson. At 11am he will head out of class an hour early to go outside. Ataf will wait outside of the Bards College for three hours before heading inside again. An hour later, he will head to the library for [Giraud Gemane](Skyrim_Giraud_Gemane.md)'s history class. Once the college ends classes for the day at 6pm, he will spend the remainder of his time doing whatever he pleases. During the festival he can be found playing a flute.

## Notes
- His Editor ID in the [Creation Kit](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Creation_Kit) is "**At Af Alan**", varying from his name in the game by giving him a last name. Taking into consideration modern [Redguard Naming Conventions](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Redguard_Names#Naming_Convention), it is possible that his full name was originally going to be "**At af-Alan**". - In the *[Prima Official Game Guide](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Books:The_Elder_Scrolls_V__Skyrim__Prima_Official_Game_Guide)*, his name is actually given as "**At-Af-Alan**", which also lines up with how the internal name is capitalized.
- He will occasionally be called on to perform for teachers. During Inge's class she will call on Ataf to play a piece for the class: *"Be sure to watch your finger positions. Especially during the transitions. Ataf, please play back that piece for me."* After completing the teacher's request, she will compliment him on his work with the line: *"Very good. Let's continue with our practice."*
- His fellow bards, [Illdi](Skyrim_Illdi.md) and [Aia Arria](Skyrim_Aia_Arria.md), will talk about his possible infatuation with Illdi. Illdi will start the dialogue by saying he has been looking at her: *"Ataf keeps making eyes at me."* Aia will respond to this by saying: *"You should tell him to mind his own business."* Illdi will turn down her advice: *"Aia. You're so mean. I don't think he means anything by it."* Aia will brush off her response by saying: *"Have it your way, then."*
