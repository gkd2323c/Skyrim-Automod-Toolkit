[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/3c/ON-place-Silsailen.jpg/200px-ON-place-Silsailen.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Silsailen.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Silsailen.jpg) Silsailen circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) Silsailen or **Silsailen Point**<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Arena-1)</sup> is a little farming town located in southern [Auridon](Lore_Auridon.md), over the hills of [Iluvamir](Lore_Iluvamir.md), northeast of [Vulkhel Guard](Lore_Vulkhel_Guard.md) and south of [Phaer](Lore_Phaer.md).

## Layout & Geography
Silsailen's landscape is bucolic, surrounded by farms and barns.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Tendil-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Elen-3)</sup> The unwalled town<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Silsailen Arch-4)</sup> overlooks the Eastshore river delta, where a multitude of inlets and coves are open to the sea separating southeastern Auridon from the [Drowned Coast](Lore_Drowned_Coast.md) in [Greenshade](Lore_Greenshade.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Ent-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-LES-6)</sup> It was located next to several settlements, including [Skywatch](Lore_Skywatch.md) in the east, and [Vulkhel Guard](Lore_Vulkhel_Guard.md) in the south.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Map Arena-7)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/2d/ON-npc-Auridon_Marine.jpg/200px-ON-npc-Auridon_Marine.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Auridon_Marine.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Auridon_Marine.jpg) First Auridon Marine helping rebuild Silsailen It is unclear when Silsailen was founded—possibly as early as the Early [Merethic Era](Lore_Merethic_Era.md), when [Torinaan](Lore_Torinaan.md) first landed in Auridon, to as late as the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md), when Silsailen was undoubtedly a well-established [Altmer](Lore_Altmer.md) settlement.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-Au Ex Ch XII-8)</sup><sup>[[9]](#cite_note-9)</sup> But circa [1E 2150](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_2150),<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-LAS-10)</sup><sup>[[11]](#cite_note-MGC-11)</sup> at least a small mining settlement had already been established at that location.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-FOTI-12)</sup>

In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), Silsailen was under the administration of Canonreeve Valano, who began to secretly work with the [Veiled Heritance](Lore_Veiled_Heritance.md).<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-TJ-13)</sup> [High Kinlady Estre](Lore_Estre.md), the Veiled Queen, promised Valano would become Canonreeve of the larger city of [Skywatch](Lore_Skywatch.md) in return for his loyalty and assistance.<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-BN-14)</sup> Before [Queen Ayrenn](Lore_Ayrenn.md) visited the town during her tour of Auridon, Valano ordered the Heritance to raze Silsailen while they were under the guise of the First Auridon Marines stationed there, imprisoning the real marines in a cellar. Many villagers died during the attack, while others fled and the settlement almost burned to the ground entirely.<sup>[[15]](#cite_note-Reminder-15)</sup><sup>[[16]](#cite_note-Valano-16)</sup><sup>[[17]](#cite_note-Resc-17)</sup> An [Eye of the Queen](Lore_Hero.md#Soulless_One) managed to free the captive Marines and save some of the villagers.<sup>[[18]](#cite_note-Real-18)</sup> In the meantime, the Canonreeve tried to escape with help from Advisor Norion, a high-ranking member of the Heritance, but was captured before he could.<sup>[[19]](#cite_note-GL-19)</sup><sup>[[20]](#cite_note-Teldur-20)</sup> Ultimately, the Heritance was forced from Silsailen and Valano was captured by the actual First Auridon Marines.<sup>[[20]](#cite_note-Teldur-20)</sup>

After the settlement burned and the rebellion was defeated, the First Auridon Marines began to rebuild the town alongside the surviving townsfolk under the guidance of their new Canonreeve, Elenwen, the daughter of the former one.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Elen-3)</sup><sup>[[20]](#cite_note-Teldur-20)</sup>

During the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md), Silsailen was still thriving as a town, led by Lord Soliron. It had a rivalry with [Karndar Watch](Lore_Karndar_Watch.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Arena-1)</sup>

## Government
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5c/ON-place-Silsailen_02.jpg/200px-ON-place-Silsailen_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Silsailen_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Silsailen_02.jpg) Silsailen Canonreeve's Manor Silsailen has always been an integral part of the realm of the [Summerset Isles](Lore_Summerset_Isles.md) since its foundation. A canonreeve is tasked to rule the settlement.<sup>[[16]](#cite_note-Valano-16)</sup>

## Culture
Silsailen is primarily a farming town, worked by local Altmer,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Tendil-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Elen-3)</sup> as opposed to its northern neighbor Phaer, which mainly uses foreign manpower.<sup>[[21]](#cite_note-Minique-21)</sup> A unique breed of cat, the Silsailen Tortie-Calico, originated here.<sup>[[22]](#cite_note-Cat-22)</sup>

The many secluded inlets and coves in the region are used by criminals as hideouts, allowing them easy access to the nearby Silsailen.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-LES-6)</sup>
