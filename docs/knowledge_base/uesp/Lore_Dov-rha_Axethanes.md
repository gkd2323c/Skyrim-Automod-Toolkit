# Dov-rha Axethanes

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/16/ON-item-Dov-rha_Sabatons.jpg/200px-ON-item-Dov-rha_Sabatons.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-item-Dov-rha_Sabatons.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-item-Dov-rha_Sabatons.jpg) Dov-rha Sabatons Dov-rha Axethanes were a group of elite warriors who served as guards and champions to the kings of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) in the period between the height of [First Empire of the Nords](Lore_First_Empire_of_the_Nords.md) and the last years of the [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md). It was theorized that some members of the Dov-rha Axethanes were powerful [Tongues](Lore_Tongues.md), who wielded [thu'um](Lore_Thu%27um.md) in battle. Dov-rha warriors or Dov-rha thanes were occasionally mentioned in the ancient Nord chronicles. Their name originates from the Nordic word for Dragon - dov-rha.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DRS-1)</sup>

Sometime around [1E 2260](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_2260), some members of the group joined [All Flags Navy](Lore_All_Flags_Navy.md) and sailed to Thras to fight the [Sload](Lore_Sload.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TRHOKE-2)</sup><sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DRS-1)</sup>

High-rank members of the group were known to wear armor made of [dragonhide](Lore_Dragon.md). Such equipment was occasionally awarded by the rulers of Skyrim to their bravest warriors and was extremely durable. According to some scholars, their equipment could be a gift from the Dragons themselves.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DRS-1)</sup>

A piece of leg armor dated back to the fourth or fifth century of the First Era that belonged to a member of Dov-rha Axethanes was found by [a member](Lore_The_Vestige.md) of the Antiquarian Circle of the [University of Gwylim](Lore_University_of_Gwylim.md) in [the Systres](Lore_The_Systres.md) circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DRS-1)</sup> This armor originates from times when dragons and mortals took to the skies as bonded battle-mates and still bears enchantments gifted from dov to man that translated striking speed to martial might.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-DRSI-3)</sup> It was presumably handed down for generations until a [Nord](Lore_Nord.md) hero of the All Flags Navy brought them to [High Isle](Lore_High_Isle.md). It was still of great quality and fully functional when it was discovered by archeologists.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DRS-1)</sup>
