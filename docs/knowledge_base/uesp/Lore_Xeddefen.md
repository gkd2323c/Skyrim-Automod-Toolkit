[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/7c/SI-place-Xeddefen.jpg/200px-SI-place-Xeddefen.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SI-place-Xeddefen.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SI-place-Xeddefen.jpg) Xeddefen at the end of the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md) Xeddefen was a ruin in the [Shivering Isles](Lore_Shivering_Isles.md) at the end of the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), located on the [Fringe](Lore_The_Fringe.md) territory.

"Once a bustling city, Xeddefen has become home to a tribe of Grummites who worship the strange obelisk in the depths of the ruins."<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-LSSI-1)</sup>

In [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), [Sheogorath](Lore_Sheogorath.md)'s [Champion](Lore_Hero_of_Kvatch.md) was forced to go to Xeddefen to find a source of Knights of Order power, namely Obelisk right under the [Passwall](Lore_Passwall.md) and deactivate it to stop [Greymarch](Lore_Greymarch.md) proceed.
