[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/ea/SR-item-Dragon_Priest_Dagger.jpg/200px-SR-item-Dragon_Priest_Dagger.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Dragon_Priest_Dagger.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Dragon_Priest_Dagger.jpg) A Dragon Priest Dagger A Dragon Priest Dagger is a type of dagger associated with the [Dragon Cult](Lore_Dragon_Cult.md). They can be found in the [Hall of Countenance](Skyrim_Hall_of_Countenance.md), [Volunruud Elder's Cairn](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Volunruud_Elder%27s_Cairn), [Volkihar Keep](Skyrim_Volkihar_Keep.md)<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup>, and [Ysgramor's Tomb](Skyrim_Ysgramor%27s_Tomb.md). This weapon may also be wielded by [dragon cultists](Skyrim_Dragon_Cultist.md) in [Forelhost](Skyrim_Forelhost.md).

A unique version of this dagger, named [Kahvozein's Fang](Skyrim_Kahvozein%27s_Fang.md), can be found during the [Alteration Ritual Spell](Skyrim_Alteration_Ritual_Spell.md) quest.

## Bugs
- The Dragon Priest Dagger cannot be tempered at a grindstone. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Legendary_Edition_Patch), version 3.0.10, addresses this issue. The Dragon Priest Dagger is now tempered with Steel ingots, and benefits from the Steel Smithing perk.
