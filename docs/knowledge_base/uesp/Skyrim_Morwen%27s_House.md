[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/75/SR-place-Morwen%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-place-Morwen%27s_House.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Morwen%27s_House.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Morwen%27s_House.jpg) Morwen's House **Morwen's House** is the home of [Morwen](Skyrim_Morwen.md) in [Skaal Village](Skyrim_Skaal_Village.md).

It is on the north side of the village, between [Edla's House](Skyrim_Edla%27s_House.md) to the west and the [Greathall](Skyrim_Greathall.md) to the southeast. The player can choose to move into the home if they have [married](Skyrim_Marriage.md) Morwen.

## Walkthrough

### Exterior
To the left of the entrance is a bench. Along the west wall are two fish barrels and an archery target. Behind the house is a sparring dummy and a wood pile. Along the east wall is a bench often occupied by [Nikulas](Skyrim_Nikulas_(Dragonborn).md), a crate with a [horker tusk](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Horker_Tusk) on top, and a fish barrel. The entrance has a novice lock.

### Morwen's House
The entrance leads into a two-room home. There is a small area on your left with a dresser against the east wall. On the dresser is a [steel horned helmet](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Horned_Helmet), a pair of [steel Nordic gauntlets](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Nordic_Gauntlets), and a wooden plate with a [silver jeweled necklace](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Silver_Jeweled_Necklace) on top of it. To the right in the southeast corner is a chair. There is an open crate with a [long bow](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Long_Bow) resting in it and a basket with a quiver of [steel arrows](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Arrow) to the right. There is a shield plaque with a [banded iron shield](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Banded_Iron_Shield) and two crossed [iron greatswords](Skyrim_Iron_Greatsword.md) on the west wall of this small area.

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/2b/SR-interior-Morwen%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Morwen%27s_House.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Morwen%27s_House.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Morwen%27s_House.jpg) House-proud To the right of the door against the east wall is a set of shelves containing [Banded iron armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Banded_Iron_Armor), a [steel mace](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Mace), a [steel plate helmet](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Plate_Helmet), a large [coin purse](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse) and a [goat hide](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Hide). to the left against the north wall is a narrow table with a bottle of [ale](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ale). To the left of this still against the north wall are two shelves, on the shelves is a [strongbox](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Strongbox), a bottle of [wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wine), a [green apple](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Green_Apple), a bottle of ale, copies of *[Beggar Prince](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Beggar_Prince)* and *[Mixed Unit Tactics](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mixed_Unit_Tactics)*, a [horker tusk](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Horker_Tusk) and two sacks. Beside the shelves, on the left is a chair, a barrel with a mead barrel on top and a cupboard with three bottles of [alto wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine), a [gourd](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Gourd), a [red apple](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Apple) and a wooden bowl containing an [ash yam](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ash_Yam) on the shelves. On a ledge against the west wall are three sacks.

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/31/SR-interior-Morwen%27s_House_02.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Morwen%27s_House_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Morwen%27s_House_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Morwen%27s_House_02.jpg) Very house-proud In the center of the room is a fire pit with a [woodcutter's axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Woodcutter%27s_Axe), a piece of [firewood](Skyrim_Firewood.md), a [salt pile](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Salt_Pile), a cast iron pot containing a [baked potato](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Baked_Potatoes) and two [clam meats](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Clam_Meat) around it. and hanging above in front of the door are a [rabbit](Skyrim_Rabbit_(dead).md) and [elves ear](Skyrim_Elves_Ear.md) sample. There is a low railing between the door and the fire pit. Between the fire pit and the west wall is an open crate containing a [cabbage](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage) and two fillets of [horker meat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Horker_Meat). three small steps lead to the southern part of the house.

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8c/SR-interior-Morwen%27s_House_03.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Morwen%27s_House_03.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Morwen%27s_House_03.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Morwen%27s_House_03.jpg) Table for one There is a long table against the southern wall that separates the bedroom from the rest of the house. On the table is a mead barrel, three loaves of [bread](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bread), an [eidar cheese wedge](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wedge), a green apple and a plate containing a [salmon steak](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Steak). There is a shelf on the west wall above the table with a bottle of wine on it. and hanging above the table is a rack with two [salmon](Skyrim_Salmon_(dead).md) and a rabbit. To the left of the table, on the floor is a bucket containing five bottles of ale. To the right of the fire pit against the east wall is a row of three weapon racks with a [Nordic bow](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nordic_Bow) and an [iron sword](Skyrim_Iron_Sword.md). A sack is to the right of it and a bucket containing a quiver of [Nordic arrows](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nordic_Arrow) is to the left.

There is a doorway to the left of the table that leads to a small bedroom. On your left as you enter is an adept-locked display case containing a [glass war axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Glass_War_Axe). There is a shelf underneath with a mead barrel and an [eidar cheese wheel](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wheel). To the right are two barrels, a sack a in the corner a practice dummy. Half way along the south wall is an open crate with nine [iron daggers](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md), with three more on the floor next to it. In the southwestern corner is a chair. opposite against the wall that separates the bedroom from the rest of the house is a single bed with a shelf above it containing a copy of *[Troll Slaying](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Troll_Slaying)*. Finally at the end of the bed is a chest.
