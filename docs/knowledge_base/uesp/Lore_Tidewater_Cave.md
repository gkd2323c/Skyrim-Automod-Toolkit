## History
Like all natural caves, these tunnels once served as the capillaries of [Nirn](Lore_Nirn.md), pulsing with the divine essence of the [Aedra](Lore_Aedra.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup>

Following the death of Emperor [Leovic](Lore_Leovic.md) in [2E 577](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_577) and the subsequent downfall of the [Empire of Cyrodiil](Lore_Empire_of_Cyrodiil_(Interregnum).md) under Emperor [Varen Aquilarios](Lore_Varen_Aquilarios.md), Councilor Faleria of the [Elder Council](Lore_Elder_Council.md) officially retired from her position as overseer of the Imperial treasury. She moved to [Leyawiin](Lore_Leyawiin.md) and continued to offer advice to her fellow Councilors in their new roles as Leyawiin's Council of Legates.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup> Faleria brought a vast amount of bureaucratic paperwork with her from her lifetime spent as a public servant. She chose Tidewater Cave as a hiding place for this cache of documents, where it remained until circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582). After a string of assassinations targeting her fellow Councilors, Faleria relocated her records to Leyawiin in order to search for references to the [Four Ambitions](Lore_Four_Ambitions.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup> At that time, the cave was inhabited by vicious wildlife, including [bog dogs](Lore_Bog_Dog.md), [crocodiles](Lore_Crocodile.md), [death hoppers](Lore_Death_Hopper.md), and [hoarvors](Lore_Hoarvor.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ESO-5)</sup>

By [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), the cave was inhabited by wild beasts as well as Kalperklan [trolls](Lore_Troll.md), a group of trolls inexplicably obsessed with hoarding pairs of calipers.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-OB-1)</sup> The presence of the trolls made the cave an excellent hiding spot for the Eye of Nocturnal, which had been stolen by a pair of [Argonian](Lore_Argonian.md) thieves from Leyawiin. The thieves hid the Eye in a flooded passage, safe from the trolls due to their hatred of water. However, the [Daedric Prince](Lore_Daedric_Prince.md) [Nocturnal](Lore_Nocturnal.md) was displeased by this theft, and she sent the [Hero of Kvatch](Lore_Hero_of_Kvatch.md) to Leyawiin in search of the Eye. The Hero eventually located it within Tidewater Cave and returned it to the Prince.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup>
