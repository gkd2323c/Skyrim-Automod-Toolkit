These **Redoran Guards** are dead guards found in two locations of [Solstheim](Skyrim_Solstheim.md): [Old Attius Farm](Skyrim_Old_Attius_Farm.md) and [Ashfallow Citadel](Skyrim_Ashfallow_Citadel.md). Like their [living counterparts](Skyrim_Redoran_Guard.md), they are equipped with Bonemold Armor (including shields), Elven Swords, Elven Bows, and Elven Arrows, along with simple clothing in their inventories,

The dead Redoran Guard found at Old Attius Farm can be encountered during [March of the Dead](Skyrim_March_of_the_Dead.md) where he accompanied [Captain Veleth](Skyrim_Captain_Veleth.md) but lost his life to the three [Ash Spawn](Skyrim_Ash_Spawn.md). He will remain permanently as a container after the quest, with his inventory resetting periodically.

The other two dead Redoran Guards, found outside on the front steps of [Ashfallow Citadel](Skyrim_Ashfallow_Citadel.md), can be encountered during [Served Cold](Skyrim_Served_Cold.md). Both men were sent there by [Adril Arano](Skyrim_Adril_Arano.md) to aid you with killing the [Morag Tong](Skyrim_Morag_Tong.md), but both guards lost their lives before your arrival. They disappear after the quest is completed and the exterior resets.
