[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/25/SR-creature-Drascua.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Drascua.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Drascua.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Drascua.jpg) Drascua at Dead Crone Rock **Drascua** is a [hagraven](Skyrim_Hagraven.md) and the leader of the Forsworn at [Dead Crone Rock](Skyrim_Dead_Crone_Rock.md). She is one of the Keepers of the Razor and as such she bears the pommel stone of [Mehrunes' Razor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mehrunes%27_Razor).

She will sometimes cry out, *"You won't take my eye, man-thing!"*

## Related Quests
- **[Pieces of the Past](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pieces_of_the_Past)**: Retrieve the scattered pieces of [Mehrunes' Razor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mehrunes%27_Razor) and reforge this deadly blade.

## Notes
- Multiple file names, some visible in the [Creation Kit](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Creation_Kit), with others exclusively found in the Dialogue Views files, indicate that her name was **Daetra** at an earlier point.

## Bugs
- Killing Drascua would result in future radiant quests to Dead Crone Rock failing since there is no generic boss replacement. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.1, addresses this issue. A generic forsworn shaman boss has been placed near the word wall and given the boss flag. Drascua's boss flag has been removed so she will no longer count as a radiant target.
