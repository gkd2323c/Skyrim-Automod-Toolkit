[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/21/SR-npc-Stormcloak_Quartermaster.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Stormcloak_Quartermaster.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Stormcloak_Quartermaster.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Stormcloak_Quartermaster.jpg) The quartermaster at Hjaalmarch Stormcloak camp **Stormcloak Quartermasters** can be found in most of the [Stormcloak](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md) [military camps](Skyrim_Military_Camps.md). Speaking to them will open the trade screen.

Quartermasters wear a [blacksmith's apron](Skyrim_Clothing.md#0005b69f) and a pair [shoes](Skyrim_Clothing.md#0005b69e). They are equipped with either a leveled [two-handed weapon](Skyrim_Two-Handed_Weapons.md), or a leveled [one-handed weapon](Skyrim_One-Handed_Weapons.md) and a shield (all up to [steel](Skyrim_Steel.md) quality). They also carry a [steel dagger](Skyrim_Steel_Dagger.md) and a [hunting bow](Skyrim_Hunting_Bow.md) supplemented by 12 [steel arrows](Skyrim_Steel_Arrow.md).
