## Official Summary
*The crossbow has long been the weapon of choice for skilled hunters, whether the prey be animals of the forest or creatures of the night. Forged from the rarest of metals, the Ebony and Elven crossbows represent two of the most deadly ranged weapons in all of Tamriel. (Quest "Night Hunter" starts by reading Kragrash's Letter at Ironback Hideout northwest of Solitude.)*
## Overview
The quest **[Night Hunter](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Night_Hunter)** will start when you read [Kragrash's letter](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Kragrash%27s_Letter) at [Ironback Hideout](Skyrim_Ironback_Hideout.md), a remote bandit camp high in the mountains of [Haafingar](Skyrim_Haafingar.md). Completing this quest will grant you access to a hidden [cellar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ironback_Hideout_Cellar) once used by an Orcish vampire hunter named Kragrash. Inside, you will find unenchanted ebony and Elven crossbows. After completing the quest, these powerful crossbows can also be crafted and can be further improved at a [forge](Skyrim_Forge.md) to create enhanced versions.

## Notes
- The Ironback Hideout Cellar doesn't respawn making it a nice little base of operations. The three bandits outside however do respawn upon fast traveling back to the location.
- The cellar key also opens the cage door within the cellar.
- Unlike the [Crossbows](Skyrim_Weapons.md#Crossbows) added by [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md), these Crossbows can be crafted at any regular [Forge](Skyrim_Forge.md).
