[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/2a/SR-place-Eldergleam_Sanctuary.jpg/200px-SR-place-Eldergleam_Sanctuary.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Eldergleam_Sanctuary.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Eldergleam_Sanctuary.jpg) Eldergleam Sanctuary **Eldergleam Sanctuary** is a small [underground grove](Skyrim_Caves.md) and worship site of the followers of [Kynareth](Lore_Kynareth.md) south-southwest of [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md). It contains only one zone, *[Eldergleam Sanctuary](#Eldergleam_Sanctuary)*.

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/ff/SR-interior-Eldergleam_Sanctuary.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Eldergleam_Sanctuary.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Eldergleam_Sanctuary.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Eldergleam_Sanctuary.jpg) Eldergleam Sanctuary interior [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/09/SR-place-Eldergleam.jpg/200px-SR-place-Eldergleam.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Eldergleam.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Eldergleam.jpg) The Eldergleam tree The sanctuary is renowned for its remarkable beauty and the giant tree [Eldergleam](Lore_Eldergleam.md), which is said to move its roots for [one weapon only](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nettlebane). The naturally-lit cave also abounds with plants and insects that can be harvested for [alchemical ingredients](Skyrim_Ingredients.md). With the [Fishing](Skyrim_Fishing.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md) installed, [spadefish](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Spadefish) can be found swimming in the deeper water.

## Walkthrough

### Exterior
The sanctuary is north of [Darkwater Crossing](Skyrim_Darkwater_Crossing.md) among the sulphurous pools and hot springs of [Eastmarch](Skyrim_Eastmarch.md).

### Eldergleam Sanctuary
[Asta](Skyrim_Asta.md) and [Sond](Skyrim_Sond_(bandit).md) worship inside. Just before encountering Asta, you will find a chest and a [corundum ore vein](Skyrim_Corundum_Ore_Vein.md) next to a tree atop the rocks near the northern wall. If you want to mine the corundum, do so before going up to Eldergleam, as [Maurice Jondrelle](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Maurice_Jondrelle) will permanently occupy the vein once his part in the quest is complete. You can also mine it after by attacking the ore with the pickaxe equipped. A copy of the [Restoration](Skyrim_Restoration.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[Mystery of Talara, v 2](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mystery_of_Talara,_v_2)* is leaning on the rock next to Asta's knapsack.

A chest protected by Eldergleam's roots is in the back along the western wall. Hit the roots with Nettlebane to access the chest, which contains a random weapon or piece of armor and other loot.

The cave contains the following harvestable ingredients: four [blue mountain flowers](Skyrim_Blue_Mountain_Flower.md), two [creep clusters](Skyrim_Creep_Cluster.md), six [dragon's tongue](Skyrim_Dragon%27s_Tongue.md), twenty [hanging moss](Skyrim_Hanging_Moss.md), three [Jazbay grapes](Skyrim_Jazbay_Grapes.md), and two [mora tapinella](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mora_Tapinella).

## Notes
- A second corundum ore vein is outside, west of the entrance. Go past the mammoth skeleton and look for it on the other side of the nearby geyser.
- A hunter's camp is outside, southwest of the entrance. At the camp, three "naked" hunters appear to be relaxing in the volcanic hot waters of the shallow pond. Among their possessions is a copy of the [Smithing](Skyrim_Smithing.md) skill book *[Cherim's Heart](Skyrim_Cherim%27s_Heart.md)*. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=112691&y=-24574&zoom=17&legacy=true)
- A copy of the [Enchanting](Skyrim_Enchanting.md) skill book *[A Tragedy in Black](Skyrim_A_Tragedy_in_Black.md)* and various soul gems are outside, east of the entrance on a crate atop a hill. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=124040&y=-22819&zoom=17&legacy=true)
- Asta's knapsack and all of the items surrounding it are marked as owned by her. Everything else can be taken for free.
