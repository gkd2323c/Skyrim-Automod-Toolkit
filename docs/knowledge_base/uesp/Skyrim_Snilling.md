[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f9/SR-npc-Snilling.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Snilling.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Snilling.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Snilling.jpg) Snilling **Snilling** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [farmer](Skyrim_Farmer_(class).md) who lives in [Katla's Farm](Skyrim_Katla%27s_Farm.md) near [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md). He is the husband of [Katla](Skyrim_Katla.md) and father of [Knud](Skyrim_Knud.md). He does not leave the house because he has become clumsy and weak, as Katla says *"He'll only hurt himself out there."*

With his wife doing all the work, Snilling does very little but eat and sleep. He wakes up every morning at 6am and takes a one-hour breakfast then goes back to bed for another hour. Starting at 8am, and all through the day until his bedtime at midnight, he loiters lazily around the house, interrupted only by one-hour meals at noon and 6pm.

Snilling wears farm [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#000261c0) along with a pair of [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#000261bd) and a pair of [gloves](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Gloves#000261c1). He carries a spare [belted tunic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Belted_Tunic) and the [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Katla%27s_Farm) to his house. Snilling wields an [iron sword](Skyrim_Iron_Sword.md) in combat.

## Dialogue
He can politely greet you with:

*"Oh, hello. Have a seat, will you?"* *"I was a much younger man when I married Katla. Now I can hardly keep up with her."* *"My brothers would have been appalled to see a man outdone by his wife, but not me. Without Katla, this farm would fall apart."*
## Conversations
When the family is together, they may engage in conversation:

**Katla**: *"Another gorgeous gray day, right dear?"*
**Snilling**: *"It's too cold for my bones, but that never seems to stop you, dear."*
**Knud**: *"I'm gonna go play soldier with a tree!"*
**Katla**: *"That's nice, son. Just don't stomp on the crop like last time!"*
