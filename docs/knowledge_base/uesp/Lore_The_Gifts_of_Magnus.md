The Gifts of Magnus *A short text on the god Magnus*
---
Penitent, the lives of all living are touched by [Magnus](Lore_Magnus_(god).md), He Who Abstained. Lord Magnus drew up the schematics of our world, intricately sketching the diagrams of Creation. Magnus is with us always, in the magics of Mages and the warming breath of the sun.

To complete your venerations here, intone: "By the Empyrean Light of [Aetherius](Lore_Aetherius.md), may the [Eye of Magnus](Lore_Eye_of_Magnus.md) always be upon me. Grant me the wisdom to use his gifts, and patience to know his wisdom."
