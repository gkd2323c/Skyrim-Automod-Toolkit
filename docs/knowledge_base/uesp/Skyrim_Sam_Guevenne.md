[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/09/SR-npc-Sam_Guevenne.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Sam_Guevenne.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Sam_Guevenne.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Sam_Guevenne.jpg) Sam Guevenne at the [Bannered Mare](Skyrim_The_Bannered_Mare.md) **Sam Guevenne** is a [Breton](Skyrim_Breton.md) [conjurer](Skyrim_CombatMageConjurer.md) that you will meet in a [tavern](Skyrim_Inns.md), where he will challenge you to a drinking contest. He spawns in the inn or tavern of whichever hold capital you first enter after reaching level 14. Once the game has chosen his location, he will usually remain there until met. He will stay in the same one indefinitely until you start the drinking contest. Likely inns or taverns include the ones in cities, depending on how many side quests you have done and how closely you are following the [main questline](Skyrim_Main_Quest.md). He is the author of a [note](Skyrim_Repair_Supplies.md), and knows the spells [Conjure Dremora Lord](Skyrim_Conjure_Dremora_Lord.md), [Thunderbolt](Skyrim_Thunderbolt.md), and [Grand Healing](Skyrim_Grand_Healing.md). Sam wears [black robes](Skyrim_Black_Robes.md) and mage [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md), and is equipped with an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md).

After you have accepted and won the challenge, he will say that you are good company to drink with and will invite you on a trip. This triggers the quest [A Night To Remember](Skyrim_A_Night_To_Remember.md), which will hold several surprises. After completing this quest, it is revealed that he is actually the Daedric Prince [Sanguine](Skyrim_Sanguine.md), who was just looking for some fun.

## Related Quests
- **[A Night To Remember](Skyrim_A_Night_To_Remember.md)**: Find your lost friend, Sam, as you recover from a bad hangover.

## Quest-Related Events
Once you reach level 14, you may meet Sam in any inn or [tavern](Skyrim_Inns.md). Approaching him, he will challenge you:

- *"If you're looking for a challenge, you've come to the right place."*
- *"You look like someone who can hold their liquor. How about a friendly contest to win a staff?"*

- **I don't really feel like drinking...**
- *"Well come back when you do, my friend. Come back when you do."*

- **A drinking contest? You don't stand a chance!**
- *"Ha! We'll see about that. This is a special brew, very strong stuff. Let's get started."*
- *"I'll start round one. Down the hatch! Your turn!"*
- **Here we go.**
- *"One down, my friend. One down."*
- *"And another one for me. And how about you?"*
- **A second drink. Easy enough.**
- *"So says you. I think I've hit my limit on these things. Tell you what, one more and you win the contest."*
- **One more. No problemsh.**
- *"Wow. You've really done it. The staff is yours."*
- **Thash grape!**
- *"You know, you're a fun person to drink with. I know this great little place where the wine flows like water. We should head there. Hey, you don't look so good..."*

You will then black out, awaking in the [Temple of Dibella](Skyrim_Temple_of_Dibella.md) in [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md). The quest continues from here, sending you all across Skyrim, picking up the pieces of what you and Sam got up to that night. In the quest's conclusion, you will arrive in [Misty Grove](Skyrim_Misty_Grove.md) and speak to Sam once more.

- *"You're here! I was beginning to think you might not make it."*
- **It was quite a trip. Where are we?**
- *"I thought you might not remember your first trip here. You had a big night. I think you've definitely earned the staff."*
- **I have all the things needed to repair it.**<sup>[[nb 1]](#intnote_Fix It)</sup>
- *"Oh, the Hagraven feather and so on. You can throw all those out. You see...*

Sam will then reveal his [true identity](Skyrim_Sanguine.md) and continue the conversation, giving you the [Sanguine Rose](Skyrim_Sanguine_Rose.md) and completing the quest.

## Notes
- | [^](#note_Fix It) | This line and Sam's response are remnants of [cut content](Skyrim_Cut_Content.md) from a previous version of A Night To Remember, in which the note [*Repair Supplies*](Skyrim_Repair_Supplies.md) would have been used to gather ingredients to repair the staff, the mentioned Hagraven feather included. |
| --- | --- |
- Internal data refers to him under the names **Samwell** and **Sammy** as well.
- Despite the inn in which he spawns being randomly chosen, he starts out as a member of the faction for patrons of [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md)'s [Candlehearth Hall](Skyrim_Candlehearth_Hall.md). - Similarly, his ref is actually placed inside of [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md)'s [The Bee and Barb](Skyrim_The_Bee_and_Barb.md). It's possible that either of these taverns were at one point his set location.
- Sam can spawn at any of the [inns](Skyrim_Inns.md) across Skyrim, including those in [towns](Skyrim_Towns.md), not just the ones in [hold capitals](Skyrim_Cities.md). This includes [The Retching Netch](Skyrim_The_Retching_Netch.md) in [Raven Rock](Skyrim_Raven_Rock.md) with the [Dragonborn](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md) add-on. - If Sam was present at the Retching Netch when the drinking contest took place, you'll be sent back there once the quest is finished.
- In a [random encounter](Skyrim_World_Interactions.md#A_drunken_dare) after A Night To Remember, [Deep-In-His-Cups](Skyrim_Deep-In-His-Cups.md) reveals that you and Sam dared him to enter a [bandit camp](Skyrim_Camps.md) and steal a hat after breaking into the Temple of Dibella.

## Bugs
- It is possible that you will not blackout after the third drink with Sam. The reason for this is that one of the characters essential for the quest has died. - The [Official Skyrim Patch](Skyrim_Patch.md), version 1.5, addresses this issue. Senna, Ennis, and Ysolda are now marked essential and thus unkillable.
- The miscellaneous quest prompt to start A Night To Remember refers to Sam Guevenne as "**Sam Guenvere**". - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.3.3, fixes this bug.
