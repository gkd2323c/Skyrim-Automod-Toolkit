# Necromancer


The type of necromancer you encounter depends on your level. You will continue to meet low-level necromancers even at high levels, but boss-level necromancers will always be at the highest possible level.

### Necromancers
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/ea/SR-npc-Necromancer_02.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Necromancer_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Necromancer_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Necromancer_02.jpg) A Novice Necromancer firing a Frostbite spell [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/01/SR-npc-Necromancer.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Necromancer.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Necromancer.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Necromancer.jpg) A Necromancer Necromancers rely on their reanimated allies and Destruction spells in combat, and carry a leveled dagger in case of close-quarters fighting ([iron](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md), [steel](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Dagger), [orcish](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Orcish_Dagger), [dwarven](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dwarven_Dagger), [elven](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Elven_Dagger), [glass](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Glass_Dagger), or an enchanted dagger of any quality from iron to [Daedric](Skyrim_Daedric.md)).

They wear a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Clothing.md#000c36e8) along with [necromancer robes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Necromancer_Robes), [hooded necromancer robes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hooded_Necromancer_Robes), robes of [Alteration](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fortify_Alteration), robes of [Destruction](Skyrim_Fortify_Destruction.md), robes of [Restoration](Skyrim_Fortify_Restoration.md), or robes of [Fortify Magicka Rate](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fortify_Magicka_Rate).

In addition to the gear listed above, necromancers have a small chance to carry [potions](Skyrim_Potions.md), [poisons](Skyrim_Poisons.md), [soul gems](Skyrim_Soul_Gems.md) (including a higher chance for [black soul gems](Skyrim_Black_Soul_Gem.md) above level 20), Novice or Apprentice level [spell tomes](Skyrim_Spell_Tomes.md), [ingredients](Skyrim_Ingredients.md) (common, uncommon, or rare), [recipes](Skyrim_Recipes.md), and a small amount of [gold](Skyrim_Gold.md).

On death, necromancers have a 10% chance to drop potions and poisons, and a 5% chance to drop one of the following scrolls:

- **Alteration:** [Scroll of Mass Paralysis](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Mass_Paralysis)
- **Conjuration:** [Scroll of Conjure Storm Atronach](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Conjure_Storm_Atronach), [Scroll of Dread Zombie](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Dread_Zombie)
- **Destruction:** [Scroll of Blizzard](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Blizzard), [Scroll of Fire Storm](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Fire_Storm)
- **Illusion:** [Scroll of Call to Arms](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Call_to_Arms), [Scroll of Harmony](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Harmony), [Scroll of Hysteria](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Hysteria), [Scroll of Mayhem](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Mayhem)
- **Restoration:** [Scroll of Bane of the Undead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Bane_of_the_Undead), [Scroll of Guardian Circle](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Guardian_Circle)

Master Necromancers and Arch Necromancers have the same gear as lower-level necromancers, but have a 50% chance to carry a [Staff of Chain Lightning](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Staff_of_Chain_Lightning), [Staff of Fireballs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Staff_of_Fireballs), or a [Staff of Ice Storms](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Staff_of_Ice_Storms). Arch Necromancers do have the chance to carry additional soul gems.

### Wolfskull Cave Necromancers
These four necromancers are found accompanying the [Ritual Master](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ritual_Master) at [Wolfskull Cave](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wolfskull_Cave) during [The Man Who Cried Wolf](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Man_Who_Cried_Wolf). The group includes a Breton woman (00 09655b), a Dunmer woman (00 0e1036), a Nord woman (00 0e1037), and a Nord man (00 0e1049). They will have the same leveled stats as a generic necromancer of the appropriate level, but are all named "Necromancer".

## Dialogue
All members of the Warlock faction share a set of generic dialogue which they will use when in combat:

*"You cannot even conceive of the power I wield..."*

*"Fire, frost, or lightning - you will suffer at my hands!"*

*"Don't you see? I am master of the arcane!"*

*"Behold my power!"*

*"The elements are mine to command!"*

## Notes
- Most necromancers have a [morality](Skyrim_Morality.md) of "Any Crime", which means they're willing to commit any crime. The necromancers found at Wolfskull Cave, however, have a morality of "No Crime," which means they are unwilling to commit crimes of any sort. Their aggression is still very high, though, so they will attack you on sight regardless.
- All the generic necromancers that are Bretons are listed twice in the leveled lists, which means necromancers are slightly more likely to be Breton than one of the other races.
- With the [Bone Wolf](Skyrim_Bone_Wolf.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md) installed, necromancers can often be found in the company of hostile [Bonewolves](Skyrim_Bonewolf.md).
- If you have the [Necromantic Grimoire](Skyrim_Necromantic_Grimoire.md) Creation installed, Arch Necromancers will be wearing a [Elite Necromancer Hood](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Elite_Necromancer_Hood) and [Elite Necromancer Robes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Elite_Necromancer_Robes), which can be looted from them. <sup>[*verification needed — unsure about other levelled necromancers and if pickpocketing is an option*]</sup>
