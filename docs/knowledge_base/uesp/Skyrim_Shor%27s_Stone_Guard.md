[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/1b/SR-npc-Shor%27s_Stone_Guard.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Shor%27s_Stone_Guard.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Shor%27s_Stone_Guard.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Shor%27s_Stone_Guard.jpg) Shor's Stone Guard **Shor's Stone Guards** are [guards](Skyrim_Guards.md) that can be found in the settlement of [Shor's Stone](Skyrim_Shor%27s_Stone.md) in [the Rift.](Skyrim_The_Rift.md) The guards are strategically placed in the small settlement due to the proximity of [Fort Greenwall](Skyrim_Fort_Greenwall.md), located just south of town. Like the other guards of the Rift, they are allied with the [Stormcloaks](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md). If the Rift is conquered by the [Imperial Legion](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md), they will all be replaced with special [Imperial Guards](Skyrim_Imperial_Guard.md).

All Shor's Stone Guards are equipped with [Riften guard armor](Skyrim_Riften_Guard%27s_Armor.md), matching [helmets](Skyrim_Riften_Guard%27s_Helmet.md) and [shields](Skyrim_Riften_Guard%27s_Shield.md), and [fur boots](Skyrim_Fur_Boots.md). They are armed with [hunting bows](Skyrim_Hunting_Bow.md) with 15 [steel arrows](Skyrim_Steel_Arrow.md), [steel daggers](Skyrim_Steel_Dagger.md), and iron or steel [one-handed](Skyrim_One-handed.md) weapons. Additionally, each guard has a 50% chance of carrying a [torch](Skyrim_Torch.md) and a slim chance of carrying some food, drink or an amulet.

Like other guards, Shor’s Stone Guards have standard [guard dialogue](Skyrim_Guard_Dialogue.md).

There are three guards stationed in Shor's Stone, each with different patrol routes:

- One guards the entrance to [Redbelly Mine](Skyrim_Redbelly_Mine.md).
- One patrols back and forth between the mine entrance and the nearby [smelter](Skyrim_Smelter.md).
- One covers the entire settlement.
