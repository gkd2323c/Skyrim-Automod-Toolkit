Mera Stormcloak was a lawthane of [Windhelm](Lore_Windhelm.md) in the mid- [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md), during the time of the [Ebonheart Pact](Lore_Ebonheart_Pact.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-EGT-1)</sup> She was a member of the [Stormcloak Clan](Lore_Stormcloak_Clan.md), a [Nordic](Lore_Nord.md) family which would eventually rise to prominence in the city.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-BSB-2)</sup> She was also a staunch friend and ally of her liege, [Jorunn the Skald-King](Lore_Jorunn.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-MS-3)</sup>

[Flaccus Terentius](Lore_Flaccus_Terentius.md) met Mera during his imperial visit to Windhelm some time between [2E 578](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_578) and [2E 581](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_581), during which time she served as lawthane. After introducing himself to Jorunn, the Envoy-Scholar was brought on a tour of the city by Mera and the two quickly became friends. After several days of drunken revelry, Terentius accompanied Mera and her hunting party into the wilds of [Eastmarch](Lore_Eastmarch.md) until he became separated from the group and continued on his journey.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-EGT-1)</sup>

In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), Jorunn's [Konunleikar](Lore_Konunleikar.md) celebration took place in Windhelm. Among the attendees was the [Stormfist Clan](Lore_Stormfist_Clan.md), who had been in isolation following Prince [Fildgor](Lore_Fildgor.md)'s exile. Suspecting treachery, Mera appointed the [Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md) as Windhelm's Champion and investigated the clan's activities within the city.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup> Her suspicions proved correct when the Stormfists made an attempt on Jorunn's life as part of Fildgor's failed attempt to usurp his brother as High King.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-7)</sup> Ultimately Fildgor's forces were defeated.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-8)</sup><sup>[[9]](#cite_note-9)</sup>
