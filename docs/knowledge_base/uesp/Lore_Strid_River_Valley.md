[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/60/ON-place-Strid_River_Valley.jpg/200px-ON-place-Strid_River_Valley.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Strid_River_Valley.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Strid_River_Valley.jpg) Strid River Valley in 2E 582 [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a1/ON-load-Strid_River_Valley.jpg/200px-ON-load-Strid_River_Valley.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-load-Strid_River_Valley.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-load-Strid_River_Valley.jpg) Strid River Valley in 2E 582 The Strid River Valley was a settlement with a history involving [Ayleid](Lore_Ayleid.md), [Colovian](Lore_Colovian.md), and [Bosmer](Lore_Bosmer.md) cultures. It was located in the [West Weald](Lore_West_Weald.md), just downstream from [Istirus Outpost](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Fort_Istirus), along the [Strid River](Lore_Strid_River.md) delta, south of [Varen's Wall](Lore_Varen%27s_Wall.md) Gatehouse and north of Windshriek Strand of [Malabal Tor](Lore_Malabal_Tor.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SRV-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SRV2-2)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f7/ON-render-Update_44_Battlegrounds_05.jpg/200px-ON-render-Update_44_Battlegrounds_05.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-render-Update_44_Battlegrounds_05.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-render-Update_44_Battlegrounds_05.jpg) Circle of Champions activities in the Strid River Valley Little is known about the distant past of the Strid River Valley, but the presence of ancient [Ayleid ruins](Lore_Ayleid_Ruins.md) suggests that it was once inhabited by the Ayleids before their downfall.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SRV-1)</sup>

Before [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the area was home to a Colovian village. After the Dawnwood ritual, Bosmeri settlers from [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md), following the [Dawnway](Lore_Dawnway.md) philosophy of King [Nantharion Rayn](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Nantharion_Rayn), completely transformed the former Colovian settlement. However, shortly after the Daedric crisis tied to [Ithelia](Lore_Ithelia.md), the Unseen, and the invasion of Glass Daedra from [Mirrormoor](Lore_Mirrormoor.md), these same settlers were driven away, leaving behind a stunning desolation at the water's edge.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SRV-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SRV2-2)</sup>

As of [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) the [Circle of Champions](Lore_Circle_of_Champions.md) began using the settlement to host dangerous gladiator battles to the death. Fighters from all three sides of the [Three Banners War](Lore_Three_Banners_War.md) came to the Strid River Valley battleground to fight for the glory of the Fire Drakes, Pit Daemons, and Storm Lords arena teams.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup>
