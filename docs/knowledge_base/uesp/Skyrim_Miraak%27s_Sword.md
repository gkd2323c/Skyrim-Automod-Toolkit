[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e6/SR-item-Miraak%27s_Sword.jpg/200px-SR-item-Miraak%27s_Sword.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Miraak%27s_Sword.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Miraak%27s_Sword.jpg) Miraak's Sword Miraak's Sword is carried by [Miraak](Skyrim_Miraak_(person).md). It is obtainable from his corpse at the end of [At the Summit of Apocrypha](Skyrim_At_the_Summit_of_Apocrypha.md). It absorbs 15 points of [stamina](Skyrim_Stamina.md) and has a unique visual effect similar to a [Lurker](Skyrim_Lurker.md) attack, shooting a tentacle out during each swing, but doesn't affect weapon range. It can be tempered with one [ebony ingot](Skyrim_Ebony_Ingot.md) and one [Daedra heart](Skyrim_Daedra_Heart.md), and the [Dwarven Smithing](Skyrim_Dwarven_Smithing.md) perk doubles the improvement.

## Notes
- The level 60+ version of the sword has the highest base damage of all one-handed swords (without including [Creation Club](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md) content).
- Miraak's Sword is called "[Firstblade](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Firstblade_Style)" in [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Online).
- [Hendraheim](Skyrim_Hendraheim_(place).md)'s basement trophy room<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Hendraheim.md)</sup> contains a special holder for this item.
