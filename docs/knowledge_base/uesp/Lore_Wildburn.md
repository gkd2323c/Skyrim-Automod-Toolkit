# Wildburn

**Wildburn** is a term that can refer to:

- [Wildburn Seeds](Lore_Wildburn_Seeds.md), corrupted Greenspring Seeds that are the original source of Wildburn corruption

- The Wildburn, a historical region in the West Weald that existed on the edge of the Dawnwood, a forest grown from a Wildburn Seed
