[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/95/SR-npc-Matlara.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Matlara.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Matlara.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Matlara.jpg) Matlara [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/56/SR-npc-Matlara_02.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Matlara_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Matlara_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Matlara_02.jpg) Matlara alive at the road outside of Helgen following the attack **Matlara** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) woman living in [Helgen](Skyrim_Helgen.md) and mother to [Haming](Skyrim_Haming.md) as well as wife to [Torolf](Skyrim_Torolf.md). She is watching the whole time during the execution. Afterwards, she can be found lying on the floor with her arms crossed in pain when on your way to the keep.

She wears a set of farmer's [clothes](Skyrim_Clothing.md#000261c0) along with [hide boots](Skyrim_Hide_Boots.md) and [hide bracers](Skyrim_Hide_Bracers.md). She only carries an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md).

When you speak to Haming later on, he will confirm that his mother and father died in the attack.

## Related Quests

If Helgen is returned to immediately after escaping the keep, she may appear sitting on the inn's porch or leaning on an invisible rail on a destroyed section of the porch. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.3.3, fixes this bug.
