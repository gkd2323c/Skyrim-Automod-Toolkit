## Walkthrough

### Exterior
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/eb/SR-place-Riften_Fishery_Fishing.jpg/200px-SR-place-Riften_Fishery_Fishing.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Riften_Fishery_Fishing.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Riften_Fishery_Fishing.jpg) Fishing supplies This is a detached building built in the middle of [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md)'s docks on the eastern edge of [Lake Honrich](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lake_Honrich). There are numerous [salmon](Skyrim_Salmon_(creature).md) and all types of [ingredient](Skyrim_Ingredients.md) fish and both [blue](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Blue_Dartwing) and [orange dartwings](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Orange_Dartwing) found around the docks. There are several dock workers wandering the docks at [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md) who will talk about the Riften Fishery, stating their opinions on their jobs. With the [Fishing](Skyrim_Fishing.md) creation, two [Fishing Supplies](Skyrim_Fishing_Supplies.md) and a [Fishing Rod](Skyrim_Fishing_Rod.md) can be found here. Fish from [Temperate Lakes](Skyrim_Fishing_(activity).md#Biomes) can be caught here.

### Riften Fishery
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/87/SR-interior-Riften_Fishery.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Riften_Fishery.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Riften_Fishery.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Riften_Fishery.jpg) Fishery interior Both doors are adept-locked only at nights. Most of the people who work in the Fishery live at [Haelga's Bunkhouse](Skyrim_Haelga%27s_Bunkhouse.md). Inside the building, a pool of water in the middle contains a large number of salmon. Around the pool is a walkway with railings around the pool, except at the southern end where there are stairs descending into the water. There are numerous open crates around the room containing salmon. With even more hanging from the railing or from ropes strung around the room above the walkway and pool. There are at least forty-five whole [salmon](Skyrim_Salmon_(dead).md) that can be harvested, available in this room, not including any in the [fish barrels](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Barrel). There are also a [river betty](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:River_Betty), [silverside perch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Silverside_Perch) and [cyrodilic spadetail](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Cyrodilic_Spadetail) in an open crate along the east wall.

There are several fish barrels around the room, these can contain any of the small ingredient fish, fillets of salmon meat, [clam meat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Clam_Meat) and [salt piles](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Salt_Pile). On top of one of these in the northeastern corner is a piece of [mudcrab chitin](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mudcrab_Chitin). There is a narrow table against the west wall with two fillets of [salmon meat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Meat), a whole salmon and [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) with a whole [slaughterfish](Skyrim_Slaughterfish.md) from which can be harvested [slaughterfish scales](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Slaughterfish_Scales), on a wooden block beside the table.

At the northern end of the room are doors into an office area with a long wooden table against the west wall holding the [business ledger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Business_Ledger), a bottle of [alto wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine), an [eidar cheese wheel](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wheel) and a [salmon steak](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Steak). Against the north wall is a copy of *[Olaf and the Dragon](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Olaf_and_the_Dragon)* on a crate and against the northeast corner is another long wooden table with an apprentice-locked [strongbox](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Strongbox), a salmon steak, a bottle of [Nord mead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead), a bottle of alto wine, a loaf of [bread](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bread), a [note](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Things_to_Do) that appears to be written by Bolli and a copy of *[Pirate King of the Abecean](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pirate_King_of_the_Abecean)*.

## Notes
- With the [Fishing](Skyrim_Fishing.md) Creation, [crab meat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Crab_Meat) can also be found in the fish barrels here. This applies to the fish barrels inside the building, and the outside ones at the dock.
- Version 2.1.2 of the [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch) marks everything on the docks surrounding the building as owned by the Fishery, regardless whether or not they were originally marked as such.

## Bugs
- The fishery is not flagged as a public location, which can lead to trespassing crimes during the related quests. - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch), version 4.3.0, fixes this bug.
