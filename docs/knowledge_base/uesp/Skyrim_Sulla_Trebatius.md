[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c9/SR-npc-Sulla_Trebatius.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Sulla_Trebatius.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Sulla_Trebatius.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Sulla_Trebatius.jpg) Sulla Trebatius [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/6f/SR-npc-Sulla_Trebatius_and_Umana_fighting.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Sulla_Trebatius_and_Umana_fighting.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Sulla_Trebatius_and_Umana_fighting.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Sulla_Trebatius_and_Umana_fighting.jpg) Sulla Trebatius and Umana fighting **Sulla Trebatius** is a hostile [Imperial](Skyrim_Imperial.md) [spellsword](Skyrim_CombatSpellsword.md) located inside [Alftand](Skyrim_Alftand.md) ruins. He was leading a group of adventurers into the ruin, hoping to be the first one to unearth its hidden treasures. You can learn of their fates by finding various remains and journals spread throughout Alftand. His group was unknowingly stalked by the [Falmer](Skyrim_Falmer.md) within and eventually captured while they were sleeping. After the group escaped their cell, Sulla refused to give up his treasure and continued down the ruin, where he can be found near the end with [Umana](Skyrim_Umana.md), hiding from a [Dwarven centurion](Skyrim_Dwarven_Centurion.md). Despite describing the [Redguard](Skyrim_Redguard.md) as his constant companion and bodyguard, it appears their unfortunate journey in the ruins has driven them both mad and they are having a heated argument over the treasure. They will both attack anyone else on sight, but if left alone he will ultimately attack Umana.

He is clad in full Imperial Armor without a helmet, though an Imperial Officer's Helmet can be found in his pack, and fights with a rare enchanted Imperial Sword in one hand and [Destruction](Skyrim_Destruction.md) magic in the other.

There is an [expedition manifest](Skyrim_Expedition_Manifest.md) written by Sulla found in the campsite outside Alftand, inside one of the shacks, lying on the ground next to a chest.

## Conversations

- **Umana**: *"Sulla, let's just get out of here. Hasn't there been enough death?"*
- **Sulla**: *"Oh, of course you want me to leave. Just waiting for me to turn my back. So you can have all the glory for yourself!"*

## Notes
- The Imperial Sword he carries is the only enchanted Imperial Sword in the entire game. Its enchantment is random, however.
- Sulla is one of three magic-using Imperial Legion members in the game; two others are found in [Helgen](Skyrim_Helgen.md) during [Unbound](Skyrim_Unbound.md), but both either die or are removed from the game upon the quest's completion.
