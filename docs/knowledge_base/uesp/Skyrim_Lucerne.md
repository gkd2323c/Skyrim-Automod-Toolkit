## Notes
- According to game data, Lucerne was at one time going to be involved in the quest [Ill Met By Moonlight](Skyrim_Ill_Met_By_Moonlight.md), and has two lines of unused dialogue related to the quest; *"We were told you may be coming."* and *"Return when you have completed the Great Hunt."*
