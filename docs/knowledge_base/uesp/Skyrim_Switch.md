The purpose of this page is to document some aspects of Skyrim that are specific to the **Nintendo Switch** and **Nintendo Switch 2** version of the game. The vast majority of gameplay is identical on all platforms (PC, Xbox, Play Station, or Switch): the quests are the same, and the [glitches](Skyrim_Glitches.md) are the same with the exception of lag issues being less prevalent on PC.

The Nintendo Switch version of [Skyrim](Skyrim_Skyrim.md) was released on November 17, 2017, with all add-ons (Dawnguard, Hearthfire, Dragonborn) included. It is based on the [Special Edition](Skyrim_Special_Edition.md) and contains gameplay features and patches incorporated into that release. The [Anniversary Edition](Skyrim_Anniversary_Edition.md) released for Switch on September 28, 2022 is available as a standalone version or as a DLC upgrade for existing owners.

The Nintendo Switch 2 version of Skyrim Anniversary Edition was released on December 9, 2025. Owners of the Anniversary Edition on Nintendo Switch can upgrade to the Switch 2 version for free. Owners of the base game on Nintendo Switch only need to purchase the Anniversary Upgrade to get a copy on Switch 2.

The Switch edition also features motion controls and compatibility with Nintendo's [amiibo figures](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/Amiibo).

## Missing PC Features
Some of the PC features that are not available on the Nintendo Switch are:

- The [console](Skyrim_Console.md). This means that in many cases bug fixes that are used by PC players are not available to Nintendo Switch players. It also means that most cheats and exploits are not available.
- The [Creation Kit](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Creation_Kit). The Creation Kit does not directly affect gameplay; rather it allows the game files to be viewed and edited. A lot of the information on the Wiki is derived from the Creation Kit, because it allows the factors controlling gameplay to be easily identified.
- Mods cannot be used on the Nintendo Switch version of the game.
- [Achievements](Skyrim_Achievements.md) are not present in the Nintendo Switch version of the game.

## Gameplay Differences
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fa/SR-misc-Switch_Bosmer_blue_eyes.jpg/200px-SR-misc-Switch_Bosmer_blue_eyes.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-misc-Switch_Bosmer_blue_eyes.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-misc-Switch_Bosmer_blue_eyes.jpg) The new blue eye color option for Bosmer [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/26/SR-place-Throat_of_the_World_Switch.jpg/200px-SR-place-Throat_of_the_World_Switch.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Throat_of_the_World_Switch.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Throat_of_the_World_Switch.jpg) The chest at the Throat of the World - [Random events](Skyrim_World_Interactions.md) added by [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md) where vampires would attack settlements have been removed from the Nintendo Switch version.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup>
- Male [Bosmer](Skyrim_Wood_Elf.md) have been given an additional eye color option at [character creation](Skyrim_Character_Creation.md): blue, [man](Lore_Man.md) -style eyes, similar to [Link](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/Link_(The_Legend_of_Zelda)).
- Motion controls are available for fine-tuned aiming with [archery](Skyrim_Archery.md) and [spellcasting](Skyrim_Magic_Overview.md).
- Gesture controls are available for melee combat. Swinging the left or right joy-con controller will attack with an equipped weapon in that hand or [block](Skyrim_Block.md) with an equipped shield. Swinging more forcefully performs a [power attack](Skyrim_Combat.md#Power_Attacks) or [shield bash](Skyrim_Block.md#Bashing).
- Gesture and controller rumble have been incorporated into [lockpicking](Skyrim_Lockpicking.md). While standard left and right thumbstick controls can still be used, holding ZL and rotating the left joy-con will rotate the [pick](Skyrim_Lockpick.md#Lockpick), while holding ZR and rotating the right joy-con will attempt to turn the lock. Rumble represents the lock as a series of "notches," vibrating as the pick rotates across them, with a slightly stronger vibrating effect on the notches surrounding the sweet spot. The more difficult the lock, the more notches are present. This can make lockpicking substantially easier than on other versions of the game.
- Saved games are now categorized for each character and grouped and displayed separately on the main menu.
- [Torturer's Hoods](Skyrim_Torturer%27s_Hood.md) are now marked as playable and can be obtained as loot from the [Torturer](Skyrim_Torturer.md) and generic [Thief](Skyrim_Thief_(wilderness).md) NPCs. They could not be obtained or used in the original release.
- New [Champion's Tunic](Skyrim_Champion%27s_Tunic.md).
- New [Hylian Shield](Skyrim_Hylian_Shield.md).
- New [Master Sword](Skyrim_Master_Sword.md).
- New [amiibo Lesser Power](Skyrim_Amiibo.md).
- New Chest at the summit of the [Throat of the World](Skyrim_Throat_of_the_World.md) containing a Champion's Tunic, Hylian Shield, and Master Sword.
- The Nintendo Switch version of the game does not include [Aloe Vera Leaves](Skyrim_Aloe_Vera_Leaves.md). - The Nintendo Switch 2 release fixes this oversight.

## Interface Differences
Although the PC keyboard and Nintendo Switch controller clearly provide very different methods of interacting with the game, most of the controls work the same way. Some differences, however, include:

- On the Nintendo Switch there is no way to toggle [Automove](Skyrim_Controls.md#Automove). - Walking vs. running is controlled by how far forward you push the left thumbstick. Slight motions of the thumbstick will cause your character to walk; pushing the thumbstick as far as it will go will cause your character to run. (This is most obvious if you use [third-person view](Skyrim_Controls.md#Change_View)).
- The Quick Save and Quick Load shortcuts, as well as a number of other shortcuts only available on the PC, are not available on the Nintendo Switch. Save, Load and Quick Save are only accessible through the [Menu](Skyrim_Controls.md#Open_Journal) options.

## Downloadable Content
As in other Special Edition releases, all three [official add-ons](Skyrim_Add-ons.md) are already included, and cannot be disabled individually.

All owners of Skyrim on Nintendo Switch received an update on September 28, 2022 adding four [Creations](Skyrim_Creation.md) at no additional charge, namely [Saints & Seducers](Skyrim_Saints_%26_Seducers.md), [Rare Curios](Skyrim_Rare_Curios.md), [Survival Mode](Skyrim_Survival_Mode.md), and [Fishing](Skyrim_Fishing.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Ae-2)</sup>

All other [Creation Club](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md) content is available as a single DLC through the [Anniversary Edition](Skyrim_Anniversary_Edition.md), which was released for Switch on September 28, 2022. The Anniversary Edition can be purchased as an upgrade for Special Edition or as a standalone version.
