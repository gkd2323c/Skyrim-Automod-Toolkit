Ramblings of Audens Avidius **by [Audens Avidius](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Audens_Avidius)** *Twisted musings of a former Captain of the Imperial Guard* *
---
I used to be somebody. I was a captain of the guard in the [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md) until that fleabitten hero decided to poke his nose in my business. I have a plan to get out of the [Imperial prison](Lore_Prison_District.md). Apparently there is a secret escape tunnel used by the royal family. When I get out I'm going to settle the score with that hero. Then I'll take care of those two snitches Ruslan and Luronk. The sweetest of all will be Itius Hayn. I'll make him squeal first.
