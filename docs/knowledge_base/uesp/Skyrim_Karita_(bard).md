[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/11/SR-npc-Karita_%28bard%29.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Karita_%28bard%29.jpg)](File_SR-npc-Karita_(bard).jpg.md) [](File_SR-npc-Karita_(bard).jpg.md) Karita **Karita** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [bard](Skyrim_Bard.md) who works at the [Windpeak Inn](Skyrim_Windpeak_Inn.md) in [Dawnstar](Skyrim_Dawnstar.md). She is the daughter of innkeeper [Thoring](Skyrim_Thoring.md). Her mother died two years ago and she is deeply concerned about her father being left a widower, working around the clock without ever going out. Karita occasionally [sings](Skyrim_Bardic_Performances.md) for the regulars, as her mother was a graduate of the [Bards College](Skyrim_Bards_College.md) in [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md) and taught her all her songs. According to Thoring, she has 'her mother's voice'.

Karita wears a set of [tavern clothes](Skyrim_Clothing.md#000d191f) along with a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Clothing.md#000d1921). She wields an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) and carries her [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_the_Windpeak_Inn) to the inn along with a selection of [common loot and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common).

## Related Quests
- **[Rise in the East](Skyrim_Rise_in_the_East.md)**: Help the [East Empire Company](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:East_Empire_Company) take care of some [pirates](Skyrim_Blood_Horker.md).

## Dialogue
When you pass by her:

*"I envy you travelers. Dreams aren't affecting anyone not from here."* *"The Windpeak Inn features the finest bard in all of Dawnstar. Me."* *"Sorry if my father seems a bit distracted. A lot's happened."* *"Let me know if you need a drink. That's what we're here for."* If spoken to she can be asked a few questions:

**Where'd you learn to play?** *"My mother taught me. She was a Bard, from the college in Solitude. I hear they accept most people who apply, if you're ever interested."* **Do you work here all day?** *"Yes. Ever since mother died a few winters ago. I play songs and help with the drinks when I have the time."* If you have not [assassinated](Skyrim_Hail_Sithis!.md) the [Emperor](Skyrim_Emperor_Titus_Mede_II_(real).md), she will warn you about the entrance to the [Dawnstar Sanctuary](Skyrim_Dawnstar_Sanctuary.md):

*"Whatever you do, stay away from that Black Door. Being near it chills you to the bones."* If you have, however, she will instead say:

*"I've heard that shadowy figures are being seen coming in and out of the Black Door. Gods help us."*

## Conversations
Karita regularly talks to [Frida](Skyrim_Frida.md) and her father at the inn:

**Frida**: *"Dawnstar has always held its own against the bandits and wild animals of [the Pale](Skyrim_The_Pale.md)."*
**Karita**: *"Did you ever have to fight off bandits, Frida? Like in your stories?"*
**Frida**: *"Why, I once fought alongside your father when we were both younger. Saved him from getting his fool head sliced off."*
**Karita**: *"Who were you fighting against?"*
**Frida**: *"Oh, dearie, it was so long ago. But I do remember the celebration we had afterwards. Mead never tasted so good."* **Frida**: *"Did I ever tell you about the day when [Skald the Elder](Skyrim_Skald.md) was Skald the Younger?"*
**Karita**: *"I can't imagine Skald ever being young."*
**Frida**: *"Oh, he was young, and handsome. I had half a mind to go after him, myself, before I met my husband."*
**Karita**: *"Frida, you aren't saying...."*
**Frida**: *"I was a beauty in my day, dearie. Don't act so surprised."* **Karita**: *"Father do you want to talk? About Mother?"*
**Thoring**: *"No, Karita. I don't think I could."*
**Karita**: *"I'm sure she would want you to be happy, father. Entertaining the guests and drinking and making your lewd jokes like before."*
**Thoring**: *"I'm sorry. I just don't feel up to entertaining anyone just now."* **Karita**: *"I was wondering, father. Maybe you should take a day off and head into town. I could watch the inn while you're away."*
**Thoring**: *"Why would I do that? There's work to be done here."*
**Karita**: *"It'll do you good! Maybe you could visit [Irgnir](Skyrim_Irgnir.md) or [Fruki](Skyrim_Fruki.md) on your way out? I've seen them looking at you when they come in for drinks...."*
**Thoring**: *"Why would they be looking at me? Are they upset about the service?"*
**Karita**: *"Oh, never mind."*
## Notes
- She is referred to as **Kera** by game data.
- Despite what her outfit and dialogue may imply, Karita isn't actually set up as a server, and cannot give you any drinks or barter with you.
