[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5e/ON-map-Redolent_Loam.jpg/200px-ON-map-Redolent_Loam.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-map-Redolent_Loam.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-map-Redolent_Loam.jpg) Map of Redolent Loam circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/2c/ON-place-Redolent_Loam.jpg/200px-ON-place-Redolent_Loam.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Redolent_Loam.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Redolent_Loam.jpg) Redolent Loam **Redolent Loam** is a region that consists of the western portion of [Deshaan](Lore_Deshaan.md). The Redolent Loam houses the capital of the Great [House Hlaalu](Lore_House_Hlaalu.md), the city-state of [Narsis](Lore_Narsis.md). The center of the region is [Lake Hlaalu](Lore_Lake_Hlaalu.md) which is north of Narsis. It borders the [Varanis](Lore_Varanis.md) region to the north and the [Lagomere](Lore_Lagomere.md) region to the east. The Obsidian Gorge takes up the northern road of the Loam, leading into the city of [Mournhold](Lore_Mournhold.md).
