[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/ad/SR-place-Tel_Mithryn_Steward%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-place-Tel_Mithryn_Steward%27s_House.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Tel_Mithryn_Steward%27s_House.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Tel_Mithryn_Steward%27s_House.jpg) Tel Mithryn Steward's House **Tel Mithryn Steward's House** is the home of the steward [Varona Nelas](Skyrim_Varona_Nelas.md) or [Drovas Relvi](Skyrim_Drovas_Relvi.md) in [Tel Mithryn](Skyrim_Tel_Mithryn_(settlement).md).

It is one of the four mushroom structures in the settlement, between [Tel Mithryn's tower](Skyrim_Tel_Mithryn_(tower).md) to the east and [Tel Mithryn Kitchen](Skyrim_Tel_Mithryn_Kitchen.md) to the northwest.

## Tel Mithryn Steward's House
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/99/SR-interior-Tel_Mithryn_Steward%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Tel_Mithryn_Steward%27s_House.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Tel_Mithryn_Steward%27s_House.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Tel_Mithryn_Steward%27s_House.jpg) Tel Mithryn Steward's House interior The entrance leads into a small circular room comprising the entire interior. To the north is a crate, a narrow table, an unowned single bed, a sideboard holding a bottle of [wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wine) and a copy of *[Deathbrand](Skyrim_Deathbrand_(book).md)*, and another bottle of wine and an [empty wine bottle](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Empty_Wine_Bottle) on the floor in front of the sideboard. To the east are two crates, a set of shelves holding a [mammoth tusk](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mammoth_Tusk), a [horker tusk](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Horker_Tusk), six empty wine bottles, a [swamp fungal pod](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Swamp_Fungal_Pod), a bowl of [powdered mammoth tusk](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Powdered_Mammoth_Tusk), and a sample of [canis root](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Canis_Root), a narrow table holding another empty wine bottle, and another set of shelves holding a crate, five more empty wine bottles, and seven common books. To the south are two chairs, a small table holding a bottle of wine, and a [chest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chest). In the middle of the room are two chairs, two empty wine bottles, and a bottle of wine around a lit brazier.

## Notes
- Although [Neloth](Skyrim_Neloth.md) tells you he will reserve a bed and a chest for your use after completing the quest [Old Friends](Skyrim_Old_Friends.md), there will still be no chest clearly reserved for your use, and the bed in the steward's house will remain the only unowned bed or bedroll in the settlement. However, the unowned bed here will often be occupied by the current steward.

## Bugs
- If [Reluctant Steward](Skyrim_Reluctant_Steward.md) is finished and the [Sun Stone](Skyrim_Sun_Stone.md) has been cleansed, Drovas's sleep package will always be active, and therefore he will be found sleeping indefinitely here. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Dragonborn Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Dragonborn_Patch), version 1.0.5, fixes this bug.
