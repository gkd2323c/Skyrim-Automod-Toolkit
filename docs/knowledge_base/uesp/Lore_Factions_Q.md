## Quarra Clan
The [Quarra Clan](Lore_Quarra_Clan.md) is one of the three major [vampire](Lore_Vampire.md) bloodlines found in [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md) and the [Telvanni Peninsula](Lore_Telvanni_Peninsula.md).

## Quenching Grail
The [Quenching Grail](Lore_Quenching_Grail.md) was an [Imperial](Lore_Imperial.md) religious order in the [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md). They were devoted to worship of the [Aedra](Lore_Aedra.md).

At some point in the First Era, the embattled clergy of the Quenching Grail constructed the citadel of Pantherfang Chapel along the banks of the [Panther River](Lore_Panther_River.md) in [Blackwood](Lore_Blackwood.md). This fortress served as a bastion of faith, and within its walls the Quenching Grail built a grand cathedral. Although the order was eventually disbanded, Pantherfang Chapel continued to stand for many centuries.

Circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), Pantherfang Chapel was available for purchase as a private residence. By [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), all that remained at the site of the former cathedral were the ruins of an abandoned [Legion](Lore_Imperial_Legion.md) fort known as Fort Redwater.

## Quicktalon Clan
[Quicktalon Clan](Lore_Quicktalon_Clan.md) was one of numerous [clans](Lore_Reachman_Clans.md) of [Reachfolk](Lore_Reachfolk.md). They were an ancient clan that inhabited [the Reach](Lore_The_Reach.md) and was sundered by [Carinar the Sharp](Lore_Carinar.md)'s patricide. Legendary [Vateshran](Lore_Vateshrans.md) [Aydolan](Lore_Aydolan.md) was born as a member of the Quicktalon Clan.

## Quill Circus
The Quill Circus is an active organization in the province of [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md). It has been around for as long as anyone can remember, and is known to have various acts like plays and sideshows, and employ people like acrobats and archers.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-.3DTBA1-1)</sup> In [3E 405](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_405), the circus show was run by the ringmaster, [Chulmore Quill](Lore_Chulmore_Quill.md), who was an affiliate with the [Dark Brotherhood](Lore_Dark_Brotherhood.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DFQuill-2)</sup>
