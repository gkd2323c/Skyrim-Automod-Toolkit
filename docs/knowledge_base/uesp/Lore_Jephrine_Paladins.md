# Jephrine Paladins

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d2/ON-prerelease-Jephrine_Paladin_Style.jpg/200px-ON-prerelease-Jephrine_Paladin_Style.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-prerelease-Jephrine_Paladin_Style.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-prerelease-Jephrine_Paladin_Style.jpg) Jephrine Paladins [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/89/ON-item-armor-Jephrine_Paladin_Female.jpg/200px-ON-item-armor-Jephrine_Paladin_Female.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-item-armor-Jephrine_Paladin_Female.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-item-armor-Jephrine_Paladin_Female.jpg) A Jephrine Paladin Jephrine Paladins, also known as the **[Paladins](Lore_Paladins.md) of Jephre**<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AI-1)</sup> are an [Altmeri](Lore_Altmer.md) militant order of followers of [Jephre](Lore_Jephre.md). Their attire is inspired by [Indriks](Lore_Indrik.md),<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-JP-2)</sup> creatures that bear a relation to their patron deity.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-YSS-3)</sup> They are known to be the protectors of the wild.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-JP-2)</sup> It is believed that whenever a paladin of Jephre performs an act the God of Song and Forest finds worthy, an infant autumnal Indrik takes its shaky steps into the world.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AI-1)</sup>
