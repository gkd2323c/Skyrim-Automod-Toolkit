[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/da/AR-place-Roseguard.jpg/200px-AR-place-Roseguard.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-place-Roseguard.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-place-Roseguard.jpg) Roseguard (Arena) Roseguard (historically called **Rosgard**)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RGMap-1)</sup><sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-HRP-2)</sup> is a town found in southern [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md), on the eastern shores of [Hubalajad's Bay](Lore_Hubalajad%27s_Bay.md) near the city-state of [Taneth](Lore_Taneth.md).

Around [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), a [Breton](Lore_Breton.md) thief went by the alias the "Lark of Rosgard", named after the town.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ATt Ca T-3)</sup> The town had still gone by the name Rosgard since at least as late as the [Tiber War](Lore_Tiber_War.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RGMap-1)</sup> During the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md) in the late- [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), the town of Roseguard was an active settlement. It was ruled by Prince Febun and had a rivalry with [Riverpoint](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Riverpoint).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-AR-4)</sup> Roseguard is neighbored by several settlements, including [Chaseguard](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Chaseguard) to the southeast, [Corten Mont](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Corten_Mont) to the northeast, and Taneth to the northwest.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ARMap-5)</sup>

A conflict took place in Roseguard sometime in the [Fourth Era](Lore_Fourth_Era.md), roughly before [4E 180](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_180), concurrent with [Hammerfell's Resistance](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Hammerfell%27s_Resistance) against the Third [Aldmeri Dominion](Lore_Aldmeri_Dominion.md). A soldier named Angarion the Bold was a named champion of this battle.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-BLAngarion-6)</sup>
