[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/05/MW-place-Mount_Kand.jpg/200px-MW-place-Mount_Kand.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-place-Mount_Kand.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-place-Mount_Kand.jpg) Mount Kand ca [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427) **Mount Kand**, also known as the **Mountain of Fear**,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup> is a mountain in the [Molag Amur](Lore_Molag_Amur.md) region that is located northwest of [Molag Mar](Lore_Molag_Mar.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESO-3)</sup> There is a shrine inside a cave atop Mount Kand which serves as the site of one of the [Tribunal Temple](Lore_Tribunal_Temple.md)'s many pilgrimages.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup> Around [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the mountain was home to the Shrine of Pride, one of the shrines of the Seven Graces. Sometime before [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427), this shrine was relocated to the [Red Mountain](Lore_Red_Mountain.md) region.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESO-3)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup>
