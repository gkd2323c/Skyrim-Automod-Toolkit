[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/12/SR-npc-Lushak_gra-Ragdam.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Lushak_gra-Ragdam.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Lushak_gra-Ragdam.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Lushak_gra-Ragdam.jpg) Lushak gra-Ragdam **Lushak gra-Ragdam** is a hostile [Orc](Skyrim_Orc.md) necromancer hailing from the island of [Olenveld](Lore_Olenveld.md) off the coast of Skyrim. She can be found within [Brittleshin Pass](Skyrim_Brittleshin_Pass.md) during the related quest.

Lushak carries a copy of [*Arkay the Enemy*](Skyrim_Arkay_the_Enemy.md) and [*The Black Arts On Trial*](Skyrim_The_Black_Arts_On_Trial.md).

You will need to kill her and her companion [Ivara of Olenveld](Skyrim_Ivara_of_Olenveld.md) during the related quest.
