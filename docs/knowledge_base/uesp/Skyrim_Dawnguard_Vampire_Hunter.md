[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/ea/SR-npc-Dawnguard_Vampire_Hunter.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Dawnguard_Vampire_Hunter.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Dawnguard_Vampire_Hunter.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Dawnguard_Vampire_Hunter.jpg) Dawnguard Vampire Hunter The **Dawnguard Vampire Hunter** is an [Imperial](Skyrim_Imperial.md) [bandit](Skyrim_Bandit_(class).md) who can be [randomly encountered](Skyrim_World_Interactions.md) if you have accepted [Lord Harkon's](Skyrim_Lord_Harkon.md) gift of [vampirism](Skyrim_Vampirism.md), for commited betryal to Dawnguard and completed [The Bloodstone Chalice](Skyrim_The_Bloodstone_Chalice.md). He carries [orders](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Writ_of_Dawn) to "exact justice" on you for being a vampire. However, he don't appear at all if you are a member of the [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard_(faction).md).

## Notes
- You can still encounter the Dawnguard Vampire Hunter even after finishing the Dawnguard questline for Volkihar Clan Vampires.

## Dialogue
When he attacks you, he'll use three unique lines of dialogue in addition to standard combat dialogue:

- *"Halt, vampire!"*
- *"Your end has come!"*
- *"Light take you!"*
