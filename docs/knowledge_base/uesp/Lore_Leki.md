# Leki

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/78/RG-misc-Leki.jpg/200px-RG-misc-Leki.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:RG-misc-Leki.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:RG-misc-Leki.jpg) Illustration of Leki from the [Saintsport](Lore_Saintsport.md) wayshrine<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RH-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-RGCompanion-2)</sup> This article is about the goddess. For the Ansei warrior, see [Makela Leki](Lore_Makela_Leki.md).

Leki, also known as the **Saint of the Spirit Sword**<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-VOF-3)</sup><sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RH-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-RGCompanion-2)</sup> is the goddess of aberrant swordsmanship and divine daughter of [Tall Papa](Lore_Ruptga.md),<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-VOF-3)</sup> who is said to reside in the [Far Shores](Lore_Far_Shores.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONRada-4)</sup> She is said to be one of the most popular gods in [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-PGE1Ham-5)</sup>

The Na-Totambu of [Yokuda](Lore_Yokuda.md) warred to a standstill during the mythic era to decide who would lead the charge against the [Lefthanded Elves](Lore_Lefthanded_Elves.md). Their swordmasters, though, were so skilled in the Best Known Cuts as to be matched evenly. Leki introduced the Ephemeral Feint (a [Sword-Singing](Lore_Sword-singers.md) technique, acording to some stories);<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-Vivec Meeting-6)</sup> afterwards, a victor emerged, and the war with the Elves began.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-VOF-3)</sup>

According to legend, one of Leki's miracles resulted in the founding of a settlement in the [Alik'r](Lore_Alik%27r.md). [Leki's Blade](Lore_Leki%27s_Blade.md) was established after she dueled with [Rada al-Saran](Lore_Rada_al-Saran.md), a great warrior who claimed to match the gods in the way of the sword.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ONMF-7)</sup> [Sai Sahan](Lore_Sai_Sahan.md) came from a long line of Redguard swordmaster nobles, and was "something more than mortal man", descending from Leki the Yokudan Sword-Saint.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Varen-8)</sup> A lock of hair supposedly belonging to Leki the Sword-Saint was found preserved inside small, exquisitely carved [jadeite](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Jadeite) box and kept as a relic.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-Contraband J-9)</sup>
