## Redweed
This plant grows in the shade beneath cliffs and near rocks. It was used by an alchemist during the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md) to create a potion that would counteract the effects of fumes that had suddenly filled the Deepcrag Den kwama mine.

## Rice
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9d/OB-flora-Rice.jpg/200px-OB-flora-Rice.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-flora-Rice.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-flora-Rice.jpg) Rice Plants Rice can be found in food larders throughout Cyrodiil. Rice plants are sometimes cultivated in alchemy gardens. Rice was once a primary export in the [Nibenay](Lore_Nibenay.md) and around the [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-PGE1-2)</sup> Before the arrival of [Umbriel](Lore_Umbriel.md) in [4E 48](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_48), the land south of [Lilmoth](Lore_Lilmoth.md) was populated by rice plantations. One such village was the Hereguard Plantation, which at the time was one of the last farms run by [Bretons](Lore_Breton.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TIC-3)</sup><sup>:61</sup>

**Found in:** - [Oblivion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Rice)

**Harvested Ingredient:** [Rice](Lore_Rice_(alchemy).md)
## Roland's Tear
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/cf/MW-quest-Roland%27s_Tear.jpg/200px-MW-quest-Roland%27s_Tear.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-quest-Roland%27s_Tear.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-quest-Roland%27s_Tear.jpg) Roland's Tear Roland's Tear is a rare variant of [Gold Kanet](Lore_Gold_Kanet.md) once found exclusively in small amounts in the Ascadian Isles, near the ruins of [Ald Sotha](Lore_Ald_Sotha.md). It was sacred to the [Tribunal](Lore_Tribunal.md), and was sacrificially burned at shrines. It was also prized by alchemists for its wondrous powers when used in potion making. A single red-petalled specimen was held by the [Aldmeri Dominion](Lore_Aldmeri_Dominion.md) in a secure vault in [Marbruk](Lore_Marbruk.md) circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582). It is unclear if any populations of the flower remain following the [Red Year](Lore_Red_Year.md), when [Baar Dau](Lore_Baar_Dau.md)'s impact destroyed the surrounding area and created [Scathing Bay](Lore_Scathing_Bay.md).

**Found in:** - [Morrowind](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Morrowind), [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Online)

**Harvested Ingredient:** [Roland's Tear](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Roland%27s_Tear_(ingredient)), [Roland's Tears](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Stonefalls_Quest_Items#Roland.27s_Tears) (ESO)

## Rose
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d4/ON-place-Everlasting_Fair_05.jpg/200px-ON-place-Everlasting_Fair_05.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Everlasting_Fair_05.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Everlasting_Fair_05.jpg) Fountain with red roses planted in it A flowering plant known for its scent and thorns. Roses come in a variety of colors, and have uses in cooking and alchemy. Both [Azura](Lore_Azura.md) and [Sanguine](Lore_Sanguine.md) have associations with roses.

The God of War, [Ebonarm](Lore_Ebonarm.md) is associated with the red rose. He is said to possess an [ebony](Lore_Ebony.md) tower shield with a red rose emblazoned on it. In the legend, *[The Ebon Arm](Lore_The_Ebon_Arm.md)*, it is said he appeared before a bloody battle and its combatants stopped to bow before him. When both sides realized their battle was baseless, they tended to their wounded and ceased their fighting. Once they left, the land and rivers began to clear and a single rose bush grew near the grave of a fallen hero.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TEA-4)</sup> A rose bush is also said to have grown where Ebonarm stood on that day of the battle. It lied in a huge field near the [Redguard](Lore_Redguard.md) village of Granitsta.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-OH-5)</sup>

The Order of the Red Rose was one of the common temples found across [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) during the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ARTemples-6)</sup>

**Found in:** - [Daggerfall](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Daggerfall:Daggerfall), [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Online)

**Harvested Ingredient:** - [Rose (Various Colors)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Daggerfall:Ingredients), [Rose](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Rose)

## Rose-of-Archon
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e6/ON-quest-A_Rose%27s_Beauty.jpg/200px-ON-quest-A_Rose%27s_Beauty.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-quest-A_Rose%27s_Beauty.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-quest-A_Rose%27s_Beauty.jpg) Roses-of-Archon, found in Archon's Grove A flower sacred to the goddess [Mara](Lore_Mara.md), these flowers are only found on [Summerset](Lore_Summerset_Isle.md) within [Archon's Grove](Lore_Archon%27s_Grove.md). The Rose was discovered by the poet Nenamil, Archon of Song, who realized upon seeing them that Mara was beauty incarnate. The [Sapiarchs](Lore_Sapiarchs.md) use these flowers in a religious ritual during their 58th Day of the Divine Reverence of Mara.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-7)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-8)</sup>

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Online)
