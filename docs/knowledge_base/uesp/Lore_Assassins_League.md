# Assassins League

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/1f/ON-crown_store-Assassins_League_Style.jpg/200px-ON-crown_store-Assassins_League_Style.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Assassins_League_Style.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Assassins_League_Style.jpg) Assassins League equipment The Assassins League is a guild of killers for hire. It is a secret society of assassins, distinct from the [Dark Brotherhood](Lore_Dark_Brotherhood.md) in that it operates more like a business than a cult. For its part, the League claims that the Brotherhood is merely a myth.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-CM34-1)</sup>

League Assassins are held to certain standards, such as making use of League weapons and equipment.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-CM34-1)</sup> Tainted blood is used in the crafting of these items.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup> Joining requires the use of equipment tailored to League specifications, as set out by a member known only as the Gray Maybe in her writings.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-CM34-1)</sup> When an Assassin is assigned a target, the League use a particular saying: "Pass over them with the Hand of Death." This Hand of Death symbol is similar to the [Black Hand](Lore_Black_Hand.md) of the Dark Brotherhood, and appears as a motif on League arms.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-CM34-1)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESO-3)</sup>

The Gray Maybe's writings could be found in the [Gold Coast](Lore_Gold_Coast.md) region of [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md) during the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md) of the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESO-3)</sup>
