[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/41/SR-item-Trueflame.jpg/200px-SR-item-Trueflame.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Trueflame.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Trueflame.jpg) Trueflame [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d2/SR-item-Trueflame_02.jpg/200px-SR-item-Trueflame_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Trueflame_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Trueflame_02.jpg) An unearthly flame covers the weapon when wielded Trueflame is a [sword](Skyrim_Sword.md) artifact. Tempering Trueflame requires a [Dwarven Metal Ingot](Skyrim_Dwarven_Metal_Ingot.md) and the [Arcane Blacksmith](Skyrim_Arcane_Blacksmith.md) perk. Having the Dwarven Smithing perk doubles the improvement.

It first appears as the Unenchanted Weapon found on the body of [Hand Kenro Hlan](Skyrim_Hand_Kenro_Hlan.md) at [Fahlbtharz](Skyrim_Fahlbtharz.md) Forge, and a quest must be undertaken to restore the weapon.

## Notes
- Trueflame has previously appeared in *[Tribunal](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Tribunal:Trueflame)* and [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Artifacts#Trueflame). For more information, see the [lore article](Lore_Trueflame.md).
- Trueflame has no usage cost, so its charges will never drain.
- Because it has no usage cost, Trueflame does not benefit from the [Augmented Flames](Skyrim_Augmented_Flames.md) perk.
