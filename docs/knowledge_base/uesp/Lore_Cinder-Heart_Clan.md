# Cinder-Heart Clan

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/41/ON-place-Cinder-Heart_Clan_Camp.jpg/200px-ON-place-Cinder-Heart_Clan_Camp.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Cinder-Heart_Clan_Camp.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Cinder-Heart_Clan_Camp.jpg) Cinder-Heart Clan Camp ca. 2E 582 The Cinder-Heart Clan was a prominent [clan](Lore_Reachman_Clans.md) of [Reachfolk](Lore_Reachfolk.md) that lived in [the Reach](Lore_The_Reach.md), a region in western [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) during the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md) in [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md). They were a warlike clan often found near Markarth.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-OTCOTR-1)</sup> In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) their camp was located to the northwest of [Hroldan Ring](Lore_Hroldan_Ring.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-CHCC-2)</sup>

The Reachfolk of the Cinder-Heart Clan dedicated themselves to one of the [Great Spirits of the Reach](Lore_Daedric_Prince.md), [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md). They were infamous for their unique method of creating [Briarhearts](Lore_Briarheart.md). They typically filled the victim's empty chest cavity with hot coals during the ritual.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-OTCOTR-1)</sup>

They erected a shrine to Molag Bal, known as the Cinder-Heart Ritual Stone at the southwestern part of their camp.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-CHCC-2)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/84/ON-npc-Briarheart_Commander.jpg/200px-ON-npc-Briarheart_Commander.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Briarheart_Commander.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Briarheart_Commander.jpg) A Briarheart commander of the Cinder-Heart Clan ca. 2E 582 [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/18/ON-place-Cinder-Heart_Clan_Camp_02.jpg/200px-ON-place-Cinder-Heart_Clan_Camp_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Cinder-Heart_Clan_Camp_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Cinder-Heart_Clan_Camp_02.jpg) Members of the Cinder-Heart Clan praying to Molag Bal ca. 2E 582 The Cinder-Heart Clan was considered one of the more prominent clans of the Reach as of [2E 568](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_568).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-OTCOTR-1)</sup> As of [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) members of the Cinder-Heart Clan were hostile to both outsiders and their Reachfolk neighbors.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-CHCC-2)</sup> Their warriors were led by Briarheart commanders.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-BC-3)</sup>

In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) [Arana](Lore_Arana.md) of [Wayward Guardians](Lore_Wayward_Guardians.md) requested the [ally](Lore_The_Vestige.md) of the [House Ravenwatch](Lore_House_Ravenwatch.md) to gather ingredients required for the ritual to [Hircine](Lore_Hircine.md) in order to seal the alliance between the factions. One of the necessary components of the ritual was a [briar seed](Lore_Briar_Heart_Tree.md). The Vestige obtained the seed from the tree of the Cinder-Heart Clan.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-BOTR-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-MQI-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Arana-6)</sup>
