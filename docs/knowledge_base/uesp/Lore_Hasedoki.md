# Hasedoki

| Hasedoki | | | |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Race | Unknown | Gender | Male |

Hasedoki was a renowned wizard of his time period. He was said to have been very competitive, and sought to find someone who could challenge him, but he had no equal. So many feared his power that he bound his [soul](Lore_Souls.md) into his [staff](Lore_Staff_of_Hasedoki.md), where it remained.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TL-1)</sup> Those that acquired the staff were seemingly cursed to live a life that mirrored Hasedoki's, for contentment would morph into boredom, and love would fade into loneliness.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TRL-2)</sup> In [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), Hasedoki's soul was targeted by necromancers from [Olenveld](Lore_Olenveld.md), who acquired the staff and attempted to exchange the soul with the [Soul Cairn](Lore_Soul_Cairn.md)'s [Ideal Masters](Lore_Ideal_Masters.md). The [Last Dragonborn](Lore_Last_Dragonborn.md) managed to stop their ritual.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup>
