[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/0e/ON-misc-Abolisher.png/200px-ON-misc-Abolisher.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-misc-Abolisher.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-misc-Abolisher.png) Abolisher Abolisher is an artifact created by the Daedric Prince Boethiah to aid in [Ithelia](Lore_Ithelia.md)'s imprisonment, described as a pitted blade with an edge that can cut the veil between worlds.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Abolisher Quest Items-1)</sup> It held the power to open [portals](Lore_Portals.md), destroy them, or reopen sealed portals. In addition it could be used to sever the link the [Many Paths](Lore_Many_Paths.md) provided to a different reality, rendering it inaccessible.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Ithelia-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Mora-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Leramil-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Beragon-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Alea-6)</sup> Abolisher was ultimately destroyed when it was used as part of a spell originating from [Hermaeus Mora](Lore_Hermaeus_Mora.md), which was provided to [the Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md) in order to repair the [reality tears](Lore_Fate.md) created by Ithelia's power that were threatening to unravel all existence. After the spell was cast Abolisher became a normal sword, its Daedric power fully expended.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Scruut-7)</sup>
