[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b6/SR-npc-Virkmund.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Virkmund.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Virkmund.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Virkmund.jpg) Virkmund **Virkmund** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [child](Skyrim_Child.md) who lives in [Morthal](Skyrim_Morthal.md) with his father, [Thonnir](Skyrim_Thonnir.md). He's waiting for his [mother](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Laelette_the_Vampire) to return home someday, even though his father tells him not to.

He wears [child's clothes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Child%27s_Clothes) and a pair of [shoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shoes#000e80a8), and carries a [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Thonnir%27s_House) to his [father's house](Skyrim_Thonnir%27s_House.md).

Virkmund wakes up at 8am and heads out to seek his friend [Agni](Skyrim_Agni.md). After playing for 2 hours he visits [Lami](Skyrim_Lami.md) in the [Thaumaturgist's Hut](Skyrim_Thaumaturgist%27s_Hut.md) for 6 hours before heading back home, where he remains until his bedtime at 9pm.

## Dialogue
He is very concerned about the whereabouts of his mother, and constantly thinks of her.

*"Have you seen my mother? I'm still waiting for her to come home."* *"Papa says that I shouldn't keep waiting. Why not? Mama has to come home some time."* *"Who are the Stormcloaks, and why would mama want to spend time with them instead of me?"*
## Notes
- He is referred to as **Stofleid** by game data.
- Despite his mother being a [Breton](Skyrim_Breton.md), Virkmund is a Nord.
- Even after the completion of [Laid to Rest](Skyrim_Laid_to_Rest.md), Virkmund's dialogue never changes, and there's no option to inform him about his mother.
