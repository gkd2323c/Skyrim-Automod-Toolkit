Atlas of Dragons, 2E 373 **by [Brother Mathnan](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Brother_Mathnan)** *A glossary of Skyrim's dragons*
---
*Herein is recorded the list of known [Dragon](Skyrim_Dragon.md), both living and dead, including those slain by the Dragonguard since the time of its founding, as well as those slain in earlier ages, where they can be identified. Unfortunately, only a few of the dragons slain by our [Akaviri](Lore_Akavir.md) predecessors during the Crusade were recorded and thus this list is sadly incomplete.*

---

### Deceased by Report
**[Nahagliiv](Skyrim_Nahagliiv.md)** - Local tales name him as the dragon buried in the mound west of [Rorikstead](Skyrim_Rorikstead.md). No date associated with his death, although almost surely dates to the [Dragon War](Lore_Dragon_War.md) era.

**[Odahviing](Skyrim_Odahviing.md)** - Records dating to the Crusade of interrogation of captured [Dragon Cultists](Skyrim_Dragon_Cult.md) indicate that this dragon was buried in a mound in the southeast of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md), near [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md).

**[Sahloknir](Skyrim_Sahloknir.md)** - Local legends claim this is the dragon buried in the mound near [Kyne's Grove](Skyrim_Kynesgrove.md), slain by the [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) hero [Jorg Helmbolg](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Jorg_Helmbolg) in the [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md).

**[Viinturuth](Skyrim_Viinturuth.md)** - Death dating back to Dragon War era, according to documents recovered from Dragon Cult temples which record his burial near [Lake Yorgrim](Skyrim_Lake_Yorgrim.md).

**[Vuljotnaak](Skyrim_Vuljotnaak.md)** - Death dating back to Dragon War or just after, according to recovered Dragon Cult documents, which record his burial in a mound near [Granite Hill](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Granite_Hill).

### Slain by the Dragonguard
**[Grahkrindrog](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Grahkrindrog)** - Slain in [2E 184](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_184) after perpetrating great slaughter in [Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold_(region).md) and [Eastmarch](Skyrim_Eastmarch.md). Name confirmed with assistance from the [College](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(faction).md) mages.

**[Krahjotdaan](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Krahjotdaan)** - Slain in [1E 2871](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_2871) in the southern [Jerall Mountains](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Jerall_Mountains), name confirmed by the dragon's own account.

**Unnamed Dragons** - Numbering 12, as recorded in the Annals dating back to the founding of [Sky Haven Temple](Skyrim_Sky_Haven_Temple.md).

### Known to Live
**[Ahbiilok](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Ahbiilok)** - Sightings dating back to the early years of the [Dragonguard](Lore_Dragonguard.md) throughout the northern Jerralls *[[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)]*. Multiple attempts to kill him have failed. He is believed to be lairing somewhere in [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md).

**[Mirmulnir](Skyrim_Mirmulnir.md)** - Last sighted in [the Reach](Skyrim_The_Reach.md) in [2E 212](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_212).

**[Nahfahlaar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Nahfahlaar)** - Repeated alliances with mortal protectors which have prevented his elimination. His last known protector was the King [Casimir II](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Casimir_II) of [Wayrest](Lore_Wayrest_(kingdom).md), which the Dragonguard successfully ended in [2E 369](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_369). He escaped and current location is unknown.

**[Paarthurnax](Skyrim_Paarthurnax_(dragon).md)** - The legendary lieutenant of [Alduin](Lore_Alduin.md) in the Dragon War. He is now known to lair on the [Throat of the World](Skyrim_Throat_of_the_World.md) under the protection of the [Greybeards](Lore_Greybeards.md) of [High Hrothgar](Skyrim_High_Hrothgar.md). Master Araidh continues the established policy of avoiding direct confrontation with the Greybeards while waiting for an opportunity to exact justice upon him.
