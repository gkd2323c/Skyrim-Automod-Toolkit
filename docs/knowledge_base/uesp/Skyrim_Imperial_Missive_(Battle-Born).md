Imperial Missive **by [General Tullius](Skyrim_General_Tullius.md)** *About the Thalmor imprisonment of a Whiterun citizen*
---
It has come to my attention that inquiries have been made as to the whereabouts of one [Thorald Gray-Mane](Skyrim_Thorald_Gray-Mane.md). It is my duty to inform you that [Thalmor](Skyrim_Thalmor.md) agents have taken possession of the prisoner and have escorted him to [Northwatch Keep](Skyrim_Northwatch_Keep.md). I don't think I need to elaborate. It is in everyone's best interest if the matter is dropped entirely. I trust there will be no further inquiries as to this matter. [Gen. Tullius](Skyrim_General_Tullius.md)
