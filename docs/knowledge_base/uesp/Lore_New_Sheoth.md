[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/6c/SI-place-New_Sheoth.jpg/200px-SI-place-New_Sheoth.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SI-place-New_Sheoth.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SI-place-New_Sheoth.jpg) New Sheoth New Sheoth is the ever-changing capital of the [Shivering Isles](Lore_Shivering_Isles.md) and the seat of [Lord Sheogorath](Lore_Sheogorath.md). It is located in the eastern part of the Isles, at the feet of the Jester's Spine Mountains. It is placed on the border between [Mania](Lore_Mania.md) and [Dementia](Lore_Dementia_(place).md), the two halves of the Isles. The city itself is divided into two districts, **Bliss** on the Manic side and **Crucible** on the Demented.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-GNS-1)</sup> New Sheoth Palace overlooks the city; contained within is the Palace of Sheogorath, as well as the Houses of Mania and Dementia. The [Sacellum Arden-Sul](Lore_Arden-Sul.md) is located at the base of the Great Torch. The ruins of Xirethard are mostly located beneath the city. Like everywhere in the Isles, root tunnels run deep below the city. Of particular interest is [the Fountainhead](Lore_The_Fountainhead.md), where the insanity of the realm's inhabitants settles to supply the waters of the Font of Madness.

Every era, during the [Greymarch](Lore_Greymarch.md), the city is destroyed by [Jyggalag](Lore_Jyggalag.md) and the forces of Order along with the rest of the Isles. Prior to the building of New Sheoth, [Cylarne](Lore_Cylarne.md) was the capital of the realm.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup> New Sheoth survived the Greymarch of [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), when the [Hero of Kvatch](Lore_Hero_of_Kvatch.md) assumed the title of Mad God and freed Jyggalag from his curse. The city was still in existence by [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup>

## The Fountainhead
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/0e/SI-place-The_Fountainhead.jpg/200px-SI-place-The_Fountainhead.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SI-place-The_Fountainhead.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SI-place-The_Fountainhead.jpg) The entrance to the Fountainhead **The Fountainhead** is an intricate root system that lies below New Sheoth, and is home to the Pools of Madness. Tamed [gnarls](Lore_Gnarl.md) tend to the root system. The water from the Pools pours into the Font of Madness, and is the very lifeblood of the Isles.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-SIHaskill-4)</sup> The Fountainhead can be accessed from the Palace of Sheogorath through the base of the Tree of Madness, and leads deep under the palace to the Pools of Dementia and Mania.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ESI-5)</sup> Lord Sheogorath often visited the Fountainhead to escape his duties and meditate.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-SIHaskill-4)</sup>

In [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), the Pools of Madness were tainted by [Order](Lore_Jyggalag.md). Sheogorath's Champion, who needed to immerse the [Staff of Sheogorath](Lore_Staff_of_Sheogorath.md) in the waters, defeated the Forces of Order and restored the Pools, imbued the Staff with Madness, and assumed the position of Madgod. This gave the Champion the power necessary to defeat Jyggalag, thus ending the [Greymarch](Lore_Greymarch.md) and the Prince's curse.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ESI-5)</sup>
