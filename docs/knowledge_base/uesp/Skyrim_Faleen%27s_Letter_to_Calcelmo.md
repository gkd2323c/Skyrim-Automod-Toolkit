Faleen's Letter to Calcelmo **by [Faleen](Skyrim_Faleen.md)** *An amorous reply to a love poem*
---
My Dearest [Calcelmo](Skyrim_Calcelmo.md),
I always knew you to possess a great mind, but your charming [poem](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Love_Poem) revealed a depth of feeling I never suspected.
I'll admit to being a bit surprised at the direction of your feelings, but not unpleasantly so. Let us meet to discuss further.

- Yours,
- [Faleen](Skyrim_Faleen.md)
