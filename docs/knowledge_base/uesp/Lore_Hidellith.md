Hidellith was the [King](Lore_Kingdom_of_Alinor.md) of [Alinor](Lore_Alinor_(city).md) in the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md). He was the husband of [Tuinden](Lore_Tuinden.md), and the father of [Ayrenn](Lore_Ayrenn.md) and [Naemon](Lore_Naemon.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TIEG-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ATUQ-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-LCL-3)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/bd/ON-npc-The_Gardener.jpg/200px-ON-npc-The_Gardener.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-The_Gardener.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-The_Gardener.jpg) The Gardener, doubtfully speculated to be Hidellith in disguise Hidellith was believed to be a direct descendant of the [Divines](Lore_Eight_Divines.md) who created [Nirn](Lore_Nirn.md). His rule is remembered as a long and great one, and he exemplified the best traditions of High Elven Ceremoniarchy, basing his every decision on the precedents laid down in the Scrolls of Praxis.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ATUQ-2)</sup> In [2E 486](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_486), a small [Maormeri](Lore_Maormer.md) fleet was sighted off the coast of his kingdom, and King Hidellith ordered his navy to give chase. They followed the fleet to [Pyandonea](Lore_Pyandonea.md), where all but one of the ships were destroyed.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-PGE3Other-4)</sup>

His child with Kinlady [Tuinden](Lore_Tuinden.md) was born on the 5th of Second Seed in [2E 555](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_555). Following the dictates of the Scrolls of Praxis, they named her [Ayrenn](Lore_Ayrenn.md), and held celebrations for fifty-five days. He had other children, including his eldest son Prince [Naemon](Lore_Naemon.md). In [2E 573](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_573), he gathered with his family at the [Crystal Tower](Lore_Crystal_Tower.md) to celebrate what was supposed to be Ayrenn's entry for schooling as a ruler, but his daughter disappeared. She would not return until after Hidellith's death in [2E 580](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_580).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ATUQ-2)</sup>

Some speculated—though with doubt—that the mysterious Gardener of the Garland Ring was, in reality, King Hidellith.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Tanlorin-5)</sup>
