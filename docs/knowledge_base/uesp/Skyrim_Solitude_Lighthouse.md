## Walkthrough

### Exterior
There are several [spiky grass](Skyrim_Grass_Pod.md) plants and a [nirnroot](Skyrim_Nirnroot.md) nearby. Directly west of the [nirnroot](Skyrim_Nirnroot.md), in the small cavity of the rock formation is a bottle of [Skooma](Skyrim_Skooma.md) and an [Empty Skooma Bottle](Skyrim_Empty_Skooma_Bottle.md). They are somewhat difficult to see due to the gravel texture of the ground. The [chest](Skyrim_Chest.md) depicted in [Treasure Map III](Skyrim_Treasure_Map_III.md) is to the northeast, under the cliff edge. As you pass through the outer archway there is a door on your left that leads to the interior, which may be apprentice-locked. Continuing through the inner arch you come to a long spiral stone staircase that leads all the way up to the top and the fire that acts as the light for ships.

### Solitude Lighthouse
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/19/SR-interior-Solitude_Lighthouse.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Solitude_Lighthouse.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Solitude_Lighthouse.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Solitude_Lighthouse.jpg) Ma'zaka sitting in the residence Through the door on the left, you enter what appears to be a normal house, with barrels of [foodstuffs](Skyrim_Food.md) on either side of you, with a wooden table further to the left against the south wall. On the table is a [banded iron shield](Skyrim_Banded_Iron_Shield.md), an [iron helmet](Skyrim_Iron_Helmet.md), an [iron war axe](Skyrim_Iron_War_Axe.md), a [hunting bow](Skyrim_Hunting_Bow.md), and a random [sword](Skyrim_Sword.md). Against the west wall is a set of shelves holding several cheese wheels, two loaves of [bread](Skyrim_Bread.md), a [cabbage](Skyrim_Cabbage.md), and a [potato](Skyrim_Potato.md). To the right is a fireplace with a [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking_Pot.md) in front and a piece of [raw beef](Skyrim_Raw_Beef.md) and a cabbage in a cast iron pot on the hearth. To the right of the fireplace, coming out from the west wall is an internal wall. There is a plate holding [grilled leeks](Skyrim_Grilled_Leeks.md) on top of it, and a stack of two open crates on the floor beside it containing a sample of [fly amanita](Skyrim_Fly_Amanita.md), a [gourd](Skyrim_Gourd.md), a [rock warbler egg](Skyrim_Rock_Warbler_Egg.md) and an [orange dartwing](Skyrim_Orange_Dartwing.md), a [sliced eidar cheese](Skyrim_Sliced_Eidar_Cheese.md) and two loaves of bread.

Along the east wall are two stacked open crates containing a sample of fly amanita, a gourd, a rock warbler egg and an orange dartwing, three [leeks](Skyrim_Leek.md) and two potatoes. To the left of these is a set of shelves holding eight [iron ingots](Skyrim_Iron_Ingot.md), a piece of [iron ore](Skyrim_Iron_Ore.md), a [leather strip](Skyrim_Leather_Strips.md) and a [woodcutter's axe](Skyrim_Woodcutter%27s_Axe.md). Further to the left, still against the east wall are two more stacked crates containing a [goat cheese wheel](Skyrim_Goat_Cheese_Wheel.md), a [sliced goat cheese](Skyrim_Sliced_Goat_Cheese.md), a [goat cheese wedge](Skyrim_Goat_Cheese_Wedge.md), six [garlic](Skyrim_Garlic.md) bulbs, four [tomatoes](Skyrim_Tomato.md), and a cabbage. A small set of shelves next to these holds two potatoes, a portion of grilled leeks, two loaves of bread, a [pheasant](Skyrim_Pheasant.md) breast and a [raw rabbit leg](Skyrim_Raw_Rabbit_Leg.md), a [sliced eidar cheese](Skyrim_Sliced_Eidar_Cheese.md) and an [eidar cheese wedge](Skyrim_Eidar_Cheese_Wedge.md). Hanging above these are four [rabbits](Skyrim_Rabbit_(dead).md), two [salmon](Skyrim_Salmon_(dead).md), and a [pheasant](Skyrim_Pheasant.md).

Behind the internal wall is a table and chairs set for two, with two portions of grilled leeks, two [seared slaughterfish](Skyrim_Seared_Slaughterfish.md), a loaf of bread, an eidar cheese wedge, and a bulb of garlic. Above the table on the west wall is a wall shelf holding a bottle of [alto wine](Skyrim_Alto_Wine.md). Against the north wall, next to the table, is a small set of shelves holding a copy of the [Alteration](Skyrim_Alteration.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[The Lunar Lorkhan](Skyrim_The_Lunar_Lorkhan.md)*. To the right of this is a doorway into Ma'zaka's bedroom, which consists of a single unowned bed with an end table holding a common book and a bottle of [wine](Skyrim_Wine.md). There is a small [coin purse](Skyrim_Coin_Purse.md) beside the bed and an unlocked [chest](Skyrim_Chest.md) at the foot of the bed. On your right as you enter the room is a small unit holding a [horker tusk](Skyrim_Horker_Tusk.md), a [cave bear pelt](Skyrim_Cave_Bear_Pelt.md), a [red mountain flower](Skyrim_Red_Mountain_Flower.md), and a basket containing two bunches of [lavender](Skyrim_Lavender.md). To the left in the northeast corner is a stack of barrels containing foodstuffs.

## Notes
- If the fire here is extinguished, the lighthouse will never be re-lit. If you place torches into the fire pit and travel to somewhere else, the torches and the lighthouse beacon will be lit once you return. However, when viewed from a distance, there will still be no visible light. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Legendary_Edition_Patch), version 3.0.4, fixes this bug.

## Bugs
- A seemingly defined area stretching from the ship to the south up to just northwest of the lighthouse and east of the rocks may cause the game to crash upon entry. - Waiting 31 days for the world cell to reset may resolve this.
