# Cutting Lumber


In order to activate this animation sequence, you must activate an available "Lumber Pile", which will start the activation sequence of pulling a log down to the center of the mill to be cut. You must then activate a lever on the opposite side of the log to start the cutting process. The log will move forward, passing through a large cutter blade to be split down the middle before dropping down into a pile of cut lumber at the end of the mill. A [follower](Skyrim_Followers.md) can also be ordered to activate it, but will only do so once each time they are asked. Cutting lumber is only an animation in the original version of the game; the process yields no results.

## Sawn Logs
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/75/SR-activity-Log_Pile.jpg/200px-SR-activity-Log_Pile.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-activity-Log_Pile.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-activity-Log_Pile.jpg) The log pile located just outside of Windstad Manor **Sawn Logs**<sup>[HF](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md)</sup> are house building materials added by [Hearthfire](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md) which are needed for [building](Skyrim_Construction.md) and [furnishing](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Category:Skyrim-Hearthfire-Furnishing_Options) a self-built home. They are hidden, weightless items ([ID](Skyrim_Form_ID.md): [xx](Skyrim_Form_ID.md) 00300e) which do not show up in your inventory or affect your carry weight. They appear as a "Log Pile" on your property, with the number increasing as more are purchased and decreasing as they are used for building or furnishing a home. If the amount of Sawn Logs in your Log Pile drops to zero, the Log Pile disappears. Buying or sawing more Logs will make the Log Pile show up again.

You can purchase lumber from certain mill owners at 200 gold for 20 sawn logs. You can also ask your personal [steward](Skyrim_Personal_Steward.md) to purchase them for the same price, without having to leave your property. Certain lumber merchants will give you permission to cut the lumber for free if their [disposition](Skyrim_Disposition.md) has been raised high enough (value=1: friend). You will cut 10 at one time when cutting it on your own (but see [bugs](#Bugs)). No matter how you choose to obtain more lumber, it will be delivered to the "Log Pile" on your property at no charge. If you own more than one property, the Log Pile is shared between them - all your lumber can be accessed from any of the three properties.

The following table lists lumber mill owners who will sell sawn logs via dialogue. The 'Quest' column indicates *guaranteed* quests for the lumber merchant in that row which will raise their disposition enough to allow you to cut your own logs for free. You must receive permission via dialogue before activating the mill will yield results. If an entry in the column is blank, there are no guaranteed quests which raise that specific lumber merchant's disposition. However, there are other ways to raise an NPC's disposition, such as certain [radiant](Skyrim_Radiant.md#Quest_NPCs) quests for which the target can be almost any NPC in the game (e.g., [Rescue Mission](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Rescue_Mission)). While some of these quests can still raise a lumber merchant's disposition enough for them to allow you to cut your own lumber for free, they are not listed here because you are not guaranteed to receive a specific NPC as a target for that quest.

## Notes
- The [Dragon Bridge Lumber Camp](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Bridge_Lumber_Camp) is another wood mill which can be used to activate the cutting lumber animation sequence, but you can't purchase lumber there.
- You can increase the disposition of [Temba Wide-Arm](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Temba_Wide-Arm) in [Ivarstead](Skyrim_Ivarstead.md) by either of two means, [chopping wood](Skyrim_Chop_Wood.md) or [bringing her ten bear pelts](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Grin_and_Bear_It). You can even marry her afterwards... but since she doesn't actually offer to sell lumber in the first place, you still cannot cut your own lumber for free at her mill.
- At the [Solitude Sawmill](Skyrim_Solitude_Sawmill.md), you can chop wood for the mill owner [Hjorunn](Skyrim_Hjorunn.md), increasing his disposition, but he isn't the one that sells lumber. He delegates that job to his assistant [Kharag gro-Shurkul](Skyrim_Kharag_gro-Shurkul.md), but there is no way to increase Kharag's disposition unless one of the random radiant quests picks him as a target, and increasing the master's disposition will not increase the employee's. Nor can you chop wood to improve Kharag's disposition if Hjorunn dies and Kharag takes over the mill.
- Likewise, [Bolund](Skyrim_Bolund.md) in Falkreath has a disposition that can only be improved as a result of random quests. If Solaf dies somehow, it is possible that Bolund might take over the Gray Pine Goods store, and his disposition be improved by investing in the store (if you haven't already done so while Solaf runs it), but in that case, he won't be at the mill to give permission for you to cut logs, so there are still no free logs from the mill at Falkreath.
- When [building](Skyrim_Construction.md) a home with the [Hearthfire](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md) add-on, your map marker may send you to [Half-Moon Mill](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Half-Moon_Mill) to buy sawn logs. If [Hert](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hert) is dead, you will not be able to do it there. However, the objective is cleared by buying sawn logs or cutting your own lumber at any mill. (It's not possible to complete the objective by getting a steward to buy lumber, since you need to have already acquired lumber to construct enough of a house to appoint a steward.)
- When sawing your own logs, the owner of the mill has to be at or approaching the mill, otherwise you will not receive the logs after you saw them. This means lumber cannot be sawn at mills whose owner is dead.
- After cutting the lumber and it drops into the lumber pile, you must wait about 10 seconds for the freshly cut log to disappear before it is added as 10 sawn logs.
- The [construction](Skyrim_Farming_Construction.md) options at [Goldenhills Plantation](Skyrim_Goldenhills_Plantation.md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Farming.md)</sup> require [firewood](Skyrim_Firewood.md) instead of sawn logs.
- If you want to do something productive while the log is being cut, you can go chop some firewood. The amount of time it takes is approximately the same as the time needed for the lumber mill to reset.
- 20 sawn log will already be at the construction site as soon as you have bought the property.

## Bugs
- The lumber merchant might not get out of his house during work hours sometimes. - Using the lumber mill may fix this.
- The option to saw your own lumber is available, but will not always yield results, even if the lumberjack's disposition is high enough to allow you to cut your own lumber for free. - Asking the lumber merchant for permission each time you saw logs at their mill may fix this.
- Sawing logs may only yield results if the lumber merchant is at the mill while you do it. (A good location for this is [Anga's Mill](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Anga%27s_Mill), as she stays in the vicinity throughout the day. Alternatively, for vampire characters, [Half-Moon Mill](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Half-Moon_Mill) is a good choice, as the owner, being a vampire herself, will be at the mill at nighttime.)
- You may occasionally be able to view sawn logs in your inventory. When this happens, they use the model for [elven boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Elven_Boots).
- You may occasionally not be able to buy logs from the usual vendors. - This is due to the needed faction not being set on the vendor (BYOHLumber Vendor Faction, [ID](Skyrim_Form_ID.md): [xx](Skyrim_Form_ID.md) 009ba5).
- Fix: Go into the console, select the vendor and use `addfac xx009ba5`.
- When you select Use Lumber Pile, you may get a message that there is already a log waiting to be cut even though the trough is empty and without needing to select the Lumber Pile again, your character will load a log to cut. <sup>**?**</sup>
