[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/dc/SR-item-Movarth%27s_Boots.jpg/200px-SR-item-Movarth%27s_Boots.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Movarth%27s_Boots.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Movarth%27s_Boots.jpg) Movarth's Boots Movarth's Boots are a set of [hide boots](Skyrim_Hide_Boots.md) owned by the [vampire](Skyrim_Vampire.md) [Movarth Piquine](Skyrim_Movarth_Piquine.md). The boots can be found in their [owner's lair](Skyrim_Movarth%27s_Lair.md), in the northeastern portion of the cave, next to a bed. They have been enchanted to [improve sneaking](Skyrim_Fortify_Sneak.md) by fifteen points while worn. Movarth's Boots cannot be disenchanted.

## Bugs
- Movarth's Boots cannot be improved at a [workbench](Skyrim_Workbench.md). - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.5, addresses this issue. It enables upgrading with [Leather](Skyrim_Leather.md) but uses no [Smithing](Skyrim_Smithing.md) perks.
- Movarth's Boots cannot be sold to a vendor since they lack the Vendor Item Armor keyword. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Legendary_Edition_Patch), version 3.0.8, fixes this bug.
