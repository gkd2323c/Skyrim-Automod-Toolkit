[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fe/MER-art-Skyrim_Anthology_Map.jpg/200px-MER-art-Skyrim_Anthology_Map.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MER-art-Skyrim_Anthology_Map.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MER-art-Skyrim_Anthology_Map.jpg) Map of Skyrim The Broken Cape is a cape found on the coast of [Winterhold](Lore_Winterhold_(region).md), in the province of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md). The city of [Winterhold](Lore_Winterhold_(city).md) is located on the northern tip, which is known as [Hsaarik Head](Lore_Hsaarik_Head.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SRMap-1)</sup> In the late- [Merethic Era](Lore_Merethic_Era.md), a fleet of Atmorans, led by [Ysgramor](Lore_Ysgramor.md), sailed across the [Sea of Ghosts](Lore_Sea_of_Ghosts.md) and landed on Hsaarik Head after fleeing from a civil war in [Atmora](Lore_Atmora.md). From there, they built their first city, [Saarthal](Lore_Saarthal.md), which was subsequently sacked by the [Snow Elves](Lore_Snow_Elf.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Bt Ao M-2)</sup> In its vicinity, northeast of the ruined city, lies Winterhold.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-PGE1E-3)</sup> Portions of the Broken Cape fell into the Sea of Ghosts because of the [Great Collapse](Lore_Great_Collapse.md) of [4E 122](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_122).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-OBCodex SRMap-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Kraldar-5)</sup>

The [College of Winterhold](Lore_College_of_Winterhold.md) extends off the coast from the eponymous city. Several ruins such as the Skytemple Ruins, the [Pilgrim's Trench](Lore_Pilgrim%27s_Trench.md), and Ysgramor's Tomb are found between the glaciers and shipwrecks that are spread across the coast.
