# Concept Art


A [video](http://www.gameinformer.com/b/features/archive/2011/01/18/skyrim-the-time-lapse-art-video.aspx) showing the construction of a piece of art was featured on the Game Informer website. Bethesda has also released a "[team diary](http://www.elderscrolls.com/community/completely-blue-sky-concept-skyrim-0/)" that included a video of examples of concept art being shown alongside their finished models.

Following the death of [Adam Adamowicz](https://adam.bethsoft.com/), Bethesda paid tribute by releasing his concept art. For these galleries, see the following sub-pages: [Page One](Skyrim_Concept_Art_1.md), [Page Two](Skyrim_Concept_Art_2.md), [Page Three](Skyrim_Concept_Art_3.md), and [Page Four](Skyrim_Concept_Art_4.md).
