# Atronachy

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/43/DF-quest-Hunt_For_An_Atronach.jpg/200px-DF-quest-Hunt_For_An_Atronach.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-quest-Hunt_For_An_Atronach.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-quest-Hunt_For_An_Atronach.jpg) A [fire atronach](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Fire_Atronach) created with the use of Atronachy Atronachy is an [arcane art](Lore_Magic.md) focused on giving life to inanimate material.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AHing-1)</sup> The [Mages Guild](Lore_Mages_Guild.md) was known to offer atronachy classes in the region of the [Iliac Bay](Lore_Iliac_Bay.md) as of [3E 405](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_405), during which they conducted experiments in the creation of atronach [golems](Lore_Golem.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AHing-1)</sup> The creation of these golems is a separate form of magic from [Atromancy](Lore_Atromancy.md), the [summoning](Lore_Conjuration.md) of [Daedric Atronachs](Lore_Elemental_Daedra.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Atromancy-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ATRQUEST-3)</sup> Golems are typically given life by imbuing them with them with a mortal [soul](Lore_Soul.md) or Daedric [vestige](Lore_Vestige_(soul).md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AHing-1)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-KCAHC-4)</sup> Though some are animated by other forms of magic such as [blood magic](Lore_Blood_Magic.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup>

During one routine atronachy class, a test subject—a golem—escaped. In order to prevent potential fallout from the creature's rampage, the Guild hired an [adventurer](Lore_Agent.md) to eliminate the rogue golem. Rumors circulated that the creature had been created using the soul of a member of the local ruler's family, though these claims remained unsubstantiated.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AHing-1)</sup>
