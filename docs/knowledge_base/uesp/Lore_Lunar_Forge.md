[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/68/SR-place-Silent_Moons_Camp_02.jpg/200px-SR-place-Silent_Moons_Camp_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Silent_Moons_Camp_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Silent_Moons_Camp_02.jpg) The Lunar Forge circa [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201) The Lunar Forge is a forge with special properties built on top of the **Silent Moons Camp**, a [Nordic](Lore_Nord.md) ruin located northwest of [Whiterun](Lore_Whiterun.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SMC-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-NOTL-2)</sup>

Little is known of the history of the forge or the ruins. The forge was set up on top of the ruin, at some point after the ruined buildings were constructed. It was set up in close proximity to [moonstone](Lore_Moonstone.md) and [gold](Lore_Gold.md) ore veins.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SMC-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-NOTL-2)</sup> As of [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), Silent Moons Camp served as a hideout for bandits,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SMC-1)</sup> who conspired against Jarl [Balgruuf the Greater](Lore_Balgruuf_the_Greater.md) and planned his assassination.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-AJ-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-SHN-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ITS-5)</sup> They were stopped by the [Last Dragonborn](Lore_Last_Dragonborn.md), who killed the assassin and claimed their [Bow of Shadows](Lore_Bow_of_Shadows.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ITS-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-BOS-6)</sup>

Although the [weapons](Lore_Weapons.md) [crafted](Lore_Smithing.md) in the Lunar Forge were made from mundane metals, they possessed unique properties. Those weapons were known as the **Lunar Weapons**. Although they were not [enchanted](Lore_Enchanting.md) through the traditional means, they possessed innate magical abilities likened to an enchantment. Though those powers were connected to the appearance of the [moons](Lore_Moons.md) in the sky, their secret remained elusive. Some sources claimed that Lunar Weapons possess a "[vampire](Lore_Vampirism.md) -like" ability to drain a small amount of health from the victim to the wielder,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-NOTL-2)</sup> but those weapons were in fact known for their light-based powers, which burned those struck by them. Those powers worked only when the moons were seen in the night sky.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-LD-7)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-LW-8)</sup>

## Notes
- There are numerous special crafting stations scattered across [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md), known to provide unique effects to weapons, armor, and jewelry created in such places.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-Felarian-9)</sup><sup>[[10]](#cite_note-CS-10)</sup><sup>[[11]](#cite_note-CS2-11)</sup> The creative skill of the smiths who have worked these forges has imbued the sites with the divine spark of creativity, transforming the local reality such that items crafted there possess certain unique attributes.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-12)</sup> Arcane power lingers in some of those areas.<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-MT-13)</sup> The Lunar Forge may be one of such places.
- Silent Moons breed of [sheep](Lore_Sheep.md) is native to the areas surrounding [Whiterun](Lore_Whiterun.md), and shares its name from the location of the forge.<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-SMS-14)</sup>
