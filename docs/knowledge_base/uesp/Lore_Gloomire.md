[2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) Gloommire (also spelled **Gloomire**)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOLive Blackwood-1)</sup> is a swampy region on the southern side of [Blackwood](Lore_Blackwood.md), in the province of [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Bo G-2)</sup> It borders the Blackwood Bog to the north and the Niben Forest to the west. The city of [Gideon](Lore_Gideon.md), originally inhabited by the [Kothringi](Lore_Kothringi.md), can be found here. Notable locations include [Glenbridge](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Glenbridge), [Rockgrove](Lore_Rockgrove.md), [Stonewastes](Lore_Stonewastes.md), and [Xi-Tsei](Lore_Xi-Tsei.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESO-3)</sup>

## History
Although routes from [Shadowfen](Lore_Shadowfen.md) and [Leyawiin](Lore_Leyawiin.md) exist, the roads to Gloommire are often treacherous and are closed during monsoon season.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup> The area around Gideon was once considered [Kothringi](Lore_Kothringi.md) homelands before the race was wiped out by the [Knahaten Flu](Lore_Knahaten_Flu.md) of [2E 560](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_560).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-AVAYQ-5)</sup>

In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), a trade deal was brokered between Gideon and Leyawiin and the road to Gloommire was reopened.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-AVAYQ-5)</sup>

## Fauna
Birds such as [crows](Lore_Crow.md) and [hackwings](Lore_Hackwing.md) can be found here.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Bo G-2)</sup> Large frogs known as [death hoppers](Lore_Death_Hopper.md) are also found in the region.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-TOTDH-7)</sup>

## Notes
- Gloommire is based on an early version of Murkmire (also called Dregmire) which was originally meant to be centered around Gideon before later being re-purposed to focus on Lilmoth.
