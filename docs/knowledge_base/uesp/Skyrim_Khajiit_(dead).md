# Khajiit (dead)


These **Khajiit** are all female [citizens](Skyrim_Citizen_(class).md) and wear randomly determined farm [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md) (7 variations) or a [belted tunic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Belted_Tunic), a pair of [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots) (4 variations), with a 50% chance for a [hat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hat) (3 variations). Additionally, there is a slim chance they may carry a [lockpick](Skyrim_Lockpick.md) and a small amount of gold.

## Notes
- You may [randomly encounter](Skyrim_World_Interactions.md) an unnamed Khajiit wandering the roads of Skyrim. He will offer to sell you some [skooma](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Skooma) and may become hostile depending on your answer. For more details, see the [Skooma Dealer](Skyrim_Skooma_Dealer_(NPC).md) page.
